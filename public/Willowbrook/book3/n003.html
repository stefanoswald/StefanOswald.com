Night 3

Sad Is Heavy

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

The red balloon went flat.

Overnight. All by itself.

It's small now. Wrinkled.

It doesn't float. It just... sits.

And here comes a feeling.

Not hot, like frustrated.

Heavy.

It makes the shoulders droop.

It makes the bottom stair

the only place to sit.

This one is called sad.

Papa sits down next to her.

He doesn't say "it's just a balloon."

(It was never just a balloon.)

He says: "That's sad, huh."

Yes. It is. It's sad.

They sit with it together.

Sad is lighter with two people

holding it.

That's the thing about sad...

it's heavy,

but it's carryable.

Especially with help.

After a while, Papa says,

"Should we save the string?"

They save the string.

For remembering.

Sad likes to keep something.
