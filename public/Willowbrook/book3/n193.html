Night 193

The Flush Monster That Wasn't

Month 7: Potty Power

The little potty: beloved.

The BIG toilet: complicated.

Specifically: the FLUSH.

The big flush is LOUD.

A sudden roar. A swallowing.

And Olivia, champion

of the small throne...

kept her distance from

the big one's temper.

"It's too ROARY."

Fair. So: the lamp trick.

(The worry corner's method.

Look at the scary thing

together. Inspect it.)

The ceremony, conducted

from a safe step back:

Papa flushes. They watch.

"Where does da roar

come from?"

The water, rushing. Just

water, in a hurry.

"Why so LOUD?"

Big pipe. Big hurry.

Like the brook in spring...

remember the melt-thunder?

Loud water isn't mad water.

It's just water with a job.

Flush two: one step closer.

Flush three: HER hand

on the handle, Papa's over it.

ROAR. Giggle.

"BYE, water! Do your job!"

The roar, renamed: the hurry.

The monster, retired:

never was one.

Just plumbing,

enthusiastic about its work.

Inspection closes the case.

It closes every case.

Bring the lamp. Every time.
