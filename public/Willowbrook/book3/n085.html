Night 85

The Friend Who Isn't Here Yet

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

Bedtime confidences.

Girl to bear. As always.

"Baba. Somefing happening."

(The bear listens. His whole point.)

"Mama smiles at nofing.

Papa keeps hugging her.

And dey whisper. LOTS."

(All true. The bear has

noticed too. Bears notice

everything. Dresser training.)

"And Mama cried at breakfast

but she said HAPPY cried.

Happy-cried, Baba.

At TOAST."

(The bear absorbs this.)

"Somefing is coming.

I fink... somefing GOOD?"

Oh, little one.

Something is coming.

Something so good.

The best kind of friend

there is...

the kind you don't meet

at a store or a bench.

The kind that comes to live

with you. Forever.

The kind you wait for

through a whole magic winter.

But that's next month's story.

Tonight, just this:

"Somefing good," she tells the bear.

Something good, agrees the house,

which is already, quietly,

beginning to make room.

Sleep, big sister.

(You don't know that word yet.

It's on its way.

It's the best word coming.)
