Night 41

The Big Girl Bed Arrives

Month 2: Me Do It

Big boxes today!

Papa has tools out.

Something is being built

in Olivia's room.

It's... a bed.

A BIG GIRL BED.

Low to the ground.

A little rail on one side.

Her quilt, ready to move in.

The crib, the old country,

two whole years of nights...

stands watching its successor

rise plank by plank.

Olivia supervises.

(Hands Papa the wrong tool

at every request. He thanks her

every time. The bureau's way.)

"When it's ready," says Mama,

"YOU climb in yourself.

No lifting. No rail to wait behind.

Your bed. Your legs. Your choice."

Me-do-it... at BED?

The biggest me-do-it yet.

She pats the new mattress.

Bounces it once, testing.

The bear is placed on it.

Trial run. He approves.

Tonight: the crib, one last time.

Tomorrow: the new country.

Big month, little one.

The whole room is growing with you.
