Night 60

The Cookie at the Grocery Store

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

This month tells one big story.

The family's favorite story.

It happened THIS month, for real...

and it starts with a cookie.

The grocery store. Tuesday.

The weekly shop.

Mama with the list.

Olivia in the cart seat,

surveying her rolling kingdom.

And at the bakery counter,

the clerk leaned down

with a little cookie.

"For the young lady."

Free! A cookie! For her!

"This is SO GOOD, Mama!"

Mama smiled a certain smile.

The family smile.

"Maybe," she said.

(You know that word.

You know something's coming

when that word comes out.)

The cookie was sweet.

The cookie was ALSO

full of zoom.

Cookies before lunch are.

And a zoomy girl

in a big bright store...

Well.

Turn the page tomorrow.

The story's just warming up.

(It's a good one. The best one.

It ends with a best friend

and spaghetti.

But no peeking. Sleep first.)
