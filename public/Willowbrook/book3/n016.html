Night 16

Where Does Mad Go?

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

This morning: a big mad.

The boots were wrong.

(Both of them. Somehow.)

The storm boomed ten whole minutes.

And now, look...

it's gone.

Olivia is calm. Playing.

Boots on. (The same boots.)

"Mama? Where mad GO?"

What a question!

Where DOES it go?

"Same place storms go," says Mama.

"It rains itself out.

The clouds empty.

The sky clears.

The mad isn't in you anymore...

it moved through you.

Like weather through a town."

The town is still here.

The town is fine.

A little rain-washed. That's all.

Feelings are visitors.

Even the loud ones.

ESPECIALLY the loud ones.

They come, they boom, they go.

And you...

you're the town.

You stay.

You're always still here after.

That's the most important part.
