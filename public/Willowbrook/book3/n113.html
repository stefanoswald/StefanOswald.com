Night 113

Lily's Promise

Month 4: Waiting for Baby

Thursday. The fort. Grave business.

Lily has been thinking.

(Eleven days of wisdom.

Plus a cousin. She knows

how babies change things.)

"Livy. When da baby comes...

you be busy."

"I be da BIG SISTER."

"I know." A pause.

The hard part:

"...You still my best friend?"

Oh.

OH.

The worry, the very same one,

the will-you-still-love-me...

lives in Lily too.

Everyone's heart asks it

when the circle grows.

Olivia answers the way

Mama answered her.

(Everything poured in

comes back out. Watch:)

"Wiwi. My love doesn't

get littler. It gets MORE.

Like candles."

(The candle theology,

translated to fort-speak.

Close enough. Perfect, actually.)

"You my best friend FOREVER.

Da baby is my SISTER-friend

or my BRUDDER-friend.

Different shelf."

Different shelf!

Lily considers. Nods slowly.

Then, her own offer:

"...I share you, den.

And I help wif da baby.

I know babies. My cousin."

Deal.

Sealed the way Lily's cousin

taught her: pinkies, linked.

(New technology. Adopted

instantly and forever.)

The circle pre-widened,

friend-side.

The baby doesn't know it yet,

but it just got

a second big sister.

Blue jacket division.
