Night 4

Mad Is a Thunderstorm

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

Lily went home.

Olivia wanted MORE Lily.

And here comes the loud one...

MAD.

Mad is a thunderstorm.

It booms in the feet. STOMP!

It flashes in the face, red!

It knocks the tower flying...

CRASH!

Mad is big weather.

Mama doesn't yell at storms.

You can't. It just makes two storms.

She stays close. Steady.

Like the ground under thunder.

"You're mad. Lily left.

You wanted more."

YES. THAT'S IT. THAT'S THE WHOLE THING.

The storm booms a while.

Storms do.

Then... the booms slow down.

The rain part comes. (A few tears.)

Then the after part. Quiet.

Mama opens her arms.

In she comes.

Every storm in this house

lands here.

Every single one.
