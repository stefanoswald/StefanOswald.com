Night 50

The Water Fountain

Month 2: Me Do It

At the park: the fountain.

The drinking one. The button one.

Big kids use it all day.

Casual. One-handed. Cool.

Today: her turn.

"ME do it."

The step stool of the park:

that concrete ledge. Up.

Now the button, press...

WATER! An arc of it!

(Surprise! Release! Gone.)

Press again, arc. LEAN...

splutter, nose water!...

retreat.

Regroup.

The timing... press AND lean...

together...

press-lean...

SIP.

A real sip! From the arc!

Cold! Amazing! MORE.

Press-lean-sip.

Press-lean-sip.

She drinks like the big kids now.

(Wetter than the big kids.

Details.)

Some independence is huge...

beds, sandwiches, sovereignty.

And some is exactly this size:

a button, an arc,

a cold sip you caught yourself

on a warm day

at your own park.

Both kinds count.

Collect them all.
