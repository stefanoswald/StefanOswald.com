Night 187

The Tiny Underpants

Month 7: Potty Power

A week of mostly-sunshine.

The signals: arriving on time.

The throne: in regular session.

Which means: THE TRIP.

The underpants trip.

The shop. The little rack.

And the selection...

hers, per the sovereignty

statutes, entirely:

"DUCKS. DESE ONES.

DUCKS ON DA BUTT."

(Duck underpants.

Of course they make them.

Of course she found them.

The universe provides

for its faithful.)

Six pairs. Duck-butted.

Plus one pair with stars...

"for FANCY", approved.

Home. The drawer. The ceremony:

diapers, moved to the

nighttime shelf.

(Nights come later.

Months later. Different

plumbing, different timeline,

zero rush. The doctrine

covers nights too:

patience, protection,

no shame in the sleeping

body's own schedule.)

Days belong to the ducks now.

She wore them out of the store.

(Over nothing. Under her dress.

The confidence of champions.)

And at the market, inevitably,

to the checkout clerk:

"I GOT DUCKS ON MY BUTT."

(The clerk. Deadpan. Saintly.

Forever unshaken:

"Excellent choice.")

It was an excellent choice.

The ducks served with honor.

The era of the little

underpants: begun.

Small laundry. Giant leap.
