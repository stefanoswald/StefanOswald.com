Night 31

Stella and the Weather Report

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

Month's end. The watch reports.

"Big month below," says Stella.

"The small one learned

the weather words."

"Mmm?" hums the Moon.

"All of them. Mad, sad, scared,

the fizzy one, the heavy one.

She names them now.

Right out loud.

Names her mama's. Her papa's.

Named a CHICKEN'S on Sunday."

"Mmmmm," hums the Moon...

impressed, in Moon.

"And here's the part

you'll like best," says Stella.

"They taught her

she's not the weather.

She's the sky."

The Moon is quiet a moment.

The real sky, holding them both,

seems to listen.

"Mmm," hums the Moon at last.

Which means: then she knows

the oldest secret we keep up here.

We've watched every storm

that ever crossed that town.

The town is always still there.

The sky is always still blue behind.

And the small ones who learn it early...

"...weather anything," Stella finishes.

Anything at all.

Twinkle, once, for yes.

Goodnight, little sky.

Goodnight, Willowbrook.
