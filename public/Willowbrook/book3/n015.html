Night 15

Two Feelings at Once

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

The best playdate ever

just ended.

Olivia is at the window,

waving at Lily's stroller...

smiling...

AND crying.

Both?!

BOTH.

Wave-smile-tears, all together.

"Oh, sweetheart," says Mama,

scooping her up.

"You're happy AND sad.

At the very same time."

Can you BE both?

You can.

Feelings share rooms all the time.

Happy-sad: the goodbye feeling.

Excited-scared: the first-day feeling.

Proud-tired: the big-climb feeling.

Hearts are big houses.

Lots of rooms.

Sometimes two lights on at once.

That's not broken.

That's just a heart,

working at full size.

"Happy-sad," says Olivia. Sniffling.

Smiling.

Exactly.

One of the truest feelings there is.

You'll meet it at every goodbye

worth having.
