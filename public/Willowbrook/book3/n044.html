Night 44

The Jacket Flip Trick

Month 2: Me Do It

Rosa taught Mama a trick.

An old preschool trick.

The jacket flip!

Watch:

Lay the jacket on the floor.

UPSIDE down. Collar at her toes.

Now, arms in the sleeves...

both arms...

and FLIP!

Whoosh, over the head...

THE JACKET IS ON.

Right side up! Arms in!

SORCERY.

Olivia stares at her own arms.

Jacketed. By her.

In two seconds.

"AGAIN."

Off it comes. Floor. Arms. FLIP!

ON.

"AGAIN!"

Ten more flips.

The jacket has never been

put on so many times

in one morning.

This is the best kind of trick:

one that hands the whole job

to the smallest person.

No more jacket wrestling.

No more helping hands.

Just floor, arms, whoosh.

Me-do-it: powered up.

(Thank you, Rosa.

The bakery knows everything.)
