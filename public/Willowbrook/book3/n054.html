Night 54

Grandpa's Workshop Rules

Month 2: Me Do It

Sunday. The workshop.

Where the chair was born.

And today, a big day...

Olivia is ALLOWED IN.

Working allowed in.

Grandpa's workshop has two rules.

He says them slow:

"Rule one: ask before touching.

Rule two: everything back

where it lives."

That's it. Two rules.

(The sharp things live high.

The rules live low.

That's the real system.)

Her station: the low bench.

Her tools: one sanding block,

one scrap of pine.

Her job: "Make it smooth."

She sands.

Shff... shff... shff...

(Technique: devoted.

Progress: gradual.

The pine is patient.)

Grandpa works beside her.

His shff is bigger. Same song.

An hour, they sand together.

Mostly quiet. All company.

At the end, she checks:

smooth?

He runs his old thumb over it.

"Smooth as a duck's back."

The scrap goes home in her pocket.

First thing she ever made

in that workshop.

Not the last.

Not by decades.
