Night 10

Calm Is the Pond

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

One more feeling this week.

The quietest one.

Come to the pond. Look.

No wind today.

The water is flat. Still. Soft.

The ducks leave slow lines.

The sky rests on it.

This, this exact thing...

is what calm feels like.

Calm is the pond inside you.

Sometimes the wind blows...

mad-wind, scared-wind...

and the water gets choppy.

That's okay. Water does that.

But it always flattens again.

Want to help it?

Breathe like this:

In... (smell a flower)

Out... (blow a dandelion)

In... out...

Feel it? The flattening?

The pond, coming back?

Olivia breathes. The pond returns.

This trick fits in your pocket.

It goes everywhere you go.

It works your whole life.

Flower in. Dandelion out.

Welcome back, still water.
