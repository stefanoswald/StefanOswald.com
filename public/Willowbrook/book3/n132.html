Night 132

Lily Meets the Baby

Month 5: Theo Arrives

The most important visitor

saved for when things settled:

LILY.

The introduction, conducted

under full protocol.

(Her protocols. Enforced

door to crib.)

"Wash hands. Sit on da couch.

Pillow on your lap. Ready?"

Ready.

And the bundle, delivered

by Mama, arranged

by his sister...

into the lap of the blue jacket.

Lily looks down.

The famous cousin-expertise

dissolving into plain wonder.

"...He's SO small."

"I KNOW. He gets bigger.

I'm feeding him words."

"Hi, baby," whispers Lily.

And then, the pledge...

made in the fort months ago,

delivered now in person:

"I'm Wiwi. I'm your sister's

best friend. So I'm YOUR

friend too. Dat's how it works.

I know babies. I got a cousin.

You're gonna be okay."

(Theo grips her finger.

The move. His one move.

Lily gasps. "HE GOT ME."

He got you. He gets everyone.

It's how he says welcome.)

Two big sisters now,

the blue-jacket division

fully staffed.

The circle: widened. Held.

The fort's saved spot:

one occupant closer.

"When can he PLAY?"

Soon-ish. Months-ish.

"We can WAIT," rules Lily.

They can wait.

They're very good at Thursdays.

Now Thursdays have three.
