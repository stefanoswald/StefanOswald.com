Night 197

The Big Toilet Graduation

Month 7: Potty Power

The little potty served

with honor.

But the world runs on

BIG toilets.

(The tour proved it.

Eleven bathrooms. Zero

little thrones among them.)

So: the graduation kit.

The seat insert. (Duck-

adjacent yellow. Naturally.)

The step stool, the star

one, second posting.

And the ascent:

step up... turn... the

mountaineering of it...

SEATED.

On the BIG one.

Feet on the stool.

(Feet planted matter.

The books are firm.

Dangling feet, unhappy

plumbing. Stool stays.)

"I'm SO high up."

Regally high. The big

throne. The adult

altitude.

And the flush, the old

roary hurry. HER handle

now, no hand over hers:

ROAR.

"BYE! GOOD HURRYING!"

The little potty enters

its sunset season...

backup duty, nighttime

proximity, eventual

honorable harbor with

the keepsake fleet.

(Where the little tubs

and swaddles rest.

The shelf of outgrown

harbors. It gains members

every book.)

The big world's plumbing:

scaled, seated, flushed.

One insert and one stool

from fully fluent.

The tour will need

re-reviewing...

from the new altitude.

The library flush

awaits its champion.
