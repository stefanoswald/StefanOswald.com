Night 2

Happy Lives in the Whole Body

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

Ding-dong!

Olivia runs to the window.

It's LILY!

And here comes the feeling...

the golden one...

HAPPY.

Look where happy lives:

In the feet, they bounce!

In the hands, they clap!

In the voice, "WIWI!"

In the cheeks, up they go!

Happy doesn't fit in one place.

It fills the whole Olivia,

top to toes,

like sunshine poured in.

The door opens.

Running hug!

Two happies, colliding!

(Happies love colliding.

They double.)

"You're happy!" laughs Mama.

Yes. That's this one's name.

Happy.

The easiest one to spot.

It sparkles when it moves.
