Night 13

Frustrated and the Zipper

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

The zipper won't zip.

Try again. Won't zip.

AGAIN. WON'T.

Here it comes, the hot one...

frustrated.

(We met it on banana day.

It's back. It visits often

when you're two.)

Frustrated wants to throw the jacket.

Frustrated wants to yell.

Wait.

Remember the pond trick?

Flower in...

dandelion out...

Now look at the zipper. Closely.

The little teeth aren't lined up.

Wiggle the bottom... there...

now pull...

ZZZZIP.

IT ZIPPED.

Olivia zipped it.

Here's the secret about frustrated:

it shows up right before learning.

Every time.

It's not a stop sign.

It's a doorbell.

Something new is almost yours...

frustrated is just the hard part

ringing.

Breathe. Wiggle. Try again.

The door opens.
