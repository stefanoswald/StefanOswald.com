Night 338

The Remember Game: The Naming Year

Month 12: Almost Three

Birthday month means

THE REMEMBER GAME.

(The serial. The origin

liturgy. Founded two

books ago; run every

year since; hers now,

demanded by name.)

Part one: the naming

year.

"Once upon a time...

a true one, you turned

two, and you were FULL

of weather. Big hot

feelings with no names.

So we named them.

Remember?

Happy, that lives in

the whole body.

Sad, that's heavy but

carryable.

Mad, the thunderstorm.

Scared, with the big

ears.

And you learned the

biggest thing...

say it with me..."

"DA FEELINGS ARE

WEVVER..."

"...and you are..."

"DA SKY."

The sky. Still the sky.

Always the sky.

"And then you did

EVERYTHING yourself.

The jacket flip. The

big bed. The pouring.

You wore your buttons

crooked to the whole

town and the whole

town saluted."

(The album's pages,

turning under her own

hands now, the crooked-

sweater photo found,

cackled at:

"My buttons were SO

wrong."

"Your buttons were

perfect.")

Part one: told.

The teller and the

told, the same person

more and more each

year, which is the

game's whole quiet

design.

"More," at the lamp's

dimming.

Tomorrow: the friend

part.

"Da CRASH part?"

The crash part.

The best part.

Sleep fast.
