Night 340

The Remember Game: The Tools Year

Month 12: Almost Three

Part three. The tools.

"And THEN came the

storm season. The

banana. The market

floor. Thunder

everywhere.

And what did we build?"

The inventory, recited

by its owner like

a knight before

a quest:

"Da cozy corner.

Da body map.

Da roar stone.

Da four checkers.

Da five breafs...

da fist opens EMPTY.

Da repair. Arms first.

Da sorry-flower.

I invented dat one."

(She invented that one.

The family canon

credits her by name.)

"And then the counting..."

"One two free four

FIVE. Da turns. Da

five-more-minutes.

Da stairs go past

five FOREVER."

"And when things were

too hard..."

"YET. Yet means it's

COMING. Da trike came.

Da kite came. Da

puzzle came. Da

spaghetti came..."

(said perfectly,

flourished:

"spa-GHETTI.")

"And the kindness..."

"Is a MUSCLE. It grows

when you use it.

Da hardest rep is

being kind to your

own self.

And da bench one.

Sitting close and

staying."

The whole kit. The

whole year. Carried

now the way knights

carry things:

lightly, because

they're trained

into the arms.

"You built all that

in one year," said

Papa.

"WE built it," she

corrected.

We built it.

Correct as always.

Nobody builds a kit

alone.

That's the first

tool in it.
