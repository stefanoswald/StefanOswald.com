Night 168

The Rocking Preview

Month 6: Big Sister School

The mat. The gym. And NEWS.

Theo is UP.

On all fours.

Hands planted. Knees under.

And... rocking.

Forward. Back. Forward. Back.

An engine, revving.

A rocket, counting down.

The coach LOSES it.

"MAMA! LOOK! He's doing

da rocking! Da before-crawling!

I did dis! You TOLD me I did dis!"

(She did. The books hold it.

The rocking weeks. The launch.

Backward first, famously,

under the coffee table,

indignant.)

So the coach issues

her forecast, from experience:

"Fee-oh. Listen.

You gonna rock like dat

for some days.

Den you gonna GO.

And here's da secret..."

(she leans in; this is

insider knowledge)

"...you might go BACKWARDS first.

Everybody does. It's fine.

You'll end up under da table.

I'll come get you.

I know da way out."

The rocket rocks on.

The countdown runs.

Launch: forecast within weeks.

And when it comes...

backward, per tradition,

under furniture, per tradition...

somebody will be there

who knows the way out.

She's been under

that exact table.

That's what big siblings ARE:

the ones who've been

under the table already,

waiting at the edge of

everything, calling you

through.
