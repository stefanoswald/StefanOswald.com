Night 5

Scared Has Big Ears

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

A sound in the hallway.

THUMP.

Olivia freezes.

Her eyes go big.

Her shoulders go up.

Her feet grow roots.

This one is called scared.

Scared has big ears.

It hears everything twice.

It makes small sounds huge.

"Something thumped, huh," says Papa.

"Let's go look together."

Together. That's the magic word.

Scared shrinks when you say it.

Hand in hand, they creep...

around the corner...

and there...

the culprit...

Papa's coat. On the floor.

It slipped off its hook.

THAT was the thump.

A coat!

Olivia laughs. The wobbliest laugh.

Scared pops like a bubble

when you look right at it.

That's its weakness.

Looking. Together.

Works every time.
