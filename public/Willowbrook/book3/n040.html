Night 40

Doing It for Bobby

Month 2: Me Do It

A scene at the nursery table.

Olivia is teaching.

Her student: the bear.

The lesson: drinking from a cup.

His way. By himself.

She wraps his paws around it.

"You do it, Baba."

She guides the cup up...

"Good job! You did it!"

(He did nothing. He's a bear.

He's honored anyway.)

Next lesson: the sock.

His foot, the heart one...

gets a sock pulled on.

Wait, no...

"YOU do it."

The sock is placed in his paws.

A pause, for bear-effort.

Then, assisted, on.

"YOU DID IT, BABA!"

Applause. For the bear.

Listen to what's happening:

everything poured in

comes back out.

The patience. The waiting hands.

The you-did-it.

She got it all week.

Now she gives it.

The bear is being raised

exactly as she is...

slow, kind, and sovereign.

He could not be

in better hands.
