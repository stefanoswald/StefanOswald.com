Night 23

The Sorry Feeling

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

It happened fast.

Olivia swung the bucket, whee!...

and it bonked Lily.

Right on the arm.

Lily cried.

And Olivia stood very still,

with a brand-new feeling

sinking in her middle.

Heavy. Squirmy. Hot-cheeked.

This one is called sorry.

Nobody had to scold it into her.

It came by itself.

That's the amazing thing about sorry...

it grows wild in most hearts,

right next to love.

It hurts because you care.

"You didn't mean to," says Mama.

"AND her arm hurts.

Both are true. What helps?"

Olivia knows the kit.

She pats the arm. Gentle.

"Sorry, Wiwi."

And, her own idea...

she hands over the bucket.

The whole bucket. To keep. For now.

Lily sniffles. Takes it.

Scoops sand.

Hands Olivia the shovel.

Repaired.

That's what sorry is FOR.

Not for feeling bad.

For fixing things.

Feel it, say it, mend it, play on.
