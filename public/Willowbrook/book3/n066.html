Night 66

Life Happens FOR Us

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

Tucked in. The big bed.

The day still glowing on her

like sauce stains. (Some literal.)

And Mama sat on the edge,

and told her the whole thing back.

"Today, you ran away in the store.

That was scary. That seemed BAD."

Nod. It had seemed bad.

"But because you ran...

you crashed into Lily.

And because you crashed...

the mamas stood talking.

And because we talked...

you two swapped the carts.

And because of the swap...

we had tonight.

The best spaghetti night

there ever was."

Olivia's eyes, working it through.

Bad... became good?

The bad parts MADE the good parts?

"That's the secret, my love.

The one I'll tell you

a thousand times:

Life doesn't happen TO us.

Life happens FOR us.

Things that seem bad

have a way of turning...

wait for it, watch for it...

into something wonderful."

"...Maybe?" whispers Olivia.

"Maybe," smiles Mama.

"That's exactly the word.

That's why we say it.

Now you know."

Now she knows.

Sleep, spaghetti girl.

You lived the family story today.

You'll tell it yourself someday...

to somebody small,

on the edge of a bed,

after some beautiful disaster.

Goodnight.
