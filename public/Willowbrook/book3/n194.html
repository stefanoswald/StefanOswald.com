Night 194

Duck Gets Potty Trained

Month 7: Potty Power

Duck the doll has news:

she's potty training.

Her owner decided.

The program, replicated

with terrifying accuracy:

The potty: a block. (Throne-

shaped enough.)

The pressure: none.

"No hurry, Duck. When

you're ready."

(The doctrine! Verbatim!

The house eavesdrops,

delighted.)

The first try: "Nofing came.

DAT'S OKAY. You TRIED.

High five."

(A knit hand, high-fived.)

The something, eventually:

announced at full volume...

"DUCK DID A SOMEFING!"...

paraded, strutted, celebrated

at championship rates.

The accident, staged

dutifully: "Whoops. Rain

happened. Everybody rains,

Duck. Ask Papa."

(Papa, passing: "It's true,

Duck. Historically.")

And the underpants:

a doll-sized pair, begged

off the aunts by wire.

Ducks on them.

(Ducks on Duck's butt.

The aunts outdid themselves.

The aunts always do.)

Watch what's happening:

everything received,

replicated. The patience,

the celebration, the

no-shame rain. The way

she was taught is now

HOW SHE TEACHES.

It always will be.

That's the realest reason

to do all of it gently:

you're not just raising

a child.

You're raising how she'll

raise everything after.

Duck is in excellent hands.

So, someday, is everyone

downstream.
