Night 184

The First Try

Month 7: Potty Power

It happened after bath.

Her own idea. The whole point.

"I try da potty. For REAL."

The protocol, deployed:

Seated. Settled. A book

provided. (The bear one.

Thrones need literature.

This family's certainly do.)

And then...

waiting.

Reading.

Waiting.

Nothing.

Nothing whatsoever happened.

Five minutes of committed

nothing.

"All done," she announced.

"Nofing came."

And here, mark it...

the doctrine's whole hinge:

"You TRIED! You sat and

you tried! That's the thing!"

The high-five. The pride.

The celebration...

IDENTICAL to what success

will get. Same wattage.

Because the trying IS

the skill right now.

The body's timing comes

when it comes. The sitting,

the willingness, the idea

that this chair is hers...

that's today's whole win.

Winner: her.

"I try again tomorrow,"

she said, pantsed and pleased.

Tomorrow. Or not.

Her throne. Her timeline.

(The far pages know

the timeline. It's soon.

It's genuinely soon.

And it starts with a nothing

just like this one...

celebrated like a something,

which is why the something

comes.)
