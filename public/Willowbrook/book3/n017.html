Night 17

Papa Has Feelings Too

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

A quiet discovery today.

Papa was looking at an old photo.

An old man in it. Kind eyes.

And Papa's face had a flag up.

The droopy one.

"Papa... sad?"

Papa almost said "no, no."

Grown-ups do that.

Then he didn't.

"Yes," he said. "A little sad.

This was MY grandpa.

I miss him today."

Olivia knew what to do.

She'd been trained her whole life.

Pat, pat, on his hand.

"It's okay, Papa."

And a kiss. On the knuckle.

The whole kit.

"Better?" she asked.

"Better," he said.

And it was. It really was.

Big people have all the feelings too.

Same list. Same flags. Same weather.

They just have bigger coats

to hide them under.

The bravest ones

take the coat off sometimes,

so the little ones learn:

everybody feels.

Everybody. Every day.

And comfort works

in both directions.
