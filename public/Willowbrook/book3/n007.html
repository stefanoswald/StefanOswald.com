Night 7

Excited Can't Sit Down

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

Today is farm day!

Grandpa day! Chicken day!

And Olivia woke up FULL

of the fizzy feeling.

Excited.

Excited can't sit down.

It bounces on its toes.

It runs to the window. Again.

It puts its own jacket on.

(Backwards. An hour early.

Excited doesn't check details.)

"Is it time? Is it time NOW?"

Not yet.

"NOW?"

Soon.

Excited makes clocks slow.

That's its one mean trick.

So Mama gives excited a job.

"Let's pack apples for the horse."

Excited LOVES jobs.

It counts four apples.

It zips the bag. It carries it

to the door and stands guard.

And when Papa's keys jingle...

LIFTOFF.

Excited is happy

that heard the future coming.

Enjoy the fizz, little one.

Farm day is worth every bounce.
