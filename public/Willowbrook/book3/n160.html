Night 160

The Sitting Sentinel

Month 6: Big Sister School

Theo sits now.

Solo. Steady. A little tripod

of a person on the rug.

(The coach logged the arrival:

"HE SITS! Da gym works!")

And with sitting comes

his first true POWER:

the reach-and-topple.

Anything in range: grabbed.

Anything stacked: DOWN.

Which brings us to the towers.

Her towers.

Six high now. Seven on

a good day. Her records,

her craft, her pride.

The obvious move:

build out of range.

And she did, for a week.

And then, watch this,

because it's the whole

future of these two...

she started building

IN range.

On purpose.

Two-block towers.

Right in front of him.

"Fee-oh. Knock it."

SWAT. Down. SHRIEKING joy.

"Good knocking! Again!"

Build. Swat. Shriek. Build.

(The sacrifice tower!

Lily's old invention,

from the recruit days...

remembered, redeployed,

downstream.)

Her real towers live

on the table now.

The rug towers are HIS...

built by her, for the joy

of his demolition.

That's the contract:

she makes, he unmakes,

everybody laughs.

It holds for years.

(It reverses someday too...

the far books show him

building for HER kids' swats.

The contract is circular.

All the best ones are.)

Sentinel: seated.

Contract: signed in giggles.

Construction and demolition:

a family firm now.

Two partners. Unlimited joy.
