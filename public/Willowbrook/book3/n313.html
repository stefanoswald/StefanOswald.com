Night 313

The Secret Rep

Month 11: Kindness Is a Muscle

The month's advanced

technique, taught by

Mama in one sentence:

"The strongest kindness

is the kind nobody

sees you do."

"...Why?"

"Because then you know

you did it for THEM...

not for the thank-you."

She thought about that

for two days.

(You could watch the

thinking. The machinery.

Kindness without the

thank-you? The thank-you

is the WAGES. Work

without wages?

But then... the cups

at the plant-house.

The happy that traveled

loose. The give with

the string cut...)

And on day three,

on the walk, she asked

to stop at Lily's

mailbox.

And from her pocket:

the sparkly rock.

THE rock. The soul-shelf

rock. The best one

in the collection.

Into the mailbox.

No note. No name.

Flag: up.

"Don't tell Wiwi it

was me," she instructed,

walking on. "Dat's

da rule. Da secret rule."

That's the rule.

(Lily found it that

afternoon. Held it

up to the light.

Announced to her whole

house: "DA MAILBOX

GAVE ME A TREASURE."

And Olivia, told about

it Thursday. Lily

showing her the rock,

the mystery, the wonder...

said only:

"Wow. Lucky."

And glowed. Privately.

Enormously.

The wages, it turns out,

still get paid.

They just get paid

INSIDE...

in a currency

that never runs out

and never needs

counting.)

The secret rep:

mastered.

The rock: exactly

where it belongs.

Souls travel too,

sometimes.

That's how you know

the muscle's

getting strong.
