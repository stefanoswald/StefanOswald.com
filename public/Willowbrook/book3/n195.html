Night 195

Lily's Parallel Journey

Month 7: Potty Power

Thursday. The summit.

And the talk turns

professional:

potty talk.

Lily started her journey

too, her house, her

timeline, her own throne.

The notes, compared

like old campaigners:

"I got DUCKS on mine."

"I got STARS on mine."

(Respect. Mutual.)

"Da big flush is roary."

"SO roary. My papa showed

me da pipes."

"MY papa showed me

da HURRY."

(Veterans. Comparing

theaters.)

And the difference,

handled the way the

mamas taught by handling

it themselves:

Lily rains more, still.

Different clock. Different

body. Different house-

weather that month.

And when Lily's rain came

mid-playdate, it did...

watch her friend:

no teasing. No champion

strut of superiority.

Just: "Rain happened.

Everybody rains.

Want my spare pants?

Dey got ducks but

you can wear dem."

(The spare pants. Offered.

The ducks, LOANED.

Peak friendship. The record

stands all book.)

Different timelines.

One friendship.

No racing between houses...

the mamas' law, kept

by the girls better

than most grown-ups

keep anything.

Every body blooms in order.

Friends hold the umbrella

in the meantime.
