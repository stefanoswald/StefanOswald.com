Night 84

What Makes a Best Friend

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

At the gate, at dusk,

at the end of a Thursday,

the mamas got asked:

"What makes a best friend?"

(A bigger kid asked it.

The sandbox mayor, actually.

He's been around more lately.

He's all right, the mayor.)

The mamas started to answer...

grown-up words, good ones...

but the real answer was

standing right there at the gate,

not leaving yet:

A best friend is who

you crash into and laugh.

Who trades you her cart's treasure.

Who invents turns

when the handle jams.

Who builds the tower again.

Who flattens her hand

under yours at the dog fence.

Who plants the blue jacket

between you and can't.

Who mails you ducks.

Who catches your pickle

and goes down laughing.

Who is quiet with you

on the couch sometimes.

Who says two sleeps

like a promise...

and keeps it.

Every time.

That's the answer.

It doesn't fit in a sentence.

It fits in a Thursday.

They have four hundred

Thursdays coming.

More, if the books are right.

The books are right.
