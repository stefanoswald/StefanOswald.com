Night 64

The Wrong Groceries

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

Home. Unpacking the bags.

Mama lifts out...

spaghetti.

"Hm. I didn't buy spaghetti."

Then... red sauce.

"I didn't buy this either."

Then the good cheese.

The garlic bread.

The little basil plant?!

The swap!

Lily's sneaky cart game!

Half these groceries are ROSA'S.

Mama stands at the counter,

looking at the pile.

And the pile looks back,

and slowly, slowly,

it becomes obvious

what the pile IS.

Spaghetti. Sauce. Cheese. Bread.

That's not a mistake.

That's a DINNER.

A whole spaghetti dinner,

assembled by accident,

by two little girls,

under the sightline.

Mama picks up the phone.

"Rosa? You'll never guess

what's in my bags."

Laughing on both ends.

And then the words

that turn the whole day gold:

"Well, come over tonight!

Bring your half of dinner!"

The maybe is blooming.

Can you feel it?

Almost there.
