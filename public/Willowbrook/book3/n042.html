Night 42

The First Night in the Big Bed

Month 2: Me Do It

The night. The first one.

Bath: done. Jammies: on.

Story: read. (In the new bed!

It holds three for stories.

An upgrade already.)

And now...

"Okay, big girl.

Climb in."

No lifting.

Her bed. Her legs.

She walks to the edge.

Knee up... crawl in...

under the quilt...

IN.

By herself. Into her own bed.

The bear is handed up.

(He gets lifted. He's earned it.)

Across the room, the crib

stands empty for the first time

in seven hundred nights.

Thank you, old country.

You held her the whole way here.

"Nigh-nigh, crib," she says.

(Everyone's throat does a thing.)

Lamp: clicked. Kiss: delivered.

Door: to its practiced inch.

And in the monitor's silver:

one girl, one bear,

one big bed, holding.

She stayed in it all night.

(Mostly. The floor happened once.

The floor is part of the journey.)

New country: settled.

Population: two. Sovereign: her.
