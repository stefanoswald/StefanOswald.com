Night 25

The Impatient Wiggle

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

The cookies are OUT of the oven.

They are RIGHT THERE.

But they are too hot.

"Soon," says Mama. "When the timer dings."

And here comes the wiggly one...

impatient.

Impatient hops on one foot.

Impatient sighs like a whale.

"Now?"

Not yet.

Impatient lies on the floor.

(The floor of despair.)

"NOWWW?"

Almost.

Here's the trick nobody likes

and everybody needs:

waiting goes faster

when your hands are busy.

"Help me wash the bowl?"

Wash, dry, put away...

DING!

THE TIMER! IT DINGED!

The cookie is warm. Perfect.

Worth it? Worth it.

Impatient never believes

that waiting ends.

But it always does.

The timer always dings.

Hands busy. Heart steady.

Ding.
