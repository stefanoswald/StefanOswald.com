Night 6

The Name Game

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

A new game!

Mama makes a face.

Big smile, bright eyes.

"What's this one?"

"HAPPY!"

Right!

Now: droopy mouth, slow blink.

"Sad..."

Right again!

Now: scrunched brow, puffed cheeks.

"MAD!"

Yes! (Don't laugh! Okay, laugh.)

Now: mouth open, eyebrows UP!

"Uhh..."

"This one's surprised!"

Surprised! Like when the jack

popped out of the box!

Olivia tries them all on.

Happy face. Sad face. Mad face.

Her face knows them already.

Now her words do too.

Faces are feelings' flags.

Learn the flags,

and you can read

the whole world's weather...

on everybody.

"One more! What's THIS?"

That one's silly.

Silly is Papa's flag.

It flies daily.
