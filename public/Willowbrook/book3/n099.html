Night 99

The Hospital Bag

Month 4: Waiting for Baby

A bag appeared by the door.

Packed. Zipped. Waiting.

"What's dat bag?"

"The hospital bag.

When the baby decides to come,

we grab it and GO.

It stands ready. Like a firefighter."

Inside: tiny first clothes.

The camera. Snacks for Papa.

(Papas require snacks

at hospitals. It's medical.)

The list, taped to it.

And Olivia has...

concerns.

"Where MY stuff?"

Ah. The plan, explained:

"When it's baby time,

Rosa comes for YOU.

You and Lily, a special

sleepover, a real one,

while we get the baby.

Then you come meet them

FIRST. Before anybody.

Big sisters meet first."

First. Before ANYBODY.

(Before GRANDPA even.

The magnitude lands.)

Concerns: resolved.

But one addition is required.

She fetches something.

Unzips the bag's corner.

Tucks it deep, where it

can't be refused:

one small yellow duck.

Esquire's understudy...

the tiny one from the block set.

"For da baby.

First toy. From me."

It stays in the bag.

(It would have stayed

regardless of policy.

Some cargo is not negotiable.)

The bag waits by the door now,

one duck heavier,

ready for spring.
