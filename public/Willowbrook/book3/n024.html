Night 24

The Cozy Feeling Has No English Name

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

Rain outside. Fort inside.

Cocoa, half drunk.

The bear, in the crook of an arm.

Mama on one side.

Papa on the other.

The blanket at exactly

the right heaviness.

The lamp through the fort roof:

gold.

What is THIS feeling called?

Hmm.

Happy? More than happy.

Calm? Cozier than calm.

Safe-warm-together-home?

Some feelings are so good

they never got one word.

Other countries tried.

There are old words in old places

for exactly this...

snug-inside-while-rain,

together-warm,

nothing-missing.

Olivia has her own word.

She says it now, burrowed in:

"Home."

That'll do.

That'll more than do.

Some feelings don't need naming.

They just need having.

Have this one, little one.

Have it a hundred times.

It's the one the whole

rest of life tries

to get back to.
