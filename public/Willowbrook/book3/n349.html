Night 349

Theo's Turn Next

Month 12: Almost Three

Mid-party-prep, a

thought struck the

almost-three-year-old:

"Mama. After MY birfday...

Fee-oh gets one.

He's gonna be TWO."

Theo. Two.

(In the summer. The

calendar confirms.

The boy who was a

paper chain is about

to be TWO.)

And the expert...

the only person alive

who's been two

RECENTLY, who holds

the fresh field data...

began the briefing:

"Fee-oh. Listen.

Two is a LOT.

You're gonna get

da big feelings.

Da hot chest. It's

okay, it's just

wevver, and I know

ALL da tools.

You're gonna say

ME DO IT about

everyfing. Dat's

allowed. Dat's da

whole job of two.

You're gonna get

a big-boy bed.

I'll show you da

climbing.

And da potty comes.

No hurry. Rain happens.

I got da rules taped

somewhere.

And Fee-oh..."

(leaning close;

the important part)

"...I'll be FREE da

whole time you're two.

Free knows fings.

You get me da whole

way froo."

You get me the whole

way through.

The next book's whole

promise, issued at

rug level by its

co-star.

(Book four knows.

Book four is full

of it.)

"Two!" said Theo,

holding up four

fingers.

"We'll work on it,"

said his sister.

They'd work on it.

They work on

everything.

That's the deal

now. Permanent.
