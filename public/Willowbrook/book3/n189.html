Night 189

Theo Watches the Champion

Month 7: Potty Power

The champion has an audience

these days.

Rug-level. Drooling.

Deeply interested in

everything she does...

which now includes

the throne.

So the process gets

NARRATED. For his files.

"Watch, Fee-oh.

First you feel da signal.

It's like... a tummy phone

ringing. You ANSWER it.

You don't wait, dat's

how da rain happens.

Den: potty. Sit. Wait.

Somefing comes.

Den WASHING HANDS...

dat's da rule, always...

and DEN da strut."

(The strut: demonstrated.

The audience: shrieking

approval. He loves the strut.

Everyone loves the strut.)

"You'll do dis too.

When you're big. Like me.

I teach you den.

I'm good at teaching.

Ask da bear."

And here's what's actually

happening, per every book

on every shelf:

second children learn

the potty FASTER.

Years of watching the

champion. The process

demystified daily. The strut

as advertising.

When Theo's turn comes...

next book, next volume...

he'll walk in like he

owns the franchise.

Because his sister

narrated the whole manual

at him, rug-level,

years in advance.

Curriculum: transferred.

Files: complete.

The strut waits for

its second dancer.
