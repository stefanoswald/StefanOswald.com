Night 28

The Feelings Toolbox

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

One month of feelings!

Look what got collected.

A whole toolbox:

The NAMES. (Named things are tamer.)

The pond breath. (Flower in,

dandelion out.)

The lamp inspection. (For corners

and worries. Look together.)

The porch escape. (For too-full.

Shrink the world back down.)

The pat-pat kit. (For everyone

else's weather. Both directions.)

The busy hands. (For waiting.)

The lid hat. (For flat days.

Silly is medicine.)

The telling. (One feeling,

at dinner, out loud.)

And the biggest tool,

too big for any box:

You are the sky.

The weather passes.

You stay.

Two years old,

and already better equipped

than half the grown-ups in town.

(The grown-ups are borrowing

her tools, honestly.

The pond breath works

in traffic. Ask Papa.)

Toolbox: packed.

Weather: welcome.

Bring what you've got, world.

She's ready.
