Night 27

The Feelings Are Weather, You Are the Sky

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

Blanket. Grass. Look up.

Clouds today. All kinds.

A big grey grumbly one.

A little white happy puff.

A long worried wispy one.

Watch them.

What are they all doing?

Moving.

Crossing. Passing through.

"Feelings are like that," says Mama.

"They're the clouds.

Mad clouds, sad clouds,

fizzy excited clouds.

They come. They cross. They go."

And you?

"You're the sky, baby.

The clouds pass through you.

No cloud IS you.

No storm stays.

The sky holds them all...

and stays the sky."

Olivia watches the grumbly one

slide away east.

Bye, cloud.

The blue behind it: still there.

Always was.

This is the biggest thing

this month has to say.

Bigger than all the names.

You are not your weather.

You are the sky it moves through.

Blue. Whole. Boundless.

Always still there.
