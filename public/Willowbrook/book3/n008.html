Night 8

Shy Hides Behind Legs

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

At the market: a new person.

A nice person. A stranger, though.

"Well hello there!"

And Olivia... disappears.

Behind Mama's legs.

One eye peeking out.

This one is called shy.

Shy isn't rude.

Shy isn't wrong.

Shy is just careful,

wearing a quieter coat.

It likes to look first.

It comes out when it's ready...

not when it's pulled.

Mama doesn't pull.

"She'll say hi when she's ready,"

says Mama. Easy. No apology.

The nice person nods. Waits. Chats.

And after a while...

the eye becomes a face...

the face becomes a girl...

and a small hand waves.

"Hi."

There she is!

Shy took its time.

Shy is allowed to.

The hellos it gives at last

are worth the wait.

Every one.
