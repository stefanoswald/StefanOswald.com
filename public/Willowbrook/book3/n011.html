Night 11

Proud Stands Tall

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

Five blocks high.

FIVE.

All by herself.

No help. Not even a little.

And look what's happening

to Olivia's body:

The chest puffs out.

The chin lifts up.

The hands land on the hips.

She is standing like a statue

of her own self.

This one is called proud.

Proud stands tall.

Proud calls its witnesses:

"PAPA. LOOK. I DID IT."

He looks. He really looks.

"You did that. All you."

That's the food proud eats...

being SEEN.

Not just praised. Seen.

Proud isn't bragging.

Proud is the warm glow

of hard-thing-done.

You earned this one, tower-girl.

Wear it tall.

Then, CRASH, demolish it

and go earn it again.

Proud loves reruns.
