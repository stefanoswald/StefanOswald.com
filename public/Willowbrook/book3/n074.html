Night 74

The Copying Compliment

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

Something new this Thursday:

Lily arrived...

wearing a bow.

A RED one.

(Lily. Of the blue everything.

A red bow. In her black bangs.)

"Like YOU, Livy!"

Oh.

OH.

And Olivia, same morning,

no coordination, none...

had demanded her hair go

"in two"...

two little bunches...

LIKE LILY WEARS HERS.

They stand in the doorway,

each wearing the other,

delighted beyond all speech.

Grown-ups call it copying.

Grown-ups worry about it.

Don't.

At two, copying is the highest

compliment there is.

It means: I see you.

I like what you are.

I want some of it on ME.

The reddest bow.

The two-est bunches.

Friendship, worn openly,

in each other's colors.

They'll trade styles for years...

bows now, big words later,

courage always.

That's what friends are:

people you catch things from.

Catch the good ones.

These two are.
