Night 19

Brave Isn't Not-Scared

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

The doctor's office.

The little ear light is coming.

Olivia doesn't like the ear light.

Her hand finds Mama's finger.

Grips it. HARD.

Her chin wobbles...

but she holds still.

Still as she can.

The light peeks. One ear. Two.

"All done! Beautiful ears!"

And here is what just happened,

because it deserves its name:

that was brave.

Listen carefully, little one,

because everyone gets this wrong:

Brave is not not-scared.

Brave is scared...

plus doing it anyway.

The grip on the finger?

That was the scared part.

The holding still?

That was the plus.

You can't even BE brave

without some scared in the mix.

Scared is the ingredient.

Brave is the recipe.

"You were scared and you did it,"

says Mama, in the parking lot.

The whole formula, said out loud.

Remember it.

It works on ear lights,

first days, high slides,

and everything else, forever.
