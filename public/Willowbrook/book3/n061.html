Night 61

The Great Escape

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

Where were we?

The cookie. The zoom. Yes.

Mama turned to the apples...

one half-second...

and the cart seat was EMPTY.

Olivia was OUT and RUNNING.

Down the aisle! Past the beans!

The whole store, hers!

Shelves blurring by!

Freedom tastes like cookie!

"This is so good!" she sang

to the canned tomatoes.

Behind her, far behind...

"OLIVIA!"

Mama's heart doing the thing

hearts do in stores.

(Little one, when you're big:

this part scared your Mama.

Stores are big. Girls are small.

That's why hands get held.

It worked out. It became

the best day.

But hold the hand anyway.

Both things are true.

That's this family's whole way...

both things, always.)

Around the corner she zoomed...

the cereal aisle...

top speed...

and SOMEBODY ELSE

was zooming the OTHER way...

Tomorrow: the crash.

The most important crash

in this whole book.

Sleep tight, runner.
