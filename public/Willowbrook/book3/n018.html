Night 18

The Feeling Check at Dinner

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

A new dinner tradition.

The feeling check!

One feeling each, from the day.

Papa goes first:

"Frustrated. My work computer

was slow ALL day."

(Sympathy nods. The zipper is

understood at this table.)

Mama:

"Happy. The garden's first

tomato flower opened."

And Olivia?

The table waits.

She thinks hard. The whole day

flips through her...

the puddle, the crash, the goodbye...

"Happy-sad. Wiwi go home."

The whole table nods.

Happy-sad. A quality report.

The vocabulary is WORKING.

No fixing happens at feeling check.

No "but at least."

Just telling, and nodding,

and passing the bread.

Feelings said out loud

are half their old weight.

This table lifts together.

Every night. One feeling each.

Try it at your table.

Start tonight.
