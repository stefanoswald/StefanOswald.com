Night 1

The Feeling with No Name

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

Something is happening inside Olivia.

It is big.

It is hot.

It makes her fists squeeze

and her face scrunch.

The banana broke.

The morning went sideways.

And now this, this THING...

is filling her all the way up.

Mama kneels down.

She doesn't say "stop."

She doesn't say "shhh."

She says:

"Something big is in there, huh."

Yes! YES. Something BIG.

"Feelings are like that," says Mama.

"They come. They get big.

And then, watch, they go."

Olivia breathes. Hiccups.

The big thing gets... a little smaller.

"This one is called frustrated."

Frustrated.

The thing has a name!

Named things are tamer things.

That is this month's whole secret.

Every feeling has a name.

We're going to learn them all.
