Night 86

The Lily Preview

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

Thursday. The fort. Deep council.

Olivia has to tell SOMEBODY,

and Lily is the somebody

for everything.

"Wiwi. Secret."

(Lily leans in. Fort-close.)

"Somefing good is coming

to my house."

"...A PUPPY?!"

"No..."

"A TRACTOR?!"

"NO. I don't know WHAT.

But Mama happy-cries.

And Papa builded a THING

in the little room.

Wif BARS. Like my old bed."

Lily gasps.

Lily, eleven days older,

cousin-informed, worldly...

puts it together.

Her eyes go enormous.

"LIVY. I know what it is.

My cousin GOT one."

"WHAT IS IT?"

And Lily whispers

the enormous word:

"...A BABY."

A baby.

A BABY?!

The fort goes silent

with the size of it.

"...Babies are little,"

offers Lily, expert.

"Dey cry. But dey get FUN.

My cousin's one laughs now."

A baby.

Something good indeed.

The girls sit with the news,

shoulder to shoulder,

the way they'll sit

with every big news

for the rest of their lives.

First to know: the best friend.

Always. That's the law.
