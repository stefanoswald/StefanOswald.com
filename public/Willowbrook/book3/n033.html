Night 33

The Sock Standoff

Month 2: Me Do It

Today's opponent: the sock.

Socks look easy.

Socks are NOT easy.

Round one:

toes in, heel stuck. Sock wins.

Round two:

everything twisted. Sock wins.

Round three:

the sock is on the HAND somehow?!

Sock wins. (Show-off.)

Mama watches from the door.

Her hands twitch. She sits on them.

(Helping now would steal the ending.)

Round four:

toes... heel... PULL...

IT'S ON.

Crooked. Inside out. Half-heeled.

ON.

"ME DID IT!"

The sock has been defeated.

It will be defeated again tomorrow.

And the day after.

Every morning, the rematch.

Every morning, a little faster.

That's how learning looks:

losing to a sock on Monday,

beating it by Friday.

The sock doesn't mind.

Socks are good losers.

It's their whole job, eventually.
