Night 14

Love Is the Warmest One

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

Papa is just standing there.

Making toast. Minding his toast.

And Olivia, out of nowhere...

runs the whole hallway

and hugs his knees.

Full strength. No reason.

"Well hello!" he laughs.

"What's this for?"

She doesn't have the big words yet.

So here they are, for later:

This is love.

The warmest feeling there is.

It doesn't wait for birthdays.

It doesn't need a reason.

It just fills up and fills up

until it has to GO somewhere...

and it always picks

the closest beloved knees.

"Wuv you," says Olivia,

into his pant leg.

The toast burns a little.

Nobody cares at all.

Some feelings you learn to name.

This one you were born knowing.

It was the first one in.

It'll be the last one standing.

Love, love, love.

The warmest one.
