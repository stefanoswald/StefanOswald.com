Night 272

The Month of Fives, Counted

Month 9: Counting to Five

Month's end. The audit...

conducted, fittingly,

in FIVES:

Five ducks: counted

clean, three times,

minus one diving.

(Subtraction: discovered.)

Five blocks: certified,

towered, zeroed by

brotherly demolition.

(Zero: the fist number.

Also discovered.)

Five berries, two friends:

two-and-a-half each.

(Fractions: delicious.)

Five books: the library

maximum, chosen with

the weight of limits.

Five kisses: four kept,

one couriered nightly

down the hall.

Five breaths: the storm

math, folding finger

by finger to calm.

Five senses: one lane,

entered through every

door.

Five in the family:

census closed, bear

ratified.

Five minutes: the treaty

that ended the leaving

storms.

And three first steps...

"one, two, FREE"...

counted out loud by

the person they were

aimed at,

who also received,

this same month,

the first word of a

whole other person's

life.

Some months give you

one milestone.

This one gave the

whole hand.

"Good maf monf?" asked

its mathematician,

drowsy, at the tally's

end.

The best math month.

All five fingers of it.

High five, little counter.

(SMACK. Soft. Calibrated.

The family handshake,

closing the family books.)

Audited. Approved.

Carried forward:

countable, and

uncountable,

both.
