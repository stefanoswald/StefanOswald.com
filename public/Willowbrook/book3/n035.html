Night 35

The Wrong-Buttoned Sweater

Month 2: Me Do It

This morning, Olivia

buttoned her own sweater.

All five buttons.

By herself.

Each one... in the wrong hole.

The whole sweater sits crooked.

One side hangs long.

The collar points somewhere new.

She looks...

proud as a parade.

Now: the parent test.

Fix it?

The buttons ARE wrong.

The photo WILL be crooked...

Mama looks at the sweater.

Mama looks at the pride.

"You buttoned it yourself!"

That's all. That's the whole review.

The sweater stays. All day.

Crooked. Glorious.

The market sees it. The bench sees it.

Rosa sees it and, bless her...

salutes.

Here's the secret every

crooked sweater teaches:

done-by-me beats done-right,

at two.

Right will come. It always comes.

But it has to come THROUGH

done-by-me.

There's no other road.

Wear it crooked, kid.

It fits perfectly.
