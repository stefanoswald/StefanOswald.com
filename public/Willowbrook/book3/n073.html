Night 73

Missing on a Monday

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

Monday.

Thursday is three sleeps away.

Three!

And the missing feeling...

month one taught its name...

is visiting hard.

"I miss Wiwi."

The ache. The real one.

So Mama teaches

the missing TOOLS.

Because missing isn't just

for aching through.

Missing can DO things.

Tool one: the drawing.

Crayons out. A picture for Lily.

(A duck. It's always a duck.

Lily will know. Lily speaks duck.)

Tool two: the delivery.

The walk goes past Lily's house...

and the drawing goes

into the Herrera mailbox.

Flag: UP.

(The flag! Her hands! Up!)

Tool three: the telling.

"When you see her Thursday,

you can tell her you missed her.

Friends like to know."

The next morning...

the family mailbox flag: up.

Inside: a drawing.

Blue scribbles. Bold ones.

(A duck, confirms Olivia.

Obviously a duck.)

The missing, mailed both ways,

became a correspondence.

Two years old,

and already pen pals

at four houses' distance.

The ache had a job all along.

It's love. Put it to work.
