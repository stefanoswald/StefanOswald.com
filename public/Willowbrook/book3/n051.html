Night 51

Buckling the Doll Stroller

Month 2: Me Do It

Remember the car seat rule?

Climbing: hers.

Buckles: the grown-ups'.

Well.

The doll stroller arrived

this week, a hand-me-down

from the Herreras...

and it has...

a little buckle.

A toy one. Simple. Safe.

And buckles, it turns out,

were being STUDIED

this whole time.

The bear is seated. (His walk.)

And Olivia goes to work.

The two ends... aim...

press... miss...

aim... press...

CLICK.

CLICK!!

BUCKLED. BY HER.

The bear is secured

per regulations

by certified small hands.

"Safe now, Baba."

Off they roll, down the hallway.

Her passenger. Her buckle.

Her rules of the road.

The real buckles stay

grown-up business for years.

But the learning found

its practice ground...

the way learning always does:

one size down,

on somebody soft,

who trusts you completely.
