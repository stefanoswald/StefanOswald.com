Night 32

The Declaration

Month 2: Me Do It

It happened at the shoe bench.

Mama reached for the shoe...

and two small hands flew up.

Stop.

"ME DO IT."

Three words.

The whole declaration.

The flag of being two,

planted right there

between the shoe and the foot.

Me. Do. It.

Not you do it.

Not help me do it.

ME do it.

Mama's hands retreat. Slowly.

(This is hard for hands

that have helped for two years.

They twitch. They hover.

They learn.)

The shoe fight begins.

Wrong foot first. (Tradition.)

Heel folded under. Fixed. Refixed.

Four minutes. Six.

And then...

the shoe. On. By her.

"ME DID IT."

She did.

Write it on the calendar.

The month of Me has begun.

Everything takes longer now.

Everything matters more now.

Both facts. Same flag.
