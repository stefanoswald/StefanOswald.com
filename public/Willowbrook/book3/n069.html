Night 69

The Same and Different Game

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

Snack time. Thursday.

And a game breaks out...

nobody starts it, it just grows:

the Same Game.

"You like berries?"

"YES." "ME TOO!"

SAME! (Shrieking. Joy.)

"You like mushrooms?"

"No fank you."

"I LIKE mushrooms!"

DIFFERENT! (Gasping. Fascinating.)

The list grows all snack:

Both: love the slide. SAME.

Both: scared of the vacuum. SAME.

(A moment of solidarity.

The vacuum is a lot.)

Lily: scared of big dogs.

Olivia: LOVES the gold dog.

Different.

"I show you," says Olivia.

"He nice. I show you gentle."

(Filed. A friend-job. Pending.)

Olivia: red. Lily: blue. Different.

Both: spaghetti. SAME. FOREVER.

Here's what the game builds,

under the shrieking:

same is cozy...

you're like me!

And different is interesting...

you know things I don't!

A friendship needs both.

Same for the snuggling.

Different for the growing.

They're stocking up on both,

one snack at a time.
