Night 20

The Grumpy Morning

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

Some mornings arrive wrong.

Nobody knows why.

Olivia woke up... grumpy.

Scowl at the ceiling.

Scowl at the stairs.

Scowl at breakfast...

even the GOOD breakfast.

Even the red cup got a scowl!

(The red cup! Her best cup!)

"Somebody's grumpy," says Mama.

Not mean. Just true.

"NO," scowls Olivia. Grumpily.

(Proving it.)

Here's the wisdom for grumpy days:

you don't have to fix them.

You just have to be

a little softer inside them.

Quieter games. Earlier snack.

Extra rocking. Low lights.

Grumpy is usually tired

or hungry or growing,

wearing a crabby costume.

By lunch, the costume slipped.

By nap, it was off entirely.

She woke up herself again.

"Hi, Mama!"

Hi, sunshine. Welcome back.

The grumpy morning is forgiven.

It always was.
