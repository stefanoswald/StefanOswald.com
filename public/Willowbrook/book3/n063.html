Night 63

The Swap

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

The mamas talked and talked.

(Mamas do, after a scare.

The talking shakes it out.)

And below the talking,

at cart level,

the girls conducted business.

Lily's game. A quiet one.

A sneaky one.

Take a thing from THIS cart...

put it in THAT cart.

Spaghetti, slid across.

A jar of red sauce, traded.

Some cheese, migrated.

Item by item,

below the grown-up sightline,

the carts... swapped.

Olivia watched, delighted,

and a little worried.

Was this allowed?

It didn't SEEM allowed.

"Mama! This is BAD!"

she reported. (Half proud.)

Mama looked down...

at the giggles,

at the criss-crossed groceries...

and said it. Again. Calm as ever:

"Maybe."

MAYBE?!

The carts are all mixed up!

How is that maybe?!

Oh, little one.

Wait for the checkout.

Wait for tonight.

The best maybes take

a few hours to bloom.
