Night 88

Telling Everyone

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

The secret is OUT.

And Willowbrook has a crier.

The tour, as conducted:

The bench: "I getting a BABY!"

(The parliament erupts. Rosa...

who knew; mamas tell mamas...

performs perfect surprise.)

The bakery: "A baby! In da TUMMY!"

(A celebration bun, on the house.

"Big sisters eat free today.")

The library desk: "I da BIG SISTER."

(The librarian, gravely:

"Then you'll need board books

to read to the baby.

Big sisters read to babies."

THE HONOR. Three are borrowed

on the spot, for a person

who won't exist till spring.)

The fence line, Sunday:

"Gampa! A BABY!"

"Well NOW," says Grandpa Joe...

and the record notes his eyes

did the thing eyes do

in that family.

"Another one for the chair

workshop, then."

(A new chair. Already planned.

The star template kept.)

Even the gold dog was told,

through the fence, formally.

(Wag. He'll guard it too.

It's understood.)

The whole town, informed.

The whole town, glad.

The spring just got

a due date.
