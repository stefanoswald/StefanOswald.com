Night 9

The Jealous Green Pinch

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

The neighbor visited

with her brand-new baby.

And Mama held it.

MAMA. Held. IT.

And something pinched

inside Olivia.

A green little pinch.

Her arms crossed.

Her lip went out.

Her feet came over

and pushed at Mama's knee.

This one is called jealous.

It says: that's MY lap.

That's MY mama.

Jealous pinches everyone.

Even grown-ups. Even nice ones.

Mama read the flag.

"Come up. There's room."

There WAS room.

Lap for two, baby and girl.

The pinch let go, slowly.

Mama's love isn't a cookie.

It doesn't get smaller

when it's shared.

It's more like the sourdough...

it grows overnight,

every night,

enough for everyone.

Always enough.

Always, always enough.
