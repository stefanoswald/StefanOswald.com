Night 26

The Overwhelmed Bubble

Month 1: Big Feelings Have Names

A birthday party. A big one.

(A lane friend turned three.)

Balloons! Kids! Music! Cake!

Wonderful!

Wonderful...

wonder...

too much.

Olivia's eyes go glassy.

Her hands come up

over her ears.

The wonderful got too loud.

This is overwhelmed.

It's not sad. It's not scared.

It's FULL.

Too much in, too fast.

Even good things overfill.

Mama sees the flag.

No questions. Just the scoop.

Out to the porch. The quiet porch.

The world shrinks back down:

one porch, one lap, one breeze.

Breathe...

The bubble un-fills, slowly.

Five minutes. Maybe ten.

"Better?"

Better.

Back in? Back in.

(Parties are still good.

Porches make them possible.)

Everyone has a fill line.

Knowing yours isn't weakness.

It's wisdom.

Hers is being learned

one loud party at a time.
