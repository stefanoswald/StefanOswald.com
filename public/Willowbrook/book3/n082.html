Night 82

The Comfort Call

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

Thursday. But...

Lily is sick.

Just a cold. A couch cold.

But playdate: cancelled.

The missing hits immediately.

Double-force. Thursday-force.

So Mama works the old exception:

the call.

And there she is, on the screen...

Lily. Blanketed. Pink-nosed.

Rabbit under arm.

"HI WIWI!"

"HI LIVY!" (Stuffy. Still loud.)

And the visit happens ANYWAY...

through the wire:

Olivia gives the tour.

The fort (rebuilt for viewing).

The tower (five high; applause

coughs warmly from the couch).

The bear (held to the lens;

the rabbit is held to theirs;

the colleagues confer).

One story, read at the screen...

the bear book, shared page-wise.

"Feel better, Wiwi."

"Bye, Livy. Two sleeps?"

"Two sleeps."

The call ends. The missing...

softened. Manageable. Warm.

Sick days happen.

Friendship doesn't take them off.

It just changes channels

and keeps broadcasting.

Get well soon, blue jacket.

The fort is keeping your spot.
