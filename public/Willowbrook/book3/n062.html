Night 62

The Crash

Month 3: A Friend Called Lily

Two runners.

One aisle.

Opposite directions.

Neither one looking.

BONK.

Down they went, both of them...

a heap of girls

on the cereal aisle floor.

Stunned silence.

Two sets of eyes, blinking.

One red bow, crooked.

One blue jacket, askew.

And then...

you know who it was.

You knew the whole time.

"WIWI?!"

"LIVY!!"

It was LILY.

Her Lily! Bench-Lily!

Tuesday-Lily! Best-Lily!

Also escaped! Also zooming!

(Also cookie'd, the record shows.

The same kind clerk. The same aisle

of consequences.)

The stunned heap became

a laughing heap.

The mamas arrived at a run...

and found their runaways

tangled up and howling

with joy on the floor.

"This is SO GOOD!" said Olivia.

And Mama, heart still pounding...

looked at Rosa,

and Rosa looked at Mama,

and Mama said:

"...Maybe."

The word again!

Stay tuned, little one.

Maybe is still working.
