Night 170

Babyproofing, Expert Consultant

Month 6: Big Sister School

A crawler in the house means

one thing: THE AUDIT.

(The crawl-through. The knees.

The house re-read at hull height.

This family's third edition.)

And this edition has

something new:

a consultant.

An EXPERT consultant.

Recently of the crawling

administration herself.

The inspection, as led:

"He's gonna go HERE first."

(The cupboard. Correct.

Hers once. His soon.

The low one stays unlatched...

the sovereignty passes down.)

"Dis cord. He'll pull it."

(Flagged. Fixed.)

"Dese little fings..."

(the block bin's smallest,

her own graduated pieces)

"...too little for him.

Mouf-size. Up on MY shelf."

(The toilet-tube law,

administered by someone

who once tested everything

against it personally.

The best auditors

have lived experience.)

"And da stairs. GATES.

He'll want dem SO bad.

I did. Everybody does."

Gates: recommissioned.

The audit ran an hour.

The consultant billed

in crackers.

The house, re-proofed,

stands ready for its

second explorer...

mapped this time

by its first one.

Nobody knows the hazards

like the last kid through.

Nobody guards the trail

like the one who blazed it.
