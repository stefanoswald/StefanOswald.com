Night 156

The Fifth Branch Falls

Month 6: Numbers Are Everywhere

August. The rematch, by the June filing: The fifth branch. The measurement first, because she measures now: arm's reach, checked against the trunk marks. June's gap: two inches. August's gap: zero. ("The hand-a-year is distributed.

You grow all year in secret and cash it in on branch days.") The climb: gear-down, three points, the whole academy rule. The reach, the grip, the pull... The fifth branch. Corner office. New record.

The park below, the brook silver in the distance, the school roof. The school, the big one, one month out now, visible over the trees, examined levelly from altitude: "I can see you from here..." She told it. "That's the right way round.

I see you first." And Mateo, arriving below with the bucket rope: "Fifth branch at almost-five. I got there at six. You're ahead of the academy's whole curve. I had a better academy," said the corner office.
