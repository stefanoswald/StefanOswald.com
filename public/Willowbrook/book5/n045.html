Night 45

The Take-Back That Worked

Month 2: Mistakes Are How

Small moment; the story keeps it because of the speed. At the fort, a plan dispute. Lily proposed; Olivia, tired and hot, said fast and flat: "That's a dumb plan." One second. Two. And then, no lamp, no night to think, no rule summoning, just: "Wait.

Take-back. The plan's not dumb. I'm hot and the hot talked. Say it again. I'll listen right this time." Lily said it again. (The plan was good. They built it.) That's the entry. The whole entry.

A repair that took nine seconds because four years of lamps and rules and second-sorries have compressed into reflex. (The far pages clock her adult average at six. Elite.)
