Night 136

The Reading Tree Office

Month 5: The Deep Shelf

Setup project this week: the fourth branch became the office. The engineering: a bucket, a rope, a branch-hook. Books ride up in the bucket..." You never climb with your hands full; the academy's first law. "reader follows, empty-handed, per regulation.

The inauguration: two chapters, read at altitude, back against the trunk, the park's noise arriving soft and sorted, like the tree filtered it first. "Why's reading better up here?" Mateo asked from the third branch (he brings comics; the office has two departments now). She considered it professionally.

"Down there, the world's next to the book, tapping. Up here the world's under the book. It waits better from underneath. Also..." The real reason. "the willow reads over my shoulder from its hill. We're both watchers up here. Colleagues."
