Night 36

The Museum of Broken Things

Month 2: Mistakes Are How

At Petrov's this week, she found the back shelf. Broken things. Not being fixed. Displayed. "Mr. Petrov. These are still broken. You fix everything. Why not these?" Petrov wiped his hands a long time. "Those are my teachers.

That clock. I broke it worse trying to fix it fast. Forty years ago. It taught me slow. That cup, glued wrong three times before I learned you fit pieces dry first. Every mender keeps a shelf like this.

The fixed things go home. The teachers stay... A museum of mistakes. A faculty of them," said Petrov.
