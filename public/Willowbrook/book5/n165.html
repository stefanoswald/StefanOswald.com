Night 165

The Odometer Event

Month 6: Numbers Are Everywhere

Papa had been tracking it for a week, and on Sunday, farm-bound, it arrived: the odometer at 99,999.9. He pulled over. On their bedtime world road, gloaming coming on, the whole family leaned in around the dashboard, and rolled the last tenth at walking speed, together, counting it down like a launch: 99,999.9... and then the great soft click of the whole board turning over: 100,000.0. Six digits, all changing at once, nines collapsing into zeros, the one arriving out front like a sunrise.

"All the numbers turned at the same time. The whole row rest-ed. Papa, the car got a fresh page. A hundred thousand miles. Your grandpa sold me this car when you were born. Every one of those miles had us in it somewhere.

And now zeros. All the miles to come are in there, invisible, waiting..." She watched the new mile accumulate: 100,000.1. "There's the first one. We're spending the fresh page right now."
