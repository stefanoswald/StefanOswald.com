Night 13

Why Do Grown-Ups Say Hmm?

Month 1: The Question Collector

Field study this week: grown-up talk, observed from under the kitchen table. Finding: grown-ups say "hmm" Constantly. Tally: seventeen in one coffee visit. The decoding, published at dinner: "There's three hmms. The short one means 'I heard you but I'm still thinking.'

The long one means 'I don't agree but we're friends.' And the one that goes up means 'tell me the rest.' You're not saying a word. You're saying weather." The grown-ups, caught, delighted, confirmed all three.

"Why not just say the words?" Mama: "Because sometimes the kind thing is to leave the words unsaid but let the person hear them anyway."
