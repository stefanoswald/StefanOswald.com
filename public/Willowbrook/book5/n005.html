Night 5

Who Named the Colors?

Month 1: The Question Collector

The hardware store. The paint wall. And the discovery that colors have names, hundreds, printed, readable now: "Misty meadow. Ocean whisper. Mama. Buttered toast. Somebody's job was naming this white 'buttered toast'?! Somebody's job. That's the best job I ever heard of...

Some of these are wrong, dough. This green is not 'quiet.' This green is loud. I could do better." She did better. The notebook got a page: her corrections. "This one is: Frog in shade.

This one is: The undersmell but a color. This one is: Gold like the willow means it." Question, saved: "Do the namers ever come back and check if the colors still fit?" (Poets, as it happened, have asked exactly this. None at five... four. Almost none.)
