Night 84

The Day She Got It Wrong

Month 3: How Does Harry Feel?

Truth page, this month requires one. She got it wrong this week. A kid at the park, sitting alone under the oak. This month's machinery engaged automatically: alone = lonely = deploy. She landed beside him with the full kit, the near-not-with, the fourth-for-a-game offer, the works. "...

I'm reading..." said the kid, holding up the book. "I came here to read alone. On purpose. It's the good tree for it." Oh. Oh. The retreat: dignified-ish. The lesson, bigger than the sting: (She used "company or no?" for the rest of her life. Three words. Free. Never once wrong.)
