Night 76

The Worry Behind the Kitchen Door

Month 3: How Does Harry Feel?

While getting water after bedtime, Olivia heard Mama and Papa speaking quietly in the kitchen.

"Money is tight this month," Mama said. "The roof will not wait."

Olivia carried those fragments upstairs. In the dark, tight became no food, and roof became no house.

She turned around and walked into the kitchen.

"I heard a little by accident. If I keep it secret, my guesses will make it enormous. How big is the worry really?"

Mama and Papa exchanged a look.

"The roof needs a repair, and we are working out how to pay for it," Papa said. "It is a real grown-up worry, but our home is safe and you are safe. You do not have to solve it."

The answer did not make the problem disappear. It returned it to its real size.

"Knowing is lighter than guessing," Olivia said.

Mama hugged her. "Then always come ask."

Upstairs, the roof became a roof again.
