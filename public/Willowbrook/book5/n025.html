Night 25

The Question Under the Question

Month 1: The Question Collector

Theo's been stalling at bedtime with questions. Tonight the collector diagnosed it from the hallway: "Mama. Those aren't real questions. He doesn't want the answers. He wants the staying. The questions are just... rope. To keep you in the room.

So what's he really asking?'Don't go yet.' That's the question under the question." (Mama sat down on the hallway floor, right there, and looked at her daughter awhile.) "You know some grown-ups never learn to hear that.

The under-question. People ask 'how are you' meaning 'see me.' Ask 'what time is it' meaning 'talk to me.'Then everybody should learn listening under. It's not hard. You just ask: what would make them stop asking?

That's the real question." (Theo got ten extra minutes and no answers. He stopped asking. Diagnosis: confirmed.)
