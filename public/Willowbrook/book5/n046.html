Night 46

The Weed That Wasn't

Month 2: Mistakes Are How

Garden duty this week: weeding. Her row, her call. And one intruder, tall, rough-leaved, defiant, got the grip and the pull-stance before Mama's "Wait. Look at the leaves. Look close. You know those leaves...

The row. That's a mammof leaf. But the row's at the farm, how did... Bird, probably. Ate a seed at Honeywell, made a deposit here. Happens all the time. Half my best flowers are somebody's mistake."

The near-victim got a stake, a name (Scout, "he went ahead of the row"), and royal treatment all summer. (Scout topped eight feet. Beat the whole official row by a head. As it happened: of course he did.)
