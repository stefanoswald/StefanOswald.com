Night 44

Mateo's Crash Compilation

Month 2: Mistakes Are How

Fence summit. Mateo, seven-almost-eight, showing off the summer's first scab, and then, by request, the full archive: "This elbow: the gravel turn, learning to drift. This knee: Ramp one. This other knee: ramp one again, same ramp, I went back, you have to go back or the ramp keeps your nerve.

This shin: the no-hands experiment. Findings: hands. Do they hurt? Then? Yeah. Now? They're receipts. Proof I paid for the skills. You can't ride like me without a knee like this. Everybody wants the riding.

Nobody wants the knee. But it's one price, the riding and the knee. Same purchase."
