Night 60

The Leaf on Harry's Back

Month 3: How Does Harry Feel?

This month belongs to Harry Hedgehog. (The caramel one. One autumn leaf riding his back in every season, nobody knows how it stays. Brook-edge resident. And, the animals agree, the valley's best feeler: Harry always knows who's sad before they do.)

She asked him straight, brook-side, dream-adjacent dusk: "Harry. How do you always know? Who's hurting. You knew about the big kid at the park before I told anybody." Harry rustled his quills, modest.

"I'm low to the ground, miss. I see the feet. Happy feet and heavy feet walk different. Everyone watches faces, faces are trained. Feet are honest." Notebook: "Harry reads feet. Faces are trained. Feet are honest. Watch the whole person, not the part they decorate." this month had its teacher.
