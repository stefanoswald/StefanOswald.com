Night 19

Theo's First Why Chain

Month 1: The Question Collector

It began at dinner: "Why bath? Because you're muddy. Why muddy? Because you played hard. Why played hard? Because it was a good day. Why good day?" (The table looked at the veteran. The veteran set down her fork.

She'd been three once. She knew this game from the inside, knew it isn't a game.) "Fee-oh. Here's the secret. The whys never end. They go down forever. That's not broken, that's how deep the world is.

But at bedtime, you pick a floor and stand on it. Tonight's floor: it was a good day because we were all in it together. That's the bottom. Stand there... Why together... That's the floor, fee-oh." (He stood on it. It held. It's held this family for years.)
