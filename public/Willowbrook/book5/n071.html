Night 71

The Empty Chair at Story Hour

Month 3: How Does Harry Feel?

Story hour. Full carpet. And one absence only she clocked: Sam. (Library boy. Stop-start feet. Fourth-for-the-game. Three straight weeks a regular, then gone.) "Where's Sam?" she asked the librarian, after. "Sam...?

Oh, the quiet one, back corner. I don't actually know, dear." Nobody knew. That was the finding: a kid can just... blink out of a carpet, and the carpet closes over the spot. The response: a card.

("Get better or get back, whichever one is the true one. The carpet has your spot. Olivia + the story hour.") Routed via the librarian, who had an address on file and bent a rule, quietly, in a good cause.

Sam returned the next week. (Chickenpox. The card, his mother reported, lived on the fridge the whole itchy time.) "You noticed I was gone," he said. Just that.
