Night 191

The Feelings Words Kit Check

Month 7: Big Enough

Link nine, and Mama's addition to the chain: "The buttons matter. This matters more." The feelings kit check. The list, run at the lamp like gear before a climb: "Frustrated: mad-because-it-almost-worked.

Have it, use it, taught it to Fee-oh. Embarrassed: inside mistake, outside eyes. Have it. Survived it, the Mama-at-circle thing; it's survivable, tell everybody. Nervous-excited: two coats. Wearing them right now about September.

Overwhelmed..." A pause. "the new one. Mama, what's the word for too-much-at-once? Not bad-too-much. Just... All of it, arriving? Overwhelmed is the word. Big rooms, new faces, six instructions at once, first days are built of it.

And the tool for it? Name it, find a corner, breathe three, and remember: overwhelmed is weather. It passes through. You're the sky, love. First day of school, you're still the sky." The scenarios, rehearsed: can't find the room (ask; teachers are handles).

Lily's across the class (the bench at recess; whistle code adapted to wave code). Someone's unkind (the kit knows this one cold).
