Night 62

When Theo Cried at Nothing

Month 3: How Does Harry Feel?

Theo cried at dinner. At nothing, wrong-colored cup, allegedly. The old family read: tired. This month's deeper read, applied by its student: "Fee-oh. The cup's not it. Cups don't make that cry. That cry had a basement."

(Every question has one. Every cry too.) The gentle dig, floor-level, after: it came out in pieces. At preschool-for-littles, his friend Gus said Theo wasn't his best friend anymore, Milo was. "That's not a cup..."

She confirmed. "That's a heavy. Okay. The truth: best friends can be two. Or three. Gus saying Milo doesn't unsay you. And if he did unsay you, that's his mistake to find, and you got a whole family and me while he finds it." The cry: finished properly, about the real thing, and done in minutes.
