Night 201

The Chain's Last Week

Month 7: Big Enough

The chain entered its last week, seven links, torn one per porch morning, each a system check on the pattern: "Buttons. Laces. The route. The latch. The alarm." (Eleven consecutive self-risings. The pattern and I are colleagues.

Check.) "The feelings kit." And the last link, saved for the last morning, which turned out, she unrolled it slowly, to be in Mama's handwriting, slipped in during some unguarded splice: "You were always big enough. The chain was just how you found out.

M." (The porch was quiet awhile. The story respected it.) "... The chain was a mirror..." She said finally. "Thirty badges and twenty-two skills and the whole time it was... just showing me what was already there.

That's what all the good chains do, love. Every list you'll ever make. They don't build you. They introduce you."
