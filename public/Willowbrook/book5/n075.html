Night 75

Harry and the Big Kid

Month 3: How Does Harry Feel?

The big kid, the "babyish" one, the notebook-mocker, was at the park again. Alone, this week. (He's always alone, she noticed suddenly. The noticing rearranged him.) The quill theory, tested: slow-and-front, like the Duchess.

No flinch. She sat on the wall's far end. Said nothing. Minute four, from his end: "... That your question book? Yep... My little sister had one like it. Before we moved. She's at my dad's now."

(There it was. The whole basement, in eleven words. The quills, the "babyish..." The alone, all of it guarding a missing sister who liked things.). "Wanna hear the best question this week? A fish one."

She read him the fish one. He almost smiled. It was enough. It was the crack where the light gets in.
