Night 319

The Rainy Day Operation

Month 11: The Kindness Detectives

Indoor recess week moved the agency's operations inside, and the classroom, surveilled properly for the first time, gave up its secrets: "The indoor saves: Wren, fixes the game-shelf boxes. Every indoor recess. The puzzles get their corners found, the decks get counted, the missing dice get hunted. Nobody asked.

The shelf just works because one quiet person maintains it. (The whole class thinks the shelf is naturally like that. There's no naturally, Lily. There's wren.) Elias, draws kids' pets from description. A one-boy portrait service, free, quiet, queue of one to three kids daily.

He asks two questions ('what color' and 'what's the best thing they do') and the second question is the whole art. Rosa-No-Relation, teaches the four-square kids chess with the broken set. And Noor, this one, Lily. Noor tells the globe.

Kids ask 'where's your first country' and she shows them, and then she shows them theirs, everybody's got a somewhere, and the globe went from furniture to the most-visited station in the room."
