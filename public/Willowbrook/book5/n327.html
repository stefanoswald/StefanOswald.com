Night 327

The Almost-Five List

Month 11: The Kindness Detectives

The annual rite, fifth edition: Last year's list, checked. ("Wat for can do," written at almost-four, in inventive spelling, seven items. The check checks the promises.) "One: Reed. 'For gets good.' Delivered. Chapter books.

The deep shelf. The level-free handoffs. The corner's mine on Tuesdays. Item: Exceeded. Two: Rite leders. Delivered plus: the author letter, the pen pal, the rules, the saves. Three volumes of questions. Exceeded. Three: The deep end.

'For keeps it.' Kept. Plus the pole, the seven bars, the big rock at the swimming hole. Exceeded. Four: Big sled hill. Eleven runs on the best snow day of the era. Kept. Five: Teach Theo.

The letters. The losing school. The junior detective badge. The firework. Exceeded, and the student's gaining. Six: Ten minutes ladder on reading nights. Agreed and expanded. Kept with interest. Seven: Miss three a little. It's allowed. kept.

I missed three some. Mostly in September, at the gates. Three was good. But Mama. I wouldn't go back. Not for anything. That's the check's whole finding: every year I'd miss, and no year I'd return to. That's... that's what growing's supposed to feel like, I think. Homesick forward." Homesick forward.
