Night 69

The Duchess Feels

Month 3: How Does Harry Feel?

Farm day. The Duchess, watched with this month's eyes. "Grandpa. The Duchess pecks everybody... but watch. She only pecks when somebody comes at her fast. Or from behind. Slow-and-front, she just does the stare."

Joe leaned on the rail. "Go on... Something came at her fast once. From behind. Before us, maybe. The pecking's not mean. It's a... a alarm that never got turned off." Joe was quiet awhile.

"Hawk got her sister, year I got them. Right off the run. She was next to it." The whole coop reorganized itself in her head, the meanest chicken in the world becoming, in one sentence, the most scared one, still guarding a sister three years gone. "... I'm gonna keep coming slow-and-front forever. She'd appreciate that. In her way."
