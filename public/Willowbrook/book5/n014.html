Night 14

The Library's Question Shelf

Month 1: The Question Collector

She brought the notebook to the library. ("Eleven unanswered. The pond system says the survivors come here.") The librarian led her past the story shelves to a back section she'd never ranked: fat books, serious spines.

"Reference. The answer shelf. Encyclopedias, atlases, the big dictionary. These books don't tell stories. They wait for questions." The demonstration: "Why is the sky blue," looked up, found, read slow, half-understood, wholly thrilling. "So when I have a question, the answer is already here?

Just... standing? Waiting for me to come ask? That's the deal. Been the deal for a few thousand years." (In the wainscoting: "She found Reference today. Note the date. The lantern's long game enters phase three.")
