The Deep Shelf Month, Counted

Month 5: The Deep Shelf

Month's end. At month's end, The easy-to-count part included one chapter book. One sequel. One slump. Two words rescued. One letter mailed care-of. One office opened. One brother reading his name. One orange dog.

One E. W. (found in the wall; framed by the stairs; ninety years upstream on the same river). No list could quite hold that chapter books make you the illustrator. That hearts are the same make underneath, and honest underneaths fit everyone, and the tears are proof of delivery.

That mountains become pebbles on a schedule you can trust. That she has rounds now, accreted, not assigned, and knows the chain she's a link in. "Good book-month? The deepest shelf yet. And Mama, the books aren't the destination.

I figured it out. The books are the training. For reading everything else. Feet. Rooms. Weather. People. The books teach you how, and then the whole world turns out to be written." The whole world turns out to be written. Remembered. Shelved. Read on, little link.
