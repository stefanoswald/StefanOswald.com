Night 110

The Growing-Up Rumor

Month 4: True Stories

A rumor swept the preschool crowd this week: Big school has no recess. Also: the teachers yell. Also: a kid got lost in there once and never came out. The panic: general. The response, from this month's scholar: "Stop.

Rumors are stories without the permission slip. Before we believe the scary. Who saw it? We got an actual big-school kid on our actual fence." The fact-check, done at the fence with the veteran: "No recess?"

Mateo snorted. "Two recesses. Plus gym. The rumor's backwards. Yelling teachers? Mrs. Okafor yelled once. At a dog. That came in the door. She gave us extra recess after for the excitement. The lost kid?

That's me, dummy. First week. I found the library instead of the gym and stayed there. Best wrong turn I ever took. The story grew legs after." The rumors, returned to the preschool crowd, corrected and sourced.
