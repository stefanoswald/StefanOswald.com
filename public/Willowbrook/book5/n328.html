Night 328

The Secret-Kindness Party

Month 11: The Kindness Detectives

Olivia planned her fifth birthday at the park fort with Lily, June, and Noor.

The guest rule remained everybody. Friends from school, the old park group, Mr. Petrov, Mrs. Hollis, the librarian, Rosa-No-Relation and her mother, Danny, and one invited dog with a bow all went on the list.

For the party game, every guest would receive a card naming a quiet kindness to do without being caught: refill a cup, include someone, carry a plate, return a dropped mitten.

After cake, the deeds would be read aloud without names.

"The whole party becomes a kindness hunt," Noor said.

The cake would show a magnifying glass made of stars, the year Olivia had learned to look closely.

By the end of planning, the children had a long guest list, a stack of secret cards, and one problem: the dog could not read. Theo volunteered to whisper its assignment.
