Night 354

The Jar Harvest, Fifth Edition

Month 12: Almost Five

The morning after: The harvest. The year's slips, read back on the rug, answers rendered: "'Maybe the big school is happening for me.' Confirmed beyond all arguing. The cubby knew my name before I did.

The bench. The corner. Rosa-No-Relation. The whole rooted year. 'Maybe the different rooms is happening for Wren and me.' Confirmed. The wave code. The pact. And noor, the different rooms is the whole reason there's a Noor.

The geography lost so hard it switched sides. 'Maybe the chain delay is happening for me.' Confirmed. Twenty-two extra links of training camp. The pattern ran because the rumor was wrong. 'Maybe the flooded gym is happening for us.'

Confirmed: eleven people and a bear. Best venue of the career. 'Maybe the fox trade is happening for me.', confirmed, expensively. The tuition paid. The discipline added. The fox is somewhere being rare, like the snow leopard.

Good luck, fox." And Theo's first-ever slip, retrieved with ceremony: "'Maybe the big slide.' Fee-oh. Answer? I did the whole slide. Forty times. Confirmed. The jar's five for five, all years, all slips, except the crockpot ones, and those are just... still cooking.

The jar's never once come up empty, Mama. Five years of maybes and the year always answers. The year likes the question. That's the whole secret of the jar: the maybe is a door you leave open, and years walk through open doors."
