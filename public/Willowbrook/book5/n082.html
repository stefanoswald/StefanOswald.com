Night 82

The Feelings Are Not Instructions

Month 3: How Does Harry Feel?

Hard truth page. The jealous afternoon: Lily and June had a two-person joke from a sleepover she'd missed. All afternoon: the laughing, the "you had to be there." And the feeling, green, hot, mean, wanting her to wreck it.

Say something sharp. Split the joke down its middle. She didn't. Barely. Came home clenched, and unclenched at the lamp: "I felt like wrecking it. The feeling had a whole plan. It wanted my mouth.

But? But I remembered, you said feelings are visitors. So I let it visit. I just didn't let it drive." And Mama gave this month its hardest, best line: "That's the whole secret of growing up, love, and plenty never learn it: feelings are weather, not instructions.

You feel them all the way. You obey them almost never. The feeling is real. The plan it brings is usually terrible."
