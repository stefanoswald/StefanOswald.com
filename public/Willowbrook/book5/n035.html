Night 35

The Wrong Bus Stop... Almost

Month 2: Mistakes Are How

Errand day. The bus bench. Mama distracted by the transfer schedule, and the bus that pulled up was the wrong line, and Mama, hurrying, stepped toward it... "Mama. That's the four. We want the seven.

It says on the front now." The stop. At month's end, The near-miss, averted by the shortest passenger. And Mama did the thing this family does, made it a lesson going the other way: "You just watched me almost make a mistake.

What'd you notice?... That you make them too. Even you. Even me. Even everybody. Forever. That's not the scary part, love, that's the relief. You never have to be done making them. You just have to keep a reader at the bus stop."
