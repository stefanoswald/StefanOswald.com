Night 324

The Month's Biggest Find

Month 11: The Kindness Detectives

This month's last case review, at the lamp, and the finding that closed the saves was about the detective: "Mama. The agency's biggest find isn't in the saves. It's in me. A month of hunting kindness and, the hunting changed the hunter.

I can't not see it now. The tea radar. The shelf that works because Wren. The somebodies everywhere. The town looks different permanently, like the letters, like the numbers. The kindness turned on, and it doesn't turn off.

And here's the thing under the thing: I've been kinder this month than any other month, and I wasn't trying to be kinder. The looking-for-it makes you it. You become what you hunt." Mama set down the brush.

"Say that last part again, slow. You become what you hunt. If you spend your days detecting meanness, collecting unfairness, filing grudges, you become a expert in it, and then... you're a person whose whole town is evidence of bad. Same streets.

Same people. Different saves. The town becomes whichever case you're building. So the only real question is: Which detective are you gonna be? Because everybody's already one or the other. Everybody's building a case about the world, all the time. Most people just never checked which one."
