Night 163

The Sidewalk Chalk Hundred Chart

Month 6: Numbers Are Everywhere

The driveway became a hundred chart this week, ten by ten, chalked giant, a full afternoon's public works project. And then the patterns, found by walking them: Hop the tens column: "10, 20, 30, the tens are a ladder up the side." Walk any row: "the ones digit just goes 1-2-3 again and again, every row is the same street with a different name." The fives: "a stripe down the middle.

Fives are organized." And the great discovery, hopping diagonally: "11, 22, 33: The doubles are a staircase. The chart has a secret staircase." Theo's contribution: standing on 3. His number. Refusing to move. They chalked a little house around him on the 3. He remained in residence until dinner.
