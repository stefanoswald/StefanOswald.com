Night 353

The Fifth Maybe Night

Month 12: Almost Five

On Night 353, the family lit the candle and brought out the maybe jar for the fifth year.

They retold the old blizzard story in four voices. Papa described the storm. Mama watched at the window. Olivia made the snowplow appear. Theo stood on his chair for his line and shouted, "Perfect timing!"

Then each person offered a hope to the flame.

Olivia wondered whether the next grade, bigger books, and the high playground wall might all be preparing her for something.

Theo said, "My paper chain. My school. My five, four. All of it."

Mama and Papa spoke more quietly. Together they said, "Maybe the whole point was always this exact table."

Everyone blew out the candle in one breath. They placed the new slips in the jar.

Maybe did not mean pretending every event was good. It meant leaving a little room for meaning they could not see yet.
