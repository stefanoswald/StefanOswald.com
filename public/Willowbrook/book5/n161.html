Night 161

The Half Birthday

Month 6: Numbers Are Everywhere

The tally book of days produced a finding: "If my birthday's day 361, then half a year after is... day 178-ish of this book, but from the birthday it's, today. Today I'm four and a half. Exactly. Halves count.

The clock says so, half hours are real hours' cousins." The observance, family-agreed within the hour: half a cupcake (bisected with fraction-war precision, cutter-chooses-last applied to a party of one, which she noted "still works. I was very fair to me"). Half the birthday song ("Happy birthday to..." full stop; Theo found this unbearable and finished it anyway, which became the tradition's best part).

Half a candle. One half-wish ("saving the whole ones, half-wishes are for medium wants. I half-wished for rain tomorrow. The garden's firsty").
