Night 1

The Notebook of Questions

Month 1: The Question Collector

On the first night of her fourth year, Olivia opened a new notebook.

"The word jar keeps words," she told Mama. "The brave list keeps brave things. But my questions keep falling out, and nobody is keeping those."

She printed QUESTIONS on the cover. On the first page she made one rule: EVERY QUESTION GETS WRITTEN. EVEN THE WEIRD ONES.

"What if we cannot answer them all?" Mama asked.

Olivia considered this. Through the window, the Great Willow stood in the dark, taking its time over whatever it might say next.

"Then the questions can wait," she said. "Questions do not spoil."

Her first entry was: WHY DOES THE MOON FOLLOW THE CAR?

The second was harder: WHO ASKED THE FIRST QUESTION EVER?

Olivia tucked the notebook beside Bobby Bear. Two questions were safe inside it, and hundreds more were already on their way.
