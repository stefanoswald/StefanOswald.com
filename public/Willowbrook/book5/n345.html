Night 345

The Wish Research, Fifth Edition

Month 12: Almost Five

The wish big think, fifth annual, the bear retained as counsel: The shortlist, by the notebook: "1. The fun run rematch win. (Struck: that's a training thing, not a wish thing. Wishes are for what training can't ladder to.

Rule's five years old. Holds.) 2. The snow leopard sticker. (Struck, gently: the hole is the exhibit. Wishing it filled would un-true the album. Some wishes you outgrow before they come true. That's growing's weirdest trick.)

3. That wren's voice stays out loud. (Long pause at this one. Moved to the prayer column, 'wishes are for the candle; some things are bigger than candles.') 4. [The fourth entry, as always, is not written.

This year's drawing: a magnifying glass, and behind it, the whole town, every window gold, every door open, the second grid drawn as actual rivers, gold ones, running under the streets. And around the entire town: the circle.]" (The circle around everyone. Wider every year. As it happened the progression and says what it always says: whatever it is precisely, it's the kind you draw instead of spell, and the kind that mostly comes true, by the ordinary magic of a person who keeps drawing it bigger.) Sealed. Saved. The bear: sworn, as ever. Two days.
