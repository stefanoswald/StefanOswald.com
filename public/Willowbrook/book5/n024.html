Night 24

Why Are Some Words Not Allowed?

Month 1: The Question Collector

The mud incident produced this week's question: "Why are some words not allowed? They're just sounds. The duck word and the bad word are both just mouths making air. Who decided?" Papa took it like the real question it was: "Nobody decided.

Everybody decided. Words do things to rooms, you know that; you've watched it. Some words are built like doors slamming. Say one and every face in the room does the flinch. The word's not evil.

It's just loud in a way that bruises. We save them for when something deserves a slam. Like the mud. The mud arguably deserved it." (The family standard, as it happened. Held, more or less, for life. Almost never. The bang stayed kept.)
