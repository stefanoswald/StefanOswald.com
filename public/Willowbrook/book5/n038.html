Night 38

Lily's Burnt Batch

Month 2: Mistakes Are How

Big news from the bakery: Lily's first solo batch. Bigger news: burnt. Black. All twelve. Olivia arrived to find the disaster on the cooling rack and Lily red-eyed, and Rosa doing the remarkable thing: Setting the burnt tray on the counter.

Studying it with full professional attention. "Oven's hot spot, back left. I burned two hundred rolls learning that corner. Two hundred. Now: what do we know?" Lily, sniffling: "... Back left burns. What else?... Turn the tray halfway?

That's a baker talking." And Rosa took one burnt roll, hung it from a nail by the oven, where, Olivia noticed, three other burnt things already hung, dusty, historic. "Your first burn joins the wall. Every baker's got a wall.

It means you baked alone. Welcome to the trade." The second batch, tray turned, came out gold.
