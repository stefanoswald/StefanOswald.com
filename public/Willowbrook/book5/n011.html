Night 11

Why Is the Brook in a Hurry?

Month 1: The Question Collector

"The brook runs day and night. Even winter, under the lid. Where is it going that it can't be late for?" They investigated with leaf boats, raced from the bridge. "It's going to the river..." said Papa. "The river's going to the sea. Every brook on earth is the sea, starting small and far away. The brook is the sea?! Our brook? That I can step over? Give it a hundred miles."

The notebook took it whole: "The brook is the sea wen it's little. It's not in a hurry. It's just already on the way. Question: Am I already on the way to something? Answer: Obviously.

Everything is. The trick is you can't see the sea from the brook part." (the story, from the sea part, confirms: she was. You can't. That's the mercy of it.)
