Night 116

The Two Versions of the Fence Story

Month 4: True Stories

An argument at the park, worth the whole month: Lily and Mateo told the same event, yesterday's kickball dispute, completely differently. Lily: "The big kids took the field." Mateo: "We asked and nobody answered so we started." Both honest.

The story verifies: Neither lied. Lily was by the benches (the asking was inaudible there). Mateo asked facing the wrong group. Two true stories, one event, zero liars, and one near-war. The scholar, fascinated, walked the actual ground: stood where Lily stood, then where Mateo stood.

"From here, they just took it. From here, they asked. The truth has a where-you-stood in it. Everybody's telling the view from their spot, and everybody's spot is missing something." The settlement: a re-do, both groups, asking done at center field, audible to all benches.
