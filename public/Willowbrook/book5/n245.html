Night 245

The Wren Recruitment Pays Off

Month 9: Friends Old and New

The October part..." A Ms. Alvarez-room friend by October or I recruit you one. "had gone unenforced. And this week the mural bench delivered the ending nobody drafted: Wren arrived at first recess with someone.

A small fierce-eyed girl from Wren's room. "This is Noor..." said Wren, clearly, audibly, making the introduction herself. "She draws maps. I told her about your dragons." (Noor, it emerged, draws maps of everywhere, her old country, her new street, the school, imagined islands, and labels the unknown parts with her own honest mark: small waves, meaning "more sea than I have seen."

Two cartographers, one bench, instant treaty. The dragons and the waves, compared at length.) The real headline, saved at the lamp: "Mama, I promised to recruit Wren a friend. But Wren recruited her own. And then, this is the part, she brought her to me.

The quiet one did the introducing. Wren's voice came all the way back and the first big thing she did with it was build a bridge."
