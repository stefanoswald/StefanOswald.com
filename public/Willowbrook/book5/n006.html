Night 6

The Question June Couldn't Stop

Month 1: The Question Collector

June's question ran away with her this week: "Why don't ducks' feet get cold?" Then: "Why is pond water colder than bath water? Why don't they care? Why?" The trio took it seriously, this administration takes all questions seriously.

Lily proposed the test: hands in the pond. Olivia proposed the library. ("Questions that survive the pond test go to the lantern. That's the way things worked.") The answer, found in a bird book, read aloud slow: duck feet run cool on purpose, special blood plumbing, so the pond can't steal their warm. June's face. "They built them for the cold."
