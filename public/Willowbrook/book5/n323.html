Night 323

The Agency's Succession Plan

Month 11: The Kindness Detectives

The agency held its succession ceremony this week, because its founders did the math: "Big school gets bigger every year, Lily. The agency needs depth." The commissioning, at the fort, care: Theo: promoted from junior asset to junior detective, first class. (Badges: the snack-dog case, solo; the bell operation, year-long, self-initiated; the dandelion supply chain.

The magnifier: his own now, not loaned. He held it like a knighthood.) Danny and the ball-crew kid (recruited via the open door she'd left; shy kindness, grown proud, ready for tradecraft): sworn in as field agents, blacktop division. And the oath, drafted for the occasion, administered at the mitten flag: "Witness what wants witnesses.

Guard what wants guarding. Report the dark in stacks. Never spend the anonymous. The kindness comes first. Even before the saves. So sworn. The agency outlives the founders now..." She said, watching Theo practice magnifying a ladybug with forensic intensity.

"That's the whole test of a built thing. The code passed to the windows. The rule escaped the house. The bench game went official. And now the saves got heirs. Build things that outlive the building, Lily. It's the only shape worth the name."
