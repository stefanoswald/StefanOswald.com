Night 20

The Question Race

Month 1: The Question Collector

The trio invented a sport: Question racing. Rules: strangest question wins; must be real (asked because you want to know, not just to be weird); judge rotates. This week's heat, under the oak: Lily: "Do fish know they're wet, or is wet just... normal to them, like air is to us?" June: "When a bug walks on the ceiling, is the ceiling the floor to him?

Is our whole room upside down to somebody?" And Olivia, reigning champion, from the fourth branch: "When I remember yesterday, where do I go to look at it? Because I'm not there anymore. But I can see it.

So where's it kept, and who lets me in?" (Silence. The judge, June, ruled it unanswerable, therefore ineligible, therefore the greatest question of the season. All three notebooks took it. The back sections grow together now. That's what friends' windows do: stay open onto the same sky.)
