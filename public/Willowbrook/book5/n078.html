Night 78

The Give-Away Growl

Month 3: How Does Harry Feel?

Playdate at the house: Lily, June, and June's cousin Vera (older, loud, takes up rooms). Vera admired the bear, the bear, and June's cousin's admiring has hands. And Olivia said "you can hold him" in a voice the room believed, except Lily. Lily, who has eleven-days-younger-than-her ears, heard it: the too-fast yes.

The scared-generous. And ran her own quiet operation: "Vera! The fort needs a inspector. You're the biggest. Only you can reach the roof." Vera, ennobled, departed roofward. The bear migrated home. After, at the window seat: "You gave him too quick.

Real yes is slower than that... I didn't want her to say I was a baby about him. Vera's opinion isn't worth the bear. Nobody's opinion is worth the bear."
