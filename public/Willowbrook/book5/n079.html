Night 79

The Loud House Guest

Month 3: How Does Harry Feel?

House guests this week: Papa's old friend's family, passing through. Their boy, Casper, six: Loud. Louder than Theo. Louder than weather. Grabbed toys, interrupted everything, narrated his own tricks, "Watch this. Watch this. Are you watching..."

The house's patience: thinning. And this month's student, watching feet and basements: "Mama,"..." He's not showing off. He's showing... Asking. Every 'watch this' is 'do you like me yet.' He does it more when nobody claps.

Regular showing-off does it more when they do clap. His is backwards. His is hungry. "The adjustment: she started catching the tricks early..." Saw it! The spin one! Good one! "before the volume climbed.

And Casper, fed, quieted. By dinner he was almost regular-boy loud. By goodbye he'd given Theo his best paper airplane.
