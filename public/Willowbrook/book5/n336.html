Night 336

The Fifth Interview

Month 12: Almost Five

The fat notebook. The questions. Fifth annual administration: "Favorite color? Still gold. Gold's got tenure now. Favorite food? Grilled cheese, and the rustic roll. My rustic roll. The wall's first family member. You eat your diplomas in this family.

Best friend? Lily. Check the book. Five for five. Some answers are steady. What do you want to be? The list grew: wever scientist, author, librarian, and kindness detective. Papa, write this part. I found out they might be one job.

They're all looking close at things and telling the truth about them. I want the looking-close-and-telling-true job. Whatever it's called when I get there. Scariest thing this year? The first day.

For about six hours. Then it was mine. Best thing? The eyes. The kindness ones. Permanent. Anything to tell Five?... Tell Five: the town's better than its accounting, the hot thing is a sneeze, and the whole shelf was the training.

She's got the best kit anybody ever packed. Tell her: spend it. Kits only keep what they give."
