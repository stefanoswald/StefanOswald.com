Night 213

The Third Week Shift

Month 8: The Settling In

It happened on a Tuesday, week three, and she caught it happening, because catching things happening is her whole instrument: "Mama. I walked the route today and didn't see it. My feet did the lampposts and the cinnamon and the guard-wave and I was thinking about something else the whole time, and then I was just... There.

At my cubby. Without arriving. The school stopped being a place I go and started being a place I am. The backpack broke in. The backpack broke in. Day two me should see me now.

Regular became comfortable right on the schedule you said. Somebody should tell day-two kids the schedule is real, it's a ten-day schedule, I timed it, it's practically a law..." Notebook: "Regular → comfortable: 10 days, confirmed by experiment. Tell all the day-two kids: The schedule is real.

Also: When the feet go underground, the head gets free. The route thinks for me now. That's where the good thinking comes from. Roads your feet already know."
