Night 247

The Birthday Party Invitation Dilemma

Month 9: Friends Old and New

Rosa-No-Relation's birthday: a small party. "Mama says three friends only..." She reported miserably, holding the invitation she'd received, and knowing Lily and June had not. The web, meeting arithmetic. The dilemma, worked at the lamp: "If I go, am I picking Rosa-No-Relation over Lily?

The old guard versus the school wing? The web's not supposed to have versus in it... Does Lily think it has versus in it?" The consultation: Lily's ruling, instant and clean: "Go, obviously. Free-friend parties are free-friend parties, everybody's mama has a number.

I don't get invited to everything and neither do you. The friendship isn't the guest list, Olivia. The friendship is the friday-after, you'll tell me everything and we'll laugh at the parts. That's our party."

(The Friday-after. As it happened this law held for decades of guest lists, both directions.)
