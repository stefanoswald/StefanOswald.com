Night 331

When the Helpers Noticed Olivia

Month 11: The Kindness Detectives

At Sunday dinner, Papa placed a folder in front of Olivia. Her friends and family had secretly filled it with kindnesses she thought no one saw.

Lily wrote that Olivia always took the smaller half when they split something and rebuilt agreements stronger after breaking them.

Theo wrote about the bell he used to comfort people. "She thinks the bell is my idea. I learned it from the books she left me."

Ms. Alvarez wrote: SHE READS THE ROOM BEFORE SHE READS THE STORY. EVERY TUESDAY, SHE CHANGES THE READING CORNER FOR WHOEVER IS HAVING THE HARD DAY.

Mrs. Hollis thanked her for forty-one Friday visits.

Olivia had spent months watching for goodness that went unseen. She had not realized that other people were watching too.

Mama closed the folder. "When you teach a family to notice, eventually they notice you."

Olivia's face grew warm. She read every page again before bed.
