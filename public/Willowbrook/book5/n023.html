Night 23

The Rain Delay Question

Month 1: The Question Collector

Rain took the park day. The window got the vigil, and the vigil got a question: "Where does rain go? After. It lands, it puddles, the puddles dry. Where?" Mama drew it on the steamed glass: gutter, ground, brook, river, sea, and then up, sun-lifted, into clouds, and around again.

"The same water? The same water. Going around forever? Some of this rain has been ocean. Been clouds. Been rivers a thousand times. Been the puddle you stomped when you were two, maybe." The stillness.

Then: "So the water never gets used up. It just keeps getting to be things. Puddle, then cloud, then brook, then puddle again..." And Theo, at the glass, catching the shape of it his way: "Water plays again and again!"
