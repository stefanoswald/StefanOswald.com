Night 16

The Unanswerable Drawer

Month 1: The Question Collector

Some questions defeated everybody. Papa, Mama, the reference shelf, even the librarian ("and she knows where everything is"). "What happens to Tuesday when it's over? Where does the flame go when you blow it out?

Who did the Duchess love, ever?" The solution, hers: The back section. Label: "The unanswerables. The front questions get answers and close. These ones stay open. Open questions are like open windows, stuff comes in through them forever.

If I closed them with a bad answer, the window shuts." Papa read the section twice. "Some of the smartest grown-ups I know never learned that. Learned what? To leave the window open."
