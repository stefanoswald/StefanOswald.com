Night 64

The Quill Question

Month 3: How Does Harry Feel?

She asked Harry the impolite question. "Harry. You're the gentlest one. Why are you covered in points?" Harry laughed his rustling laugh. "Miss, it's the other way round. I can afford to be the gentlest one because of the points.

The soft ones without armor spend all day defending. I never have to. The quills do the worrying so the heart doesn't... So the prickly ones might be the softest inside. The prickles might be why there's still soft.

Often..." said Harry. "Not always. But when you meet a prickly one, a big kid, say, at a park, it's worth asking what the quills are guarding."
