Night 298

The Fifth Candle Countdown Begins

Month 10: Losing Well

The chain returned this week, fifth edition, the almost-five countdown, self-built start to finish now (Theo on scissors support, supervised, "the apprentice cuts the easy links"). The words of the year, one per link, the tradition holding: "Questions. The wobble morning. Mistakes.

The salt cake. Harry's feet. Mrs. Hollis. The truth month. Gloaming. The deep shelf. The author letter. Numbers. The bean jar. The pattern of me. The first day. The mural bench. Rosa-no-relation. Noor.

The web. The rule. The hot thing. The bench crown. The snow leopard..." (Sixty-some links. The years keep needing more chain. "The years get denser, Mama. That's either the years or me. Probably both.")

And the hanging ceremony gained a witness with opinions: "My birthday chain when?" (Theo. Four in the fall. The apprentice sees all setup and wants it.) "Yours starts at your almost-four, same as mine did.

The chain waits for you. Chains keep." (Chains keep. Like stairs. Like poles. Like routes. The family physics, complete.)
