Night 335

The Final Links

Month 12: Almost Five

The chain entered single digits this week. The morning tearings, down to the year's densest words: "The eyes. The second grid. Homesick forward. The trap. Case 9. Nine links, Mama. And here's the fifth-year finding about chains: the first year, the chain counted down to the birthday.

Now it counts up the year. The birthday's just the... the binding. The chain's the book. I don't need the counting anymore. I need the remembering. Maybe that was always the job and the counting was the disguise."

The counting was the disguise. (Most of the best traditions run this way, as it happened. The advertised purpose gets you started; the real one keeps you.) Notebook: "The counting was the disguise. The remembering was the job. Nine links. The year was dense and good. Bind it."
