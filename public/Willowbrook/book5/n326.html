Night 326

The Five-Year Chain Shortens

Month 11: The Kindness Detectives

The chain crossed into its final stretch this week, fourteen links, the birthday two weeks out, the morning tearings gone ceremonial: "The hot thing. The somebody wall. Noor." ("The paper grew.") "The trap. Case 9." And the tearing developed an audience: Theo, every morning, at her elbow, doing the math out loud, because his chain, his first, starts at his almost-four, this fall, and he is studying.

("The apprentice checks the tradition before inheriting it. Correct procedure..." The founder approved. "Watch the tearing, Fee-oh. The tearing is the easy part. The words are the work. You gotta live a year worth chaining first. Yours is coming. Live it big.")
