Night 99

The Tooth Wiggle Cover-Up

Month 4: True Stories

It happened. The first wiggle. Bottom front. Two years after Mateo's preview, right on the early edge of schedule. And she, the town's leading advocate of truth. Hid it. Two days. Chewed on the other side.

Smiled carefully. The discovery: Mama, watching her eat. The confession, and under it, this month knows where to dig, the basement: "If the tooth is wiggly, the big school stuff is really coming. The tooth is the... the first domino.

I wanted the dominoes to wait. So you hid the tooth to stop the calendar... It sounds silly out loud. It sounds human out loud. Everybody's tried to hide something from time itself. Doesn't work.

Never has. But here's the true thing: the dominoes were always coming, love. Hiding just meant carrying them alone." The wiggle: publicized. Theo: fascinated ("The upgrade!" the briefing held, two years later). The fairy question: reopened for negotiation.
