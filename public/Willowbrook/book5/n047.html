Night 47

The Swim Stroke Correction

Month 2: Mistakes Are How

Swim lessons hit a wall this week: her crawl stroke stalled. Kicking harder made it worse. Frustration: rising. The teacher's fix: Papa filmed one lap. Then, poolside, dripping, towel-caped, she watched herself. "... My knees.

They're bending all the way. The teacher said knees but I fought my knees were straight, they feel straight... That's the trap..." said the teacher. "Everyone's mistake feels straight from the inside. That's why swimmers watch film.

Why writers read out loud. You can't fix what you can't see, and the inside view lies." Next lap, knees long: faster. Instantly.
