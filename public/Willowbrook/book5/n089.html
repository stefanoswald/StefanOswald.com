Night 89

The Feelings Month, Counted

Month 3: How Does Harry Feel?

Month's end. At month's end, Some changes fit neatly on the page: one hedgehog faculty. One Duchess: understood at last. One Derek: cracked open. One Sam: carded back to the carpet. One Priya: second-Saturdayed.

One Casper: fed early, quieted. One Vera: fort-inspectored. One empty carrier: witnessed, saved in the smallest writing. One Grandpa freight-day: kept alongside, near-not-with. One "just ears" treaty: agreed at the window seat.

One Theo: operational, dandelion supply chain and all. Other changes resisted numbers: that feelings are weather, not instructions. That alone comes in two kinds and asking is free. That mean is mostly alarm. That being known is half the fix.

That everybody, everybody, is carrying. "Good feelings-month? The deepest one. Mama, the other months taught me things. This one taught me people. People are the only subject that's also everybody." The only subject that's also everybody.

Remembered. The notebook: full. Walk where they can see you know, little one. All the far pages say you did.
