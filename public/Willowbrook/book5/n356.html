Night 356

The Five List

Month 12: Almost Five

The annual charter, drafted at the lamp: "Wat five can do. A list.1. Reed anything. (The maps-in-front era opens. The golden one, the librarian says.) 2. Rite everything. (Books, rules, saves, the wall letter.

The second fat notebook starts this summer. I'm collecting the parents.) 3. The whole playground. (Bars, pole, wall pending. The wall is first grade's yet. Yets are a direction.) 4. Teach fee-oh the reading. (The campaign continues.

He's got six letters on. The lights come faster now.) 5. Run the agency. (The eyes are permanent. The saves have heirs. Looking close, looking kind.) 6. Fifteen minutes ladder on reading nights, winter part permanent. (Agreed.

The union holds.)" And item seven, the tradition, written last, slow: "7. Miss four a little. (It's allowed. Four was the densest one yet. They keep being the densest one yet. That's either the years or me. Still probably both.)"
