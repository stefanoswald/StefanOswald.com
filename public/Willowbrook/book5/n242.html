The Settling Month... Months, Counted

Month 8: The Settling In

Season's end. The check, covering the whole settling stretch, because settling doesn't keep to month borders and neither does this entry: The notebook could hold one route gone underground. One reading corner job. One jar franchised.

One election amended. One conference. Seven monkey bars. One pole. One class book. One Halloween forecast. One Thanksgiving. One solstice. One teacher letter ("colleague who happened to be free," framed). Some changes belonged outside the count: that regular becomes comfortable on a real schedule.

That the rules travel and only the geography changes. That fix-the-spills-watch-the-bridges might be the whole rule. That the quiet ones witness the steady things. That she built a there there, and the there holds.

"Good settling? The deepest kind. Mama, in September the school was a building I was brave about. Now it's a place my feet know in the dark. That's not the school changing. That's roots. I grew roots into a whole new ground and didn't even feel them going down, you never feel the roots.

You just notice one day the wind doesn't move you." You never feel the roots. You just notice the wind doesn't move you. Remembered. Rooted. Grow on.
