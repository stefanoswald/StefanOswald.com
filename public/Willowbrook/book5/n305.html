Night 305

The Agency Opens

Month 11: The Kindness Detectives

This month's founding, at the fort, Lily co-signing: The kindness detective agency. The mission statement, drafted on case-notebook page one: "The town is full of secret kindness. Anonymous is the biggest size of warm. Which means the biggest warms are all hiding.

Somebody should catch them. Not to bust them. To know. The unwitnessed good deserves witnesses. " The equipment: two magnifying glasses, the case notebook, and the agency's one hard rule, agreed at founding: "We catch them.

We log them. We never tell the town who. The anonymous stays anonymous, we're witnesses, not journalists. The log is for the story, not the headlines." Case zero, saved within the hour, proof of concept: the mitten tree's off-season replenishment.

(Someone restocks it in april. Nobody needs mittens in April. Someone is filling it early, for next winter, eight months ahead. "That's premeditated kindness, Lily. That's planning-ahead warm. Case zero is already the best case.")

Notebook: "The agency is open. The unwitnessed good gets witnesses now. Case zero: The april mittens. Premeditated warm. The town is already better than anybody's counting. That's the whole theory. Now we prove it."
