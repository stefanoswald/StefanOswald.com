Night 126

The Second Book Gets Written

Month 5: The Deep Shelf

The second book got written this week. ("The bear and the river." The influence: acknowledged. "It's not copying. The river's from the book but the bear's from my life and the underneath is from buttons.

That's a trail, not a copy.") The craft, upgraded with this month's stolen tools: a cliffhanger, added at the page turn ("the bear's boat tips, page turn, you have to keep going, I made you"). An underneath, written honest (the bear misses someone; it has an empty-cage shape; the author knows what she's doing now). And an ending with a door: sad thing happens, sad thing is survived..."

Nigh-nigh. "but earned this time, the author explained. "The first book's nigh-nigh was just the shape. This one's nigh-nigh got paid for." The reading: family, dinner, four performances. Theo cried at the middle and demanded the end twice. ("He cried. My book borrowed his real heart. It works. The pattern works.")
