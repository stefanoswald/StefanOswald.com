Night 182

The Chain's New Date

Month 7: Big Enough

The official letter came and dropped its bombshell: the first day is four weeks later than the fence-rumor date the chain was built on. The outrage: brief but sincere. ("I did the math on a rumor. Rule one!

'I heard' is not a witness! I broke my own rule and now my chain's wrong!") The recalibration: twenty-two new links, spliced in. But the upgrade, because she doesn't just fix, she improves: the new links hold skills instead of badges.

"The first dirty proved I'm ready inside. These ones make me ready outside. One skill a link: buttons, laces, the backpack, the route, the lunchbox latch. By the real first day, the whole machine of me is school-ready.

So the delay is... Happening for me. Obviously. Four extra weeks of training camp. The jar wins again." Notebook: "Check the source before you build the chain. Also: Delays are training camps wearing disguises. Month 7: the pattern of me. Let's go."
