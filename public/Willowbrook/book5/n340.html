Night 340

The Summer Office Opens

Month 12: Almost Five

Summer opened for business this week, and so did the office, fifth branch, corner suite, first session of the season. The reopening rules: the bucket rope, inspected and re-knotted (veteran knots, doubled, "the office runs on trust and hardware; check the hardware, the trust checks itself"). The summer reading list, hauled up in the first delivery: five chapter books (the librarian's summer stack, pre-assembled, waiting at the desk with the note: "For the office. M.").

And the season's first chapter, read at altitude, the park's noise arriving soft and sorted below. Mateo's third-branch return, comics in hand, and the department's annual review: "Same office. New altitude inside us both. You're going into first grade.

I'm going into fifth. And the tree..." He patted the trunk. "just keeps adding branches at the top like it knows we're coming. It knows..." She said, opening the book. "It's the best waiter there is.

It's got branches banked for both our whole childhoods. I checked the top. There's plenty."
