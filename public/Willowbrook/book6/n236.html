Night 236

The Class Book, Volume Two

Month 8: School Stories

Room 6 wrote its class book this spring (the tradition travels; Ms. Alvarez sent the template over; "Our Town, Volume Two"). Her contribution to the work: the consulting. The shape, shared when asked: lost, sad part, found, nigh-nigh..."

It works for towns too. "And the quiet joy of watching the pages wobble up without her: the garden got three pages. The mill whistle. The worm farm. Elias. Room 4's laureate, visiting via the buddy system, contributed the sequel to his legend: the crossing guard page, part two, four words this time: "He still waves.

Everyday." (The guard, presented his page at the corner by delegation: the glasses off, the barn dust, the kneel. "Part two..." He said. "I'm in a series." He is. Sure enough: the series runs as long as the corner does. Some posts get their literature. All of them should.)
