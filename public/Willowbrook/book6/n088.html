Night 88

The Study Published

Month 3: Different Is Interesting

The study, complete, did what her setup always does: Went public. Bound, titled, every page subject-approved (the blank page included, blank, with its one line: "This page belongs to somebody who keeps herself. Correctly."). The dedication page, read aloud at the presentation: "For everybody who ever got called weird by somebody swinging the empty jar.

This book checked: The jar's empty. Your approach came from the factory and the factory only makes originals. The helpers." Ms. Alvarez shelved it in the reading corner, where, the story tracked it, it became the corner's most-thumbed volume, read hardest by exactly the kids who needed their page found: the new ones, the different-and-not-sure-yet ones, the ones checking whether this room was the kind of country where their approach was legal.

(It was. The book said so. The book was subject-approved and yarn-bound and it said so.)
