Night 200

Night Two Hundred: The Lot at Dusk

Month 7: Team Willowbrook

Night two hundred, and the round-number rite went on location: the garden gate, gloaming, whole family, cocoa. The survey, from the gate: the beds (twenty-two, held, various). The bench. The shed. The stand.

The string lights. The whole forty-year nothing, one season into everything. "Two hundred nights of this year..." She said, on the gate's bottom rail. "and the garden's the biggest thing in it. But Mama, count the small things that made it: the bench.

The proposal. The marigold introduction. The attention watch. The whole enormous garden is just... small things, compounded. Same as the piano. Same as the doorframe. Same as everything this whole year keeps shouting: Nothing big is big. It's all small-times-days. The garden's just the proof you can eat."
