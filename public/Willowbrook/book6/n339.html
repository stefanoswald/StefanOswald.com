Night 339

Theo's Secret Gift

Month 12: Almost Six

Theo reopened his office behind the couch to make Olivia's birthday gift.

For two weeks he released only carefully measured clues.

"It is not a picture. It is not a book. It is a completely different thing."

He wrote every word without asking how to spell it. He borrowed string and visited Mr. Petrov for the smallest hinge in the shop.

"For a secret," Theo explained.

Mr. Petrov supplied the hinge and asked no questions.

Olivia was desperate to know more. She passed the couch slowly. She noticed scraps. She did not lift the blanket or question Mama.

"Not looking is much harder when the answer is three feet away," she said at bedtime.

But the secret belonged to Theo until he chose to give it. Respecting that boundary was part of the gift she could give him in return.

Behind the couch, a tiny hinge clicked shut.
