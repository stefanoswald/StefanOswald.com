Night 234

The Growth Spurt Reckoning

Month 8: School Stories

The body saved its own report this month: Everything shrank. (The shoes: pinching. The sleeves: wrists out. The reading-fort blanket configuration: recalculated twice.) The unofficial doorframe check (early; unceremonial; the birthday line holds the official record, but the pencil hovered and the gap said): a big year.

Maybe the biggest. "The growing went underground too, Mama. Same as everything, invisible daily, enormous eventually, and the shoes get the news before I do. The body runs its own campaign board and never posts it.

It just... deposits, every night, quiet, while the night librarians file the day. And then one morning the sleeves are short and the wall at the playground is reachable and the whole yet-schedule has to be recalculated because the equipment upgraded..." A pause. The grin, arriving.

"Six is gonna need all new hardware. Good thing the birthday's coming. The years know their business."
