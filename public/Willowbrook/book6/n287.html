Night 287

The Invention Notebook

Month 10: The Long Winter

This month's exports got their manifest this week: The invention notebook. (The lobby's whole winter of deliveries, catalogued for spring: "ideas are seeds; the winter grows them; the spring plants them; write them down or the thaw eats them.") The manifest, sampled: the ferry system. The radio drama form ("do the summer episodes from the fort, the whole squadron as cast").

The skill circuit ("run it all year, why did we only think of it snowed-in?"). The Hawk 2's wing revision (Mateo's, phoned in weekly all winter; the chalkboard's philosophy section now has a winter wing). The funds-nobody-knows map (drafted; the council gets it in spring; "the glasses fund was invisible for a year, never again, for anybody, for anything"). And the knowing exchange follow-through ("the February proposal. The watering begins. Nothing grows from the asking alone, six's homework, on schedule").
