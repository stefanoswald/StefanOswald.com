Night 245

The Haircut Case

Month 9: Truth With Kindness

Case one arrived wearing bangs: Rosa-No-Relation's drastic haircut and the direct ask, eyes already wet: "It's bad, right? Tell me the truth. You always tell the truth." The old tools, insufficient: kind and honest wants a true-good-part, and the haircut, sure enough, did not have one yet.

The upgrade, found under pressure: "The truth? Right now it's a shock, because yesterday's you is what everybody's eyes expect. That's not ugly, that's sudden. Sudden wears off in about a week; I've watched it with Lily's glasses and the winter coats and everything.

And hair, hair's the one mistake that fixes itself. It's the only thing you own that regrows. Ask me the real question in two weeks." (Two weeks: the bangs settled, got named "the new era..."

Got copied by a four-square kid. The truth had been: not bad. Early. "Wrong is right that hasn't finished..." She saved. "Sometimes ugly is cute that hasn't finished. The time-axis is half of honest.")
