Night 227

The Hundred Chart Legacy

Month 8: School Stories

A report from Miss Dana's, delivered by the Wednesday deputy at the corner, casual as weather: "We hopped the hundred chart today. Chalk. The big one. The teacher says the tens are ladders and the fives are stripes and the doubles are...

The secret staircase," they finished together. The investigation: Miss Dana got it from Ms. Alvarez, who got it from a certain chalked driveway via a certain Friday folder, three years back. The chart's whole hop-the-patterns method: Lesson now.

Both schools. ("The driveway went to school, Mama. The thing me and Lily and Theo chalked one afternoon is teaching kids who never met us. The setup escaped again, it always escapes; that's how you know it was setup and not just a afternoon.")
