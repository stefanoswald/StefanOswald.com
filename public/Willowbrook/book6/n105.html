Night 105

The Egg Route

Month 4: My Jobs

The coop's arithmetic overflowed this week: the hens (Duchess included; seven eggs now; Joe has moved from "huh" to silent hat-adjustment) out-lay the farm's kitchen. Surplus: two dozen weekly. The solution, self-evident to a route-minded postholder: The egg route. Saturday afternoons, the wheelbarrow, a junior partner (Theo; Saturdays are his now too; the posts hire family), and the lane's regulars: Mrs.

Hollis. Number Five's family (the dog gets the cracked ones, by arrangement with everyone's conscience). Petrov (payment refused, then negotiated: eggs for "standing maintenance on all fleet and barrow hardware," the barter economy finds its level). Mrs.

Alvarez the elder (soup-jar Alvarez; the widow at the lane's end; hers arrive first, "the farvest walk gets the freshest, that's route logic and second-grid logic agreeing, which is how you know a route's drawn right").
