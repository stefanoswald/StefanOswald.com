Night 338

The Aunts' Sixth Star

Month 12: Almost Six

The package. The twine. The early opening: The sweater: deep gold-on-gold this year..." The stars are the same color as the wool, Mama. You can only see them when the light moves. Secret stars.

The aunts made the constellation private. "and the letter explained why, in the looping hand she reads fluently now: "Dear Olivia. Six stars, gold on gold. You'll understand: The best work of your sixth year was the invisible kind. The posts, the funds map, the unsigned walls.

We hear everything. So this year's stars shine the way you've learned to: Only when the light moves, only for those who look close. Wear your years. All our love, the aunts. P. S. The scarf you sent the tree was seen by a cousin of ours passing through.

Eleven mistakes, she counted. All eleven holding. That's the dynasty, child. That's the whole dynasty."
