Night 174

The Seed Starting

Month 6: Hard Things Take Days

The garden campaign opened indoors this week: The starting. (Tomatoes, yellow dynasty, year three. The okra, Margaret's line. The question row's new dragons: rainbow chard "because the name's a dare..." And something called a tomatillo. "with a paper lantern, like the ground cherries' big cousin, the lantern plants are a whole family, we're collecting the set.")

And the campaign's teaching, delivered to the cup-filling deputy: "The seeds are the slowest multi-day there is, Theo. The map's got weeks where nothing shows, the whole plateau, underground, literal underground this time. You water dirt that looks like nothing and you trust the map and one morning: the green hooks. Every campaign this whole month was secretly this shape.

The piano, the scarf, your bike. The seeds are just the shape with the costume off."
