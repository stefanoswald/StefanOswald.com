Night 89

The Different Month, Counted

Month 3: Different Is Interesting

Month's end. At month's end, The easy-to-count part included one thesis (the differences are the wealth). One method. Noor's five appointments. Marcus's arrows. Two calendars. One volume knob. One lefty (rare like the snow leopard; the tools checked, not the kid).

One pause. Two addresses (goodnights are goodnights). One pair of sky-blue aids. One food map. One hamster wheel. One farm interview (the asking runs in the family). Beyond the numbers, there was that the underneaths always match and the on-tops never should.

That every smooth kid has a hill and every hill kid has a smooth. That the world has secret handednesses and half of every "can't" is wrong-handed tools. That the quiet is a language, the pause is a residence, the different is not a problem to be saved, it's the collection itself. "Good different-month?

The widest-eyed one. Mama. I started collecting questions at four. I know what I collect now, for real: ways of working. Ways of being a person. The web's got a dozen and the town's got hundreds and the world's got... all of them.

The collection can't finish. Collections that can't finish are the only ones worth starting. The snow leopard taught me that and the snow leopard was right." Remembered. The collection continues. It can't finish. That's the point.
