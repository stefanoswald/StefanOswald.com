Night 260

The Tooth Truth, Second Generation

Month 9: Truth With Kindness

Theo's first wiggle arrived this week (bottom front; the dynasty's schedule holds), and with it, at his lamp, the question, asked straight because this family raises straight-askers: "The toof fairy. Real real? Wivy knows. I want the true.

I can hold it." The family council: the policy holds, the truth, asked for directly, gets given. And the giving fell to the sister, fairy-emerita: "True answer: the fairy's a game the family plays. The word's from Mama and Papa.

The game is real, the love part, the tradition part, the word part. The wings are pretend. You get the same choice I got: keep playing knowing, or retire the game." (His big think ran shorter than hers had: "Keep playing.

Obviously. But now I'm inside the game: what's the rate?" The negotiation, later, was fierce, the boy cited her precedent, dollar-for-first..." And inflation, Papa says everything's got inflation... "Settled: one dollar, plus the tooth returned in a labeled jar, per house tradition. "what they saw jar. Like Wivy's." The archives grow. The dynasty holds. The straight-askers keep coming.)
