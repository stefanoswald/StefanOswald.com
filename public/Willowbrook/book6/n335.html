Night 335

The Birthday Month Begins

Month 12: Almost Six

Olivia's birthday month began with the paper chain hanging beside the calendar.

The familiar traditions waited ahead: the yearly interview, a new mark on the doorframe, a parcel from the aunts, choosing the cake, the party, the quiet morning with only the family, and the last night of five.

Then came the walk to the Great Willow.

Each birthday, another word appeared in gold on its bark. Olivia felt the coming word pulling gently at the month, though she did not know what it would be.

She tore the first paper link and read what she had written months earlier: LEARN THE SOUND OF SNOW.

That had happened. The next link was blank, waiting to receive whatever the day brought.

The month was full of ceremonies, but none of them could hurry the days. Olivia folded the first link into her keepsake box and went downstairs for breakfast.
