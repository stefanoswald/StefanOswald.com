Night 171

The Impatient Friend

Month 6: Hard Things Take Days

Wes quit guitar this week. One week in. ("It hurts your fingers and I still can't play anything and the video kids learned it in a day...") The intervention, run gentle: "The video kids' day was their day four hundred, Wes.

Videos cut the four hundred out, that's the whole trick of videos. Everybody's day one hurts and plays nothing. The fingers grow armor, calluses, my papa's got them, they take three weeks, and the three weeks are the toll boof. Most people turn around at the toll.

The map just says: the toll's real, it's three weeks long, and the whole guitar's on the other side of it... Anyway. The door's open if the want survives." (The want survived. Two weeks of silence, then: "how long'd you say the calluses take?"

The map, lent, gets consulted eventually. That's why you lend it without preaching, preached maps get burned; lent ones get read at midnight.)
