Night 354

The Maybe Jar at Six

Month 12: Almost Six

The morning after the party, Olivia emptied the year's maybe jar across the rug.

MAYBE THE KITE MONTH IS HAPPENING FOR ME. It had brought the squadron and the redesigned Willowhawk.

MAYBE GRANDPA'S MONTH IN TOWN IS HAPPENING FOR ALL OF US. It had brought fireside stories, seed knowledge, and new hands at the farm.

MAYBE THE GARDEN DAMAGE IS HAPPENING FOR THE GARDEN. After the vandalism, fifty neighbors came to rebuild, and the lot became more closely watched and loved.

Theo's slip said: MAYBE MY SHEEP STRATEGY WILL WIN. It had won an entire tournament bracket after traveling to another town.

They tied the confirmed slips into the sixth yearly bundle.

"The jar has never come up empty," Olivia said.

Not every difficulty had secretly been good. The jar did not pretend that. It showed that life often grew another meaning beside the first one, if they stayed long enough to see it.
