Night 192

The Zucchini Diplomacy

Month 7: Team Willowbrook

Mid-season brought the garden's oldest comedy, right on schedule: Zucchini. (Four plants. The bench warned them. Nobody listens about zucchini until it's too late. It is now, sure enough, too late: the lot produces zucchini at rates the town cannot legally consume.)

The response, systematized with a grin: The zucchini desk. Porch-drop rules ("ring, leave, run, the classic. Zucchini is the only vegetable delivered like a prank"). Rosa's bakery conversion program (zucchini bread; "the bakery eats all surpluses eventually; it's the town's stomach").

The recipe card campaign (bench faculty recipes, printed, rubber-banded to each delivery, "the zucchini comes with instructions now; the complaints dropped ninety percent"). And the lesson, saved laughing: "The garden's first overflow problem, Mama! We planned for scarcity, the barrels, the brigades, and the lot went and made too much. Abundance needs systems too. Nobody teaches that part. Generosity without logistics is just... zucchini on porches, running."
