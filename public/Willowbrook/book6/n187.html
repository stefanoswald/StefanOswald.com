Night 187

The Class Bed War That Wasn't

Month 7: Team Willowbrook

Room 6's bed nearly had its war this week: The carrot faction (practical; "you can eat carrots") versus the flower faction. Votes: deadlocked. Voices: rising. The mediation, run by the room's resident architect: "Stop. Wrong fight.

You're voting like the bed's a pie, carrot half, flower half, everybody gets less. The bench says carrots and flowers aren't enemies. Marigolds chase the bugs off vegetables. Gus-Senior says his wife never planted carrots without them.

The flowers protect the carrots. The beautiful and the useful aren't factions, they're teammates that nobody introduced. Plant them together: the bed gets prettier and the carrots get bodyguards." (Adopted unanimously. The bed, by summer: orange and gold, carrots and marigolds interplanted, the most-photographed plot on the lot. "Half of every deadlock..." She saved. "is two good ideas that never got introduced.")
