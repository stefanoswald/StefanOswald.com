Night 356

The Year in Pictures, Sixth Folder

Month 12: Almost Six

The folder. The floor. The sixth retrospective: "September: the clock's insides. I drew every gear; the first drawing of the year and its whole thesis. October: the Willowhawk at three seconds, drawn mid-air, from memory, the same day.

The kite with the cargo words. The garden's first radishes (drawn proud, look at those radishes; they know what they did). The vandal morning and next to it, I saved them facing, on purpose, the fifty coming. The Duchess..."

Held a moment; placed gently; the folder keeps the weights with the wins. "on the boot. Eleven minutes. Drawn from the best memory I got. The fire month: Grandpa mid-story, the book open, the different ink drawn as actual different ink.

And the last one, this week: the gold squares. The lane at night, every window lit, ours among them. The year I learned everybody's light is somebody's landmark." The new folder, labeled seven, empty, waiting. The museum of her: seven folders deep. The proof, accumulating, always.
