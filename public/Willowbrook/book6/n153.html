Night 153

The Hate-the-Bike Day

Month 6: Hard Things Take Days

Day four delivered, on schedule: the hate-the-bike day. Three crashes, one scraped palm, one helmet thrown (retrieved; apologized to; helmets are crew). And mid-tears, the coaching that only a mapped landmark makes possible: "Theo! This is it!

The day I told you about! You're not failing, you're on schedule. The hate day means halfway. Check the map!" (He checked the map, literal; she'd drawn one; day four's square says "The worst day.

Halfway. Keep.") The tears slowed on the arithmetic. "... Halfway? Halfway. The map doesn't lie. Tomorrow's easier. The map says." The map said. Tomorrow was.
