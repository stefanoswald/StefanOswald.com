Night 299

The Almost-Spring List

Month 10: The Long Winter

The thaw turned honest this week (the almanac and the cows concur; the flag agrees; even March has stopped lying), and the season's handoff got its survey, office-window edition: "List, Mateo: The winter built, the tapes, the circuit, the manifest, the sky puzzle, the lantern technique, fourteen books, four mittens, one tournament humbling. The spring inherits, the Hawk 2. The garden's third year. The knowing exchange (the proposal watered; the council's 'considering,' which the councilman says means yes slowly).

The funds map. The festival. And the birthday..." The fence went quiet a moment; the big one, coming up the calendar like the spring itself. "the sixth one. The willow writes. The chain hangs. The whole gold machinery of almost.

Big spring..." said Mateo. "They're all big. That's the years' whole trick. But this one's got the word coming, and I think I know it, and I'm not saying, and the not-saying is getting heavy in the best way. Some cargo you carry glad."
