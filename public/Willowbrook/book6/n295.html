Night 295

The Mitten Tree Restock Run

Month 10: The Long Winter

The coldest week found the mitten tree bare. (Stripped, which is the tree working; every empty branch is warm hands somewhere, but the cold had weeks left and the tree had nothing.) The restock run, circuit-wide: her needles (the dynasty's output: four mittens, two "rustic," the wall's standards apply; warm outranks even). Lily's and Noor's (the skill swap's first harvest, taught in January, hanging in February; "the circuit compounds fast").

The outgrown bins, checked house by house. Mrs. Hollis's needlework reserves ("April mittens, early," agent zero's stock, deployed ahead of schedule). And the hanging, gloaming, all hands: the tree re-flowered in an afternoon, wool blooms, every branch.

("The tree got emptier and warmer, the librarian's old line, but Lily, the real find is the speed: the whole restock took one day, because the systems already existed. The knitting circuit, the bin checks, the agent network. Emergencies are fast when the setup's pre-built. That's the whole argument for building it before the cold.

Everything good is pre-built. The cold just reveals what got built in the warm.")
