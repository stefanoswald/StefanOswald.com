Night 146

The Skating Pond Opens

Month 5: The Brook's Long Story

The almanac called it; Joe confirmed the ice; the pond opened. First skate, full crew, and the hand chain re-formed with its annual new blood: Theo. Owen (first ice ever, "the chain absorbs the wobble; grab on; the pond's a minnow school and we're teaching you the matching"). Zoe, rink-side, dispatching ("the chair does snow-pack, not ice, but the brain skates fine; left, chain!

The new kid lists left!"). And the season's inaugural crash, absorbed as tradition demands: the whole chain, one snowbank, six kids, zero injuries, one shared laughing pile. ("The chain crashes together or not at all," Theo announced from the bottom, four years old, quoting law he was cargo for last winter. The rule walks. It always walks.)
