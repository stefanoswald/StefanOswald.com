Night 201

The Second Grid Tour

Month 7: Team Willowbrook

A visiting class came through this week (the far school; the toy-drive route's school; the district heard about the lot), and got the tour, guide: obvious: "Welcome to the garden. The vegetables are the visible crop. The tour's about the other one. That bench: harvests fifty years of knowing, plant seats, get wisdom.

That barrel system: drinks a library's roof, ask your library; roofs are generous. That shared bed: the whoever's-cold part; no tally book. That stand: runs on warm promises; the notes are the money. That window up there: the Warden; attention guards places.

And the marble in the shed, proves the ground keeps its kids and the long ones always land. Questions?" (One hand, from the far school: "How do we get one?" And the answer, handed over like a loaner kite: "One page.

The council. The lever's free. Here's a copy of ours..." She keeps copies now; of course she keeps copies. "change the names and push the button. The caring's already in your line too. It's in everybody's. That's the whole tour, actually.")
