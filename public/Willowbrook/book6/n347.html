Night 347

The Almanac Entry at Six

Month 12: Almost Six

The almanac. The family pages. The sixth entry, porch-written, solo, the arrangement's newest season: "Olivia. Year six. Lerned: The insides of everything, and at the bottom of all of them: Attention is the love.

Held four posts. Handed two on. Became the fourth hand in this book. The duchess died in her own good time and is distributed through half the coop. I count her kids every saturday and the count is the love, see above.

The farm re-rigged and flies. The word comes this week. The farm says I'm ready for seven. The farm would know. O, fourth hand." And Joe's line beneath, the capitals slower this year and surer than ever: "Confirmed.

The book agrees with the farm. And for the story, in the different ink: The porch hours remain the best hours. The arrangement continues. It continues as long as there's a porch. J."
