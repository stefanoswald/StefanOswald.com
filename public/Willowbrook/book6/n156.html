Night 156

The Knitting Needles

Month 6: Hard Things Take Days

Her own multi-day mountain, chosen for this month: Knitting. (The aunts sent needles two birthdays back, "When she's ready," the tag said. The five-star sweater dynasty deserves a domestic apprentice. Ready: declared.) Week one's honest tally book: cast-on.

Eleven dropped stitches. One row that became, mysteriously, wider than its predecessor ("the scarf is growing sideways. Scarves shouldn't do that"). One unraveling, self-ordered, jaw set: "the fifty-cent lesson: bad rows compound. Rip early. The fox rule, in wool."
