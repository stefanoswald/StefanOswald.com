Night 358

The Squadron's Birthday Flight

Month 12: Almost Six

The squadron's gift, announced by summons and delivered at gloaming: The full fleet flight. Everything airworthy, aloft at once, in her honor: the willow kite (twelve tails, the scar collection, the painted words. Home family growing kind, one space waiting). Lily's bakery diamond.

Theo's red rocket. Wes's stunt looper, writing circles. Pip on the loaner. The Hawk 2's sail, re-rigged, flying its annual memorial of itself. And Noor's new one, a box kite of her own design..."

The first kite I ever built in any country. "steady as her maps. The hill, at full gold, seven strings up: the whole year's sky, crewed. ("They flew it for me..." She said at the lamp. "but Mama, the real gift was the looking up.

Seven kites is seven strings of attention, all paying at once. The sky got attended. The squadron gets it now, they all got it this year: The attention is the love. You can see it when it flies.")
