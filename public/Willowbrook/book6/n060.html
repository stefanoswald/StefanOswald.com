Night 60

The Month's Plan

Month 3: Different Is Interesting

At the start of the month, Olivia said, "Lily. Look at the web. Noor maps in waves. Wren talks in quiet. June thinks in bugs. Marcus thinks in ramps. Wes reads fermals. Elias sees the faithful things.

Rosa-No-Relation draws horses and holds a papa-shaped quiet. Mateo builds. You bake-fink, everything's ovens and rises and patience with you. And me..." the honest pause, "I collect. That's mine. So? So: nobody in the whole web runs the same approach.

And the web works, not despite that. Because of it. If everybody was a collector, nobody'd bake. If everybody baked, nobody'd map. The differences aren't the web's problem. They're the web's wealth. This month I'm studying that."
