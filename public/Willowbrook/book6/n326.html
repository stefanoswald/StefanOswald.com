Night 326

The Knowing Exchange Launches

Month 11: Grandpa Joe's Almanac

The crockpot finished cooking this week: The knowing exchange, first official session. (The February proposal; the council's "yes slowly"; the watering, faithful, and now: the library's meeting room, twelve elders, twenty kids, one program nobody had a template for because nobody'd built one before.) The format, hers, garden-proven: no lectures. Tables.

The mending table. The wind table (kite lore; Gus-Senior subbing for the late Margaret's pages, an honor he asked for). The recipe table. The story table. And the reverse tables, the part the elders hadn't expected and loved hardest: kids teaching back (the window codes; the hundred chart's staircase; one small girl teaching three grandmothers to whistle through an acorn cap, patiently, to tears of laughter).
