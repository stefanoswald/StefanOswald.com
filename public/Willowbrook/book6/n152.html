Night 152

The Two-Wheeler Handoff

Month 6: Hard Things Take Days

This month opened with the family's next multi-day mountain: Theo's two wheels. The coach (veteran of the wobble morning; keeper of the whole method): "Theo. The truth up front: this takes days. Not one movie-day where it magically clicks.

Days. The balance crosses first: glides, turns, stops. The wrench comes last. And somewhere in the middle there's a day you hate the bike. That day's on the map. It's not a sign to quit.

It's a landmark that says you're halfway." Day one: glides. Day two: glides, better, one crash, one "good work in there" to the knee.
