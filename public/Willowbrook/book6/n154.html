Night 154

The Glide That Held

Month 6: Hard Things Take Days

Day six: The glide held. The gentle slope, feet up, twenty yards, straight, the balance not learned but arrived, the way it does, underground, while the days piled. "The skills crossed!" (He knows the rule.

He was raised in it.) "The wrench! The wrench!" The ceremony: scheduled for Saturday, full family, per tradition. And at the lamp, the coach's report: "Six days, Mama. The wobble morning took me one morning, but only because two summers of scooter and glides went first.

Theo's doing the whole staircase in one campaign. Different roads, same summit. The map holds for everybody, you just gotta draw their map, not reuse yours."
