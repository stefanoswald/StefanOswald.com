Night 254

The Rumor Refusal

Month 9: Truth With Kindness

A rumor reached her this week, a juicy one, about a classmate's family, arriving pre-winged, and the carrier waited for the trade: rumors are currency; passing them buys belonging; the schoolyard exchange rate is ancient. The refusal, done with this month's full machinery: "Is it true? ('I mean, everybody says, ') 'Everybody says' is not a witness, the oldest rule I got. Is it kind?

Is it ours to carry? Then I'm not a link. The chain breaks here." And the addendum, gentler, to the carrier, because carriers are mostly just kids buying belonging with the only coin they've found: "You don't need it either.

The rumor buys you five minutes of being interesting. You got real coins, you do the best birdcalls in the school. Spend those. The interesting that's yours doesn't cost anybody a basement."
