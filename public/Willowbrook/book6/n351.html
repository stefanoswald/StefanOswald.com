Night 351

The Six List

Month 12: Almost Six

The annual charter, at the lamp: "Wat six can do. A list.1. The fat-spine shelf. (The era is mine; the librarian's note says so; the crown jewel waits half-read on the nightstand right now.)

2. The piano, year three. (The music box plays the Gentle Brook. The least I can do is keep up with the hardware.) 3. The whole saturday coop, open to close. (Joe says six holds it. The post grows with the postholder.

The count is the love.) 4. The exchange, season two. (Tables, not lectures. The reverse tables doubled. Follow-through is the watering.) 5. Teach Theo the chapter books. (His paper-chain year needs its reading. The faculty guarantees delivery.

The lights come faster every year.) 6. The funds map, delivered. (The council has it. Six makes sure it lands. Nobody goes a year not knowing about the glasses fund. Not in this town. Not anymore.)

7. miss five a little. It's allowed. Five was the openest one yet. They keep being the, they keep being. That's the whole point of them."
