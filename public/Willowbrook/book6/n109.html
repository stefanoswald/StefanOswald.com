Night 109

The Snow Shovel Crew

Month 4: My Jobs

First shovel-snow of the season, and the squadron converted, per its charter's all-weather part, into the shovel crew. (Mateo: heavy lifting. Lily: the corners and steps. Zoe: supervision and route optimization from the cleared paths..."

The cambers don't do snow; the brain does routes; I'm the dispatcher. "Her: the door-knocks and the list.) The list, and this is the part the story keeps, ran on second-grid intelligence: the quiet doors first. Number Nine.

The elder Alvarez. The new family (Biscuit the Third's; "new ones don't know which neighbors to ask yet; be the answer before the question"). The two-houses kid's mama's week-house ("shoveling both addresses would be showing off. Shoveling the current one is just Tuesday").

Payment: refused as policy, accepted as cocoa. ("The crew runs on the other currency. The given column, but with backs.")
