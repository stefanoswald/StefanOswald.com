Night 2

Mateo's Workshop

Month 1: How Things Work

The fence summit moved venues this week: Mateo's garage. The workshop. Inside: wheels off four different things. Planks. Rope in honest coils. A chalkboard with a drawing that made her sit down on a paint can: a flying machine.

(A glider, technically, wheels, ramp, wings of bedsheet and lath. The chalk title: "The willowhawk. Summer project.") "The bear's letter said..." She breathed. "The whole town's gonna talk about this. The whole town's gonna laugh at this..." said Mateo. "right up till it flies. Inventions get laughed at first. It's part of the build, my papa says. You in? I need a detail person. Somebody who checks the hardware." Check the hardware.

The trust checks itself. "... I'm in. Bandages and belief, equal amounts. I was literally told to bring both."
