Night 252

The Team Selection Truth

Month 9: Truth With Kindness

Case: The draft. (Kickball captains, alternating picks, and the ancient cruelty at the end of every schoolyard list: the last-picked kid, standing alone in the arithmetic of unwantedness. This week: twice in a row, same kid.) The reform, proposed as umpire, adopted by the court: "The skill-truf is real, teams want the strong kickers; pretending everybody kicks the same is a lie and lies rot games.

But the dignity-truf is real too, and the draft as-built says the last kid out loud, every game, for nothing. Both truths can hold: captains draft pairs. Strong-plus-learning, every pick. The strong carry the teams; the learning kids arrive attached, never last, never alone in the countdown.

The skill-maf stays honest. The cruelty-maf gets deleted. It was never steady. Cruelty never is, it just squats in the systems nobody checks."
