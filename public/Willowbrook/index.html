About the Tales

In the little town of Willowbrook, a girl named Olivia grows up one night at a time, watched over by a star named Stella, a sleepy moon, a honey-brown bear, and a willow tree that writes one word into its rings each year.

The Willowbrook Tales are seven books, one for each year from birth to age seven, with a story for every single night: 2,555 stories in all, and every one of them is here. Each night pairs a short read-aloud tale with a full-page watercolor. Book One, From Zero to One, is written to be read to the baby and for the parent: beneath the lullaby prose, each story quietly carries the parent through the first year: the swaddle, the three a.m. kitchen, the first step, the first word.
