Night 213

Who Helped Make Me?

Month 8: The Town That Made Me

For gratitude month, Olivia began the largest list she had ever attempted.

"I want to name everyone and everything that helped make me who I am," she told Mama.

She started with people: family, teachers, friends, neighbors, and those she knew only through stories. Samuel, Margaret, Wendell, and even the difficult Duchess belonged there.

Next came places: the farm porch, the brook, the park fort, the reading corner, Maple Lane, and the doorframe that had measured her growing.

Things received their own page. Bobby Bear, bicycles, books, the lamp, the maybe jar, and an unusually educated fleet of paper boats.

At last Olivia wrote down what could not be touched: whistle codes, family promises, town traditions, and the feeling that Willowbrook itself had watched over her.

"Will the list ever be complete?" Mama asked.

Olivia looked at three filled pages and one fresh page waiting.

"Probably not. People keep making one another."

She left the final line open. A gratitude list that could still grow felt more truthful than a finished one.
