Night 326

The Bear Agreement

Month 11: Bobby Bear's New Kid

Olivia and Theo wrote down what would happen when Bobby Bear moved to Theo's room.

First: on the agreed night, the bear would become Theo's. Olivia would not demand him back.

Second: visits could happen whenever Theo invited them.

"Standing invitation," Theo said. "Forever. Also, if anybody has the hardest night, that person gets the bear."

Olivia had not thought of that. It became their best rule.

Third: Olivia would teach Theo any bedtime tradition he wanted to learn, but Theo could change the traditions and invent his own.

Fourth: the five old birthday letters would remain in Olivia's box. Future letters would belong to Theo.

They signed O and T at the bottom. Theo added a third line for Bobby Bear and pressed one velvet paw into the ink pad.

The mark looked like a small brown cloud.

"Official," Theo said.

For once, Olivia agreed that a little paperwork made the heart feel lighter.
