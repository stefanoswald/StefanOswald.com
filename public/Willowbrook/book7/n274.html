The Made-Not-Bought Plan

Month 10: The Gift Month

At the start of the month, The made season. "The final stories's last full month before the bear's month, Mama, and the plan writes itself: everything the year taught, aimed at giving. Made, not bought, the whole town taught me why: the made things carry their five weeks. The pattern scarves carry nobody's.

This month makes: one gift per maker on the deepest list, each one built in their approach, the craft's last exam: can the hands say thank-you in twelve different languages?" The list, drafted: Lily. Wren. Noor.

Mateo. The trio's whole web. Mrs. Hollis. The librarian. Petrov. Rosa. The guard. Joe. Mama and Papa. And Theo, "his goes last and biggest. The bear's month comes after, and the handing... the handing needs a gift with it.

I've known since the chain's mystery links. The biggest making of the whole month. Maybe the whole shelf."
