Night 327

Theo Brings the Bear

Month 11: Bobby Bear's New Kid

A hard night arrived before the bear had officially moved.

Olivia was thinking about the gate, Grandpa Joe's slowing steps, and the last page of her first shelf. All the feelings came at once. She said she was fine at dinner, but Theo watched her feet beneath the table.

Later he knocked on her door with Bobby Bear in his arms.

"Our agreement says the hardest night gets the bear," he said. "This one is yours."

He placed Bobby in the old hollow beside Olivia.

"What about your night?"

"There is room for both. You taught me that."

Theo sat nearby without asking questions or offering fixes. He stayed until Olivia's breathing slowed.

She had spent years teaching him what to do with fear and sadness. Now the lesson had traveled back to her, changed by Theo and made kinder.

When he finally went to bed, Bobby Bear remained between their rooms in the way that mattered most: available to whoever needed him.
