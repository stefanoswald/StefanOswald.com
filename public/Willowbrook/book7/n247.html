Night 247

The Vote Olivia Lost

Month 9: Brave Out Loud

The fort planned a prank on the rival hill's kite crew. Everyone thought it was harmless, everyone except Olivia.

The joke was aimed at a new boy who still watched the group as if his welcome might vanish.

"It will be funny to us because we already feel safe here," Olivia said. "He may hear it differently. I vote no."

The vote was five to one. The prank went ahead.

It landed exactly as Olivia had feared. The new boy tried to smile, then folded his kite and left.

No one celebrated.

Lily found Olivia. "You were right."

"That is not the important part," Olivia said. "We have to repair it."

The fort apologized, helped rebuild the boy's kite, and invited him to choose the next flight game. At their following meeting, they added a rule: before aiming a joke at someone new, ask whether that person feels safe enough to laugh.

Olivia had lost the vote. She had still needed to speak.

Sometimes a warning failed to stop the mistake. It could still become the first piece of the repair.
