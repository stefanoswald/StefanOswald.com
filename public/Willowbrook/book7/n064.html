Night 64

The Repair Record

Month 3: What Makes a Friend?

Component four: The repair record. "The best friendships in the saves aren't the unbroken ones. They're the best-mended ones. Us: the scared-note fib, the wobble, the marker fight of the Rosa-No-Relation era, every break, rule'd, paid-first, repaired, and the friendship's stronger at every mended place; the gold-crack cup; the kintsugi thing; Petrov's whole religion.

The never-broken friendships, the saves got a few, they're untested boats. You don't know what they carry till they carry it. The mended ones, you know. Part four: a friend is somebody you've survived something with, and the something can be small; the marker fight was dumb, because the surviving is where the trust gets its receipts.

The repair record is the friendship's strength certificate. Petrov should inspect friendships. He basically does. The whole town basically does."
