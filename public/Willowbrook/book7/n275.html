Night 275

The Gift in Wool

Month 10: The Gift Month

Gift one, built in the first language the aunts taught: Lily's scarf. The making: three weeks of evenings. The mistakes: four, left in, by the dynasty's whole law ("the pattern scarves carry nobody. The mistakes are the five weeks, showing.

She'll find them. She finds everything, eleven days younger and eleven years wiser; she'll read the dropped stitch at row forty and know exactly which evening that was, because she was probably there"). And the label, sewn in like the aunts sew theirs: "For L. From the before-memory.

Seven years, four mistakes, all holding. O." (The delivery, later ahead per its custom with the sweetest ones: the window seat; the unwrapping; the row-forty stitch found in four minutes..." The Tuesday the cocoa spilled. "she said, correct, instantly, and the scarf worn through every winter the story has checked since, which is all of them.)
