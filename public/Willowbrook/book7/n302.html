Night 302

The Secret Book Is Finished

Month 10: The Gift Month

At the workbench, Olivia finished the secret gift she had been making for Theo.

It was a cloth-bound book built from seven years of notebooks, recordings, drawings, and remembered conversations. Mama helped with the binding. The aunts had mailed special paper for the cover.

At the last moment, Olivia unlocked her private volume and copied one page, the only page she had ever shared from it.

Mama read the addition and pressed a hand to her mouth.

"It belongs in his book," Olivia said. "It was always about him."

They wrapped the finished volume and hid it in the couch office, a place Theo considered his own secret headquarters and therefore never suspected anyone else would use.

The gift would wait until the night Bobby Bear moved down the hall.

At bedtime Olivia felt something settle inside her.

"Finishing the book made me ready for the handing," she said. "I thought I was making a present, but I was also learning how to let the next part begin."

Mama turned off the lamp. The secret waited in the dark, full of everything Olivia was ready to give.
