Night 311

Bobby Bear's Letters

Month 11: Bobby Bear's New Kid

Olivia untied the ribbon around Bobby Bear's five birthday letters and read them in order.

The first promised that the dream meadow would remain open. It had. Another predicted sunflowers taller than Papa. They had grown taller a month early. One described the morning when Papa would stop running behind her bicycle and she would not notice for a whole block. That had happened to both Olivia and Theo.

The last letter held three promises still waiting: the gate would open when she was seven, Bobby Bear would walk her there, and the Sleepy Moon would hum three times.

"You have never been wrong late," Olivia told the bear. "Sometimes things happened early, but never late."

The letters had not really been forecasts. They were messages saying that change was coming, that someone had looked ahead, and that Olivia would not meet it alone.

She returned them to their ribbon. The final promises were only a few weeks away. Bobby Bear sat beside them, quiet as ever and entirely certain.
