Night 110

The Chain Check-In

Month 4: Theo's Big Sister

Midwinter chain summit, the doorway hung double: The comparison, run by its junior archivist: "My links so far: The hallway. The line. The do-over. The flow..." The truck link, decorated with an actual truck sticker; archives innovate.

"The rope. The flower fund. The willow question." (Private. Spelled his way. Kept his way. Correct both ways.) "And yours..." He read across the doorway, the unspooling side. "says... The gate stuff I don't know.

The word stuff. The. Wivy, your chain's got links that are just '?'." ("The mystery links. Some of the year can't go on paper till it happens. The chain holds spots for them, that's new this year; the chains evolve.") "...

Mine should have a? Too. For the thing that's getting ready. The secretly-half-mine thing." (The spot, cut and hung, blank, that evening: his first mystery link. The two chains, as it happened, now hold matching question marks, facing each other across one doorway, both waiting for night 364. The doorway knows. Doorways always know. They're frames. Frames keep everything.)
