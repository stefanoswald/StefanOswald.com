Night 287

The Anonymous Gift Round

Month 10: The Gift Month

This month's unsigned wing (the anonymous: the biggest size of warm; the oldest law; the wanting-credit tax, paid in full as ever): The quiet round. The deliveries, logged only here: the new family at the old Carver place (the sour man's house, sold at last; new kids, dragon-mapped), a loaner kite, fort-refurbished, porch-left: "The hill's this way. The thermal's free. The squadron."

The shelter van's gloaming stop, mittens, the winter's knitting overrun, tagged only "Warm." The school's lost-and-found, eleven new-made pairs seeded among the orphans ("the matching service's annual restock; the mismatch fashion holds; some fashions deserve forever"). And Number Sixteen, the round's deepest stop, the eggs-plus upgraded once, this once: a whittled bird. Basswood.

Thin-winged and proud of it. Left on the rail with no note at all, because the mark reads itself now, and the reader reads it, and some correspondences have graduated past words entire.
