Night 309

The Account of the Witness

Month 11: Bobby Bear's New Kid

Account three: The witness years. "The other-kind witnessing, bear, the service nobody names: you were the presence I brought so I'd behave like myself. The solitaire games, played honest with you watching. The wish big thinks, every year; the drawings-not-spellings; you saw all the circles and kept every one.

The private volume's two-a.m. Sessions, you attended every entry; the lock never applied to you; you were inside the lock's whole territory from the start. The inner compass, bear, this month's deepest settling: the inner compass got built with you watching. Seven years of unwatched votes that were never quite unwatched, because the velvet witness made the alone-integrity possible.

You were the training wheels of the actual me. And the training wheels came off years ago, the wallet, the cup, the unwitnessed everything; the votes run clean solo now, but the wheels don't get discarded, bear. The wheels get thanked. Account three: the witness years.

Settled in the only currency witnesses take: Witnessed back. I see you, bear. I see the whole seven years of you. The story's got it all. Both records. All the records. Settled gold."
