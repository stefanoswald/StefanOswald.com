Night 112

The Valentine Handover

Month 4: Theo's Big Sister

The Valentine factory's fourth session ran under full new helpers: Theo's operation, top to bottom. The rule of everybody: recited to his cutting crew (the crane kid, recruited; the little sister, glitter-apprenticed, "Contained glitter. The ceiling remembers"). The special editions: His craft now.

And the founder's own valentine, delivered from the new helpers, structurally tape, entirely his: a drawing, the two of them, the formation, two birds in a V, the front one breaking wind, the back one drafting, and the caption, hunted from his own vocabulary: "To the front bird. Thanks for the wind-breaking. The formation holds. T." (they remembered it in the box beside the scribble-hands drawing of five years past, the two documents, the whole arc of the long family pattern, wing to wing.

The box, as it happened, is nearly full. The box was always going to fill this year. Everything fills this year. That's what last books do.)
