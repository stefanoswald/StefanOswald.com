Night 66

The Test Case: Dana

Month 3: What Makes a Friend?

The pattern, validated against its hardest historical case: Dana. (The possessive one. The scarcity approach. The fort's slowest conversion, two years from "you're my best friend" to... the check:) "part one, attendance: she's at the fort every Saturday, and she came to lily's flu week with soup, uncalled.

Pass. Part two, truth: she told Lily last month 'you let people interrupt you too much', a hard true, carried, and Lily's been finishing her sentences ever since. Pass. Part three, different: she's the web's only... the only dana, the careful one, the checks-the-rules-twice one; the parliament seats her approach and uses it (she catches every unfair rule change in every game; the court consults her).

Pass. Part four, repairs: the whole friendship is a repair, the possessive era, mended into membership. The strongest kind of pass. Part five, quiet: eleven minutes at the fort last week. Growing. Pass.the pattern works..."

The report concluded. "The girl who fought friendship was a pie is now a whole candle in the fort's window. The parts turn on anybody who lets them. That's the pattern's best feature: No approach excluded. The parliament always has a seat."
