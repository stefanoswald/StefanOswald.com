Night 98

The Coaching Mistake

Month 4: Theo's Big Sister

Truth page, this month requires one and the coaching seat earns one: She got it wrong this week. The case: his spelling list. The map she drew: Hers, the ghost-taxonomy, the wanted posters, the etymology games, the whole word-nerd arsenal, delivered with love and enthusiasm and zero weather-forecast, steps-first, her order... and watched him wilt under it. The posters felt like more wall to a kid mid-wall.

The taxonomy felt like taxonomy. By Wednesday he was hiding the list. The catch and the correction, made at the sandbox, coach to student, rule-clean: "Theo. I-did: I drew my map for your hill.

The posters are how I love words, they're not how you climb. That was the fixing seat wearing the coaching seat's hat. Sorry. Next-time: weather first, how does the list make you feel?" ("... Like the wall got a brother.")

"Okay. Then we shrink it: Three words a day, not ten. With the truck book. The words hide in the truck book and you hunt them..." The approach, matched at last: the boy hunts; the boy has always hunted; the dandelions, the cases, the letters-in-the-wild. "and the hunting is the studying. Your order. Your map. My sorry."
