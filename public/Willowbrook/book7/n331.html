Night 331

One Last Week

Month 11: Bobby Bear's New Kid

Olivia spent Bobby Bear's last full week in her room doing the things they had always done.

On Monday she read a chapter and gave tomorrow's weather. On Tuesday she told him her whole day. Wednesday required no words; they sat beneath the lamp until her thoughts became quiet.

On Thursday she played the remembering game, speaking for both of them. "You were there," she said after every memory. "You were there for all of it."

Friday brought the new bear agreement. Saturday brought the farm report, just as Saturdays had for years. On Sunday, they rested and did nothing at all.

The week was not a performance of seven years. It was simply their life in miniature.

"No regrets," Olivia told Mama on Sunday night.

Down the hall, Theo's room was ready. Bobby Bear's face looked calm.

Perhaps bears were always ready for the next child. Or perhaps being ready meant trusting the love that had brought them this far.
