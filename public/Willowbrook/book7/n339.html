Night 339

The Braid: The Web's Night

Month 12: The Maybe Year

Braid night four: The web's thread. (The fort; the full assembly, the trio-plus-plus-plus; the squadron; the districts; the whole seven-year shape, gathered for one gold evening under the mitten flag:) The night's program, run in every approach at once: the story swap (one premise, ten tellings..." A kid finds a door where no door should be. "the old premise, rerun on purpose; and every teller's door led somewhere their seven years built; and hers, this time, led exactly where everyone knew it would, and nobody spoiled it, and the gold path in the telling glowed the way it glows in the fact). The quiet minutes (held, all together, the whole fort.

Wren's workshop at maximum enrollment; eleven kids and the dusk and the comfortable everything). The bench crowns (dandelion-woven; awarded universally; "everybody wins the bench game eventually; tonight eventually arrived"). And the constitution, recited entire, in chorus, by kids who span its whole history, founders to freshest: "This fort is for the three of us and whoever's cold. The three of us is permanent. The whoever's cold is infinite. Both forever."
