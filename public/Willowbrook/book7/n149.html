Night 149

Questions for the Road

Month 5: The Question Nobody Can Answer

At the end of the month, Olivia opened her notebook to the questions that still had no answers.

Why is there something instead of nothing? Where does the first feeling of friendship come from? How can a thing matter after it is gone? Those questions would travel with her for years.

Others had closed gently. She knew what she was for: keeping. She understood that the Duchess had loved in the difficult, prickly way some creatures do.

Three answers still waited close ahead: the Great Willow's final word, what lay beyond the meadow gate, and how it would feel to hand Bobby Bear to Theo.

Olivia did not cross out the open questions. She drew a small lantern beside each one.

"They are not unfinished homework," she told Bobby Bear. "They are traveling companions."

Her paper chain stirred near the door, growing shorter one night at a time. Olivia closed the notebook and slid it into her satchel. The questions would come with her. Of course they would. Good questions knew how to walk.
