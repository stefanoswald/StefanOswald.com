Night 332

The Night Before the Gold Month

Month 11: Bobby Bear's New Kid

On the final night before her birthday month, Olivia stood at the nursery window with Mama and Bobby Bear.

The town glowed below. The Great Willow was a dark shape on the hill.

Theo's room was ready. The bear agreement was signed. Bobby's little traveling coat fit. Olivia's paper chain had only a few links left. The wooden box still held five gold words and one empty place.

During the coming month, the willow would finish its sentence. The dream gate would open. Bobby Bear would move down the hall. Olivia's first shelf of stories would end.

"That is a great deal at once," Mama said.

"It is. But I don't feel as if I have to brace for it."

"What will you do instead?"

Olivia opened both hands on the windowsill. "Pay attention."

For years she had met large changes by holding tight. Now she was learning another posture: hands open, eyes awake, ready to receive the days as they came.
