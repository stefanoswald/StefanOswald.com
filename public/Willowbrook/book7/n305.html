Night 305

The Bear's Month Opens

Month 11: Bobby Bear's New Kid

The bear's month opened at the lamp, the two of them, the way the whole account opened seven years ago: The settling begins. "this month, bear: we close our seven years properly, the family way; care; no skipping the end. The accounts of the needing, read out. The craft, taught down. And the last week... the last week is the final stories's, and yours, and his.

This month is ours first. The whole month. Every night. The lamp's got a lot of minutes and they're all booked." (The bear, from the pillow, presiding, and as it happened what the room noted: the velvet face, seven years worn, wore this month the way the willow wears its seasons: sure, unhurried, made for it.

"The needing changed shape a hundred times, bear..." The opening entry ran. "and you held every shape. This month reads them all back. Starting from the crib. Starting from the first night of the first book, the night you moved in and the whole shelf started.

You were the shelf's first citizen, bear. This month makes sure the story shows it." the story shows it. The story was there. The bear moved into the crib on night 365 of the first book, and everything since has been the two of them plus everybody. The account opens for its closing. The lamp's minutes are all booked.)
