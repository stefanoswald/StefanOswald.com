Night 321

Bobby Bear's Practice Night

Month 11: Bobby Bear's New Kid

Before Bobby Bear moved to Theo's room for good, the children planned one practice night.

"The real handing is still Night 364," Olivia said. "Tonight is only a sleepover."

Theo placed Bobby on the worry side of his pillow beneath the welcome drawing. Olivia returned to her own room and looked at the empty place beside her.

She expected sadness. Instead, the room felt wider.

In the morning Theo gave his report over breakfast. "I told him my whole day. The worry side works. Then I slept like the ferry after a busy afternoon."

"And you?" Mama asked Olivia.

"I slept too. My hand kept reaching for his spot, but I knew where he was."

She looked down the hallway toward Theo's door.

"It did not feel as if the keeping left my room. It stretched. The hallway was part of it."

After breakfast Bobby returned to Olivia's pillow for the remaining nights.

The practice had not made the final change smaller. It had shown both children that love could move rooms without leaving either one empty.
