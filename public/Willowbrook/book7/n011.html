Night 11

The Empty Practice Box

Month 1: When No One Is Watching

Olivia skipped piano practice on Tuesday.

She was not sick or busy. Her book had reached its most exciting chapter, and she chose to keep reading until bedtime.

On Wednesday, the practice chart waited on the wall. One false check mark would preserve her perfect row. No one would know.

Olivia left the box empty and wrote beneath it: CHOSE MY BOOK.

Papa noticed at dinner. "Any regrets?"

"About the chapter? No. About the empty box? A little."

They looked at the chart together. Its title said PRACTICE STREAK.

"That makes the gaps look like disasters," Olivia said. "Practicing matters, but pretending never to miss matters less."

They renamed it PRACTICE RECORD. The empty square stayed. The next six filled honestly.

By the end of the month, Olivia no longer saw a broken streak. She saw a faithful musician who had once chosen a very good book.
