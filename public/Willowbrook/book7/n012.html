Night 12

Five Dollars in the Park

Month 1: When No One Is Watching

Olivia found a five-dollar bill on the path at Dandelion Park. The office was closed, and no grown-up nearby had seen who dropped it.

She wrote a note: MONEY FOUND HERE. IF IT IS YOURS, ASK THE PARK OFFICE TOMORROW. Then she slid the bill through the office mail slot.

Before leaving, she sat beneath the oak to finish a chapter. A father and a tearful little boy came along the path, searching the grass.

"I lost my five dollars," the boy said. "It was for Grandma's birthday card."

Olivia showed them the note on the office door and explained where she had put the money. The father read the note twice.

"May we thank the person who found it?"

Olivia smiled. "You just did."

The boy's face changed from storm to sunlight. Olivia walked home with no money in her pocket and the pleasant feeling of carrying something much larger.
