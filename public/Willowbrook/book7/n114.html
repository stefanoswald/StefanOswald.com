Night 114

The Snow Fort Federation

Month 4: Theo's Big Sister

Fort City, fourth annual, and the founder toured it, for the first time, as a visitor. (The mayor's government, matured: the districts federated under a drafted plan, "The fort city laws," crayon, posted at the gate: "1. Everybody builds. 2.

Nobody wrecks. 3. The warming rotation is law. 4. The little district gets the soft snow. 5. Snacks at the big fort. 6. Whoever's cold comes in." the constitution's whole lineage visible, amended by its new generation: the soft-snow part is his, original, tenderhearted legislation.)

The tour, guided by the mayor personally: the districts. The target range. The warming rotation's chalkboard (the somebody's handwriting, as it happened, has changed, the torch of anonymous logistics passing to persons unknown, as it should, as it always should). And at the founding structure.

Fort together's fourth winter edition, the mitten flag flying, the mayor's briefing: "The old fort's the capitol now. Nobody builds on it. It's where the laws live." ("The city runs without me, Lily. Entirely. The laws grew local new rules.

The somebody changed handwriting. The capitol keeps the founding and the founding keeps the city and I just... Toured my own setup like a tourist. That's not loss. That's the spec. The us outlives its builders, and the builders get to visit. The visiting's the retirement plan. Best pension in town.")
