Night 15

Theo and the Last Cookie

Month 1: When No One Is Watching

Theo brought an important story to dinner.

"There was one cookie left," he said. "The kitchen was empty. I looked at it for a long time. Then I cut it in half and left half for Wivy. I wrote a note."

He produced the note from his pocket. HALF FOR YOU. I WAS ALONE AND I STILL DID THE SHARING. T.

Olivia read it twice.

"The alone part matters," she said. "You chose to be generous when no one was watching."

Theo shook his head. "The cookie was watching. You have to use manners in front of things you're about to eat."

Mama tried not to laugh. Papa failed completely.

After dinner, Olivia found her half on a plate. She broke it again and carried one piece to Theo.

"Now there are witnesses," she said.

They ate the quarters together at the kitchen table. The note went into the family keepsake box, and that night the house settled softly around them.
