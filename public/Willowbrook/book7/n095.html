Night 95

Theo's First Solo

Month 4: Theo's Big Sister

Theo received an eleven-word solo in the winter concert.

He practiced it for the mirror, Bobby Bear, Olivia, and the stuffed cow. Still, the thought of the full gym made his voice shrink.

Olivia shared the trick Mama had once taught her.

"Do not sing to the whole room. Choose one person and give the words to that face."

"You," Theo said.

"Then I will be in row three."

She also taught him what to do if a word went wrong: smile, take a breath, and continue. A mistake became less frightening when it already had a plan.

At the concert Theo found Olivia in row three. He sang all eleven words clearly to her.

There was no mistake, though his prepared grin appeared anyway.

When the song ended, Olivia shouted his name. The family applauded so loudly that Theo laughed onstage.

Later he said, "I saw everybody, but I only had to sing to one."

The crowd had not become smaller. He had found one safe place inside it.
