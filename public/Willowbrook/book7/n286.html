Night 286

The Theo Gift Takes Shape

Month 10: The Gift Month

This month's summit project opened in deep secrecy (her own couch-office; the techniques come back up the pattern too; Mama as quartermaster, sworn): Theo's gift. (For the handing. Night 364. The bear's night, and the gift that goes with the bear, because the handing needs a gift and she's known since the mystery links.) the story honors the operation's classification and saves only the supplies: paper, the good kind, the archive kind.

The binding equipment. The tapes, borrowed. Seven years of notebooks, consulted nightly, pages flagged. And one request to the quartermaster that made her eyes go bright and stay bright, held under the household's highest classification, twin to the one the boy's operation ran last month.

(Two operations. Two bright-eyed quartermasters. One night, 364, bearing down on one doorway with two mystery links. The story, which knows both secrets, keeps both, and has waited seven books for this particular night, says only: the deliveries are equal to each other.

The tenderhearted and the keeper built for the same moment without conferring. They always build for the same moment. That's the whole us of them. Eighty nights.)
