Night 3

The Broken Cup Nobody Saw

Month 1: When No One Is Watching

Mama stepped next door for ten minutes, leaving Olivia alone in the kitchen. Olivia reached for a book, caught the handle of Mama's everyday cup, and knocked it against the counter.

Crack.

The cup did not shatter, but a thin line ran from its rim to the painted blue flower. No one had seen what happened. Olivia could have left it by the sink and said she found it that way.

For four uncomfortable seconds, she considered doing exactly that.

When Mama returned, Olivia spoke before Mama had taken off her shoes.

"I cracked your cup. My hand caught it. I thought about pretending I didn't, but only for four seconds."

Mama examined the cup and then Olivia. "That may be the fastest confession in the history of this house."

"Does that make it uncracked?"

"No. But it keeps one crack from becoming two."

Mr. Petrov later repaired the cup with a neat silver brace. Mama kept using it, blue flower, silver line, and all.
