Night 3

The Spoon That Flew

Month 1: My World

Olivia has a spoon.

The spoon has oatmeal.

Up goes the spoon. Up, up...

Whee! Down goes the spoon.

"Uh-oh," says Olivia.

Mama picks it up.

Up goes the spoon again. Whee!

"Uh-oh!"

Spoons love to fly.

But spoons love oatmeal more.

One bite for Olivia.

One bite for the spoon.

Mmm. Warm oatmeal.

No more flying tonight, little spoon.

Time to rest in the drawer.
