Night 5

The Door Says Hello

Month 1: My World

Push.

The door goes open.

Pull.

The door goes closed.

Open. Closed. Open. Closed.

Creeeak, says the door.

That is how doors say hello.

Behind the door: the hallway!

Behind the door again: the room!

Everything is still there.

Every single time.

"Hi," says Olivia to the hallway.

"Hi," says Olivia to the room.

Doors keep everything safe.

Then they say goodnight. Click.
