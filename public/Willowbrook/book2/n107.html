Night 107

The Duchess Is Many Colors

Month 4: A World of Colors

The Duchess looks brown.

That is what she looks like.

But stand her in the sun.

Look close.

Her feathers have gold in them.

And copper. And cream speckles.

And a green-black shimmer,

like oil on a puddle!

"Fancy bird," says Grandpa Joe.

The Duchess turns. Slowly.

Showing all sides.

She knew all along.

Nobody is just one color.

Not hens. Not anybody.

Stand in the sun.

Let them see your shimmer.
