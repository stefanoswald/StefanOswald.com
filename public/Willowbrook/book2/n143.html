Night 143

The Shadow Friend

Month 5: All About Me

On sunny days, someone follows Olivia.

Someone flat. Someone dark.

Look, on the fence!

A shadow!

Olivia waves.

The shadow waves.

Olivia jumps. (Almost.)

The shadow jumps. (Almost.)

It copies everything!

It is stuck to her feet.

Where she goes, it goes.

On cloudy days, it hides.

On sunny days, there!

Hello again, flat friend.

Everyone has one.

Even Papa. His is huge.

Shadows are the sun's way

of drawing your picture.
