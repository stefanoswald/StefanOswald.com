Night 121

Ten Little Fingers

Month 5: All About Me

Hold up your hands.

Look what you have!

Fingers!

Mama counts them with kisses.

One, two, three, four, five!

That whole hand, done.

Now the other.

Six, seven, eight, nine, ten!

Ten fingers.

They hold cups.

They pat bears.

They point at ducks.

They do everything, all day.

Then at night, they rest.

Curled up like little sleeping snails.

Goodnight, fingers.

Ten out of ten.
