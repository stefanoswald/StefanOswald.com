Night 82

Pop!

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Bubbles have a sound.

It is very small.

You have to be close.

Mama blows... there they go!

Round and floaty and slow.

One lands on Olivia's finger.

Wait for it...

Pop!

Did you hear it?

A tiny, tiny pop.

Like the smallest bell in the world

ringing once, then done.

"More!"

More bubbles. More pops.

Pop! Pop-pop! Pop!

The smallest concert ever played.

Encore, every time.
