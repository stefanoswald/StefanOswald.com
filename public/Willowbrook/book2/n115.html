Night 115

The Cozy Brown Bread

Month 4: A World of Colors

Rosa sent a loaf home.

Brown on the outside.

Soft white inside.

Warm all the way through.

Mama slices. The knife whispers.

Butter goes on. It melts gold.

Brown, white, gold.

The colors of cozy.

Olivia gets the first slice.

Crust first? Middle first?

Both-at-once first.

"Mmm," she says.

Rosa's bread always says mmm back.

Some colors you see.

The best ones you eat warm

with butter going gold.
