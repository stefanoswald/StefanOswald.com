Night 169

The Birthday Cake Memory

Month 6: Yum

On the fridge: a picture.

A baby. Covered in frosting.

Covered! To the eyebrows!

One gold star on the cake.

"Who is that?" asks Mama.

Olivia studies it.

The bow. The cheeks.

"Livy!"

Yes! You! Your first cake!

You wore most of it.

Rosa made it. The star was gold.

Everybody sang.

Olivia pats the picture.

Good cake. Good day.

"More?" she asks. Hopeful.

Next birthday, says Mama.

Cakes come once a year.

That is what makes them cakes.
