Night 155

The Berry Hunt

Month 6: Yum

The berry bushes are ready.

Grandpa Joe brings the pail.

Look for the red ones.

The dark red ones.

There! Pinch... pull... got it!

A raspberry!

Into the pail. Plink.

There's another! Pinch...

Into the... into Olivia.

"Pail's over here," says Grandpa.

He is smiling though.

Plink. Munch. Plink. Munch.

Half for the pail.

Half for the picker.

That is berry law.

Grandpa follows it too.

Munch.
