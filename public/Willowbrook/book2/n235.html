Night 235

The Longest Bath Ever

Month 8: Splash

Tonight's bath went long.

So many experiments!

The boat sailed. The cups poured.

The contest happened. (A tie.)

And now. Olivia looks

at her fingers.

Something is WRONG.

They're... wrinkly!

Like little raisins!

She shows Mama. Alarmed.

"Pruny fingers," laughs Mama.

"The water hugged them so long

they wrinkled."

Do they stay like that?!

No, no.

The towel fixes everything.

Dry, warm, smooth again.

Fingers back to normal.

The bath just likes to

leave a little mark that says:

she really, truly stayed.
