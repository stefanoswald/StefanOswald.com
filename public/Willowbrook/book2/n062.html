Night 62

Knock, Knock

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Knock, knock!

Who is that?

It is Papa, playing the door game.

He knocks outside.

Olivia knocks inside.

Knock-knock. Knock-knock!

The door is a drum with a Papa behind it.

"Who's there?" calls Papa.

"Da-da!" shouts Olivia.

The door opens...

It WAS Papa!

It is always Papa.

That is the whole joke.

It is funny every single time.

Knock, knock, laugh, hug.

Best game a door ever learned.
