Night 179

Milk Before Sleep

Month 6: Yum

The day is nearly done.

One more cup.

Warm milk, in the rocking chair.

The chair creaks its old song.

Creak... creak...

Sip... sip...

The milk is warm as the bath.

It settles in the tummy

like a cat settling to sleep.

This chair has held

every bedtime drink

Olivia ever drank.

It remembers all of them.

Sip. Creak. Sip.

The cup empties slow.

The eyes get heavy slow.

Everything slow now.

Slow is the door to sleep.
