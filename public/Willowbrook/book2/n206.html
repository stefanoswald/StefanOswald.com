Night 206

The Thunder Count

Month 7: Outside!

A storm tonight!

Watch the window.

There, the flash!

Now count with Papa:

one... two... three...

BOOM.

Three counts away.

The storm is three hills off.

Flash again!

One... two... four...

(Counting is still wobbly.)

BOOM.

Further now!

The storm is walking away.

Counting makes storms friendly.

They stop being big noises.

They become slow giants,

stomping off over the hills,

their drums getting sleepy.

Boom... boom...

Bye, storm.

Count your way home.
