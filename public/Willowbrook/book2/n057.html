Night 57

The Firefly Lanterns

Month 2: Animal Friends

The lawn is turning on its lights.

Blink... here!

Blink... there!

Fireflies!

Little bugs with lanterns.

They float up slow.

They never hurry.

Olivia reaches, miss!

Reaches, miss!

Fireflies are hard to catch.

That is fine.

They are easy to laugh at.

Blink, blink, all across the dusk.

Who lights them?

They light themselves.

Some friends carry their own light.

Be one of those, little one.
