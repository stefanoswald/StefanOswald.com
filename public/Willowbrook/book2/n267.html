Night 267

The Whole Hand

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

"How old are you, Olivia?"

The old answer: one finger.

But a birthday is coming.

Time to learn the new answer!

"You'll be TWO."

Two fingers. Like this.

Olivia tries.

Up goes...

the whole hand.

All five!

"Five? You're five?"

Giggles. No!

Try again.

The thumb helps hold down

the extra fingers...

Two! Two fingers!

Wobbly. But two!

"TWO!"

The new trick.

It needs practice.

There is time.

The birthday is coming slowly,

the way birthdays do...

then all at once,

the way birthdays also do.
