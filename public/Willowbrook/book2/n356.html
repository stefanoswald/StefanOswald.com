Night 356

Two Candles Wait

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

On the shelf: a little box.

Inside: candles!

Birthday candles!

Last year, we used one.

One candle, for one year.

This year...

count them out...

one...

TWO!

Two candles!

Because two whole years

of Olivia have happened!

Two springs. Two summers.

Two of every season,

all filled up with her.

The candles stand on the shelf,

side by side,

like two little soldiers

waiting for their big moment.

Their moment is soon.

Cake beneath them. Song around them.

One big breath, whoo!...

coming for them both.

"Soon," Olivia tells them.

Soon, little twins.

Practice your flickering.
