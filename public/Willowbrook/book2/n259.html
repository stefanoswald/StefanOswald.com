Night 259

Two Scoops of Everything

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Baking day!

The recipe says: two scoops of flour.

Who scoops?

Olivia scoops!

Scoop one...

steady... steady...

IN! Poof!

(A little flour snow. Always.)

"One!"

Scoop two...

IN! Poof!

"Two!"

Two scoops. Done.

The recipe is happy.

Recipes are just counting

you can eat later.

Two scoops, one egg,

some of this, some of that,

stir ten stirs (or forty),

bake.

Numbers go in the oven.

Muffins come out.

Best math there is.
