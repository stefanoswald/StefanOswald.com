Night 19

The Broom Dance

Month 1: My World

Mama has a big broom.

Swish, swish.

Olivia has a little broom.

Swish, swish!

Mama sweeps the crumbs.

Olivia sweeps the... floor.

Just the floor. All of it.

Swish here. Swish there.

The dust runs away laughing.

"Good helping," says Mama.

Helping is Olivia's best job.

Two brooms. One kitchen.

Swish, swish, swish.

The floor has never been happier.
