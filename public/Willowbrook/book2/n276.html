Night 276

Hot and Cold

Month 10: Opposites

Winter day. Two lessons.

Outside: snow.

Touch it. COLD!

Brr! Sharp! Sparkly cold!

Inside: cocoa.

Hold the cup. Warm!

(Hot at first. We waited.

Blowing helps. Whoo, whoo.)

Cold makes you shiver.

Warm makes you sigh.

The best days have both:

cold cheeks from playing,

warm hands from coming home.

Cold is for adventures.

Warm is for after.

They work as a team.

One makes the other

feel like a present.
