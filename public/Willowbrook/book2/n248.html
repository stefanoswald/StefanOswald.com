Night 248

One Duck, Two Ducks, All the Ducks

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Let's count the ducks!

"One duck!"

Easy. There he is.

"Two ducks!"

Got them.

"Three..."

Wait. The third one swam

behind the first one.

Start over.

"One! Two! Th..."

Now they're all moving!

Ducks will not hold still!

They mix! They merge!

They dive, subtraction!

"How many ducks, Olivia?"

She spreads both arms wide:

"ALL the ducks!"

Correct.

The truest count there is.

All the ducks.

The census closes.

Everyone quacks approval.
