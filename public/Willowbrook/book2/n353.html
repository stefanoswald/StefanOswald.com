Night 353

The Maybe Story

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

Tonight, the special story.

Not from a book.

From the family.

"Once, we spilled the milk.

The whole cup. Remember?"

"Uh-oh," says Olivia. Solemnly.

"Uh-oh is right!

It seemed bad.

But because we stopped to clean it,

we were late going out.

And because we were late..."

"Da TRUCK!"

"...the snowplow came by!

The big one! With the lights!

And you waved, and he waved,

and it was the best morning."

So was the spilled milk bad?

Wait for it...

"Maybe!" shouts Olivia.

MAYBE!

She knows it now. The family word.

Things that seem bad

have a way of turning good,

if you wait and watch.

Life doesn't happen TO us,

little one.

It happens FOR us.

"Maybe," she says again, softer,

holding the word like the bear.

Yes. Keep it.

It's yours now too.
