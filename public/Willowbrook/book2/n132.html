Night 132

The Heart That Thumps

Month 5: All About Me

Shhh. Listen.

Put your ear right here.

On Mama's chest.

Thum-thum. Thum-thum.

That is Mama's heart!

It is a little drum.

It never stops playing. Never.

Now Mama listens to Olivia's.

Thum-thum-thum! Quicker!

Little hearts drum faster.

They have more to do.

Two hearts. Two drums.

They played together

before Olivia was even born.

They still match at bedtime.

Thum-thum. Thum-thum.

The oldest song there is.
