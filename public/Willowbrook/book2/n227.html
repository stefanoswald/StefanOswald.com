Night 227

The Car Wash Adventure

Month 8: Splash

Today the CAR gets a bath.

Drive in slowly...

The doors of the wash close behind.

Then...

WHOOSH! Water everywhere!

On the windows! On the roof!

Then the brushes come.

Giant spinning soapy ones!

Womp-womp-womp over the glass!

Bubbles slide down everywhere.

It's raining soap!

Olivia's eyes are enormous.

Papa holds her hand back to her seat.

"Just a bath," he grins. "Car bath."

The dryers roar. The doors open.

Sunshine! Shiny car!

"Again?" asks Olivia.

Everyone asks that.

Next month, says Papa.

Car baths are once-a-months.
