Night 20

The Night Light

Month 1: My World

In the corner glows a little light.

Not big. Not bright.

Just enough.

It looks like a tiny moon.

It stays on all night.

When Olivia wakes in the dark...

there it is.

Hello, little light.

The crib is still the crib.

The bear is still the bear.

Everything is where it was.

The little light says so.

That is its whole job.

It does it every night.

Without even trying hard.
