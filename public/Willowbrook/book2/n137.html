Night 137

Big Girl, Little Girl

Month 5: All About Me

Mama shows a picture.

A baby. Tiny. Sleeping.

"Who is that?" asks Mama.

Hmm.

"Baby," says Olivia.

"That baby is YOU."

What!

That little one?

Look how small the socks!

Look how tiny the hands!

Olivia looks at her own hands.

Big now. Busy now.

She grew.

She is still growing.

Right now! This minute!

Growing is quiet work.

You never feel it happening.

You only see it in pictures.
