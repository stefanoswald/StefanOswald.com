Night 250

The Piggy Toes

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Five little toes on this foot.

And they are... piggies!

This little piggy went to market.

(The big toe. The bold one.)

This little piggy stayed home.

(He's shy.)

This little piggy had roast beef.

This little piggy had none.

(Poor piggy. He gets a kiss.)

And THIS little piggy...

wait for it...

wait for it...

went WEE WEE WEE...

all the way home!

(Tickle-run up the leg!)

Shrieks! Giggles!

"MORE!"

The other foot has piggies too.

Five more.

Same market. Same wee.

Toes were made for this.
