Night 308

The Star Shape

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Tonight's shape is special.

The star!

Five points, going out

like a little burst of light.

Mama draws one. Point, point,

point, point, point.

Olivia draws one.

Hers is... wobblier.

Hers is wonderful.

(Wobbly stars shine the same.

Everyone knows that.)

Who else is this shape?

Look out the window.

Della! The gold one!

She's a this-shape!

Wave the drawing at her.

Twinkle, says the sky.

That means: nice portrait.

The chair has this shape carved.

The cake had one, gold.

Stars follow this family around.

Lucky family.

Five points. One friend. Forever.
