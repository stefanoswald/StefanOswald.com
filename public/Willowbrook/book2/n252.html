Night 252

One More Story

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

The story is over.

The book closes.

And up comes the finger.

One finger.

"One more?"

Ah. The famous request.

The oldest one there is.

Papa pretends to think.

(He decided already. He always does.)

"ONE more."

One more it is!

A short one. The bear one.

Button lost... button found... the end.

The book closes again.

Up comes the finger...

"One mo..."

"That WAS one more."

The finger considers this.

It's fair. It folds.

One means one.

(Tomorrow it will try for two.

Tomorrow numbers get bigger.)
