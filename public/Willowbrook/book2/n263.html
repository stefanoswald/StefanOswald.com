Night 263

Counting the Stars (Impossible)

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Clear night. Porch time.

Let's count the stars!

"One!"

(The bright one. Della. Hers.)

"Two!"

"Three!"

"Fo... "

Um.

There are more behind those.

And more behind THOSE.

The sky spills over with them.

Hundreds! Thousands!

The count drowns happily.

"How many stars, Papa?"

"Nobody's ever finished counting."

Really?

Really. Nobody. Ever.

The sky keeps its number secret.

Some things are for counting.

Some things are just for

leaning back and saying ohh.

The stars are the second kind.

One of them twinkles. Once.

Counted or not, she's there.
