Night 176

The Restaurant Bread

Month 6: Yum

Sometimes dinner goes out.

To the corner place. The friendly one.

The big wooden high chair waits.

Up goes Olivia.

And here comes the waiter

with the old, old ritual:

the bread crust.

"For the lady," he says.

He remembers her.

She was littler last time.

Gum, gnaw, munch.

The bread lasts two whole courses.

Mama and Papa eat warm food.

They hold hands on the table once.

The crust makes it all possible.

"Ta!" says Olivia to the waiter.

Best table. Best crust.

Same time next month.
