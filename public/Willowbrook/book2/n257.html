Night 257

Ten Fingers of Paint

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Paint day!

And today: finger counting.

Dip one finger. Press!

One dot.

Dip two fingers. Press, press!

Two dots!

Now the whole hand...

FIVE dots at once!

A five-dot flower!

Other hand: five more!

Count all the dots:

one, two, three, fo, fi...

and the rest.

Ten! (Mama helped.)

Ten fingerprints.

Nobody else's fingers

would make these exact dots.

These are Olivia-prints.

Ten little proofs

that she was here,

painting,

on this exact happy morning.

Fridge-bound. Obviously.
