Night 339

The Sleepy Animal Ways

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

How does everybody sleep?

Every animal has its way.

The horse: standing up!

(Really! Locked legs. Old trick.)

The hens: in a row on the roost,

shoulder to shoulder, murmuring.

The cat: in a circle.

Nose tucked under tail. A furry bun.

The gold dog: flopped by his door,

one ear still on duty.

The ducks: afloat! Heads back,

pillowed on their own feathers.

The snail: home. Obviously.

Ellie: leaning on her favorite tree,

balloon tied to a branch.

And Olivia?

On her back. Arms up. Starfish.

Her way. Since always.

Every sleeper finds their shape.

Every shape works.

Goodnight, every way.

Goodnight, everyone.
