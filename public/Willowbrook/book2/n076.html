Night 76

The Zipper's Zzzip

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

The coat has a zipper.

The zipper has a sound.

Zzzzzip! Up.

Zzzzzip! Down.

Olivia wants to do it.

Mama starts it. Click.

Olivia pulls. Slow... slow...

ZZZZIP!

All the way up!

The coat hugs her closed.

"I did it," say her proud hands.

Zip up: time for outside.

Zip down: home again.

The zipper sings both trips.

Small song. Big deal.

Zzzzip!
