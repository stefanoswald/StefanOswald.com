Night 34

Ellie's First Steps

Month 2: Animal Friends

Ellie is an elephant.

She lives behind the farm.

When Ellie was little...

well, littler...

she learned to walk.

Wobble, wobble, step!

Wobble, wobble, step!

Elephants have four feet.

That is a lot of feet to learn.

Her balloon bobbed behind her.

It cheered her on.

Step, step, step, step!

Soon she walked everywhere.

Just like someone else we know.

Everyone wobbles first.

Everyone walks after.
