Night 133

When the Belly Is Hungry

Month 5: All About Me

Grrrrl.

What was that?!

Olivia looks down.

It was... her belly!

Bellies talk.

They only know one word:

Grrrrl.

It means: food, please.

The belly is never rude.

It just tells the truth.

"Snack time," says Mama.

Cheese. Apple slices. Water.

Munch, munch.

The belly goes quiet.

That is belly for thank you.

Full and happy.

Until the next grrrrl.

The belly keeps perfect time.
