Night 193

Clouds Are Slow Animals

Month 7: Outside!

Lie back on the blanket.

Look up.

The sky has animals today!

That cloud: round and puffy.

"A bunny?" says Mama.

It has ears... yes! Bunny!

But wait. Watch.

The ears stretch...

The bunny gets longer...

Now it's a... a...

"Ellie!" says Olivia.

An elephant! Yes!

Clouds are slow animals.

They become each other.

Bunny to elephant to boat to fluff.

No hurry at all.

The sky plays this game all day.

It just needs watchers.

Today, it has two good ones.
