Night 302

The Coming Home Opposite

Month 10: Opposites

Every day has two doors.

The same door, twice.

Morning:

the door opens OUT.

Boots on! Adventure!

The lane, the park, the everything.

Going, going, gone!

Evening:

the door opens IN.

Boots off. Lamplight.

Soup smell. Slippers.

Home, home, home.

Going out is exciting

because coming home is coming.

Coming home is sweet

because going out happened.

The door swings both ways

so the day can too.

Out for the world.

In for the love.

Click. Latched. Both done.

Perfect day.
