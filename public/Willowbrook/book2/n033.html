Night 33

Oliver Owl's Nighttime Quest

Month 2: Animal Friends

Oliver Owl wakes up at night.

That is when owls work.

He has a quest.

Every night, the same one.

He flies over the town. Slow and quiet.

Is the farm asleep? Yes.

Is the bakery asleep? Yes.

Is the little house on Maple Lane asleep?

He looks close. His glasses shine.

Yes. Fast asleep.

"Whoo," says Oliver softly.

That means: all is well.

Quest complete.

He glides home to the big willow.

Even owls sleep, eventually.

Morning is their nighttime.
