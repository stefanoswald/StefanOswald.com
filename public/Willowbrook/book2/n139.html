Night 139

The Tickle Map

Month 5: All About Me

The body has secret buttons.

Tickle buttons!

Papa knows the map.

The ribs? Tickle-tickle...

HAHAHA! Found one!

The neck? Tickle...

Hee hee hee! Another!

The feet? The FEET?

HAHAHAHA! The best one!

Every body has its own map.

Olivia knows Papa's too.

Under his chin. In the beard.

Tickle-tickle!

"HO HO!" Found it!

Fair is fair.

Two maps. Two gigglers.

The whole house wins.
