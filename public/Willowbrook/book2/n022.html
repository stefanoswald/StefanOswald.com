Night 22

The Rug's Roads

Month 1: My World

The rug has lines.

Look closely.

The lines go around and around.

They are little roads!

Olivia has a little car.

Brmm, brmm.

The car drives the rug roads.

Around the flower. Under the chair.

Beep beep! Coming through!

Where is the car going?

Everywhere.

And then?

Right back home.

Little roads always lead home.

That is how rugs draw them.
