Night 122

Where Is Your Nose?

Month 5: All About Me

"Where is your nose?"

Olivia knows this game!

Her finger flies up...

Nose! Found it!

"Where are your ears?"

One... two! Both ears!

"Where is your belly?"

Belly! Drum on it! Boom boom!

"Where are Mama's eyes?"

Careful, careful... there.

"And where is Mama's nose?"

BOOP.

Booped it!

Everybody has a nose.

Everybody's nose can be found.

That is the whole game.

Nobody ever loses.
