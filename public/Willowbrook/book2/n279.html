Night 279

Fast and Slow

Month 10: Opposites

Two ways down the hallway.

Way one: FAST.

ZOOM!

Arms up! Full speed!

Wind in the curls!

Done in three seconds!

Way two: slow.

Sloooow.

Tiptoe... tiptoe...

Like Tessa Turtle.

Like syrup. Like clouds.

The same hallway lasts

a whole minute this way.

Fast is for chasing.

Slow is for noticing.

The rug pattern. The dust sparkle.

Fast misses things.

Slow finds them.

Run when you're happy.

Slow down when it matters.

Both feet know both speeds.

Lucky feet.
