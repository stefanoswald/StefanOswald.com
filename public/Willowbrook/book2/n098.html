Night 98

Pink

Month 4: A World of Colors

Pink comes out at the edges.

The edge of morning: pink sky.

The edge of evening: pink again.

The inside of a shell: pink.

Olivia's cheeks after the bath:

pink, pink, pink!

Mama kisses one. Mwah.

"Pink," she says. "Like the sunrise."

The sunrise is out right now.

They watch it together.

Pink spilling over the roofs.

It doesn't last long.

Pink never does.

That is why you watch for it.

Quick and soft and gone.

Like a kiss. Mwah.
