Night 207

The First Firefly Catch

Month 7: Outside!

All last summer, she reached.

Miss. Miss. Miss.

Fireflies were too floaty.

But this summer...

Papa cups his hands. Gentle.

Around one slow blinker.

"Open your hand, flat."

Olivia holds out her palm.

Papa opens his hands over hers.

And there...

on her palm...

a firefly!

Blink.

It's LIGHTING! On her HAND!

Blink.

Little lantern. Little legs.

Then wings, up, away.

Off into the dusk.

"Bye!!"

Not a catch, really.

A visit.

Even better.
