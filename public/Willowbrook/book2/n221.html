Night 221

The Boat That Was a Soap Dish

Month 8: Splash

The bath needed a boat.

There were no boats.

There was... a soap dish.

Hmm.

Flip it over.

Set it on the water.

It floats!

IT'S A BOAT!

Load it: one small cloth. The sail!

Push it, off it sails.

Across the bubble sea.

Around Cape Knee.

Through the Strait of Duck.

(Esquire watches it pass.

He does not wave. Ducks don't.)

The admiral directs everything.

"Go! Boat! Go!"

Some toys are bought.

The best ones are found.

The bath makes sailors

of soap dishes and everyone.
