Night 80

The Wind in the Willow

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

The big tree talks in wind.

Shhhhh... shhhhh...

Its long branches swish and sway.

It sounds like the sea.

It sounds like the bath filling.

It sounds like Mama's shhh

at two in the morning, long ago.

All the soft sounds are cousins.

Olivia stands under the green.

Shhhh, says the willow.

It is not saying be quiet.

It is saying rest here.

Trees say it best.

This tree says it best of all.

The whole town comes to hear it.
