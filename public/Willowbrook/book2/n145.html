Night 145

Being Gentle

Month 5: All About Me

Hands can be strong.

Hands can also be gentle.

Gentle is the special setting.

Show me gentle on the bear.

Soft pats. The floppy ear. Perfect.

Show me gentle on the flower.

One finger. Barely touching.

The petal stays!

Show me gentle on Mama's cheek.

Pat... pat...

That is the gentlest one of all.

"Gentle girl," says Mama.

Strong is for cushions and climbing.

Gentle is for everything

you love.
