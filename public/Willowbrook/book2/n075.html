Night 75

The Echo Under the Bridge

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Under the bridge, it is cool and stony.

"Hi!" says Olivia.

"Hi!" says the bridge.

Wait. What?

"Hi?"

"Hi?"

The bridge copies her!

That is an echo.

Your own voice, coming home.

"Livy!"

"Livy!"

The bridge knows her name!

Well, she taught it just now.

Echoes learn fast.

They only know what you give them.

Give them something nice.

It comes right back.
