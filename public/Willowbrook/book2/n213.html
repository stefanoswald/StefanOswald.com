Night 213

The Bath Is Ready

Month 8: Splash

Listen! The water is running.

Shhhhhh into the tub.

That sound means one thing:

bath time!

Olivia is already there.

Clothes half off. Helping. Sort of.

Mama checks with her wrist.

Warm. Not hot. Just right.

The duck is aboard.

The cup is aboard.

The water rises... rises...

Off with the last sock!

And...

in!

Splash of arrival!

The best moment of the bath

is the very first splash.

Every single night,

the water says welcome back.
