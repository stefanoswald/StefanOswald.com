Night 97

Brown

Month 4: A World of Colors

Brown is the ground color.

The dirt: brown.

The tree trunks: brown.

The bread crust: brown.

The cow at the farm: brown.

And somebody else...

somebody soft...

Bobby Bear! Brown as honey.

He matches the garden.

He matches the bread.

He matches the whole warm earth.

Olivia pats the dirt.

Pat, pat.

Then she pats the bear.

Pat, pat.

Brown things are steady things.

They hold everything else up.
