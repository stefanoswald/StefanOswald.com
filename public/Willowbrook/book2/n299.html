Night 299

The Sun Sets, the Moon Rises

Month 10: Opposites

Come to the porch. Quick.

Both lights are out!

Look west:

the sun, going down.

Gold. Sleepy. Sinking

behind the willow hill.

Now turn around. Look east:

the moon! Coming up!

Silver. Fresh. Rising

over the rooftops.

One going. One coming.

They pass like this,

every clear evening,

trading the sky.

"Your turn," says the sun.

"Rest well," says the moon.

They never argue about it.

The oldest opposite,

handled the oldest way:

taking turns.

Wave to both.

One goodnight. One hello.
