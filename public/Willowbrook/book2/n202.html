Night 202

When It's Too Hot

Month 7: Outside!

Some days the sun is TOO on.

Blazing. Baking.

The pavement: too hot for paws.

The slide: too hot to slide!

So: shade plans.

The little tub under the oak.

Two inches of cool water.

Feet in! Ahh.

A cold cloth for the neck.

Ahh again.

And, the freezer's treasure...

a fruit pop!

Lick fast. It drips fast.

Purple drips. Race them!

The oak holds the shade steady.

Hot days are slow days.

Slow and drippy and shady.

That is the whole recipe.

Summer approves it.
