Night 148

All By Myself

Month 5: All About Me

Watch what Olivia can do.

All by herself!

The spoon: loaded, delivered. Mmm.

The cup: two hands, no spills.

(Almost no spills.)

The sock: halfway on!

(Halfway counts.)

The step stool: climbed.

The light switch: clicked!

The bear: tucked in, patted.

"All by myself," says her face,

every single time.

Yes. All by yourself.

Mama watches, close by.

Close by is her job now.

Watching. Cheering. Not helping.

It is the hardest, proudest job

there is.
