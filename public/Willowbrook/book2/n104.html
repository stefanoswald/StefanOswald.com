Night 104

The Crayon Box

Month 4: A World of Colors

New in the house: crayons!

Fat ones. Toddler-sized.

Red crayon: scribble, scribble!

Look what came out, red!

It was inside the crayon

the whole time.

Blue crayon: scribble!

Green: scribble, scribble!

The paper fills up with everything.

"What is it?" asks Mama.

It is a duck.

It is always a duck.

"Beautiful," says Mama. It is.

On the fridge it goes.

The first masterpiece.

The fridge is very proud.
