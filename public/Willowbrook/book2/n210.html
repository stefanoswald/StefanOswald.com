Night 210

The Whole Sky Pink

Month 7: Outside!

Tonight the sky put on

its biggest show.

Pink!

Not a stripe of it.

ALL of it.

The whole west, glowing.

Pink to gold to orange to pink.

The clouds caught fire, softly.

The windows down the lane

all turned to copper.

The family sat on the steps.

Nobody said much.

"Ohhh," said Olivia. Once.

That covered it.

The show slid slowly down

behind the willow's hill.

Gone. Blue now. Star now.

Some evenings the sky

just wants to be looked at.

Look every time.
