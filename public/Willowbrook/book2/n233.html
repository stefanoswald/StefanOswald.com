Night 233

The Ice Cube Boat

Month 8: Splash

A hot afternoon trick:

one ice cube,

one bowl of water.

Plip!

The cube floats!

A tiny iceberg!

Push it, it glides!

Spin it, it twirls!

So slippery! So cold!

And look... it's getting smaller.

Sailing and shrinking.

Shrinking and sailing.

Where does the ice go?

Into the water.

It becomes what it sails on!

Smaller... smaller... a sliver...

gone.

"More!"

One more cube.

Summer science, freezer-fresh.

The bowl never minds.
