Night 32

Bobby Bear Learns to Share

Month 2: Animal Friends

When Olivia sleeps, Bobby Bear dreams.

In his dream, he found a berry.

One red berry. Just one.

A little bird hopped close.

The bird was hungry too.

One berry. Two friends.

Hmm.

Bobby Bear broke the berry in half.

Half for the bird.

Half for the bear.

The berry tasted extra sweet.

Shared things always do.

That is the whole secret.

The bird sang him a thank-you song.

Bobby Bear dreamed it all night long.
