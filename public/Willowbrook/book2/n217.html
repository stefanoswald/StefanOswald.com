Night 217

The Beard of Bubbles

Month 8: Splash

Tonight: extra bubbles!

A whole white mountain of them.

Scoop some up...

Put them on the chin...

A beard!

A bubble beard!

"Ho ho ho," says Olivia.

(That is beard-talk.)

Mama scoops some too.

Now MAMA has a beard!

Two bearded ladies in the steam,

ho-ho-ho-ing at each other.

The beards drip. The beards slide.

Beards don't last in the bath.

Nothing does. That's the fun.

Wash the beards away.

Underneath: same faces.

Clean ones.

Ho ho ho. Goodnight, beards.
