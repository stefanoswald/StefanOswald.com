Night 191

The Hill Roll

Month 7: Outside!

The park has a little hill.

Grassy. Soft. Not too steep.

Papa lies down sideways at the top.

And ROLLS!

Roly-poly-roly, to the bottom!

"Again!" says Olivia. For him.

Then... for her?

Lie down sideways. Grass tickles.

Papa's hands guide the first roll.

Over... over... over!

The world spins! Grass-sky-grass-sky!

At the bottom: stop.

Everything wobbles.

Dizzy!

Dizzy is funny!

"More!"

Up the hill. Roll down.

Up the hill. Roll down.

Best ride in the park: free,

grass-powered, slightly spinny.
