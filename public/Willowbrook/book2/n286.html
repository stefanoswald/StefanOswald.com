Night 286

Happy and Sad

Month 10: Opposites

This morning: sad.

The tower fell. All of it.

Tears came.

Real ones. Hot ones.

Sad is allowed here.

Sad got a hug and a listen.

"The tower fell. That's hard."

It was hard.

Then, after the rain of it...

the blocks were still there.

Build again?

Build again.

One... two... three high!

And there it came...

the smile. Back.

Happy.

Happy and sad take turns.

Nobody is one forever.

The heart has room for both.

It was built with room for both.

That is why it works so well.
