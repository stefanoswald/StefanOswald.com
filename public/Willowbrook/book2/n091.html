Night 91

Red

Month 4: A World of Colors

Red!

Olivia knows red.

Red is her color.

The little cup: red.

The rain boots: red.

The bow in her hair: red.

The balloon from the market: red!

She gathers them on the rug.

A red meeting.

Everyone is here.

"Red," says Olivia. "Red, red."

Red is the color of hers.

Red is the color of yes.

Somewhere far off, an elephant

has a red balloon too.

Red friends, everywhere.
