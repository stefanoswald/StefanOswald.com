Night 160

Water Is the Best Drink

Month 6: Yum

After running: thirsty!

After climbing: thirsty!

After the whole busy morning:

so thirsty!

What does a thirsty girl need?

Water.

Cool, clear water.

Two hands on the cup.

Tip... glug, glug, glug.

Ahhh.

That sound means it worked.

Water fixes thirsty

like kisses fix knees.

Every time. No tricks.

The rain drinks it. The brook is it.

The flowers sip it. The town runs on it.

Best drink there is.

Free, forever, from the tap.
