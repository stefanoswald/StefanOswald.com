Night 225

Washing the Red Cup

Month 8: Splash

After dinner, dishes.

Mama washes the big ones.

And the red cup?

The red cup is Olivia's job now.

Up the star stool.

Sleeves up. (Too late. Fine.)

Dip the cup. Swish the bubbles.

Rub inside. Rub outside.

Rinse, hold it under the little stream.

All clean!

Shake, shake.

Onto the rack. Her spot.

The red cup shines.

She washed it.

Herself.

It has never been cleaner.

(It has been exactly this clean.

But don't tell her that.

It has never been PROUDER.

That part is true.)
