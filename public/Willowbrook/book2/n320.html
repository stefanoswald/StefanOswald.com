Night 320

Round Food Is Happy Food

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Tonight, by accident,

dinner came out ROUND.

All of it!

Peas: tiny circles.

Meatballs: middle circles!

Orange boats: circle pieces!

And for after...

one pancake.

The roundest thing

the pan knows how to say.

Olivia surveys the plate.

Round, round, round, round.

It looks... happy.

Round food looks happy.

(Square food looks serious.

Crackers mean business.)

Roll a pea. Chase it. Eat it.

Dinner and a game.

The circle strikes again.

Happiest shape at the table.

Every table.
