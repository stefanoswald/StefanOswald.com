Night 140

Washing the Hands

Month 5: All About Me

Up on the step stool.

The one with the star.

Turn on the water. Warm.

Wet the hands.

Now the soap!

Rub, rub, rub.

Bubbles between the fingers!

Front and back.

Sing while you scrub.

The washing song, one time through.

Now rinse, bye-bye, bubbles.

Down the drain they go

with all the dirt from

digging and patting and playing.

Shake, shake. Towel.

Clean hands!

Ready for dinner.

They earned it.
