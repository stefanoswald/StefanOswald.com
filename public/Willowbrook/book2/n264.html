Night 264

Two Ears, One Mouth

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Grandpa Joe has old math.

Farm math.

"Count your ears," he says.

One... two!

Two ears.

"Count your mouth."

One!

One mouth.

"Know why?"

Why?

"So you can listen twice

as much as you talk."

Hmm.

Olivia considers this.

Then talks for three minutes

about a chicken.

Grandpa listens with both ears.

The whole report.

"Good telling," he says.

The math isn't a rule.

It's a secret.

It works better

the older you get.

He should know.

He's had it seventy years.
