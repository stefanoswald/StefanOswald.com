Night 87

What the Fire Says

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

The fireplace is awake tonight.

First fire of the cold season.

Listen to it talk.

Crackle. Snap!

Pop. Hisss...

It is telling wood stories.

Old sunny days the log remembers.

Snap! A good part.

Olivia watches from Mama's lap.

Warm face. Safe distance.

Fire is pretty and fire is hot.

We watch. We don't touch.

Crackle, settle, sigh.

The fire talks itself to embers.

Then it whispers.

Then, like everyone, it sleeps.
