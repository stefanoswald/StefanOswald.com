Night 328

The Puzzle of the Sky

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

The sky plays puzzles too.

But sky puzzles never finish.

Look up:

the sun: one gold circle. Fixed.

The birds: little flying triangles!

There, a V of them! Geese!

A whole letter, flying south!

The clouds: every shape,

becoming every other shape.

A square-ish one... going round...

now long... now gone.

Sky pieces don't click.

They drift.

The puzzle rearranges all day

and never once complains.

Maybe that's the lesson

of the big blue box:

some puzzles aren't for solving.

Some are just for watching,

flat on your back,

in the good grass,

pointing.
