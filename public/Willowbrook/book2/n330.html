Night 330

The Shapes Go to Sleep

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Bedtime for the shapes.

The circle piece: click.

Home in its round bed.

The square: click. Home.

The triangle: click. All in.

The blocks: into the box.

Squares with squares,

a tidy sleeping stack.

The ball rolls to its basket.

(It gets a nudge. Balls dawdle.)

The star shape...

the star shape stays out.

On the sill. Face to the window.

Where the real one can see it.

They like to sleep

in sight of each other.

All shapes home.

All corners resting.

All circles still.

The room is geometry, dreaming.

And one more shape in the crib:

a small warm curl,

the shape of sleep itself.

Goodnight.
