Night 10

The Bath Says Splash

Month 1: My World

The bath is full of warm water.

In goes Olivia!

In goes the duck!

Splash, says the water.

Splash, splash!

Wash the toes. Wiggle, wiggle.

Wash the belly. Tickle, tickle!

Wash the hair. Gentle, gentle.

The duck watches everything.

Ducks are good at baths.

Now the water goes down the drain.

Gurgle, gurgle, bye-bye water.

Warm towel. Warm hug.

Clean Olivia.
