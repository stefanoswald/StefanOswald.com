Night 260

The Three-Step Race

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Hallway race!

Papa versus Olivia.

But Papa is big.

So: head start rules!

Papa takes three GIANT steps.

"One... two... three!"

He's nearly at the end!

Ready...

"GO!"

ZOOM!

Here she comes!

Little legs, top speed!

Papa runs, oh no...

his shoe is untied...

oh no, he must hop...

oh NO...

She passes him!

She WINS!

Again!

Papa has lost every race

this month.

His shoes are mysteriously

always untied.

Some races are counted in steps.

The best ones are fixed

with love.
