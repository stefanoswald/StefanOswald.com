Night 190

Rain Coat Weather

Month 7: Outside!

The sky is dripping today.

Drip, drip, drip.

Stay in?

No!

Dress for it!

The rain coat: on. Bright as a boat.

The hood: up.

Now the rain can't win.

Out into the drips!

Hold out a hand.

Plip! Caught one!

Plip, plip!

The hood drums softly.

The world smells washed.

Worms are out. Puddles are filling.

Rain is just weather.

Weather is just the sky

trying different things.

Dress for it, and it's all yours.
