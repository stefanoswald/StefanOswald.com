Night 335

Why We Sleep

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

"Why?" asks Olivia.

(Her new favorite word.

It gets a whole month soon.)

Why sleep?

Good question. Here's the answer.

While you sleep,

your body does its quiet work.

Little builders wake up inside.

They grow your legs longer.

They fix the day's bumps.

They sort all the new words

onto their shelves.

They file today's shapes,

water the dreams,

and sweep up the tired.

By morning: all done!

A fresh, taller, readier Olivia!

But the builders only work

when you're asleep.

They're shy that way.

So: eyes closed.

Let the crew in.

They love their job.

Goodnight, little building site.
