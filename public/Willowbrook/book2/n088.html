Night 88

The Sound of Snow Boots

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

New snow has a sound.

Boots find it.

Crunch-squeak!

Crunch-squeak!

Every step talks.

Olivia marches.

Crunch-squeak, crunch-squeak!

Her breath makes little clouds.

Puff! Puff!

Even breathing is loud in winter!

Papa's boots go CRUNCH-SQUEAK.

Big steps. Big talk.

They march the whole lane.

The snow squeaks the whole way.

It only talks when it's fresh.

Listen while it lasts.
