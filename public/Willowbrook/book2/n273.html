Night 273

Stella Counts Her Town

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

High above, Stella counts too.

Her nightly census.

One town. (Hers.)

One hill, one willow on it.

One brook, running its singing.

One farm. One barn. Six hens.

(Or seven. The Duchess counts double.)

One bakery, ovens resting.

One library, lantern low.

One lane of porch lights: out now,

one... by one... by one.

And one window. The one.

One crib. One bear.

One girl, asleep,

who counted to five today

and kissed past a hundred.

"All counted," Stella reports.

"Mmm," hums the Moon. "All there?"

All there.

Always all there.

That is the count that matters.

Goodnight, Willowbrook.

One town, fast asleep.
