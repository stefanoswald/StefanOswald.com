Night 54

The Bee at Work

Month 2: Animal Friends

Buzz. Buzz.

The bee is at work.

Flower one: buzz, sip.

Flower two: buzz, sip.

She wears fuzzy yellow pants.

The pants are covered in gold dust.

That is flower dust. Pollen!

She carries it flower to flower.

That is her job.

Flowers need the mail delivered too.

"We watch bees," says Mama.

"We don't touch bees."

Watching is plenty.

Buzz, buzz, says the bee.

So much work! So little time!

Bees never, ever stop being busy.
