Night 295

Awake and Asleep

Month 10: Opposites

The last opposite of the day.

Awake:

eyes open, world on,

one more question, one more wiggle.

Asleep:

eyes closed, world soft,

dreams warming up backstage.

And in between...

right now...

the yawn.

Ahh-hum.

The bridge between the two.

Olivia is crossing it.

Eyes at half...

wiggle slowing...

breath going long and even...

Awake waves from one shore.

Asleep waves from the other.

The crossing takes a minute.

The minute is almost done.

There.

Safe on the far shore.

Goodnight, awake.

Hello, dreams.
