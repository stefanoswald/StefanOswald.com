Night 228

The Watering Can Round

Month 8: Splash

Evening. Watering time.

The little can, filled at the tap.

Heavy! Two hands!

First stop: her row.

The lettuces. Glug, glug.

"Grow."

Second stop: the porch pots.

The flowers tip their faces up.

Glug, glug, glug.

Third stop: the fern.

The fern is always thirsty.

Extra for the fern.

Can empty! Refill!

(The refill is half the fun.)

Last drops for the tomato.

Round complete.

Every plant, served.

The garden drips gently,

saying its green thank-yous.

The waterer bows. Bedtime.
