Night 358

The Quiet Before the Party

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

Tomorrow: the party!

The banner is up. (It says TWO.)

The cups are out.

Rosa's cake box arrives at ten.

The candles wait on their shelf.

But tonight?

Tonight is just... tonight.

The regular magic.

Bath. (Splash contest: a tie.)

Pajamas. (The star ones. Zzzip.)

Story. (The bear book. Landslide.)

Song. (Wrong notes: all present.)

The last ordinary bedtime

of being one.

Mama holds her a little longer.

Papa's watch at the door

runs a little late.

Tomorrow she wakes up

almost-two-for-real.

Tonight she's exactly this.

This exact size.

This exact girl.

This exact, perfect, ordinary night.

Hold it a minute more.

There. Kept.

Goodnight, one-year-old.

See you at two.
