Night 306

The Square Window

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

The window is a square.

Four sides. Four corners.

Trace it!

Across the top... corner!

Down the side... corner!

Across the bottom... corner!

Up the side... corner!

Home again. Four of each.

Squares are steady shapes.

They sit flat. They stack true.

The blocks: squares.

(That's why towers work.)

The crackers: squares.

The napkin: square.

Fold it, smaller square!

Squares are the sitting-still shape.

Circles roll away.

Squares stay where you put them.

Builders love them.

Snack time loves them.

Corner, corner, corner, corner.

Solid as a good house.
