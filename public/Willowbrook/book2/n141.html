Night 141

The Nose Knows

Month 5: All About Me

The nose has a secret talent.

It knows things!

Close your eyes.

Sniff.

Bread! Warm bread!

The nose knew!

Sniff again.

Orange! Mama is peeling one.

Sniff...

Cinnamon. Rosa's kind.

The nose catches every smell

floating through the house.

Dinner cooking. Rain coming.

Papa's coffee in the morning.

The nose reports everything.

Quietly. All day.

Good nose.

Boop.
