Night 38

Harry Hedgehog's Prickly Problem

Month 2: Animal Friends

Harry Hedgehog is prickly.

Not mean-prickly.

Spiky-prickly.

One day, bubbles floated by.

Harry wanted to catch one.

Pop! His spikes burst it.

Pop! Pop! Every time.

Harry sat down, sad.

Then Bella Butterfly came.

She caught a bubble on the wind...

and set it on Harry's nose.

The one soft spot!

The bubble stayed.

Harry went cross-eyed looking at it.

"Thank you," he whispered. Very still.

Friends find your soft spots.

That is their magic.
