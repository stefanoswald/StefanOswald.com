Night 149

The Two of Us Are Different

Month 5: All About Me

Lily and Olivia. Side by side.

Look!

Olivia's hair: curly. Boing!

Lily's hair: straight. Swish!

Olivia loves red.

Lily loves blue.

Olivia runs first, talks after.

Lily talks first, runs after.

Same size. Same blanket.

All different, everywhere else!

Isn't that funny?

Isn't that perfect?

If they matched exactly,

one of them would be extra.

Nobody is extra.

Two girls. Two ways.

One best-friendship.

It fits because it's different.
