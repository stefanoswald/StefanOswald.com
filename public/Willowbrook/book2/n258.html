One Missing Boot

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Time for outside!

Boot one: here.

Boot two: ...

Boot two?

BOOT TWO?

One boot is missing!

The pair is broken!

Search party!

Under the couch? No.

In the toy box? No.

Behind the door? No.

Olivia checks HER spots.

The cozy corner? No...

The bear's chair?

...

YES.

The boot. On the chair.

Was Bobby wearing it?

Nobody confesses.

Boot reunited with boot.

Two again! A pair again!

Out the door at last.

(The bear looks innocent.

Too innocent.)
