Night 164

Trying the New Thing

Month 6: Yum

On the plate tonight:

the usual friends.

Bread. Cheese. Peas.

And... a new thing.

Small. Just one piece.

What is it?

It is zucchini.

Hmm.

Olivia looks at it.

She pokes it.

She sniffs it.

Nobody says eat it.

Nobody says anything.

The new thing just sits there.

Friendly. Patient.

Maybe today: no.

That is okay.

It will visit again.

And again.

One day, maybe, a bite.

New things wait nicely.

That is their job.
