Night 65

The Bell on the Bakery Door

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

The bakery door has a bell.

Jingle!

That means: someone is here!

The bell tells Rosa every time.

Jingle, hello, neighbor!

Jingle, hello, hungry person!

Jingle, hello, Olivia!

"There's my girl," says Rosa.

The bakery smells like cinnamon.

It always does.

That is the bakery's other hello.

One for the ears. One for the nose.

Jingle, sniff, warm bread.

"More," says Olivia, at the whole thing.

More indeed.

The bell agrees, on the way out.
