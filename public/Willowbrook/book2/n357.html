Night 357

The Remember Game, Age Two Edition

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

Tonight: the remember game.

The story of THIS year.

"Once upon a time,

you turned one.

The bear moved into your crib

that very night.

And then...

you talked!

Duck first. Then everything.

You ran. You built.

You painted green

out of yellow and blue.

You met a worm, gently.

You waded the brook.

You stomped every puddle

this town could make.

You learned round and square

and heavy and light

and happy and sad

and MAYBE.

You got taller. Look at the door.

You got kinder. Look at Lily.

You got more YOU.

Look in the mirror. Livy!

That was this year.

The best one yet."

"Again," says Olivia.

The whole year? Again?

"Again."

Oh, little one.

Yes. Exactly.

That's what birthdays say too.

Again! Another year! Again!
