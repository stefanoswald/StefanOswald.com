Night 50

The Horse with the Long Face

Month 2: Animal Friends

At the next farm lives a horse.

A big, big horse.

His head alone is bigger than Olivia.

He leans over the fence.

Sniff... sniff...

His nose is soft like the blanket.

His breath is warm like the bath.

"Easy," says Grandpa Joe.

The horse is easy.

Big things are often gentle.

They don't need to hurry.

"Horse," says Olivia. Quiet.

The horse blinks his long lashes.

Hello, small one, says the blink.

Some hellos are enormous

and soft at the same time.
