Night 112

The Market of Colors

Month 4: A World of Colors

Saturday market!

It is a rainbow you can eat.

Red apples, in a pyramid.

Orange carrots, in a bunch.

Green cabbages, big as drums.

Yellow squash, curly beans,

purple plums!

Olivia names the ones she can.

"Appo! Nana!"

The stall lady laughs.

"Best customer I've got."

One apple rides home in the bag.

One rides home in Olivia.

Well, slices of it.

Markets are proof:

the prettiest colors

are the ones you can eat.
