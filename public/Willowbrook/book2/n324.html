Night 324

The Cookie Cutters

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Rosa lent the cookie cutters!

The good ones. The old tin ones.

The dough is rolled flat.

A whole field of dough!

Now: stamp the shapes!

The star cutter: press... wiggle...

lift...

A dough STAR!

The heart: press, wiggle, lift.

A dough heart!

Circle, circle, star, heart...

The field fills with shapes!

Into the oven. Watch the window.

The shapes puff. The shapes gild.

Out they come: cookie geometry!

Which to eat first?

The star. Point by point.

Then the heart. (Sorry, heart.

You were delicious.)

Shapes you can bake:

the best homework ever assigned.
