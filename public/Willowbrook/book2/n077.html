Night 77

The Owl's Whoo at Night

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

It is dark. It is quiet.

Then, far away:

"Whoo... whoo..."

Olivia knows that sound!

It is the owl. The round one.

He is doing his rounds.

Whoo means: I am checking.

Whoo means: all is well.

He checks the farm.

He checks the square.

He checks Maple Lane. Whoo.

Olivia's window: checked.

All well. All safe.

She holds Bobby Bear a little closer.

"Whoo," she whispers back.

The night has a night watchman.

Sleep easy, town.
