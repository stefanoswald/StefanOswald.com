Night 277

Loud and Quiet

Month 10: Opposites

Olivia has two volumes.

Volume one: LOUD.

The pot drum: BOOM!

The zoomies shriek: WHEEE!

The truck greeting: HI TRUCK!

Loud is for outside,

for parades,

for joy that can't fit inside.

Volume two: quiet.

The library voice: "buh."

The sleeping-Papa tiptoe.

The goodnight whisper: "nigh-nigh."

Quiet is for libraries,

for nap-house visits,

for secrets and stars.

Loud and quiet.

Both good. Both hers.

The trick, the lifelong trick...

is knowing which room

you're in.
