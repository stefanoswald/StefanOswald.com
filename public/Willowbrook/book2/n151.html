Night 151

Stella Knows Her Name

Month 5: All About Me

Every night, the star comes.

Olivia knows her now.

There, past the curtain gap.

Low and gold and warm.

"Stella," says Mama. "Her name is Stella."

"Della," says Olivia.

Close enough. Stars aren't fussy.

Olivia waves from the crib.

The star twinkles once.

That means: I see you too.

It always means that.

"Nigh-nigh, Della."

The Moon hums above, approving.

Now they know each other's names.

The girl and her star.

That is how the long watch works:

both directions.

Goodnight, Willowbrook.
