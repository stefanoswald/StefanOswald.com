Night 325

Everything Is Made of Shapes

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Walk the lane with shape eyes.

Look at the houses. Really look!

Square walls...

triangle roofs...

rectangle doors...

circle doorknobs!

The houses are shape piles!

Friendly, painted shape piles!

The truck: rectangle box,

circle wheels.

The fence: little rectangles,

holding hands, forever.

The sun: circle. The pines: triangles.

Windows: squares full of lamplight.

Once you see the shapes,

you can't stop seeing them.

The whole town is built

from the puzzle box.

Somebody clicked it all together

long ago.

They did a beautiful job.

Click by click by click.
