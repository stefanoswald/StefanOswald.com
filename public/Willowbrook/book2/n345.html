Night 345

The Lullaby Museum

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

This family has a music vault.

Bedtime songs, collected for years.

Tonight: the whole museum.

Exhibit one: Papa's song.

The wrong notes, in their places.

Learned wrong. Kept wrong. Beloved.

Exhibit two: Mama's hum.

No words at all.

It's the sound of being carried.

Exhibit three: Grandpa's tune,

the one he sings to the chickens.

(It works on children too.)

Exhibit four: the new one...

Olivia's own addition...

"nigh-nigh, nigh-nigh,"

sung to her bear,

to her own melody,

invented this very year.

The collection grows.

Someday she'll sing them all

to somebody small.

Wrong notes included.

ESPECIALLY the wrong notes.

That's how heirlooms work.

Softly now. All together.

Hmm-hmm. Nigh-nigh.
