Night 338

The Story Chooser

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

Every night, a big decision.

WHICH story?

The chooser approaches the basket.

The candidates:

The bear book. (The classic.)

The duck one. (Strong contender.)

The new one about the tractor.

(Grandpa-approved.)

She pulls one... considers...

pulls another... weighs them...

(This is not fast.

Democracy never is.)

And the winner...

raised high overhead...

THE BEAR BOOK.

(The bear book wins

four nights of five.

The bear book is the incumbent.)

To the lap! Open the covers!

The chooser settles in,

deeply satisfied with governance.

The best part of choosing:

you always choose right.

It's your bedtime.

Every choice is the right book.
