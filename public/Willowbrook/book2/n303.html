Night 303

Little Word, Big Word

Month 10: Opposites

Words come in sizes too!

Little words:

"Hi." "No." "Up." "Ta."

Small as pebbles.

Mighty as boulders.

("No" is the mightiest pebble

ever mined.)

And now, big words!

Olivia is collecting them.

Today's treasure:

"buttahfly."

THREE pieces long!

But-tah-fly!

She carries it carefully,

like the watering can,

with both hands of her mouth.

"Buttahfly!"

Everyone cheers.

Little words do the daily work.

Big words are for parades.

Her parade is just starting.

Wave as it goes by.
