Night 18

The Cozy Corner

Month 1: My World

In the living room is a corner.

A little corner. A cozy one.

It has a cushion, just Olivia's size.

It has a shelf, just Olivia's height.

And the shelf has books.

Olivia picks one.

She sits. She opens it.

There is the bear. There is the button.

She knows this one!

She reads it her way.

"Buh. Buh. Nigh-nigh."

That is exactly how it goes.

Corners are little houses.

This one is hers.
