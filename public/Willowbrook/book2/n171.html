Night 171

The Blueberry Count

Month 6: Yum

Blueberries in a bowl.

Round and blue and little.

Let's count them in!

"One..." Pop. Mmm.

"Two..." Pop.

"Three!" Pop, pop!

(That was two at once.

The counting wobbles. It's fine.)

Blueberries are counting food.

Small enough to number.

Sweet enough to keep going.

"More one?" asks Olivia.

More one! The best request.

One, two, three, again and again.

The bowl empties by numbers.

Math has never been

this delicious.
