Night 245

Two Is a Pair

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

After one comes two.

Two is a pair!

Two hands. Tap, tap.

Two feet. Stomp, stomp.

Two eyes. Blink, blink.

Two ears, one, two!

The bear has two ears too.

(One floppy. Still counts.)

Two boots by the door.

Two socks in the drawer.

(Usually. Socks wander.)

Mama and Olivia: two!

A pair of people,

counting pairs all morning.

Two is the together number.

It takes two to hug.

Two to seesaw. Two to share.

One is you.

Two is you-and-somebody.

Two is very good.
