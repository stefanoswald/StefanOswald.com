Night 182

The Door to Everywhere

Month 7: Outside!

This is the front door.

Inside it: home.

Outside it: everywhere!

Boots on. Coat zipped. Zzzip!

Mama turns the handle.

Click... creeeak...

Light! Air! Birds!

The outside was there all along,

waiting on the step.

It waits very well.

"Out!" says Olivia.

Out it is.

One step. Two steps.

The door watches them go.

It will hold home safe

till they come back.

That is the deal.

Doors always keep it.
