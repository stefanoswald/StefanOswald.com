Night 208

The Farm's Evening Sounds

Month 7: Outside!

Evening at the farm.

Everything is going in.

Listen with Grandpa.

The hens murmur to their roost.

Bok... bok... settling down.

The horse blows softly next door.

Pfffff. That means good hay.

The barn door rolls shut.

Ruh-rumble... boom.

Goodnight, barn.

The tractor ticks as it cools.

Tick... tick...

Even tractors wind down.

"Hear that?" says Grandpa.

"That's a farm saying its prayers."

The first star comes up

over the willow line.

Right on time.

Farms and stars keep the same clock.
