Night 109

The Color of Quiet

Month 4: A World of Colors

At day's end, colors get sleepy.

The red cup dims.

The yellow blanket softens.

The green rug goes grey-green.

Everything turns

the color of quiet:

dusk blue.

It comes in through the window.

It fills the room like slow water.

Shapes stay. Colors rest.

"See?" whispers Mama.

"Even colors have a bedtime."

The lamp clicks on. Small gold.

One color stays up late,

to read the story by.

The rest sleep.

Like Olivia. Now. Soon. Now.
