Night 329

Round and Round the Garden

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

One more circle tonight.

The oldest one.

Open your hand, little one.

Here comes the finger:

"Round and round the garden,

like a teddy bear..."

(The finger circles the palm.

Round... and round...)

"One step..."

(Up the arm!)

"Two step..."

(Higher!)

"And TICKLE YOU UNDER THERE!"

(Armpit! Shrieks! Collapse!)

Again?

Again.

Round and round the garden...

Her grandmother played this.

Her grandmother's grandmother.

The circle on the palm

goes back and back and back.

Now it's hers.

Someday she'll trace it

on a hand smaller still.

Round and round the garden.

Round and round the years.
