Night 154

Warm Soup Night

Month 6: Yum

Cold outside. Soup inside.

The pot cooked all afternoon.

Now: bowls!

A big one for Papa.

A middle one for Mama.

A little one for Olivia.

Careful, warm.

Blow on it. Whoo, whoo.

Now hunt with the spoon.

A carrot! Captured!

A potato! Got it!

The broth at the bottom:

tip the bowl, Mama helps.

Mmm. Warm all the way down.

Soup is a hug you can eat.

The pot knows the recipe.

It is mostly love and carrots.
