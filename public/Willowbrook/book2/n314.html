Night 314

Snack Shapes

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Today, lunch is a shape lesson.

Look at the plate!

The crackers: squares.

Crunch, now it's a broken square.

(New shape. Delicious shape.)

The cucumber slices: circles!

Little green wheels.

The sandwich, cut corner to corner:

triangles!

Two of them!

Sails on a bread boat.

Eat the point first? The wide part?

Point first. Obviously.

The banana: cut into circles.

The cheese: cubes! Square's chunky cousin.

"What shape is the plate?"

Circle!

The circle holds all the others.

Circles are good at holding.

Lunch: eaten.

Geometry: delicious.
