Night 116

The Balloon That Was the Reddest

Month 4: A World of Colors

The day was grey.

The lane was grey.

Then, at the market corner...

the balloon man!

And his bouquet of colors.

One red one, right in the middle.

The reddest red there ever was.

It glowed like a little lantern.

"That one," pointed Olivia.

Of course that one.

Tied to her wrist, it bobbed.

The grey day didn't stand a chance.

One red balloon

can fix a whole afternoon.

Ask anybody.

Ask the elephant.
