Night 55

Ellie and the Puddle

Month 2: Animal Friends

Ellie is big.

The puddle is small.

But Ellie is scared of it.

It is shiny. It is strange.

It has another elephant inside it!

She peeks. The other elephant peeks.

She backs up. It vanishes!

Oh dear. Oh dear.

Her balloon pats her back. Bob, bob.

Ellie tries one toe.

Splash!

The other elephant was... water.

Just water. All along.

Ellie stomps it! Splash! SPLASH!

Now it is her favorite thing.

Scary things often are.

Once you try one toe.
