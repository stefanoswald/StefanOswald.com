Night 72

Freddy's Ribbit Choir

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

At dusk, the brook gets loud.

Ribbit! Ribbit! Ribbit!

It is the frog choir.

And who is up front, waving a reed?

Freddy.

Of course it is Freddy.

"ALTOS!" he yells. "MORE RIBBIT!"

Ribbit, ribbit!

"BASSES! BIG ONES!"

RRRRIBBIT.

The fireflies blink like stage lights.

The brook burbles along.

Does the choir sound beautiful?

Not exactly.

Does the choir sound happy?

Completely.

That is the better one anyway.
