Night 262

The Domino Line

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Papa is building something odd.

Blocks. Standing up.

In a line.

Not a tower. A LINE.

One... two... three... fo... fi...

Five blocks, all standing.

"Ready?" says Papa.

Ready for what?

"Touch the first one.

Just a tiny push."

Push...

CLICK!

click-click-click-click!

They ALL fell!

One push, five falls!

The falling traveled down the line!

"AGAIN!"

Stand them up. Push.

Click-click-click-click-click!

One little push

can move a whole line.

Remember that.

It works on blocks.

It works on lots of things.
