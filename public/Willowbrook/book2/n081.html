Night 81

Beep Beep Truck

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Best day of the week!

The big truck is coming.

Hear it first: rumble, rumble.

Then: BEEP. BEEP. BEEP.

That means it is backing up.

Then the great CLANK!

Up goes the bin!

CRASH-tumble, in it goes!

Down comes the bin. Clonk.

The truck workers wave.

Olivia waves with both arms.

Rumble, rumble, gone.

Same time next week.

She will be at the window.

She is never, ever late

for the truck concert.
