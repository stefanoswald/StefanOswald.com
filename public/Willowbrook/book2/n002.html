Night 2

The Red Cup

Month 1: My World

This is Olivia's cup.

It is red. It is little.

It is just her size.

Two hands on the cup.

Lift. Tip. Sip!

Some water goes in Olivia.

Some water goes on Olivia.

That is how cups work.

"More," says Olivia.

More water. More sips.

Good drinking, Olivia!

The red cup sits by the sink now.

Tomorrow it will be ready.

Cups always are.
