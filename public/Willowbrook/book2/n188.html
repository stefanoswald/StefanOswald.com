Night 188

The Ant Highway

Month 7: Outside!

Look down. Look close.

On the path: a tiny highway.

Ants!

Marching in a line.

One after another after another.

Where are they going?

Home, with groceries.

One carries a crumb.

The crumb is bigger than he is!

Strong little ant.

Olivia watches. Crouched low.

One finger hovers... gentle...

No touching the traffic.

Just watching it flow.

Busy, busy, busy.

The smallest rush hour in town.

"Bye, ants."

Step OVER the highway.

Big feet are careful here.
