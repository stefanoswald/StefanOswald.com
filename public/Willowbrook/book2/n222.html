Night 222

Water Hands

Month 8: Splash

Try to grab the water.

Go on. Grab a handful!

Squeeze...

open...

gone!

Where did it go?

It was RIGHT THERE.

Try again. Squeeze tight!

Open...

Empty!

Water will not be held.

It slips through every finger.

It runs off every palm.

You cannot keep it in your fist.

But cup your hands, gently...

like holding a baby bird...

Look. A little pool. Staying.

Water can't be grabbed.

It can only be held gently.

Like a lot of the best things.
