Night 6

The Lamp and the Dark

Month 1: My World

Click!

The lamp is on. Hello, light!

Click!

The lamp is off. Hello, dark!

The dark is not empty.

The bear is still there.

The blanket is still there.

Mama is still there. Always.

Click! Light again.

See? All there.

"Again," says Olivia.

One more click.

Then the lamp goes to sleep.

The dark tucks everyone in.

The dark is just the day, resting.
