Night 35

Freddy Frog's Hopping Adventure

Month 2: Animal Friends

Freddy Frog lives at the brook.

Freddy loves to hop.

Little hops: hop, hop, hop.

Middle hops: boing, boing!

And the big one. The famous one.

Freddy backs up.

Freddy wiggles.

Freddy JUMPS...

over the whole brook!

Splash-landing. Ta-daa!

"DID ANYONE SEE THAT?" yells Freddy.

The brook saw.

The brook always sees.

"Do it again," burbles the brook.

So Freddy does.

All day, every day.

Some friends are like that.
