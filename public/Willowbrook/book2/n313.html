Night 313

The Puzzle's First Piece

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

A new thing: a puzzle!

Wooden. Chunky. Three pieces.

Each piece has a home.

The circle piece...

where does it live?

Try the square home. No.

Try the triangle home. No.

Try the round home...

CLICK.

Yes!!

It fits! Perfectly!

Nothing else fit.

Only its own home fit.

That's the puzzle secret:

every piece has one place

that's exactly its shape.

The square finds its square.

The triangle, its triangle.

Click. Click.

All home. All flat. Done!

Dump it out.

Again!

(The finding is the game.

The click is the prize.)
