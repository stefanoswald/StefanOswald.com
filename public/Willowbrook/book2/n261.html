Night 261

How Many Kisses

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Time for goodnight kisses.

How many tonight?

Count them!

Forehead: "one."

This cheek: "two."

That cheek: "three."

Nose: "fo!"

Chin: "fi!"

This ear... that ear...

"More!"

The count is losing track.

The count does not care.

Seven? Ten? A hundred?

"How many kisses do you get?"

asks Mama.

Olivia spreads her arms

as wide as they go.

ALL the kisses.

Correct.

The truest count again.

All of them. Every night.

Forever.

That's the arithmetic of this house.

Sleep tight, well-counted girl.
