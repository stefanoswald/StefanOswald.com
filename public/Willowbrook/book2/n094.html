Night 94

Green

Month 4: A World of Colors

Green is the growing color.

The grass: green.

The leaves: green.

The garden: green, green, green.

Olivia lies down in it.

Green tickles her ears.

And look, something greener!

A frog. THE frog.

Freddy, bright as a leaf.

"GREEN?" says Freddy. "I INVENTED IT."

He did not.

But he wears it best, maybe.

Hop, and he's gone. Green into green.

Everything growing is green.

The whole spring runs on it.

Grow on, green.
