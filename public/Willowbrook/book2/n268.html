Night 268

The Last Cracker

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Snack is nearly done.

The plate has...

one cracker left.

One.

Two friends.

Everyone sees it.

Everyone counts it.

One is not two.

Lily looks at Olivia.

Olivia looks at Lily.

Somebody's hand moves...

it's Olivia's...

she picks up the cracker...

and...

crack.

Half and half.

"Ta," says Lily.

Munch. Munch.

The oldest problem.

The oldest answer.

It still works.

It will work at every table

they ever share.

And there will be many, many tables.
