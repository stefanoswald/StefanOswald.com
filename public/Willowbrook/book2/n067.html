Night 67

The Tick and the Tock

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

The clock says tick.

The clock says tock.

The rocking chair says creak.

Creak... tick... creak... tock.

Mama rocks slow.

The room keeps time.

Olivia's eyes go heavy.

Tick. Creak. Tock.

Everything gentle keeps a beat.

The clock. The chair.

Mama's heart under Olivia's ear.

Thum-thum. Thum-thum.

That is the first sound she ever knew.

It still works best of all.

Tick, tock, thum-thum, sleep.
