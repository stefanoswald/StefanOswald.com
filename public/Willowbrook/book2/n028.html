Night 28

The Plant That Drinks

Month 1: My World

On the windowsill lives a plant.

It is green. It is quiet.

It gets thirsty. Just like Olivia!

But plants can't hold cups.

So Olivia helps.

Tip the little can... gently...

The water goes to the roots.

Glug, glug, says the dirt.

"More?" asks Olivia.

Not too much. Plants sip slowly.

One leaf shines wet.

That is a plant saying thank you.

Grow, little plant.

Olivia is growing too.
