Night 31

Stella Peeks In

Month 1: My World

Way up high, there is a star.

Her name is Stella.

Every night, she peeks in the window.

What does she see?

A blanket. The softest one.

A bear. The floppiest ear.

A little girl, fast asleep.

"All there," says Stella.

"Mmm," hums the Sleepy Moon.

That means good.

Stella twinkles once. That means yes.

The whole sky watches Willowbrook.

The whole night keeps it safe.

Goodnight, little town.

Goodnight, Olivia.
