Night 41

Henrietta's Big News

Month 2: Animal Friends

Henrietta is a chicken.

This morning she is LOUD.

Bok! Bok! BOK!

What is it, Henrietta?

She marches to the nest.

She points with her beak.

An egg!

One brown egg. Warm and smooth.

"Well now," says Grandpa Joe.

"Egg!" says Olivia.

Henrietta puffs up proud.

It is a very good egg.

It might be the best egg ever.

Henrietta thinks so every day.

She is right every day, too.
