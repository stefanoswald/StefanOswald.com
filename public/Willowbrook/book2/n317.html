Night 317

Building with All the Shapes

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Tonight: build EVERYTHING.

The squares stack.

One, two, three, FOUR high!

(A record! The crowd goes wild!)

Now the triangle...

on TOP.

A roof!

It's a house!

The circle blocks...

don't stack.

(They roll off. Every time.

Circles refuse roofs.)

So: lean them! Wheels!

The house has wheels now.

It's a house-truck!

The best building

uses every shape

for what it's best at.

Squares hold. Triangles top.

Circles go.

The city rises on the rug.

Population: one bear, watching.

Zoning approval: pending crash.

CRASH.

Approved! Rebuild at dawn!
