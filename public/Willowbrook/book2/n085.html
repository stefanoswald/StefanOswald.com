Night 85

Mama's Hum

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Mama hums when she works.

She doesn't even know it.

Hmm-hm-hmm...

In the kitchen: humming.

Folding towels: humming.

Olivia follows the sound.

Room to room.

The hum means Mama is near.

The hum means all is well.

It is better than any clock.

Better than any bell.

Hmm-hm-hmm...

Olivia hums too, following.

Two hums in one house.

The house keeps them both.

Houses collect their family's sounds.
