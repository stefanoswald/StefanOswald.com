Night 243

The Moon on the Water

Month 8: Splash

At night, the pond plays a trick.

Look at the water.

There's a moon in it!

A silver one. Floating.

Is it the real Moon?

Look up. Still there.

Two moons?

One real. One reflection.

The pond copies the sky...

remember? It always has.

The water-moon wobbles

when the fish turn over in bed.

The sky-Moon hums.

"Mmm."

She likes having a twin.

Everyone does, a little.

Stella shines beside them both...

above, and on the water too.

Four lights. Two skies. One town.

All of it asleep.

Goodnight, both moons.

Goodnight, Willowbrook.
