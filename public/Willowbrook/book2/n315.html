Night 315

The Zigzag Path

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

The park path does something

funny by the pond.

It zigzags!

This way... turn!

That way... turn!

This way again!

Nobody walks it straight.

You CAN'T. It won't let you.

Olivia walks the zigs.

Olivia walks the zags.

Whole body tilting into turns!

It's like being lightning,

slowly.

Zig... zag... zig...

Why did they build it bendy?

Maybe the builder was happy.

Maybe the pond wanted company

a little longer.

Either way:

best part of the path.

Straight paths get you there.

Zigzags let you dance there.
