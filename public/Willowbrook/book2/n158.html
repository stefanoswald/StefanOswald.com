Night 158

Peas, One by One

Month 6: Yum

Peas!

Little green balls.

Tricky to catch.

But Olivia's fingers are experts now.

Pinch, got one!

Eat it? Not yet.

First: line them up.

One pea. Two peas. Three.

A little green parade.

NOW eat them.

Pop. Pop. Pop.

The parade marches in.

Some children push peas away.

Some days Olivia does too.

Not today.

Today, peas are a game.

Tomorrow? The peas will find out.

Peas are patient.
