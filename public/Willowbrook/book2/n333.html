Night 333

The Blanket Fort Blueprint

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Tonight: the biggest build.

The FORT.

Papa is the crane.

Olivia is the architect.

Walls: the square cushions.

Stack! Steady! Square!

Roof: the big blanket,

couch to chair...

a triangle roof! Like the houses!

Door: the round pillow,

rolled aside. A circle door!

Inside: one lamp's glow

through the blanket. Gold.

One bear, imported. One book.

The architect inspects.

Walls: solid. Roof: cozy.

Door: rolls open, rolls shut.

Every shape, doing its best job.

"Good house," she rules.

The best one on the lane tonight.

Population: two, plus bear.

Story time in the triangle light.
