Night 220

Hair Wash Night

Month 8: Splash

Some bath nights are easy nights.

Some are hair wash nights.

Tonight: hair wash.

"No," says Olivia. On principle.

This soap or that soap?

That soap. Okay.

Bubbles in the curls.

Scrub, scrub, actually...

that part feels nice.

Now the rinse. The tricky part.

Head back. Way back.

Mama's hand shields the eyes.

"Look at the ceiling!"

Warm water, back of the head.

Once. Twice. Done!

Done!

No eyes stung. No tears.

"All done," says Olivia. Proudly.

As if it were her idea.

Tonight, everyone wins.
