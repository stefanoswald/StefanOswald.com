Night 205

The Rock Collection

Month 7: Outside!

Every walk, Olivia finds rocks.

Not just any rocks.

THE rocks.

Today's finds, on the step:

One smooth grey one.

One with sparkles. (Sparkles!)

One almost round.

One just... good. Hard to explain.

She lines them up.

She rearranges them.

Sparkly first? Round first?

Big decisions.

Papa is shown each one.

"Ooh," he says. Correctly.

Other people walk past rocks.

Collectors know better.

The ground is full of treasure.

You just have to be

low enough to see it.
