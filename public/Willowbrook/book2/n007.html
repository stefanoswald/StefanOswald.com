Night 7

Bobby Bear's Chair

Month 1: My World

Olivia has a chair.

Grandpa Joe made it.

It has a star on the back.

But who is sitting in it?

Bobby Bear!

"My chair," says Olivia.

Bobby Bear does not move.

Bears are slow about chairs.

Olivia pats his ear. The floppy one.

Then she squeezes in beside him.

Two friends. One chair.

It fits just right.

Grandpa made it that way.

Grandpas always do.
