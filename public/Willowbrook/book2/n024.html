Night 24

The Mailbox Flag

Month 1: My World

At the end of the walk: the mailbox.

It has a little red flag.

Flag up: something inside!

Papa lifts Olivia. Open the door.

Creak...

A letter!

Olivia carries it. Very important.

Letters are for carrying carefully.

This one is from the aunts.

It has a drawing inside.

And kisses, folded flat.

Mama unfolds them at the table.

"For you," she says.

Mail is love with a stamp on it.
