Night 321

The Crescent Moon Boat

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

The Moon is thin tonight.

A little silver curve,

lying on its back.

What does it look like?

A smile?

A banana?!

(Points for that one.)

A boat.

A little silver boat,

sailing the dark sea of sky.

Who rides in the moon boat?

Dreams do.

They climb in every night.

The Moon rocks them gentle,

humming her one hum,

sailing them slow

over all the sleeping towns

to morning's shore.

Climb aboard, little dreamer.

Your seat is saved.

It always is.

Push off. Rock, rock.

Sail on. Shhh.
