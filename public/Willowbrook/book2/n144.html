Night 144

The Hiccups

Month 5: All About Me

Hic!

What was that?

Hic!

There it is again!

Hiccups.

They live in the middle of you.

They jump out. Hic!

Nobody invites them.

They come anyway.

Hic! Hic!

Each one bounces Olivia a little.

It is funny. It is strange.

Sips of water help.

Sip... sip... wait...

Quiet.

Are they gone?

...

Gone!

Bye, hiccups.

Come back never.

(They will come back.)
