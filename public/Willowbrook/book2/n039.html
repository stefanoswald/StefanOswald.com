Night 39

Little Bunny's Fluffy Tail

Month 2: Animal Friends

In the park lives a little bunny.

She has a fluffy white tail.

But here is the problem:

she cannot see it!

It is behind her.

She turns. It turns too!

Around and around and around.

Olivia watches from the path.

"There!" Olivia points.

The bunny stops. Dizzy.

Olivia can see the tail just fine.

Fluffy as a dandelion puff.

"Fluff!" says Olivia.

The bunny wiggles it proudly.

Sometimes we need a friend

to see our best parts.
