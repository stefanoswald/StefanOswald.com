Night 197

The Bug Hotel Under the Log

Month 7: Outside!

In the garden lies an old log.

It looks quiet.

It is NOT quiet.

It is a hotel!

Papa rolls it back. Gently.

Look who lives there!

Pill bugs! Grey and round.

Touch one, it rolls into a ball!

A tiny armored marble!

A beetle hurries deeper.

Excuse us, beetle.

Everyone home? Everyone fine?

Good.

Roll the log back. Gently, gently.

The hotel closes its door.

"Bye, bugs."

Sleep tight, little guests.

Sorry about the ceiling.
