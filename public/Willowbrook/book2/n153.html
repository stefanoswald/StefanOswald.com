Night 153

The Apple's Crunch

Month 6: Yum

The apple is red and round.

Papa slices it thin.

Thin slices for little teeth.

Now, bite!

CRUNCH.

Did you hear that?

The apple talks when you eat it!

Crunch! Crunch!

It is the loudest fruit.

Sweet juice. Cold and bright.

This apple grew on a tree.

Maybe the orchard's tree!

Maybe Olivia picked it!

Twist, pull, just like the lesson.

Crunch, crunch, all gone.

"More appo."

More apple it is.

The tree made plenty.
