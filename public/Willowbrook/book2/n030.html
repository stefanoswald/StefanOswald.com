Night 30

The House Goes to Sleep

Month 1: My World

Listen. The house is going to sleep.

The kettle sleeps in the kitchen.

The pots sleep in the cupboard.

The broom sleeps by the door.

The shoes sleep in their line.

The toy box sleeps with its mouth closed.

The clock stays up. Tick. Tick.

Someone has to count the night.

Upstairs, one little light glows.

One little girl yawns.

One soft bear waits.

The house holds them all.

Houses are good at holding.

Goodnight, house.
