Night 126

Two Eyes That See

Month 5: All About Me

These are eyes.

Olivia has two.

They see everything!

The bear. The cup. Papa's face.

Now, hands over the eyes.

Dark! Where did everything go?

Hands away...

It all came back!

Eyes are little windows.

You can close the curtains.

You can open them.

The world waits outside

the whole time.

Blink, blink.

Two quick curtains.

At bedtime, the eyes close long.

And the world? It waits. It waits.
