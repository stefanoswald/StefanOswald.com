Night 17

The Clock Says Tick

Month 1: My World

On the wall lives the clock.

It has a round face.

It has two thin hands.

And it talks! Very quietly.

Tick, says the clock. Tick. Tick.

Olivia listens close.

Tick. Tick. Tick.

What is it saying?

It is counting the day.

Tick: time to play.

Tick: time to eat.

Tick: time to sleep, little one.

The clock never forgets bedtime.

That is its favorite tick of all.
