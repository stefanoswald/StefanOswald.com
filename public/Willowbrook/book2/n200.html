Night 200

Two Hundred Nights of Stories

Month 7: Outside!

Two hundred nights.

Two hundred stories.

That is how many this book holds

so far, in this house, for this girl.

Tonight's story is an old friend.

Olivia tells parts herself now.

"Buh!" yes, the bear.

"Buh-buh!" the button, found.

She turns the pages.

She knows where everything lives.

Two hundred nights of lamp and lap.

Some sleepy. Some silly.

Some through colds. Some through storms.

Every one counted.

Every one kept.

Stories stack up like blocks

into a tower called childhood.

Tonight: one more block.

Goodnight. Again tomorrow.

Always again tomorrow.
