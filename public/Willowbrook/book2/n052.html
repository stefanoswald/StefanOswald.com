Night 52

The Chick Brigade Grows Up

Month 2: Animal Friends

Remember the little chicks?

The peeping golden ones?

Look at them now!

Bigger. Fluffier. Faster.

They run around the coop.

A feathered parade with no line at all.

"Peep!" says Olivia.

"Bok," says a chick.

Their peeps are turning into boks!

Chicks grow up.

Their voices grow too.

Just like someone else.

Someone whose babble turned into words.

"They grew," says Grandpa Joe, "same as you."

Everybody at the farm grows.

The farm likes it that way.
