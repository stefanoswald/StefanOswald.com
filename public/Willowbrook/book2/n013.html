Night 13

Papa's Glasses

Month 1: My World

Papa has round glasses.

They sit on his nose.

They help his eyes see.

Olivia touches them. Careful, careful.

"Gentle," says Papa.

Gentle it is.

Papa takes them off.

Now his face looks different!

Same Papa. Silly eyes.

On they go again.

There he is.

Glasses off: Papa.

Glasses on: Papa.

Always Papa, every way.

That is the best part.
