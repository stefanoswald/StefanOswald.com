Night 124

The Belly Laugh

Month 5: All About Me

Where do laughs live?

Little giggles live in the nose.

Hee hee.

Medium laughs live in the chest.

Ha ha ha!

But the big ones...

the BIG ones...

live in the belly.

Way down deep.

When one comes up...

look out!

It shakes the whole Olivia!

HA HA HA HA!

It makes everyone else laugh too.

Belly laughs are catching.

The belly keeps making more.

It never runs out.

Bellies are generous like that.
