Night 185

The Dandelion's Two Tricks

Month 7: Outside!

The park is named for this flower.

The dandelion!

It has two tricks.

Trick one: gold.

A little sun on a stem.

Pick one. Hold it. Gold!

Trick two: the puffball.

The gold turns white and fluffy.

And then...

blow!

(Mama helps. Big breath.)

Whoosh!

The puff flies apart!

A hundred tiny umbrellas,

off to plant new suns.

"More!" There are always more.

The park is full of both tricks.

Gold and puff, gold and puff.

All summer long.
