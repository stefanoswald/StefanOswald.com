Night 174

When Tummies Are Full

Month 6: Yum

The tummy talks at the start.

Grrrl. Food, please.

And the tummy talks at the end, too.

It says: enough.

Not with a growl.

With a feeling.

Olivia knows the feeling.

She pushes the plate. Gently.

Hands up: all done.

"All done?" asks Mama.

All done.

The plate goes away. No fuss.

Maybe three bites are left.

The tummy knows its size.

It is the boss of enough.

Trust the tummy.

It has one job,

and it does it well.
