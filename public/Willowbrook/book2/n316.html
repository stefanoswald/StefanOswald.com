Night 316

The Spiral Snail

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

The snail is back!

And he carries the fanciest

shape in the garden.

Look at his shell.

A line that goes

round... and round... and IN.

Never crossing itself.

Curling to the middle.

A spiral!

Trace it, round and round

and round and in.

Where does it end?

At the very middle.

Where the snail keeps

his coziest, oldest room.

Tessa's shell has one too!

A cinnamon-roll swirl!

And the cinnamon roll itself...

spiral! (A delicious one.)

Water going down the drain: spiral!

The fern's new leaf: spiral!

The garden loves this shape.

Round and round and in.

Home at the center.

Always home at the center.
