Night 254

Half for You, Half for Me

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

One cookie.

Two friends.

Oh no?

Oh yes!

Watch:

Crack.

TWO cookie pieces!

"Half for you, half for me."

Olivia gets one half.

Lily gets one half.

They compare them. Closely.

Same? Same-ish.

Same enough.

Munch.

Munch.

One cookie fed two friends.

That is the oldest math trick

in the whole world.

It works on cookies.

It works on oranges.

It works on almost everything

except gum boots

and Bobby Bear.

Some things don't halve.

Cookies do. Gladly.
