Night 36

Tessa the Turtle's New Friend

Month 2: Animal Friends

Tessa Turtle walks slowly.

Very slowly.

Everyone at the brook hurries past.

Hop, hop! Flit, flit!

Then one day, Tessa met a snail.

The snail walked slowly too.

Even slower than Tessa!

They walked together.

Slow... and slow.

They saw everything.

The dew on the grass.

The ladybug on the leaf.

Fast friends miss those.

"Nice pace," said the snail.

"Nice pace," said Tessa.

Slow is also a speed.

It might be the best one.
