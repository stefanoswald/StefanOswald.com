Night 342

The Dream Garden

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

Where do dreams come from?

Here is what Mama says:

There's a garden. Inside.

All day long, you plant it.

The duck pond, plip...

a dream seed.

The star cookie, plip.

The fort's gold light, plip.

Lily's giggle, plip, plip.

Every good moment,

a little seed, tucked in.

And at night,

when the eyes close,

the garden grows.

Up come the dreams!

Duck-flavored. Fort-lit.

Giggle-watered.

Tonight's crop is planted.

It was a good planting day.

Rich soil. Fine seeds.

Close your eyes.

Walk in, little gardener.

See what's blooming.
