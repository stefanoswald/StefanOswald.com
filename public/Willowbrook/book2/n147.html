Night 147

Stretching Up Tall

Month 5: All About Me

Stand at the door frame.

Back straight. Heels down.

(No tiptoes! Nice try.)

Papa's pencil comes down.

Mark!

Now look.

The new mark...

and the old mark, below it.

See the space between?

That is growing!

That is how much more Olivia

there is now!

"So tall," says Mama.

Taller than the last mark.

Shorter than the next one.

The door frame keeps them all.

It is her tallness diary.

Growing up, mark by mark.
