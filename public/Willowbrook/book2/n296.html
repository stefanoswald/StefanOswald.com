Night 296

Freddy Explains Opposites (Loudly)

Month 10: Opposites

"OPPOSITES!" announces Freddy.

"I WILL DEMONSTRATE!"

(Nobody asked. As usual.)

"WET!"

Splash! He's in the brook.

"DRY!"

Hop! He's on the rock.

"WET!" Splash!

"DRY!" Hop!

"LOUD!" (That one, he nails.)

"...quiet."

(Two seconds. A record.)

"FAST!" Zip around the rock!

"SLOW!" He points at Tessa.

"Excuse me," says Tessa. Slowly.

"UP!" Jump! "DOWN!" Land!

"THE END! APPLAUSE!"

The brook burbles.

Tessa nods. Eventually.

It was, honestly,

a pretty good lesson.

Don't tell him.

He'd do an encore.
