Night 301

Inside Voice, Outside Voice

Month 10: Opposites

The voice has two homes.

Inside voice lives... inside.

Small. Soft. Table-sized.

"More, please."

"Nigh-nigh."

Outside voice lives... outside!

Where the sky can hold it!

The park is its palace!

Ready?

Release the outside voice!

"HELLOOOOO!"

It rolls across the lawn!

The ducks look up!

Magnificent!

Again! "WHEEE!"

Outside voice, fully stretched.

It needed that.

Voices are like dogs:

walk them daily,

let them run somewhere open,

and they'll rest politely

indoors.

Mostly.
