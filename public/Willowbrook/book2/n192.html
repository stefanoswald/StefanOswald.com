Night 192

The Fence Friends

Month 7: Outside!

The fence has two sides.

Olivia's side: the yard.

The other side: the lane.

And every day, at walk time,

the gold dog stops there.

They meet at the slats.

"Fuff!" says Olivia.

Wag, says the tail.

His nose pokes through. Sniff.

Her hand stays flat. Gentle.

The fence keeps everyone comfy.

Close enough to be friends.

Spaced enough to be safe.

Good fence. Good dog. Good girl.

Meeting adjourned.

Same time tomorrow.

The fence will be here.

Fences always are.
