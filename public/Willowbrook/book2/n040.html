Night 40

The Gold Dog's Patrol

Month 2: Animal Friends

Every evening, the gold dog walks.

Down the lane. Past every gate.

It is his patrol.

He checks on everything.

The hedge: fine.

The mailboxes: fine.

And at Olivia's fence, he stops.

"Fuff!" says Olivia.

Wag, says his tail. Slow and kind.

That is dog for hello, small friend.

He sniffs her hand. Gentle.

She waves with the other one.

Patrol complete.

On he walks, into the dusk.

The lane is safe another night.

Good dog. Best dog.
