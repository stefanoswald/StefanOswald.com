Night 129

Arms Are for Hugging

Month 5: All About Me

Arms can do many things.

They reach. They carry.

They wave bye-bye.

They fly like airplanes. Whoosh!

But arms have one best job.

Do you know it?

Hugging.

Wrap them around Papa's neck.

Squeeze!

That is a hug.

It says I love you

without one single word.

Arms were built for it.

Big arms hug little people.

Little arms hug big people.

It works every direction.

It works every time.
