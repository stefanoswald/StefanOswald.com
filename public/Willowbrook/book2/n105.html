Night 105

Grey Day

Month 4: A World of Colors

Some days are grey.

The sky: grey.

The rain: grey.

The puddles: grey mirrors.

Grey days are soft days.

Nothing shouts.

Nothing hurries.

Olivia and Mama sit at the window.

Blanket. Tea. Toast.

Watching the grey go by.

"Cozy," says Mama.

That is grey's secret.

It makes the inside warmer.

Bright days are for going.

Grey days are for staying.

Both days are good days.

Stay, says the grey. So they do.
