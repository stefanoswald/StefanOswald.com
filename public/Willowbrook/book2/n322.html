Night 322

The Shape Hunt in the Snow

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Fresh snow!

A whole blank yard!

Perfect for... shape stamping!

Boot print: little rectangles!

Stamp, stamp, stamp!

Mitten print: mitten-shape!

(Its own shape. Rare. Collectible.)

Now the big stick.

Drag it... around... around...

A giant CIRCLE!

Biggest circle in Willowbrook!

Stand in the middle. Queen of it!

Three steps this way, that way,

back, a TRIANGLE path!

The yard is a drawing now.

The snow took every shape

and kept them.

Till the next snow.

Then: blank page again.

Winter's own puzzle,

resetting nightly, free.
