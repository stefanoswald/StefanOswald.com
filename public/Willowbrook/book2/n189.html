Night 189

The Climbing Tree's Low Branch

Month 7: Outside!

The big oak has many branches.

High ones for birds.

Higher ones for squirrels.

And one low one...

just right for a first sit.

Papa lifts her up.

Onto the branch!

Olivia is IN the tree!

Sitting in its old wooden hand.

The leaves whisper around her.

The bark is rough under her legs.

Birds sat here. Squirrels ran here.

Now: Olivia.

The tree doesn't mind the weight.

She is light as a leaf to it.

"Someday you'll climb me,"

creaks the oak.

Someday. For now: sitting.

Sitting in a tree is plenty.
