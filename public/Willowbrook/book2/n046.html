Night 46

The Ladybug's Red Coat

Month 2: Animal Friends

Something lands on Olivia's hand.

Something tiny.

Something red!

A ladybug.

She wears a little red coat.

The coat has black dots.

She walks across Olivia's palm.

Tickle, tickle, tickle.

Olivia holds very, very still.

Ladybugs like still hands.

Then, the coat opens!

Wings!

Off she flies.

"Bye!" says Olivia.

Fly home, little red coat.

Come back anytime.

Still hands will be waiting.
