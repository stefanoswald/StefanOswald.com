Night 66

Papa's Big Sneeze

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Papa's nose is tickly.

His eyes go squinty.

His breath goes in, in, in...

"Ah... ah... AH..."

Wait for it...

"CHOO!"

The biggest sneeze in Willowbrook!

Olivia jumps!

Then Olivia laughs.

"Choo!" she says.

"Bless you," says Papa.

"Choo! Choo! Choo!"

Now she is a sneeze train.

Papa laughs till his glasses slip.

Sneezes are only scary for a second.

Then they are the funniest thing

a nose ever said.
