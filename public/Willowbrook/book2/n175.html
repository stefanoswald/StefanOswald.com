Night 175

The Smell of Dinner Coming

Month 6: Yum

Olivia is playing. Busy, busy.

Blocks stacking. Tower rising.

Then... sniff.

Sniff, sniff.

What is that?

Something warm.

Something oniony. Something good.

It floats down the hall.

It curls around the tower.

Dinner!

Dinner is talking to her nose!

The nose tells the tummy.

The tummy says grrrl.

The feet start moving.

Block down, kitchen-bound.

No dinner bell needed.

The smell IS the bell.

Oldest bell in the world.

Still rings perfectly.
