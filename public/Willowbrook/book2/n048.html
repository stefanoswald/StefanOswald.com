Night 48

Bella's Visit

Month 2: Animal Friends

Someone is visiting the garden.

Someone with wings.

Bella!

Her wings have all the colors.

They open... and close.

Slow as breathing.

Olivia goes still.

Butterfly-still.

Bella lands on the fence post.

Right there. So close.

Hello, Bella.

She fans her rainbow. Once. Twice.

Then up she floats, like a petal

that decided to fly.

"Bye-bye!"

She comes back every spring.

She always comes back.
