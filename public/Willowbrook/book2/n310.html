Night 310

Rolling Things

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Big experiment today!

What rolls?

The ball: rolls! Of course.

Champion roller.

The orange: rolls! Wobbly-rolls.

The cup, tipped over: rolls

in a circle! Round and round itself!

The block:

...

The block does not roll.

The block sits.

Push it, flop. Sits again.

Why?

Corners!

Corners stop the rolling.

Round things roll.

Cornered things sit.

The wheel figured this out

a long, long time ago.

That is why wheels are circles

and houses are squares.

Rolling for going.

Corners for staying.

The floor knows all of it.
