Night 244

One Is Olivia

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

The first number is one.

One is easy.

One is the finger trick!

Hold it up. There.

One!

One Olivia. (The main one.)

One nose on her face.

One bear in the crib.

One sun in the sky.

One red cup.

One Mama. One Papa.

Some things come in ones.

The important things, mostly.

"How old are you?"

The finger! "One!"

Well, nearly two now.

But one is still the answer.

One is hers.

She earned it at a birthday.

She'll hold it while she can.
