Night 152

The Banana Opens

Month 6: Yum

The banana wears a coat.

A yellow one.

To eat the banana,

the coat must come off.

Mama starts it. Crack!

Now Olivia pulls.

One strip... two strips...

Down they fold, like petals.

And inside: the banana!

Pale and sweet and ready.

It was wrapped up the whole time.

A present from the tree.

"Nana," says Olivia. Reverently.

Bite. Mmm.

Thank you, tree.

Thank you, coat.

Best unwrapping of the day.
