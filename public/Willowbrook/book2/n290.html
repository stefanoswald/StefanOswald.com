Night 290

The Opposite Game

Month 10: Opposites

Dinner game tonight!

The opposite game.

Papa says: "BIG voice!"

"HELLO!" everyone booms.

Mama says: "little voice..."

"hello," everyone whispers.

Fast bites! Nibble-nibble-nibble!

Slooow bites. Chewwww.

Hands UP! Hands down.

Eyes open! Eyes... closed.

(Peek. Everyone peeked.)

Happy face! Grumpy face!

(The grumpy faces collapse

into giggles. Every time.)

The world comes in pairs,

and the pairs make a game,

and the game makes dinner

last twice as long,

which, opposite alert...

is both slow AND wonderful.

One more round.

Then: bath. (Wet!)

Then: bed. (Cozy!)
