Night 209

Mud Pies

Month 7: Outside!

The garden corner has mud.

Good mud. Thick mud.

And Olivia has an old tin.

You know what that means.

Mud pies!

Scoop the mud. Pat it in.

Pat, pat, pat.

Smooth the top.

Now decorate:

One leaf, in the middle.

Three pebbles, around.

Beautiful!

A pie for looking,

not for eating.

(Very important rule.)

"Who's it for?"

For Papa.

He will admire it loudly.

He always does.

Mud chefs need good customers.

He is the best one.
