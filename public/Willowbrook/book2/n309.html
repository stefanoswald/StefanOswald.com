Night 309

The Heart on the Bear's Foot

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

One more shape lives

in this very crib.

Look at Bobby Bear's foot.

The left one.

A patch. A special patch.

Two bumps on top,

a point below.

A heart!

Trace it. Bump, bump, point.

Hearts are the love shape.

Nobody knows who decided.

Everybody agrees.

This one is stitched on strong.

(Mama fixed it once, at night.

The bear remembers.

Bears remember everything.)

A heart on a foot means:

this bear walks with love.

Everywhere. Always.

Even in dreams.

Pat the heart. Goodnight, heart.

Keep walking, bear.
