Night 274

Big and Little

Month 10: Opposites

Papa's boot: BIG.

Olivia's boot: little.

Stand them together.

Ha!

The little boot could live

inside the big boot!

(It gets tested. It can.)

Big spoon, little spoon.

Big chair, little chair.

Big hand...

little hand, inside it.

That one is the best pair.

Big keeps little safe.

Little keeps big soft.

They need each other,

big things and little things.

That is why they come in sets.

Like boots.

Like Papas and Olivias.
