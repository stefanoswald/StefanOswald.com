Night 365

Goodnight, Willowbrook

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

The last night of the book.

Say it with us. All of it.

Goodnight, farm.

Goodnight, hens in a row.

(Goodnight, Duchess. Yes, you twice.)

Goodnight, Ellie by the willows,

balloon tied to your branch.

Goodnight, brook. Sing on.

Goodnight, Freddy. (Softly!)

("GOODNIGHT!" Close enough.)

Goodnight, Tessa. Take your time.

Goodnight, bakery. Rest, ovens.

Goodnight, library. Sleep, stories.

Goodnight, Molly, in your wall.

Goodnight, gold dog. Good dog.

Goodnight, lane. Goodnight, porch lights.

Goodnight, willow on your hill,

keeping all our words in your rings.

Goodnight, Oliver.

("Whoo?" You, dear. Sleep.)

Goodnight, Moon. Hum on.

("Mmm.")

Goodnight, Stella.

Twinkle once for yes.

...

She did.

And goodnight, most of all...

to one girl, two years old,

asleep with a bear in her arms

in the middle of everything,

loved by every name on this list.

The book closes.

The town stays.

The love stays.

See you in the next book,

little one.

Goodnight, Olivia.

Goodnight, Willowbrook.
