Night 212

The Night Outside

Month 7: Outside!

Where does the outside go at night?

Nowhere!

It just changes shifts.

The fireflies take the lawns.

Blink... blink...

The owl takes the sky.

Whoo. All well.

The brook keeps singing.

It never once stops.

The flowers close. The moths open.

The dew starts its quiet work

on every blade of grass.

And over all of it: Stella.

Gold and faithful.

"The outside is fine," she reports.

"It's always fine. It's just dark."

"Mmm," hums the Moon.

Sleep, little walker.

Your whole outside

will be there in the morning,

washed and new.

Goodnight, Willowbrook.
