Night 15

The Blanket Mountain

Month 1: My World

The laundry is done.

Warm from the dryer.

Papa dumps it on the couch.

A mountain!

A warm, soft mountain!

In goes Olivia.

Burrow, burrow, burrow.

Where is Olivia?

Only one foot shows.

"Where did she go?" says Papa.

The mountain giggles.

Mountains don't giggle!

Up pops Olivia. "Boo!"

Found. Folded. Both.

Warmest game in the house.
