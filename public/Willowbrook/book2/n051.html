Night 51

The Pig Who Loved Mud

Month 2: Animal Friends

The pig has a puddle.

A mud puddle.

And the pig is IN it.

Roll! Squelch! Flop!

Mud on his back.

Mud on his belly.

Mud on his curly tail.

Olivia laughs and laughs.

"He likes it," says Grandpa Joe.

"Mud keeps a pig comfy."

The pig grunts. Happy, happy.

Some like baths with water.

Some like baths with mud.

Everybody likes being comfy.

That part is the same.

Oink, says the pig. Exactly.
