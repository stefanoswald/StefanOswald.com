Night 319

The Kite Above the Hill

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Windy day! Kite day!

The big kids are on the hill.

And up above them...

look!

A diamond!

A flying diamond!

Red, with a long dancing tail.

It swoops! It climbs!

It wiggles like it's laughing!

The diamond is a square

that stood on its corner

and decided to fly.

Someday, Olivia will fly one.

Hold the string. Feel the tug.

(The tug is the kite saying

"higher, please!")

For now: watching.

Head all the way back.

Pointing at the dancing shape.

"Up! Up!"

Up indeed.

The best shapes don't stay

on the paper.
