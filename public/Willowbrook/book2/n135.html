Night 135

The Boo-Boo and the Kiss

Month 5: All About Me

Sometimes the body gets a boo-boo.

A bump. An ouch.

Today: one knee. Small bump.

"Owie," says Olivia.

She holds up the knee.

There is a fix for this.

An old fix. The oldest.

Mama kisses the boo-boo.

Mwah.

There.

"All better?"

Olivia checks the knee.

It IS better!

Kisses fix knees.

Nobody knows how.

They just do.

They always, always have.

Off she runs. Fixed.
