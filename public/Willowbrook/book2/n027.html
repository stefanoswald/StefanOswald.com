Night 27

The Big Bed Visit

Month 1: My World

Saturday morning!

Olivia visits the big bed.

It is so big.

It is so soft.

Pillows everywhere!

She sits in the middle.

Mama on one side.

Papa on the other.

This is the whole world,

right here, in one bed.

Tickles happen. Giggles happen.

Then everyone flops down.

Quiet. Cozy. Together.

Saturday knows how to start.
