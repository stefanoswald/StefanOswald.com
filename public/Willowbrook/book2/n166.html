Night 166

Grandpa's Garden Carrot

Month 6: Yum

In Grandpa's garden, carrots hide.

Underground!

Only their hair shows.

Green feathery hair.

"Grab hold," says Grandpa.

Small hands on big hands.

Pull...

Pull!

POP!

A carrot! Orange as autumn!

It was down there growing

the whole summer long.

Wash it at the pump.

Crunch.

Sweet as anything.

"Dirt makes 'em sweet,"

says Grandpa.

He has said that for fifty years.

He is right, too.
