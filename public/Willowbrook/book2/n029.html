Night 29

Everything in Its Place

Month 1: My World

The day is done.

Time to check the house.

Where is the red cup? By the sink.

Where are the shoes? In their line.

Where are the toys? In the box.

Where is the blanket? In the crib.

Where is Bobby Bear? In the crib!

Everything has a place.

Everything is in it.

The house is all tucked in.

Only one more thing to tuck.

Guess who?

Up we go.

The place for Olivia is bed.
