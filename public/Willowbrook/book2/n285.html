Night 285

In and Out

Month 10: Opposites

A box on the floor.

You know what happens.

IN!

Climb in. Sit. Home!

The box holds one Olivia. Snug.

"In!"

And now...

OUT!

Climb out. Free!

The whole room again!

"Out!"

In... out... in... out...

Each direction is a whole event.

In: cozy, hidden, mine.

Out: big, bright, everywhere.

The best games are doors.

In and out of boxes.

In and out of forts.

In and out of the bath, the boots,

the day itself.

In, out, in, out.

Both sides good.

The climbing is the game.
