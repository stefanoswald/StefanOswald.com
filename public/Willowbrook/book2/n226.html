Night 226

The Fish Who Breathe Water

Month 8: Splash

At the pond's edge, look down.

Look past the shine.

There!

Fish!

Little quick ones.

Flick, there one goes!

They live IN the water.

All day. All night.

They don't come up for air.

Water IS their air.

Imagine that!

Their whole world is a bath

that never drains.

Flick, flick, they scatter.

Hover, they gather back.

"Fsss," says Olivia. New word try.

Fish. Nearly.

They can't hear it, down there.

Wave anyway.

Neighbors are neighbors,

wet or dry.
