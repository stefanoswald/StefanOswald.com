Night 172

Hot and Cold Lunch

Month 6: Yum

Lunch has two temperatures today.

The soup: warm.

Whoo, whoo, blow first.

Careful sips. Cozy inside.

The apple slices: cold!

Straight from the fridge.

Crunch, brr! sweet!

Warm, then cold.

Cold, then warm.

The mouth is amazed every time.

"Hot," says Olivia at the soup.

Warm, really. But close.

"Co!" at the apple.

Cold. Yes!

Two words. Two feelings.

One lunch teaching both.

Eat your opposites, little one.

They make meals interesting.
