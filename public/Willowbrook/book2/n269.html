Night 269

Counting Backward to Sleep

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

The day counts down now.

Backward. Softly.

FIVE kisses.

Mwah, mwah, mwah, mwah, mwah.

FOUR pats for the bear.

Pat, pat, pat, pat.

THREE tucks for the blanket.

Tuck... tuck... tuck.

TWO whispers:

"Night-night."

"Love you."

And ONE...

click.

The lamp.

Zero is the dark.

But zero isn't empty.

Zero is full of sleep,

and the night light's glow,

and tomorrow, loading quietly.

Five, four, three, two, one...

The gentlest countdown there is.

No blastoff.

Just landing.

Goodnight.
