Night 64

Shhh, Says the Library

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

The library has a sound too.

It is the quietest one.

"Shhh."

Olivia knows it!

One finger on the lips.

Shhh.

It is not a mean sound.

It is a cozy one.

It means: the books are dreaming.

Walk soft. Talk small.

Olivia whispers: "Buh."

That is library-sized talking.

Perfect.

Even Molly Mouse, in the wall,

holds her whiskers still.

Shhh, says everyone together.

The books sleep on.
