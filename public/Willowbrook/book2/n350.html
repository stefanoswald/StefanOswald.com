Night 350

The Morning Is Loading

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

While Olivia sleeps,

tomorrow gets ready.

It's being built right now!

Behind the hills,

the sun is warming up.

(Stretches. Light exercises.)

The birds are rehearsing.

(Quietly. Beaks closed.

The dawn chorus takes practice.)

The dew crew is out,

placing one drop

on every blade of grass.

(Big job. They never miss one.)

The bakery's ovens

are dreaming of dough.

Tomorrow's puddles, plotting.

Tomorrow's games, stretching.

Tomorrow's words...

maybe a brand-new one!...

lining up on the tongue.

Everything getting ready.

All for one girl

who will wake up and say:

"Hi!"

Sleep on. Let them work.

Morning is almost loaded.
