Night 247

Counting Stairs

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

The stairs love to be counted.

Every trip up: count!

"One!" Step.

"Two!" Step.

"Three!" Step.

"Fo!" Step.

(Four is new. Wobbly. Fine.)

"Fi!... Free!... Two!..."

(The numbers go on adventures

after three. That's allowed.)

The counting isn't for getting it right.

It's for the rhythm.

Step-count, step-count.

Twelve steps to the top.

Papa counts the true numbers.

Olivia counts her wild ones.

Both counts arrive together.

Top of the stairs!

However you count it:

made it.
