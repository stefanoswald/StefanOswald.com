Night 203

The Evening Walk Ritual

Month 7: Outside!

After dinner, before bath:

the walk.

Every good evening has one.

Olivia leads. Her pace.

(Slow. Wonderfully slow.)

Down the lane as the light goes gold.

Hello, fence. Hello, cat.

Hello, flowers doing their evening smell.

The porch lights come on

one... by one... by one.

Like the lane is saying

goodnight, goodnight, goodnight.

Turn around at the corner mailbox.

Pat it. Tradition.

Home as the gold goes blue.

The bath is running.

The day is folding.

The walk tucked the whole town in.

Now the town returns the favor.
