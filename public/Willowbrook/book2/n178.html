Night 178

The Garden on the Plate

Month 6: Yum

Look at tonight's plate.

Everything on it has a story.

The tomato: from the garden!

Olivia watered it herself.

The carrot: from Grandpa's rows.

She helped pull it. POP!

The beans: from the market.

She picked the bag.

This is not just dinner.

This is the whole summer,

arranged on a plate.

"Mato," says Olivia, pointing.

Yours, says Mama. You grew it.

Growers eat their growing.

It tastes better that way.

It really does.

Ask any gardener.

Ask this one.
