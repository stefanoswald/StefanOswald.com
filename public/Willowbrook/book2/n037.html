Night 37

Molly Mouse and the Big Book

Month 2: Animal Friends

Molly Mouse lives in the library.

Inside the wall. Behind the shelf.

At night, the library is hers.

Tonight she found a big book.

Bigger than Molly!

Push... push... creak!

The book fell open.

Inside: a picture of the sea.

Molly has never seen the sea.

Now she has.

That is what books do.

They bring the sea to mice.

"More," squeaked Molly.

She turned the page with both paws.

Mice read with their whole bodies.

So do little girls.
