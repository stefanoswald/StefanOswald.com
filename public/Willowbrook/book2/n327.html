Night 327

Star Light, Star Bright

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

First star tonight, there!

The gold one. Of course.

Time for the old words.

Mama knows them.

Her mama knew them too.

"Star light, star bright,

first star I see tonight..."

Olivia watches the star.

The star seems to listen.

(She does listen. We know her.)

"...wish I may, wish I might,

have the wish I wish tonight."

Now: the wish.

Close eyes. Squeeze.

What did Olivia wish?

She won't say.

(Wishes work best quiet.)

But she looked at her whole room...

bear, blanket, Mama...

before she closed her eyes.

Some wishes are just

"this, again, tomorrow."

Granted, twinkles the star.

Granted nightly.
