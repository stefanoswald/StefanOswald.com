Night 272

The Counting Walk

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Tonight's walk is a counting walk.

Count everything!

Porches with lights on:

one, two, three!

Dogs: one (gold, waving)...

two! (the little new one, yapping).

Cats: one. (On her wall. Obviously.)

Pumpkins on steps:

one, two, three, fo!

Fire hydrant: one. Red.

Mailboxes: lots. Too many. Skip.

Stars coming out: one... two...

(We know how that count ends.)

The lane is full of numbers

wearing their everyday clothes.

Counting is just noticing,

one thing at a time.

The walk home counts itself:

one girl, one Papa,

one lane, home.
