Night 183

The Walk to the Corner

Month 7: Outside!

Today's adventure: the corner.

It is one block away.

One block is very far

when you stop for everything.

Stop! A pebble. Inspect it.

Stop! A fence. Pat each slat.

Stop! A crack in the sidewalk.

Step over it. Carefully. Twice.

Stop! A gate. Does it creak? It does.

Papa doesn't hurry.

Corners don't run away.

Finally: the corner!

Touch the corner mailbox. Made it!

Now, the journey home.

Same block. All new.

The best walks are slow ones.

The slowest ones are best of all.
