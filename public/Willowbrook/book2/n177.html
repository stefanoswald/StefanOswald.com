Night 177

Popcorn Is for Watching

Month 6: Yum

In the pot: little gold kernels.

Hard as beads.

On goes the lid. Glass lid.

On goes the warm.

Wait for it...

POP!

One jumped!

POP-POP! POP!

Fireworks in a pot!

The kernels turn into clouds!

Olivia watches from Papa's arms.

Pop-pop-pop-pop!

Then... quiet. Show's over.

The popcorn is for big teeth.

Not for Olivia's club. Not yet.

That's okay.

She got the show.

The show is the best part.

(Papa says the eating is.

They agree to differ.)
