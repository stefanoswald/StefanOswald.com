Night 231

Where Rain Comes From

Month 8: Splash

See that cloud?

The big grey one, over the hill?

It is full of water.

Full up. Heavy.

Like the watering can

when it's too full to carry.

And when a cloud gets too full...

Drip.

Feel that?

Drop!

Here it comes!

The cloud is watering the town.

The grass. The gardens. The brook.

Everybody's rows, all at once.

The sky does the whole round

in one grey visit.

Run for the porch!

Watch it pour.

Thank you, cloud.

The fern especially says thanks.
