Night 249

Two Hands Full

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

A math problem!

Olivia has two hands.

Block one: left hand. Got it.

Block two: right hand. Got it.

Now Papa offers...

block three.

Hmm.

Two hands. Three blocks.

The oldest problem there is.

Think... think...

She tucks block one

under her arm!

Takes block three!

THREE blocks! Two hands!

"Clever girl!"

The elbow is the secret pocket.

Every toddler finds it.

Math isn't numbers first.

It's problems first.

She solved hers.

The tower will be tall tonight.
