Night 170

The Kitchen Says Thank You

Month 6: Yum

The eating day is done.

Three meals. One snack.

Now the kitchen gets tucked in.

Dishes: washed. Drip, drip, dry.

The little cup: last one up.

Olivia hands it to Mama.

"Ta," says Mama.

The high chair: wiped.

The floor: swept of crumbs.

(The birds get those. Tomorrow.)

The fridge hums its night song.

The kettle rests.

"Thank you, kitchen," says Mama.

It fed everyone today.

It will do it again tomorrow.

Kitchens never take a day off.

They love the work.

Goodnight, good room.
