Night 184

Grass Under Bare Feet

Month 7: Outside!

Boots off.

Socks off.

Mama nods: go ahead.

One bare foot on the grass...

Ooh!

Cool! Tickly! Alive!

Ten thousand little green fingers

holding up one small foot.

The toes curl in.

The grass springs back.

Step, step, step.

Every step tickles.

Barefoot walking is slow walking.

The feet want to feel it all.

All summer, the grass waits

for exactly this.

Little feet. Big green welcome.

Both very glad.
