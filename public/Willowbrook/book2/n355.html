Night 355

The Birthday List

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

The birthday is coming close!

Time to plan.

Mama has paper. Papa has coffee.

"What do we need?"

Olivia knows this!

"CAKE!"

(Written down. Obviously. Rosa's.)

"WIWI!"

(Lily. First guest. Written.)

"BOON!"

(Balloons. Red ones. Written.)

"Duck?"

(...The pond ducks are invited

in spirit. Written, sort of.)

Grandpa. The aunts. The colleagues.

The whole little list...

it's the same beloved names

as last year,

plus one new small friend

from the bench.

Small party. Big love.

That's the family recipe.

"Anything else, birthday girl?"

She thinks hard.

"More."

More indeed.

More of everything good.

That's what birthdays are.
