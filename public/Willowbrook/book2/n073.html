Night 73

The Yawn That Went Around

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Papa yawned first.

A big slow one. Ahhhh-hum.

Then Mama caught it.

Ahhh-hum.

Yawns are catchy!

Then Olivia caught it.

Ah-hum!

A little one. Her size.

Who is left?

Bobby Bear.

Did his mouth open, just a bit?

It did. It definitely did.

The whole family, yawning.

That is bedtime's doorbell.

Ahh-hum. Ding-dong.

Everyone in. Everyone down.

The yawn did its job.
