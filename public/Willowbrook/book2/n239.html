Night 239

Brushing Teeth Is Water Play Too

Month 8: Splash

Last stop before stories:

the teeth!

The little brush. The tiny dab.

Brush, brush, brush.

Front ones. Side ones.

The new back ones, important!

Papa has a turn too.

"My turn, your turn."

That's the deal.

Now the cup. A little water.

Swish...

and the big finish:

the SPIT.

Ptooey!

(Mostly dribble. Excellent effort.)

Rinse the brush. Tap, tap.

Into its cup it goes,

standing up like a tiny soldier.

Clean club. Shiny members.

See you in the morning, teeth.
