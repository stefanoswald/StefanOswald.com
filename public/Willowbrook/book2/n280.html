Night 280

Wet and Dry

Month 10: Opposites

Bath time: WET.

Wet hair. Wet hands.

Wet duck. (Always wet. His life.)

Water everywhere it should be

and some places it shouldn't.

Then: the towel.

Rub, rub, wrap, snuggle...

DRY.

Dry toes. Fluffy hair.

Warm and toasty dry.

Wet is fun.

Dry is comfy.

The bath gives you both,

in the right order.

(Dry then wet also happens.

It's called rain.

Also fine. Bring boots.)

Wet washes the day off.

Dry tucks you in.

Splash, rub, snuggle, sleep.
