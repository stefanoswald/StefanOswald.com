Night 336

The Pajama Parade

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

Presenting: the pajama parade!

Tonight's uniform: the star ones.

(There are also duck ones.

And stripe ones. Big decisions.)

Leg one, in!

Leg two, in!

Arms up, through the tunnel...

head pops out, hello!

And the grand finale:

the zipper.

Her job now.

Zzzzzip! To the chin!

Uniform: complete.

Inspection: passed.

Pajamas mean the day is off duty.

No more boots. No more busy.

Just soft, and warm,

and ready for the pillow country.

March, little soldier of sleep.

Your bunk awaits.

The parade route ends

at the coziest place in town.
