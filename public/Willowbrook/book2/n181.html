Night 181

The Moon's Midnight Snack

Month 6: Yum

Does the Moon get hungry?

Look up. Watch a while.

A little cloud drifts by.

The Moon leans close...

and the cloud gets thinner.

And thinner!

Nibble, nibble.

Is the Moon eating clouds?!

"Mmm," hums the Moon.

That is not an answer.

That is a full mouth, maybe.

Stella giggles gold.

Below, the town sleeps.

Tummies full. Kitchens clean.

Dreams of pancakes rising slowly,

like batter, like the sun.

Sleep, little eaters.

Breakfast is coming.

It always comes.

Goodnight, Willowbrook.
