Night 326

The Shape of the Day

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Here is a secret:

the day has a shape too.

Guess which one.

It starts with waking. Gold.

Rolls to breakfast...

around to playing...

down to napping...

up again to afternoon...

over to dinner, to bath,

to this exact story,

to sleep. Dark.

And then?

Morning again!

Right where it started!

The day is a CIRCLE.

It rolls around,

smooth, no corners,

and comes home every night

to this crib, this bear,

this kiss.

Round and round.

You never fall off.

Tomorrow the circle

rolls again. Ride it well.

Goodnight, round day.
