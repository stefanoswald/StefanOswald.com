Night 74

The Song with the Wrong Notes

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Every night, Papa sings the song.

The same song.

With the same wrong notes.

He learned it wrong long ago.

He sings it wrong on purpose now.

The wrong notes are the best part.

They are ours.

Nobody else sings it this way.

Nobody else ever could.

Olivia hums underneath.

Hmm-hmm-hmm.

She learned it wrong too.

Perfectly wrong.

Two wrongs, one song.

Completely right.

Goodnight, sing the wrong notes.

Goodnight.
