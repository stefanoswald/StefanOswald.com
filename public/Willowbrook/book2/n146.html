Night 146

The Runny Nose Day

Month 5: All About Me

Today the nose is runny.

Sniff. Sniff.

A little cold came to visit.

Colds do that.

The nose runs. The tissue chases.

Wipe... "No!"

This tissue or that tissue?

That one. Okay. Wipe.

Better.

Runny nose days are couch days.

Blanket. Books. Cuddles.

The body is busy inside,

shooing the cold away.

It knows exactly how.

It just needs rest and water

and somebody warm to lean on.

That last one is the medicine

that works best.
