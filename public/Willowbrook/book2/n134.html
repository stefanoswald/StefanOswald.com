Night 134

The Sleepy Body

Month 5: All About Me

At the end of the day,

the body gets sleepy.

Piece by piece.

The legs go first. So heavy.

They ran so much today.

Then the arms. Slow now.

They hugged and carried and waved.

Then the eyes. Blink... blink...

The curtains want to close.

Last comes the yawn.

Ahh-hum.

That is the body saying:

all done, all done.

Mama carries the sleepy pieces

up to the crib.

The body sleeps all night.

It is building tomorrow's running.
