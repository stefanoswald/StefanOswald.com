Night 215

The Pouring Professor

Month 8: Splash

Two cups tonight.

That means: experiments!

Fill the red cup.

Pour it into the blue cup.

It fits!

Fill the blue. Pour into red.

Also fits! Every time!

Now pour from way up high...

a waterfall! Splashy!

Now from down low...

a quiet little glug.

High is loud. Low is soft.

The professor takes notes.

(The notes are in her head.)

Fill, pour, fill, pour.

Water always agrees to play.

It is the best lab partner.

It never says no.
