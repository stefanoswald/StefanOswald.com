Night 9

The Stairs Are Sleeping

Month 1: My World

These are the stairs.

Up, up, up they go.

In the day, Olivia climbs them.

With Papa. Holding the rail.

One step. Two steps. So many steps!

But now it is night.

The gate is closed.

The stairs are sleeping.

"Nigh-nigh, stairs," says Olivia.

She blows them a kiss.

The stairs keep the kiss.

Tomorrow they will give it back.

Step by step by step.
