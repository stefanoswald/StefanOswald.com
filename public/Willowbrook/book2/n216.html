Night 216

Washing Bobby Bear's Face

Month 8: Splash

(Bobby Bear does not take baths.

Real bears of his kind

prefer to stay dry.

So a bath-bear stands in.

A little rubber one.)

Olivia has a little cloth.

The bath-bear needs washing.

First his face. Gentle circles.

"Eyes closed," she tells him.

That is what Mama says to her.

Behind his ears. Don't forget!

Under his chin.

There. Clean bear.

"All done," she announces.

She pats his head.

Washed, the way she is washed.

Gentle, the way she is gentled.

Everything Mama pours in

comes back out

on somebody smaller.
