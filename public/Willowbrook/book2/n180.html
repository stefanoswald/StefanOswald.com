Night 180

The Thank-You for Food

Month 6: Yum

Before dinner, a little pause.

Just a beat.

"Thank you for the food," says Mama.

Thank you to the garden.

Thank you to the farm.

Thank you, Henrietta, for eggs.

Thank you, Rosa, for bread.

Thank you, rain and sun.

So many helpers

in one bowl of soup!

Olivia pats the table.

Pat, pat.

That is her amen.

It counts. It more than counts.

The food tastes better

after the thank-you.

Try it and see.

Now: eat!
