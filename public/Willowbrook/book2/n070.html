Night 70

The Quietest Sound

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Some sounds are loud.

Some are little.

And one sound is so quiet

you hear it with your eyes.

Snow.

It is falling right now.

Look.

No pit-pat. No drum.

Just... hush.

The whole town goes soft.

The lane. The roofs. The trees.

"Shhh," says the snow.

It is the library of weather.

Olivia watches, nose to glass.

The quietest sound in the world

is filling up the whole world.

Hush. Hush. Goodnight.
