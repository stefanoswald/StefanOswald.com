Night 53

Where the Snail Carries His House

Month 2: Animal Friends

The snail is moving.

Slowly. Slowly.

He leaves a silver road behind him.

And look what he carries:

his house!

Right on his back.

Wherever he goes, he is home.

Rain? Go inside. He is home.

Tired? Curl up. He is home.

Olivia watches him travel.

One fence rail. All morning.

"Slow," says Olivia.

Slow, but never lost.

You cannot be lost

when home comes with you.

Lucky snail.
