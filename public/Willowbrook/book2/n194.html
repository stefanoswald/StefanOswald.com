Night 194

The Swing Goes Up

Month 7: Outside!

Into the bucket swing.

Legs through. Hands on.

Ready?

Push!

Up goes Olivia...

whee!

Back she comes...

hello, Mama's face!

Up again, the trees! The sky!

Back again. Mama!

Sky, Mama, sky, Mama.

The swing shows you both,

over and over.

The tummy does a little flip

at the top.

That flip is the best part.

"More! More!"

More pushes. More flips.

The swing never gets tired.

Neither does the laughing.
