Night 307

The Triangle Tree

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

The pine tree is a triangle!

Wide at the bottom,

point at the top.

Three sides. Three corners.

Make one with your hands:

tip the fingers together...

Triangle!

The roof of the house: triangle.

The mountain far off: triangle.

The sail on the pond boat: triangle!

Triangles are pointing shapes.

They all point UP.

At the sky. At the stars.

"Up," they say. "Look up."

The pine tree says it

all winter long,

wearing snow on its point

like a hat.

Point, point, point.

Message received, tree.

Looking up.
