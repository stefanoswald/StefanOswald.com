Night 251

Counting the Chickens

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Dusk at the farm.

The hens go in to roost.

Count them in with Grandpa!

"One!" in hops a red one.

"Two!" the speckled sister.

"Three, four!" hustling together.

"Fi!" Henrietta, announcing herself.

Bok! Counted, thank you.

And last, taking her time,

pausing at the door for effect:

the Duchess.

"Six," says Grandpa.

"Two!" says Olivia.

The Duchess pauses. Considers.

She likes being counted twice.

Six hens. Or seven. Or two.

All in. All safe.

That is what evening counting is for.

Everybody home. Door shut.

Goodnight, numbers.
