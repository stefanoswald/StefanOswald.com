Night 298

Before and After

Month 10: Opposites

Before the bath:

behold, the garden goblin!

Mud on the knees.

Yogurt on the eyebrow. (Still. How?)

Sand in the hair.

A leaf... attached somewhere.

The whole wonderful day,

worn as a costume.

After the bath:

behold! A brand-new child!

Pink. Fluffy. Pajama'd.

Smelling like soap and sleep.

Same girl.

Different chapter.

Before and after.

The bath is the page-turn.

The day washes off.

The night begins clean.

Both versions are perfect.

One is just

better for the sheets.
