Night 102

Bella's Wings Have Everything

Month 4: A World of Colors

The rainbow went home.

But somebody kept a copy.

Look, on the fence post.

Bella.

Her wings open in the sun.

Red... orange... yellow...

green, blue, purple...

All of it! All at once!

Shimmering like the rainbow

folded up small enough to carry.

"All the colors," whispers Mama.

Bella fans them slowly.

Why choose one,

say the wings,

when you can be the whole sky?

Fly on, little rainbow.

Show everybody.
