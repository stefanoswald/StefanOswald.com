Night 96

Purple

Month 4: A World of Colors

Purple hides in the garden.

You have to find it.

There! The lilac bush.

Purple, purple everywhere,

in little soft bunches.

Olivia puts her nose right in.

Sniff!

Purple smells sweet.

(Not every color has a smell.

Purple got lucky.)

Mama tucks one bloom

behind Olivia's ear.

A purple ear!

"Pretty," says Mama.

The bees agree. Buzz.

Everyone loves purple.

It only visits in spring.

Sniff it while it's here.
