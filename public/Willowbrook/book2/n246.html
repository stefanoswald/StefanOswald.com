Night 246

Three Bears on the Bed

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Tonight we count to three!

We need three things.

Bears! Line them up.

Bobby Bear: one.

(Tap his head.)

The little bath bear: two.

(Tap.)

The... cow?

The cow from the fair.

Is a cow a bear?

Tonight, yes. Honorary.

Three!

One, two, three bears.

Count them again.

One... two... three!

Same answer!

That is the magic of counting.

The number stays.

Three bears at bedtime.

Three heads to pat goodnight.

Pat, pat, pat.

One, two, three, sleep.
