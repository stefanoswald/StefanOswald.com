Night 195

The Slide Decision

Month 7: Outside!

The slide is shiny.

The slide is tall.

(Well. Tall-ish.)

Up at the top: Olivia.

On Mama's lap. Safe seat.

Down at the bottom: the ground,

waiting politely.

Ready?

Deep breath.

Go!

Whooooosh!

The wind! The speed! The swoop!

Bump, landed!

That was...

that was...

"MORE!"

That was more, apparently.

Up the steps. Down the whoosh.

Seven times. Ten times.

The slide's whole job

is turning up into down.

It does it perfectly, every time.
