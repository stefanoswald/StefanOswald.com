Night 93

Blue

Month 4: A World of Colors

Blue is the big color.

Look up.

The whole sky: blue.

All of it! So much blue!

The pond copies the sky.

Blue water, blue sky.

Lily's jacket is blue too.

Sky-blue, Mama calls it.

The sky shared its color with Lily.

That was kind of the sky.

Olivia lies back on the blanket.

Blue, blue, blue, everywhere up.

It never runs out.

The biggest color

for the biggest places.
