Night 311

The Egg's Special Shape

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Henrietta's egg.

Hold it gently. Look close.

What shape is it?

A circle? Almost...

but one end is rounder

and one end is pointier.

It's not a circle.

It's not anything else.

It's an EGG shape!

It has its own name: oval.

"Oh-vul," tries Olivia.

The egg shape is clever.

It rolls, but in a circle!

Round and round, never away.

So it can't roll out of the nest!

Grandpa shows her. Roll...

it curves right back.

"Smart shape," he says.

"Chickens knew it first."

Chickens are underrated.

The egg agrees, quietly.
