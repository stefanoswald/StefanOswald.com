Night 255

Counting Sheep

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Can't sleep?

There's an old trick.

The oldest.

Counting sheep!

Close your eyes.

See the little fence?

Here comes a sheep.

Fluffy. Bouncy.

Hop! Over the fence.

"One sheep."

Here comes another.

Hop!

"Two sheep..."

They keep coming.

Soft as clouds. Slow as clouds.

Hop... three...

Hop... fo...

Hop...

...

The counter is asleep.

The sheep tiptoe off.

Their work is done.

They are very good at their job.

They've been doing it

for a thousand years of bedtimes.

Hop. Shhh. Goodnight.
