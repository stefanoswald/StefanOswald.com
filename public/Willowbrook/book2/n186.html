Night 186

Puddle Rights

Month 7: Outside!

It rained all night.

You know what that means.

Puddles!

And Olivia has boots.

Red ones. Puddle-proof.

The big puddle waits by the gate.

Walk up to it. Look at Mama.

Mama nods.

STOMP!

Water everywhere!

Wings of it! Splashes of it!

Stomp! Stomp! STOMP!

The puddle refills between stomps.

Puddles are generous.

Boots are brave.

Mornings after rain

are the best mornings.

The lane dries. The boots drip.

Worth it. Every stomp.
