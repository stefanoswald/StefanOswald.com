Night 23

Grandpa's Hat

Month 1: My World

Grandpa Joe has a hat.

It is made of straw.

It is very, very big.

Olivia tries it on.

Whoops! Where did Olivia go?

The hat swallowed her!

Only her chin shows.

"Well now," says Grandpa Joe.

He tips the hat back.

There she is!

The hat is big because Grandpa is big.

Someday hats will fit.

For now, they are caves.

Wonderful straw caves.
