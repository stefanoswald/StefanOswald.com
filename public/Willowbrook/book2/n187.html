Night 187

The Wind Plays Tag

Month 7: Outside!

Someone is playing with Olivia.

Someone she can't see!

It lifts her hair. Whoosh!

It tugs her coat.

It rolls a leaf right past her feet...

chase it!

The leaf stops.

Reach down...

whoosh! Away it goes!

The wind is playing tag.

The wind always wins.

You can't catch wind.

But you can laugh at it.

And lean on it. Lean!

It holds you up. A little.

Windy days are wild days.

The whole world is playing.

Whoosh! Tag. You're it.
