Night 265

The Muffin Tin Count

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

The muffin tin has holes.

Six little rooms.

And rooms want tenants!

One rock, in a room.

One block, in a room.

One acorn, room three!

One pom-pom. One shell. One ring.

Every room: full!

Count the tenants:

one, two, three, fo, fi, six!

(Six arrived with help.

Six is far. Six will come.)

Now: dump them out!

CRASH-rattle!

Empty rooms!

Fill them again. Differently.

The tin never minds.

Landlord of the rug,

six rooms, always renting,

rent free forever.
