Night 224

The Puddle That Stayed a Week

Month 8: Splash

After the big rain,

a puddle moved in.

The big pothole by the gate.

Day one: STOMP. Magnificent.

Day two: still there! STOMP.

Day three: a bird was bathing in it.

(They waited. Birds first.)

Day four: smaller. Stompable. STOMP.

Day five: just a splash left.

One last little stomp.

Day six...

gone.

Dried up. Traveled skyward.

"Bye, puddle."

It was a good puddle.

A faithful puddle.

It will come back

with the next big rain.

Puddles always come home

to their own potholes.
