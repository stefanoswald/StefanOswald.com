Night 100

Black

Month 4: A World of Colors

Black is the night color.

Some people fear it.

They shouldn't.

Look what black does:

it holds the stars.

Without black, no twinkle.

Without dark, no little light.

The cat crossing the lane: black.

She loves the night. She owns it.

The sky right now: black velvet.

And on it, look...

Stella. Shining her best.

Stars need the black

like songs need the quiet parts.

Thank you, black.

Goodnight, dark. Good dark.
