Night 284

New and Old

Month 10: Opposites

A gift came! A rabbit!

Brand NEW.

Bright fur. Stiff ears.

Smells like the store.

And here is Bobby Bear.

OLD.

Fur worn soft as velvet.

One ear flopped from love.

Smells like every night ever.

The rabbit is nice.

(Welcome, rabbit. Truly.)

But at sleep time,

whose ear gets held?

The floppy one.

Obviously.

New things are shiny.

Old things are yours.

The rabbit will get there.

Give him a year of nights.

Old is just new

that stayed.
