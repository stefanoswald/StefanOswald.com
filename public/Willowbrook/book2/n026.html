Night 26

The Towel with Ears

Month 1: My World

After the bath comes the towel.

Not just any towel.

This towel has a hood.

And the hood has ears!

Little bear ears.

On it goes.

Warm, warm, warm.

Now Olivia is a bear.

"Grr," says Olivia.

A very small bear.

A very clean bear.

The realest bear waits in the crib.

He likes matching.

Two bears. One bedtime.

Grr. Nigh-nigh.
