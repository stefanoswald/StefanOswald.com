Night 253

The Countdown to Blastoff

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Attention! Rocket ready!

The rocket is... Olivia!

Papa holds her sideways.

Destination: the big bed pillows.

Begin countdown!

"Three!"

(Wiggling. The rocket giggles.)

"Two!"

(The rocket squeals!)

"One!"

Wait for it...

"BLASTOFF!"

Whoooosh!

Across the sky!

Landing: soft! Pillows everywhere!

The rocket survives!

The rocket demands relaunch!

Countdowns work backward.

Three, two, one, go!

It is the most exciting

direction to count.

Every rocket knows it.

Five more launches till bath.
