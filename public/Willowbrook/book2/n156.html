Night 156

Cheese, Please

Month 6: Yum

Snack time.

And today it is the best one.

Cheese!

Little soft cubes.

"Cheese, please," says Mama,

showing the magic words.

"Cheeee," says Olivia.

Close enough. Plate granted.

One cube. Mmm.

Two cubes. Mmm mmm.

Cheese is quiet food.

No crunch. No slurp.

Just soft and good.

The mouse in the library

would agree completely.

Cheese, please.

Always cheese, please.
