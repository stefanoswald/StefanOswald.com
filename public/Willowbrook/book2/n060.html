Night 60

Moo Says the Cow

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

The cow has one word.

It is a good one.

"Moo," says the cow.

"Moo!" says Olivia.

The cow looks up. Interested.

"Moo," she says again. Slower.

"Mooooo," says Olivia. Slower too.

Now they are talking!

What are they saying?

Nobody knows.

But both of them mean it.

That is what matters in talking.

"Moo," says Olivia at dinner.

"Moo," says Olivia in the bath.

Some words are just right.

Moo is one.
