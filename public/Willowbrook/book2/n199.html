Night 199

The Little Garden Row

Month 7: Outside!

This year, the garden grew a row

just for Olivia.

Her own!

A stake marks it.

Three baby lettuces.

One flower, for pretty.

Every morning: inspection.

Are they growing? They are!

A little. Slowly. Surely.

Then: water.

The small can, tipped careful.

Glug, glug, drink up.

"Grow," Olivia tells them.

That is farmer talk.

The lettuces listen.

By summer's end: salad!

From her row. Her salad.

Farmers eat their rows.

Even the small farmers.

Especially them.
