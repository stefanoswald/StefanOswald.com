Night 229

Tears Are Water Too

Month 8: Splash

Today there were tears.

The block tower fell.

The banana broke in half.

(It was a wrong-shaped day.)

Tears came. Big ones.

Down the cheeks. Drip.

Mama caught one with her thumb.

"Tears are water too," she said.

"They wash the inside."

The crying rained a while.

Mama held her through it.

Storms pass. All storms.

Even inside ones.

The tears slowed. Stopped.

Hiccup. Sigh.

All washed.

The banana was still broken.

It tasted the same, though.

Better, almost.

Rain does that to afternoons.
