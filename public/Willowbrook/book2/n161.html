Night 161

The Picnic Lunch

Month 6: Yum

Today, lunch goes outside!

The blanket under the big oak.

The basket opens. Look:

Little sandwiches, cut small.

Apple slices. Cheese cubes.

Berries in a box.

Water in the good bottle.

Eat one bite on Mama's lap.

Eat one bite standing up.

Eat one bite watching ducks.

That is picnic eating.

The rules are softer outside.

A breeze visits the blanket.

An ant inspects a crumb.

Let him have it.

Picnics have plenty.

The oak keeps the shade coming.
