Night 56

Oliver's Question

Month 2: Animal Friends

On the walk to the hill,

someone was waiting.

An owl. A round one.

With tiny glasses!

He sat on a low branch.

He tilted his head.

Olivia tilted hers.

"Whoo?" said the owl.

That is a question.

Owls only ask questions.

"Livy!" said Olivia.

The owl blinked, pleased.

Good answer.

The best answer, really.

"Whoo," he nodded. He knew all along.

Owls just like to hear you say it.
