Night 331

Ellie Is Every Shape

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Sunday. The willow line.

And there, she's out today!

Ellie!

Come for her look at the fence.

And look at HER.

She is a whole shape lesson!

Her feet: big round circles.

Her ears: grand grey triangles,

waving slow.

Her trunk: a curling spiral,

like the snail's shell, unrolled!

Her tail: one line,

with one red circle floating on it.

An elephant is many shapes

stacked kindly together

and taught to walk.

"Ellie!" waves Olivia.

The trunk lifts. Curls. Dips.

That is elephant for

hello, small collection of shapes.

Takes one to know one.

All creatures are geometry

that learned to love.
