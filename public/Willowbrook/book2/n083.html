Night 83

The Snore

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

What is that sound?

Rrrr-shhhh. Rrrr-shhhh.

Is it a bear? No.

Is it the truck? No.

It is... Papa!

Asleep on the couch.

Snoring.

Rrrr-shhhh. Rrrr-shhhh.

Olivia looks very closely.

His mustache-place puffs out. Puff!

She looks at Mama.

Mama's finger: shhh.

They tiptoe away, giggling.

Papas make the funniest sounds

when nobody is listening.

Rrrr-shhhh. Sweet dreams, Papa.
