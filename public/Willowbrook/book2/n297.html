Night 297

Give and Take

Month 10: Opposites

Watch the little table.

Olivia gives Lily a block.

"Ta," says Lily.

Lily gives Olivia a berry.

"Ta," says Olivia.

Give... take... give... take...

The toys flow one way.

The snacks flow the other.

Nobody is counting.

(We counted. It's even.

It's always somehow even.)

Giving feels warm going out.

Taking feels warm coming in.

Two warms. One table.

This is the oldest trade

in the world,

and the only one

where both sides

always win.
