Night 113

The Leaves Change Their Minds

Month 4: A World of Colors

All summer, the leaves wore green.

Every day. Only green.

But look at the big oak now.

The tips are turning!

Gold, here.

Red, there.

The leaves are changing their minds.

"Pretty," says Mama.

Why do they change?

Because the season says so.

Fall is coming.

The trees dress up for it.

Their fanciest colors. Their last ones.

Then they let them all go.

Brave trees.

They know spring keeps green safe

for next time.
