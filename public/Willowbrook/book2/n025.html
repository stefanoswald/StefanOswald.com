Night 25

The Pot Drum

Month 1: My World

Olivia has a cupboard.

Her very own. The low one.

Inside: pots. Spoons. A funnel.

Out comes the big pot.

Out comes the wooden spoon.

Boom! Boom! Boom!

The pot is a drum.

It was a drum all along.

Mama cooks up high.

Olivia drums down low.

Boom, stir. Boom, stir.

They are making dinner together.

One part soup.

Two parts music.
