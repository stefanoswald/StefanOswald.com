Night 283

Heavy and Light

Month 10: Opposites

The watering can, full:

HEAVY.

Two hands! Lean back! Waddle!

So heavy it makes you walk funny.

The feather from the sill:

light.

So light the hand barely knows

it's holding anything.

Puff, it FLIES.

You can't puff the watering can.

(Tested. No.)

Heavy things stay.

Light things go.

Rocks are heavy. Bubbles are light.

Pumpkins: heavy!

Dandelion fluff: light as wishes.

And Olivia?

Heavy enough to stay put in a hug.

Light enough for Papa's shoulders.

Exactly the right weight

for everything that matters.
