Night 150

The Body Says Goodnight

Month 5: All About Me

Time to tuck in the whole Olivia.

Start at the bottom.

Goodnight, toes. Wiggle, wiggle, rest.

Goodnight, strong legs.

So much running today.

Goodnight, belly. Pat, pat.

Thank you for the grrrls.

Goodnight, fingers. All ten.

Curl up, little snails.

Goodnight, ears. Keep the song.

Goodnight, eyes.

One kiss each. Mwah. Mwah.

Curtains closed.

The whole body, tucked in.

Every piece did its best today.

Every piece gets to rest.

Goodnight, you. All of you.
