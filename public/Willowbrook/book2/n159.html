Night 159

The Bread Rosa Bakes

Month 6: Yum

Jingle! The bakery bell.

"There's my girl!"

Rosa is dusted with flour.

She always is.

The ovens are warm behind her.

And here comes the ritual.

The crust end.

Warm. Fresh. Hers.

Rosa saves it every visit.

"For the best customer."

Olivia gums it happily.

Warm bread in a warm bakery

with a warm friend.

That is a whole lunch of good.

"What do we say?"

"Ta!"

Ta indeed.

Jingle, wave, home.
