Night 14

The Kettle Sings

Month 1: My World

In the kitchen lives a kettle.

Mostly it is quiet.

But when the water gets warm...

it sings!

Wheeee-ooooo!

"Hot," says Olivia.

Yes. Hot. We look, we don't touch.

Mama holds her up to listen.

The kettle sings its one song.

It only knows one.

Then Mama makes the tea.

The kettle rests.

Good singing, kettle.

Same song tomorrow, please.
