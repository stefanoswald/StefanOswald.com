Night 43

The Duck Parade

Month 2: Animal Friends

Here they come!

The duck parade!

One mama duck in front.

Five baby ducks behind.

Waddle, waddle, waddle.

In a line. In a row.

Nobody gets lost.

Everybody follows.

"Duck!" says Olivia. "Duck duck duck!"

That is almost all of them.

Into the pond they go.

Plop. Plop. Plop plop plop.

Now it is a swimming parade.

Quack, says the mama.

That means: everyone here? Everyone here.

Mamas always count.
