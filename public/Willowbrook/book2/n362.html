Night 362

Bobby Bear's Birthday Letter

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

Dear Olivia,

Two! You're TWO!

I watched the whole party

from the good chair.

(The sweater is warm.

Tell the aunts a bear says thank you.)

One year ago tonight,

I moved into this crib.

Best move a bear ever made.

Here is my year report:

Nights held: all of them.

Bad dreams caught: several.

(You never even knew. My system works.)

Good dreams let through: hundreds.

Ear-holds survived: beyond counting.

The ear is fine. The ear is honored.

And you...

you talk to me now.

You tell me things.

About ducks. About Lily.

About "maybe."

I understand every word.

Bears do. It's our whole point.

Next year there will be

a big-girl bed, I hear.

Don't worry.

I move with the sleeper.

That's the contract. Permanent.

Happy birthday, my girl.

Two more arms than anyone

could ask for.

B. Bear

(Crib Division. Yours. Always.)
