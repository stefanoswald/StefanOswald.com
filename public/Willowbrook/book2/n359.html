Night 359

The Birthday Morning

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

Eyes open.

Something is different.

The light? The air?

The...

BALLOONS!

Red ones! At the crib!

It's THE DAY!

"Happy birthday, big girl!"

Up and out and downstairs...

the banner! TWO!

Pancakes, with berries...

arranged like a smiley face!

"How old are you today?"

The fingers fly up.

TWO. First try. Nailed it.

"TWO!"

The whole kitchen cheers.

Two years ago, this morning,

she arrived and the world tilted.

Every day since,

it has tilted toward her.

Today the town comes to say so.

But first: pancakes.

Birthdays run on pancakes.

Eat up, two-year-old.

Big day ahead.

Your biggest yet.
