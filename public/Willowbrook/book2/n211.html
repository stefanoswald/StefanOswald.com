Night 211

Bringing the Outside In

Month 7: Outside!

The walk is over.

But look what came home!

One sparkly rock. (Of course.)

One gold leaf.

One small grey feather.

They go on the windowsill.

In a row. Arranged just so.

The outside, sleeping over.

"Night, rock. Night, leaf.

Night, feather."

Through the window behind them:

the real outside.

The lane. The tree. The stars.

It stays out there, working.

Growing things. Blowing things.

Making tomorrow's treasures.

The sill museum will grow.

The outside never runs out.

That is the wonderful thing.

It never, ever runs out.
