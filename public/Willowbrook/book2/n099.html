Night 99

White

Month 4: A World of Colors

In the night, white came.

It covered everything.

The lane: white.

The roofs: white.

The fence posts: little white hats!

White is the quiet color.

It falls without a sound.

Olivia holds up her mitten.

A flake lands. A tiny white star!

Look quick...

gone. Melted.

Another lands. Gone.

White visits. White doesn't stay.

That is its way.

Catch it while it falls.

Hello, white. Hello. Hello.
