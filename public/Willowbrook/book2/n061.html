Night 61

The Rain on the Roof

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Listen.

What is that sound?

Pit. Pat. Pit-pat-pit.

It is the rain!

It is drumming on the roof.

The roof is the rain's drum.

Pit-pat-pit-pat.

The rain never learned songs.

It just plays what it feels.

Olivia listens with her eyes big.

Mama listens too.

The whole room fills with pit-pat.

It is the coziest sound there is...

rain outside, warm inside.

Sleep music.

The rain plays all night. For free.
