Night 218

Rain Is the Sky's Bath

Month 8: Splash

It is pouring out there.

Really pouring!

The lane is getting soaked.

"The sky is giving the world a bath,"

says Papa.

Look, it's true!

The leaves are getting scrubbed.

Drip, drip, shiny.

The pavement is getting rinsed.

The flowers hold up their faces

like Olivia does for the washcloth.

The whole town, in the tub.

Tomorrow it will all be clean.

Washed grass. Washed roofs.

Fresh-smelling everything.

The sky does this for free.

No towels needed.

The sun is the towel.

It comes after. It always comes after.
