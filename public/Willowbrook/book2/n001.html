Night 1

The Softest Blanket in the World

Month 1: My World

Olivia has a blanket.

It is yellow. It is soft.

It is the softest blanket in the world.

Cheek on the blanket. Ahh.

Nose on the blanket. Ahh.

Even Bobby Bear gets a turn.

"Soft," says Olivia.

Soft, soft, soft.

The blanket says nothing.

Blankets never do.

They just hold you, all night long.

Goodnight, blanket.

Goodnight, Olivia.
