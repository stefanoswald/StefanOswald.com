Night 238

The Towel Hug

Month 8: Splash

Out of the bath!

Quick, the cold air is coming!

But faster than the cold:

the towel.

Swoop!

Wrapped! To the chin!

A towel cocoon!

Only the face peeks out.

Pink cheeks. Wet lashes.

Mama squeezes the bundle.

That is the towel hug.

Warmest hug of the day.

The drips get drunk up.

The shivers never land.

Little bear ears on the hood.

"Grr," says the cocoon.

"Grr," agrees Mama.

Carried to the warm room.

Some hugs have corners.

They work just the same.
