Night 237

The Water Everywhere Game

Month 8: Splash

Today's game: find the water!

It hides everywhere.

The tap: water! (Turn it off, love.)

The cup: water!

The kettle: water, resting.

The bath: water, waiting.

The window: rain-water, running down.

The dog bowl by the fence: water!

(The gold dog shares his finds.)

The puddle: water.

The brook: so much water!

The clouds: water, flying!

Even Olivia: mostly water,

says Papa, which is strange

and apparently true.

Water everywhere.

The whole world drinks it.

The whole world IS it, nearly.

Splash, sip, rain, repeat.
