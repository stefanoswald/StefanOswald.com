Night 323

Tracing Papa's Hand

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Papa puts his hand on paper.

Olivia traces it.

(Papa helps the crayon. A little.)

Around the thumb...

up each big finger...

down, around, done!

A Papa-hand shape!

Now, her hand.

INSIDE his traced one.

Trace it! Little thumb,

little fingers, done!

Look:

a little hand,

inside a big hand.

That's not just shapes.

That's a picture of

how things are.

The little one fits inside

the big one's keeping.

For now. For years.

Someday the little hand

will trace an even littler one.

The paper goes on the fridge.

The fridge understands.
