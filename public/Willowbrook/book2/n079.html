Night 79

The Spoon Band

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Tap the cup. Ting!

Tap the bowl. Tong!

Tap the pot. BONG!

Every thing has its own voice.

You just have to tap and ask.

The little glass: ting-ting! High.

The big pot: bong. Low.

Mama taps. Olivia taps.

Ting, tong, bong, ting!

It is a spoon band.

Dinner will be a little late.

Music comes first sometimes.

Ting!

Okay. NOW dinner.

One more.

BONG.
