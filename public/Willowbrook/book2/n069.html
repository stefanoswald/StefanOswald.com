Night 69

The Crunch Walk

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

The leaves are down.

The lane is covered.

And leaves have a secret:

they crunch!

Stomp! Crunch!

Stomp, stomp! Crunch, crunch!

Olivia finds the crispiest ones.

The big brown curly ones.

CRUNCH!

That was a good one.

Papa stomps too. His crunches are huge.

They crunch the whole lane home.

The leaves don't mind.

Crunching is their last job,

and they love doing it.

Crunch. Crunch. Home.
