Night 352

The Almost-Two Girl

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

Something is coming.

A birthday!

The second one!

Practice the answer:

"How old will you be?"

Two fingers... wobble... TWO!

(The trick is mastered. Mostly.)

Look at the door frame.

The marks climb like stairs.

Baby-mark... one-mark...

and today's new mark: higher!

Almost-two is tall!

Almost-two runs and talks

and builds four high

and pours mostly-in

and loves big

and says NO like a queen

and "more" like a poet.

The birthday is coming.

There will be cake.

(Rosa's. Obviously.)

There will be a candle. Two!

The almost is almost over.

The two is almost here.

Grow, girl. Right on time.

You always were.
