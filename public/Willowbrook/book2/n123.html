Night 123

The Strong Legs

Month 5: All About Me

Look at these legs.

They used to just kick.

Kick, kick, in the crib.

Now look!

They walk.

They run, zoom!

They climb the porch step.

Up! All by themselves.

They bounce at music.

They stomp in boots.

They squat down low

to look at bugs.

Strong legs. Busy legs.

At night they get heavy.

So much work today!

Rest, little legs.

Tomorrow: more everything.
