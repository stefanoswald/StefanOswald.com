Night 21

The Coat Hook

Month 1: My World

By the door are three hooks.

High hook: Papa's coat.

Middle hook: Mama's coat.

Low hook: guess whose?

Olivia's!

She stretches up on her toes.

Up, up, uuuup...

Got it!

The coat hangs all by itself.

Well. Almost all by itself.

"I did it," say Olivia's hands, clapping.

Yes you did.

Three coats, three hooks.

Everybody home.

That is what the hooks say.
