Night 127

The Ears That Hear Everything

Month 5: All About Me

On the sides of Olivia: ears.

One here. One there.

What do they do?

Everything!

The bird outside, the ears catch it.

The truck, caught!

Mama's hum from the kitchen, caught.

Ears never close.

Even at night, they listen.

That is why the wrong-note song

can tuck her in:

the ears carry it inside.

Little ears. Big job.

They hear "I love you"

every single day.

And they never, ever drop it.
