Night 318

The Shape of a Hug

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

One shape isn't in any puzzle.

It has no block.

You can't draw it.

But it's the best one.

Watch:

Mama's arms... around Olivia.

Papa's arms... around them both.

Olivia in the very middle,

like the snail's coziest room.

What shape is this?

A circle? Almost.

A heart? Kind of.

It's a hug.

The hug shape.

Round-ish. Warm-ish. Squeeze-ish.

It fits any number of people.

It has no corners to bump.

Nobody falls off it.

The middle is the best seat,

and the little one

always, always gets it.

Geometry's finest work.

Patent: everyone's.
