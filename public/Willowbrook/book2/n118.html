Night 118

Painting the Fence with Water

Month 4: A World of Colors

Papa gave Olivia a brush.

A big fat one.

And a bucket of... water.

Just water?

Just water.

"Paint the fence," says Papa.

Swish! The wood goes dark!

The water paints it!

Every stroke shows.

Swish, swish, swish.

Olivia paints and paints.

Then the sun dries it.

The fence goes back.

A magic canvas! Never full!

She paints it all again.

Best paint in the world:

free, endless, and washes itself.
