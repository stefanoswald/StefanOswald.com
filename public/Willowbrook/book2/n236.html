Night 236

Freddy's Swimming Lesson

Month 8: Splash

"SWIMMING LESSON!" announces Freddy.

Nobody asked.

Freddy teaches anyway.

"WATCH THE LEGS!"

He pushes off...

kick, glide...

kick, glide...

The famous froggy kick!

Smooth as butter on a warm day.

Olivia watches from Papa's arms.

Her own legs start kicking!

Kick, kick, in the air!

"EXCELLENT!" yells Freddy.

"BEST STUDENT I EVER HAD!"

(She is the only student

he ever had.)

Someday: real swimming.

Lessons at the town pool.

For now: air-kicking at the brook,

certified by the loudest

swim coach in Willowbrook.
