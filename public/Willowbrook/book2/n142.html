Night 142

Goosebumps

Month 5: All About Me

The wind blew cool.

And look, the arms did something!

Little bumps!

Tiny ones, all over.

"Goosebumps," says Mama.

The skin makes them

when it feels chilly.

It is trying to fluff up

like a bird fluffs its feathers.

But Olivia has no feathers.

Just the little bumps, trying.

Silly skin. Sweet skin.

Mama rubs the arms warm.

The bumps go home.

Until the next cool wind.

The skin never stops trying.

It takes care of her, always.
