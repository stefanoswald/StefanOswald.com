Night 266

First, Then

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

Some counting isn't numbers.

It's order!

First... then.

First socks. Then shoes.

(Shoes first doesn't work.

It was tested. Thoroughly.)

First dinner. Then story.

First bath. Then jammies.

First jammies... then teeth...

then story... then song...

then bed.

The whole evening is a line

of first-thens,

holding hands,

leading to the crib.

Olivia knows the line by heart.

Her feet walk it on their own.

"Then story?" she checks.

Then story. Always then story.

The order keeps its promises.

That is why order is nice.
