Night 16

The Shoe Line

Month 1: My World

By the door, the shoes wait in a line.

Papa's boots. Big, big.

Mama's shoes. Middle-sized.

And two little red ones.

Whose are those?

Olivia's!

Big, middle, little.

A whole family of shoes.

They wait all night by the door.

Shoes are very patient.

In the morning: feet!

That is what shoes dream about.

Feet, and puddles, and going.

Night-night, shoe family.

Stay in line.
