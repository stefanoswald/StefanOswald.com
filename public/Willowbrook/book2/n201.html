Night 201

The Berry-Stained Smile

Month 7: Outside!

The berry rows again!

This time: blackberries.

Dark and fat and ready.

Papa picks the high ones.

Olivia picks the low ones.

Her bucket fills... slowly.

Very slowly.

Suspiciously slowly.

Papa looks over.

Olivia smiles.

Her smile is PURPLE.

Purple chin. Purple fingers.

"Where are the berries going?"

The purple smile answers.

Half for the bucket, they agreed.

The bucket got... some.

"Berry law," sighs Papa.

He checks his own smile

in his phone. Also purple.

Berry law binds everyone.
