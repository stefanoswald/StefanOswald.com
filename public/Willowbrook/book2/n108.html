Night 108

Mixing at the Easel

Month 4: A World of Colors

Finger paint day!

Yellow on this hand.

Blue on that hand.

Smear the yellow. Smear the blue.

Now smear them TOGETHER...

Wait.

What is that?

GREEN!

Where did green come from?

Yellow and blue made it!

Right there! Under her fingers!

"You made green," says Mama.

Made it! A whole color!

Olivia stares at her hands.

Magic hands.

The bath will be interesting.

The masterpiece is already done.
