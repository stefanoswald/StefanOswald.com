Night 110

The Color Goodnight

Month 4: A World of Colors

Goodnight, red.

Sleep well, little cup.

Goodnight, yellow.

Softest blanket, warmest dreams.

Goodnight, green.

Rest, rug. Rest, garden.

Goodnight, orange pumpkin.

Goodnight, purple lilac smell.

Goodnight, brown.

Extra pats for you, Bobby.

Goodnight, pink cheeks.

Goodnight, white snow, black sky.

The whole rainbow, tucked in.

Every color in its bed.

Now the last one:

goodnight, gold star, way up high.

Twinkle once for yes.

She did. She always does.
