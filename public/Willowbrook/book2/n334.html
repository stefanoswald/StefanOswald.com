Night 334

The Shape of the Month

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

A month of shapes!

Count what the eyes learned:

The circle, sun, plate, day itself.

The square, window, cracker, block.

The triangle, tree, roof, sail.

The oval. Henrietta's clever egg.

The spiral, snail, drain, fern.

The diamond, flying on the hill.

The crescent, the Moon's boat.

The star, on the chair,

in the sky, in the cookie,

five points every time.

And the heart...

stitched on one bear's foot,

shaped into every hug.

The world was made of shapes

all along.

Now she sees them everywhere.

That is what learning is:

the world, coming into focus,

click by click by click.

Sleep, shape-finder.

"Mmm," hums the crescent boat.

Twinkle, says the five points.

Goodnight, Willowbrook.
