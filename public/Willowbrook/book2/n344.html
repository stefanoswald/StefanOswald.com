Night 344

The Middle of the Night

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

Sometimes you wake up

in the middle.

The deep middle. The quiet part.

It's very dark.

It's very still.

Is everything okay?

Check:

The bear? Right here. Warm ear.

The night light? Glowing. Faithful.

The house? Humming its house-hum.

Pipes and beams, murmuring.

Mama and Papa? Down the hall.

Near. Always near.

Everything is exactly

where it was at bedtime.

The middle of the night

is just the night's middle.

Like the middle of the pancake.

Nothing scary. Just more night.

Roll over. Find the cozy spot.

The bear scoots closer.

Sleep comes back for you.

It never went far.

It was just in the hallway,

checking on everyone too.
