Night 232

The Steam on the Mirror

Month 8: Splash

After the bath, look...

the mirror is foggy!

The steam painted it white.

Where did the mirror-girl go?

She's under the fog.

And the fog...

you can DRAW on it!

One finger.

A circle.

Two dots.

A curve underneath.

A face!

Hello, fog-face!

Behind it, the mirror-girl peeks through

the finger-lines.

There she is!

The fog fades slow.

The face fades with it.

Gone till next bath.

The mirror keeps no drawings.

It just loves fresh ones nightly.
