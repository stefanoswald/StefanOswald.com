Night 63

The Brook's Long Song

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

The brook is always singing.

Burble, burble, gurgle, splish.

It never stops to breathe.

What is it singing about?

The stones it knows.

The fish it carries.

The rain it used to be.

Olivia listens from the bridge.

Head tilted. Very serious.

"Wawa," she says.

Yes. Water. Singing water.

The brook has sung this song

since before the town was here.

It will sing it all night.

It knows the whole thing by heart.
