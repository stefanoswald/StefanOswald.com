Night 234

The Fountain in the Square

Month 8: Splash

In the middle of town:

the old fountain.

Stone. Round. Always burbling.

Water leaps up in the middle,

falls back, leaps again.

It never gets tired.

Look in the bottom.

Coins! Shiny ones!

People throw them in with wishes.

Olivia has no coin.

She has... a pebble.

(From the collection. A good one.

The sacrifice is real.)

Toss!

Plip.

Make a wish!

What did she wish?

She points at the fountain.

"More."

More of this. More of everything.

Granted, says the burble.

Granted, granted, granted.
