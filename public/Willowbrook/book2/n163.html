Night 163

The Yogurt Painting

Month 6: Yum

Yogurt. White and cool.

And one red berry, dropped in.

Now watch the spoon work.

Stir, a pink swirl!

Swirl it more. Pink everywhere!

Some yogurt gets eaten.

Some yogurt gets... painted.

On the tray. In swoops.

An eyebrow gets some. How?

Nobody knows. It happens.

"Is it good?" asks Mama.

"Mmm," says the artist.

Breakfast and art.

Two jobs, one bowl.

The washcloth waits calmly.

It has seen everything.

Masterpieces wash off.

The breakfast stays in. Mostly.
