Night 111

Lily's Blue Day

Month 4: A World of Colors

Lily came to play!

Lily loves blue.

Blue jacket. Blue cup. Blue block.

Olivia loves red.

Red bow. Red cup. Red block.

Lily holds out her blue block.

For Olivia!

Olivia holds out her red one.

For Lily!

Trade.

Now Olivia has some blue.

Now Lily has some red.

Best friends do that.

They swap their favorite colors

and both get richer.

Blue and red, side by side.

Just like their strollers, long ago.
