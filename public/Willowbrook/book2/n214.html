Night 214

Esquire the Duck's Whole Career

Month 8: Splash

This is Esquire.

The duck.

He has worked this bath

since Olivia was small.

His duties:

One. Floating. He never sinks.

Push him under, pop! up again.

Every time. Undrownable!

Two. Squeaking. Squeeze, eek!

Three. Watching. He is good at it.

He wears one expression.

It works for everything.

Baths, storms, Tuesdays.

"Duck," says Olivia.

She pats his yellow head.

Esquire bobs.

That is duck for

happy to serve.

Longest career in the house.

No retirement in sight.
