Night 204

The Caterpillar on the Fence

Month 7: Outside!

On the fence rail: a traveler.

Small. Striped. Fuzzy.

A caterpillar!

Inch... scrunch... inch...

That is how caterpillars walk.

Scrunch and stretch. Scrunch and stretch.

Olivia follows the whole journey.

Fence post to fence post.

An epic, at caterpillar speed.

"You know a secret?" says Mama.

"Someday, this little one

will grow wings."

Wings! Like Bella!

Maybe it IS a little Bella.

Maybe they're cousins.

Inch on, little future-flyer.

Eat your leaves.

The sky will wait for you.

It waits for everybody.
