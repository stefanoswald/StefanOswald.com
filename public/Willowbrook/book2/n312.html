Night 312

The Moon Changes Shape

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

Something strange tonight.

The Moon...

is HALF.

Half a moon!

Where did the rest go?!

"She does this," says Papa.

"Watch her all month."

Some nights: a thin smile.

Some nights: half, like now.

Some nights: full and round,

a great silver plate.

Then back again. Slowly.

The Moon tries on shapes

like Olivia tries on hats.

Smile... half... plate... half... smile...

She never loses the rest.

It's just resting in the dark.

It always comes back.

"Mmm," hums the half-Moon.

All of her is always there.

You just see different amounts

of the shine.

Like everybody, really.
