Night 4

Where Is Olivia's Sock?

Month 1: My World

One foot has a sock.

One foot is bare.

Where is the other sock?

Is it in the basket? No.

Is it on the bear? No.

Is it under the couch?

Peek...

There it is!

Socks love to hide.

But Olivia always finds them.

On goes the sock.

Two feet, two socks.

Wiggle, wiggle, wiggle.

All found. All warm.

Good hunting, Olivia.
