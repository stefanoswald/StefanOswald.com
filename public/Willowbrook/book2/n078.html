Night 78

The Giggle

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Of all the sounds in the house,

one is the best.

It is not the kettle.

It is not the bell.

It is this one:

Olivia giggles.

So Papa giggles.

So Olivia giggles MORE.

So Papa laughs for real.

So Olivia falls over laughing.

Nobody remembers what was funny.

It doesn't matter.

The giggle feeds itself.

Round and round it goes.

It is the house's favorite song.

The house would tell you so.
