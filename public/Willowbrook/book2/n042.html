Night 42

The Duchess Says No

Month 2: Animal Friends

The Duchess is a chicken too.

A very fancy one.

"Good morning, Duchess!"

The Duchess turns her back.

Hmph.

That is Duchess for no.

No to mornings.

No to visitors.

No, no, no.

Olivia knows that word!

It is her word too, sometimes.

"No!" says Olivia, giggling.

The Duchess peeks over her shoulder.

Maybe this small person understands her.

Maybe they can be friends.

"Hmph," says the Duchess.

But softer this time.
