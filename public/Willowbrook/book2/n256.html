Night 256

The Counting Caterpillar

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

The caterpillar is back!

On the big leaf.

And he walks in counts!

Watch:

Scrunch, "one!"

Stretch,

Scrunch, "two!"

Stretch...

Scrunch, "three!"

He is a counting machine!

The slowest one ever built.

Perfect for learning.

He never rushes past a number.

Scrunch... four...

Scrunch... fi...

How far will he count?

All the way across the leaf.

However many that is.

Caterpillar math:

one leaf = lunch.

Lunch = a nap.

Naps = wings, eventually.

Count on, little professor.
