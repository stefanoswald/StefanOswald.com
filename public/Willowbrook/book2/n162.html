Night 162

Mixing the Batter

Month 6: Yum

Saturday means pancakes.

Pancakes mean batter.

Batter means, a stirrer!

Up on the step stool.

Both hands on the big spoon.

Stir... stir...

Around and around.

The flour poofs. (Nose: floured.)

The batter goes smooth.

"Good stirring," says Papa.

He pours the circles. They sizzle.

Bubbles come up, flip!

Golden side!

The stirrer eats her pancake proudly.

She made it.

Well. She stirred it.

Stirring is the heart of making.

Everyone knows that.
