Night 287

First and Last

Month 10: Opposites

Some things are first.

The first pancake, hers.

(First pancakes are wonky.

Wonky ones taste best.)

The first star, pointed at.

The first snow, caught.

Some things are last.

The last cracker, split.

The last light, clicked off.

And the last kiss of the day...

also hers.

Always hers.

First and last, both hers.

The middle of the day

is full of everything else...

but the edges,

the gold edges,

belong to the smallest person.

That is how this house

is built.
