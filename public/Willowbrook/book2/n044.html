Night 44

The Cat on the Wall

Month 2: Animal Friends

On the wall sits a cat.

Very still. Very proud.

Her tail is curled like a question.

Olivia stops to look.

The cat looks back.

Blink... blink.

Slow blinks.

That is cat for hello.

Olivia blinks back. Slow.

Cats do not come when called.

Cats are not dogs.

Cats sit in the sun and own it.

"Hi, cat," says Olivia.

Blink, says the cat.

That is enough, from a cat.

That is a lot, actually.
