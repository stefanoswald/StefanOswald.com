Night 157

The Egg from Henrietta

Month 6: Yum

This egg came from the farm.

From Henrietta herself!

She was very loud about it.

Bok! BOK!

Now it is breakfast time.

Crack, into the pan.

Stir, stir, over the warm.

Scrambled soft. Yellow clouds.

Onto the little plate.

Fork time!

Mmm. Warm and soft.

"Thank you, Henrietta,"

says Mama.

"Bok," says Olivia.

That is the right thank-you

in chicken.

Henrietta would be proud.

She is proud anyway. Always.
