Night 291

Dark and Light Make Friends

Month 10: Opposites

People think dark and light

are enemies.

They're not.

Look at the nursery, right now.

The dark is here.

Soft. Quiet. Big.

And the little light is here.

Small. Gold. Steady.

They share the room!

The dark makes the light cozy.

The light makes the dark friendly.

Neither one wins.

Neither one wants to.

Stars need night.

Lamps need evening.

Fireflies need dusk.

Every light you love

has a dark that holds it.

They figured it out long ago.

Sleep in the middle of their peace.

It holds all night.
