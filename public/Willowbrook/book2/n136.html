Night 136

Livy in the Mirror

Month 5: All About Me

In the mirror lives a girl.

Olivia has known her forever.

They wave together.

They boing their curls together.

And now Olivia knows something new.

She points at the girl.

"Livy!"

Yes!

That girl is Livy.

That girl is YOU.

The mirror girl points back.

Right at Olivia.

"Livy," they say together.

One name. Two pointers.

Same girl, all along.

Hello, you.

Nice to finally meet you.
