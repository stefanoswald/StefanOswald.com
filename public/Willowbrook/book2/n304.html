Night 304

The Opposite of Alone

Month 10: Opposites

One more opposite.

The last one of the month.

Alone:

just one. By yourself.

(Alone is okay sometimes.

The tower builds well alone.)

But the opposite of alone...

what is it called?

Together?

Yes. But look at it.

Really look:

a girl, a bear in her arms,

a Mama's hand on the blanket,

a Papa in the doorway,

a star at the window,

a whole town around the house,

holding its breath kindly.

The opposite of alone

is this.

All of this.

It doesn't fit in one word.

It barely fits in one room.

It holds. It holds. It holds.

Goodnight, together.

Goodnight, Willowbrook.
