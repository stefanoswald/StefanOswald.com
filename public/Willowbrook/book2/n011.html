Night 11

The Toy Box Yawns

Month 1: My World

The toy box is hungry.

It is bedtime, and toys go in.

In goes the block. Clunk.

In goes the ring. Clink.

In goes the little cow. Moo, clunk.

"Night-night, toys," says Olivia.

The toy box holds them all.

It never drops even one.

Toys sleep in a big warm pile.

That is how toys like it.

Close the lid. Soft and slow.

The toy box yawns... and sleeps.

So do toys.

So, soon, does Olivia.
