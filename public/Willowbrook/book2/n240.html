Night 240

The Rain Slept Over

Month 8: Splash

It rained all night.

Shhhh, on the roof, all night long.

Now: morning. Sun!

Come see the window!

The rain slept over...

and left gifts!

Every leaf: beaded with drops.

Every drop: full of sun.

The fence wears diamonds.

The spider's web, look!...

is a necklace now.

Drip... drip...

The gifts fall slowly, one by one.

The grass drinks them.

By lunch they'll all be gone.

Morning-after mornings

are the shiniest ones.

Go out. Touch one drop.

Cold! Perfect. Yours.
