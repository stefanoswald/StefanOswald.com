Night 294

The Middle

Month 10: Opposites

Here is a secret

about opposites:

between every pair,

there's a middle.

Not hot. Not cold.

Warm. Just right.

(The bath lives there.)

Not fast. Not slow.

Walking-with-Mama speed.

Not loud. Not quiet.

Talking. Humming.

Not big. Not little.

Olivia-sized.

The middle is where

most of the good life happens.

Porridge knows it.

Beds know it.

The three bears famously

knew it.

Just right, just right, just right.

The middle was made for

exactly you.
