Night 128

The Teeth Club

Month 5: All About Me

Open wide!

Look who lives in there.

Teeth!

Little white ones.

They came one by one.

Each one worked hard to arrive.

Now they are a club.

The tooth club!

They bite bananas.

They chomp bread.

They click on the spoon. Click!

Every night, they get brushed.

Brush, brush, brush.

The club likes to be clean.

More members are coming.

The club is growing.

Just like everything else in there.
