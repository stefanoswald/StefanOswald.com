Night 84

The Cricket Clock

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

When the light goes gold,

the crickets start.

Creak-creak. Creak-creak.

One in the hedge.

One under the porch.

Then all of them. Everywhere.

Creak-creak-creak-creak.

What are they doing?

Counting the night, Grandpa says.

They are the summer's clock.

When you hear them, it is almost bed.

Olivia listens from the steps.

Creak-creak. Creak-creak.

Count on, little clock.

We are going up now.

Count us to sleep.
