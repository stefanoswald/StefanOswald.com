Night 230

The Splash Contest

Month 8: Splash

Tonight: the splash contest!

(Mama pretends not to allow it.

She brings extra towels anyway.)

Papa goes first.

One hand. One gentle... PLOP.

A big slow wave!

Impressive.

Olivia's turn.

Both hands. Full speed.

SPLASHSPLASHSPLASHSPLASH!

A dozen little storms!

Water on the walls!

Water on Papa!

Water on the ceiling?!

"Winner!" declares Papa, dripping.

The winner splashes her victory lap.

The floor towel does its duty.

The contest is a tie every night.

Somehow. Forever.

That is contest law.
