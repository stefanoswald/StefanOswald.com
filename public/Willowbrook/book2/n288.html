Night 288

Over and Under

Month 10: Opposites

The fort teaches this one.

The cushion wall:

go OVER it!

Climb, up, over, flop!

Made it!

The blanket roof:

go UNDER it!

Duck, crawl, under, in!

Home!

Over the wall, under the roof.

That's the way into every fort

ever built.

The bridge goes over the brook.

The brook goes under the bridge.

Birds go over. Worms go under.

Papa steps over the gate.

Olivia goes under the table.

(Her shortcut. Hers alone.)

Over and under:

two ways past anything.

The world is very passable

once you know them both.
