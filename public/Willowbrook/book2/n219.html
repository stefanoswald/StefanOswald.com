Night 219

The Brook Wade

Month 8: Splash

The day has come.

The wading day!

The brook's shallow edge.

Pebbly. Ankle-deep. Warm-ish.

Boots off. Both hands in Papa's.

Step...

IN.

Ohh! It moves!

The water pulls, gentle,

like it wants to show her

where it's going.

Another step. Pebbles wobble.

Toes grip. Papa holds.

The brook curls around her ankles

and hurries on, introducing itself

the whole time.

"WELCOME!" yells someone green

from a rock. "I TAUGHT HER EVERYTHING!"

He did not.

But it's nice he came.
