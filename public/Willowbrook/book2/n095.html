Night 95

Orange

Month 4: A World of Colors

Orange is the cozy color.

The pumpkin: orange.

The oranges: orange!

(They took the name. Fair enough.)

The marigolds at the stall: orange.

The leaves before they fall: orange.

Sweet potato? Orange!

Olivia's old favorite.

The mash she voted for

with her whole face, long ago.

Orange things are warm things.

Autumn things. Yummy things.

She hugs the little pumpkin.

"Mine," she says.

Orange agrees to be hers.

Orange is agreeable like that.
