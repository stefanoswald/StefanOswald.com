Night 270

One Moon

Month 9: One, Two, Three!

So many stars. Uncountable.

So many ducks. All of them.

So many kisses. Every one.

But the moon?

Count the moon.

"One."

One moon.

Just one, for the whole sky.

For the whole town.

For every window in Willowbrook.

Everybody shares her.

The farm sees the same moon.

Lily sees the same moon.

The aunts, far north, same moon.

One is enough,

when the one is that good.

"Nigh-nigh, Moon."

The Moon hums back.

One hum. For everyone.

It reaches every single window.

That is the math of moons.
