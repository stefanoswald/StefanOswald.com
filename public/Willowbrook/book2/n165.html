Night 165

The Corn's Costume

Month 6: Yum

The corn wears a costume.

Green leaves, wrapped tight.

And under those, silk!

Soft yellow threads.

Pull the leaves. Rrrip!

Pull the silk. Tickly!

And there, underneath:

gold!

Rows and rows of little gold teeth.

All in lines. All hiding.

"Corn," says Mama.

The best-dressed vegetable.

Into the pot it goes.

Later: little sweet bites,

cut off the cob.

Pop, pop, pop.

Worth the unwrapping.

Most good things are.
