Night 117

Colors in the Dark

Month 4: A World of Colors

A bedtime question:

where do colors go at night?

Olivia checks her cup.

In the dark it looks... grey.

Is the red gone?

Turn on the lamp. Click.

RED! There it is!

Click. Grey again.

Click. Red!

The red never leaves.

It just sleeps when the light sleeps.

Colors need light like songs need air.

"Nigh-nigh, red," says Olivia.

Click.

Sleep, color.

The morning will wake you.

It always does.
