Night 92

Yellow

Month 4: A World of Colors

Yellow is the morning color.

The sun spills it on the floor.

Olivia stands in the puddle of it.

Warm feet!

The banana: yellow.

"Nana!" Correct.

The butter: yellow.

The softest blanket in the world:

yellow.

And the duck. Esquire himself.

Yellow as a bell.

Yellow things are warm things.

Sun things. Soft things.

Breakfast things.

Good morning, yellow.

You always get up first.
