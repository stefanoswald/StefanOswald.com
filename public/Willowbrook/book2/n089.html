Night 89

The Goodnight Sounds

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Every night, the same little songs.

The bath drain: gurgle-gurgle.

Bye, water.

The pajama zipper: zzzip.

The book pages: fff... fff...

The lamp: click.

Hello, little light.

The kiss: mwah.

One from Mama. Mwah.

One from Papa. Mwah.

The blanket: shff, up to the chin.

And the last one. The best one.

"Nigh-nigh."

"Nigh-nigh."

Two voices. One meaning.

The day closes, note by note.

Every note in place.
