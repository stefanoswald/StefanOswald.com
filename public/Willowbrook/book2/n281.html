Night 281

Full and Empty

Month 10: Opposites

The red cup, full.

Water to the top.

Heavy. Careful. Shiny.

Sip... sip... glug...

The red cup, empty.

Light! Hollow! Tip it, nothing!

Where did the water go?

Into Olivia.

(SHE is fuller now.)

Full and empty trade places.

The cup fills. The girl empties it.

The bowl fills. The tummy empties it.

The toy box: full at night,

empty by morning.

(Fast work, that.)

Full is nice.

Empty is ready.

Ready for the next filling.

That's what empty is for.

Nothing sad about it.
