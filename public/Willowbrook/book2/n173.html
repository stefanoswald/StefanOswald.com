Night 173

The Honey Spoon

Month 6: Yum

Tonight, warm milk gets a visitor.

Honey.

Watch it pour.

Slow... slow... slooow.

A golden thread!

It spirals down.

It takes its time.

Honey never hurries.

The bees made this.

Flower by flower.

All summer long.

Buzz by buzz by buzz.

Now it sweetens the bedtime milk.

Stir, stir. Gone, but tasteable.

Mmm.

Thank you, bees.

All that work

for one golden thread.

Worth it, says the milk.

Worth it, says the girl.
