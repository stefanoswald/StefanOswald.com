Night 241

The Sea in the Shell

Month 8: Splash

The aunts sent a shell.

Big. Spiraled. Pink inside.

It came from the sea.

The real sea! Far away!

"Hold it to your ear,"

says Papa.

Like a telephone?

Like a telephone.

Listen...

Shhhhhhhh...

The sea!

There's a SEA in the SHELL!

How did it fit?!

(It is a secret about echoes.

But not tonight.)

Tonight, the sea lives in the shell,

and the shell lives on the sill,

and anybody who wants the ocean

can borrow it,

one ear at a time.
