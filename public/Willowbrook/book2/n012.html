Night 12

Mama's Bun

Month 1: My World

Mama's hair lives in a bun.

Round and soft, like a little nest.

Olivia pats it. Gently, gently.

Sometimes a piece falls down.

Boing!

Olivia laughs.

Mama laughs too.

Back goes the piece.

Mamas can fix anything.

Hair. Blocks. Bumped knees.

Everything.

"Love you," says Mama.

Olivia pats the bun one more time.

That means love you too.

Pats always do.
