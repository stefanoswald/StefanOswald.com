Night 90

The Star Chime

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

Do stars make a sound?

Listen very hard.

The town is asleep.

The kettle: quiet.

The crickets: slow now.

The brook: humming its night verse.

And way up high...

there!

Stella twinkles once.

Like a tiny bell made of light.

Ting.

You heard it with your eyes.

That is how star bells work.

"Mmm," hums the Sleepy Moon.

The night is a lullaby

that never quite stops.

Goodnight, Willowbrook.
