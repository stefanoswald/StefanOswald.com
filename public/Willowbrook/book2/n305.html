Night 305

The Circle Sun

Month 11: Shapes Everywhere

The first shape is the circle.

Round and round, no corners.

Where does it live?

Look up!

The sun! A circle!

The brightest circle there is.

Now look around.

The breakfast plate: circle.

The clock's face: circle.

The cup's rim: circle!

Trace it with one finger.

Round... and round...

you never fall off a circle.

No corners to bump.

No end to reach.

The moon is one. Wheels are two.

Bubbles are hundreds!

Circles everywhere,

rolling through the whole day,

smooth as always.

Round and round we go.
