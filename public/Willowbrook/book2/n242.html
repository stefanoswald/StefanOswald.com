Night 242

Every Water Goes Home

Month 8: Splash

Where did today's waters go?

The bath water: down the drain,

off toward the brook.

The brook: rolling to the river.

The river: rolling to the sea.

And the sea?

The sun sips it up. Slowly.

Up, up, into clouds!

The clouds sail back over the hills...

and rain!

On the town. On the garden.

Into the brook again!

Around and around it goes.

The same water. Forever.

Tonight's bath might hold

a drop the dinosaurs drank.

Imagine!

Water never says goodbye.

It only says see you soon.

Splash. Sleep. See you soon.
