Night 131

The Voice That Grew Words

Month 5: All About Me

Inside Olivia lives a voice.

It started tiny.

Just cries. Then coos.

Then ba-ba-ba, all day.

And now, listen!

"Mama! More! Duck! Home!"

Words!

The voice grew them

like the garden grew tomatoes.

Slowly. Then all at once.

Every day, a new one.

Where do they come from?

From every word she ever heard.

They all went in.

Now they are coming out.

Talk on, little voice.

The whole world is listening.
