Night 348

The Goodnight Kiss Travels

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

Watch where the kiss goes.

It starts on Mama's lips.

It's been there all day, waiting.

Here it comes...

mwah...

landing on Olivia's forehead.

Right in the middle.

And there it STAYS.

All night!

Kisses don't wear off.

They soak in.

They keep the spot warm.

If a dream gets bumpy,

the kiss whispers:

"She's right down the hall."

If the night feels long,

the kiss says:

"Morning's coming. I've seen it."

By breakfast it's soaked

all the way through...

which is why loved children

wake up warm.

Now you know.

Mwah.

Delivered. On duty.

Goodnight.
