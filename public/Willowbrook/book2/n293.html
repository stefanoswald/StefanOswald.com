Night 293

Empty Lane, Full House

Month 10: Opposites

Look out the window.

The lane: empty.

Cold. Blue. Nobody out.

Even the gold dog is home.

Now look around the room.

The house: full!

Full of lamplight.

Full of soup smell.

Full of people...

one, two, three of them,

all here, all warm.

Full of bear and blanket

and the evening's slow music.

Empty outside, full inside.

Winter's favorite opposite.

The emptier the lane,

the fuller the house feels.

Snow can have the street.

We have everything else.

Pull the curtain.

Pass the soup.
