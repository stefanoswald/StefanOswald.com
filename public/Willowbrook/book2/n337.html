Night 337

Checking on Everyone

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

Before bed: the rounds.

Somebody has to check on everyone.

Bobby Bear? In the crib.

Waiting. Ear at the ready. Check.

The bath bear? Dry on his shelf. Check.

The plant? Watered. Growing quietly.

Check.

The sill museum? Rock, leaf, feather,

shell with the sea inside.

All present. Check.

The window? The lane's asleep.

Gold dog: home. Porch lights: on.

Star: there. Moon: up.

Check and check.

The whole world: checked.

Everything where it belongs.

Everyone safe as houses.

Now the last check.

The most important one.

Olivia?

In her crib.

Warm. Held. Loved.

Check.

All rounds complete.

The night may proceed.
