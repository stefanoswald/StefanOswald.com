Night 364

From Two to Three Begins Tomorrow

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

Look at the shelf.

This book, our book...

is almost done.

Three hundred sixty-four nights!

We read through

every sound and shape,

every color and splash,

every hello and goodnight

of a whole entire year.

And look, beside it.

Another book.

A next book.

"From Two to Three."

It's already waiting.

It's full of things that

haven't happened yet:

new friends and big feelings,

a certain little brother, someday...

(shh, that's a surprise)...

whole adventures, still folded up

like seeds in spring dirt.

Pat the old book. "Ta, book."

Pat the new one. "Hi, book."

One more night in this one.

Then we turn the big page.

Books end.

Shelves don't.

That's the wonderful thing.

Shelves just keep going.
