Night 59

The Moon Counts the Animals

Month 2: Animal Friends

High above, the Moon hums.

She is counting the animals.

The dog: asleep by the door.

The cat: asleep on the sill.

The hens: asleep in a row.

The ducks: asleep on the water.

Heads under wings.

The horse: asleep standing up!

(Horses can. It's true.)

The elephant: asleep by the willows.

Her balloon sleeps too.

"All counted," says Stella.

"Mmm," hums the Moon.

Every animal, safe and sleeping.

And one little girl, the same.

Goodnight, Willowbrook.
