Night 68

The Whistle Kettle and the Hum Fridge

Month 3: The Sounds of Everything

The kitchen is full of singers.

Listen.

The fridge hums. Mmmmmm.

It hums all day. It never gets tired.

The tap drips. Plip. Plip.

The oven ticks when it is warm.

The kettle, you know the kettle.

Wheee-ooo!

Olivia points at each one.

Hum. Plip. Tick. Whee!

It is a kitchen band.

It plays every day at dinner.

Nobody claps for the fridge.

But it keeps humming anyway.

That is called being faithful.

The kitchen is full of it.
