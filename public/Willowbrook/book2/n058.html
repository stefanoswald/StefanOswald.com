Night 58

The Animal Goodnight

Month 2: Animal Friends

Time for bed.

But first: the animal goodnight.

Goodnight, gold dog on your patrol.

Goodnight, cat on your wall.

Goodnight, birds in your nests.

Goodnight, ducks on your pond.

Goodnight, Henrietta. Goodnight, Duchess.

(Hmph, says the Duchess. Softly.)

Goodnight, Ellie behind the willows.

Goodnight, worms under the grass.

Goodnight, Bobby Bear...

oh! He's right here.

Never mind, Bobby.

You sleep with us.

Goodnight, everybody, everywhere.

All the animals. All the town.
