Night 8

The Window in the Morning

Month 1: My World

Good morning, window!

Olivia puts her hands on the glass.

Cold glass. Bright sun.

Outside, the lane is waking up.

There goes a bird. Hello, bird!

There goes the mail truck. Hello, truck!

There is the big tree. Hello, tree!

The window shows Olivia everything.

That is a window's whole job.

"Hi!" says Olivia.

The morning waves back with sunshine.

Every morning. Every window.

Hello, world.
