Night 130

The Feet That Know the Way

Month 5: All About Me

Down at the bottom: feet.

Ten toes. Two soles.

And inside them, maps!

The feet know the way

to the kitchen.

To the cozy corner.

To the toy box.

To Mama, anywhere she is.

Pad, pad, pad.

No thinking needed.

The feet just go.

They learned the whole house

one step at a time.

Bare feet know it best.

They read the floor like a book.

Pad, pad. Home everywhere.

The feet never get lost here.
