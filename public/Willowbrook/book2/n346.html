Night 346

The Bear's Job at Night

Month 12: Bedtime Itself

What does Bobby Bear do

all night?

You think he sleeps?

He does. Sort of. Bear-sleep.

One ear stays on duty.

(The floppy one. It hears best.)

His jobs, all night long:

Being held. (Chief duty.)

Being soft. (He trained years for it.)

Catching bad dreams

before they land...

he's got a whole system...

and letting the good ones through.

Keeping the exact warm spot

warm.

And if she wakes in the middle,

being findable.

Right there. First reach.

That's the whole contract.

He signed it long ago,

on a dresser, by moonlight.

Best job in the world,

he'd tell you.

Worth every night of the wait.
