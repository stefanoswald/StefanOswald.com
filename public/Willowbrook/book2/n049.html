Night 49

The Bird at the Feeder

Month 2: Animal Friends

Olivia is eating breakfast.

Look! So is somebody else.

A little bird at the feeder.

Round as a pom-pom.

Peck, peck, peck.

Crack! A seed!

Olivia takes a bite of toast.

The bird takes a seed.

Bite. Peck. Bite. Peck.

Breakfast together,

with a window in between.

"More?" The bird tilts its head.

More, agrees Olivia.

Everybody eats in the morning.

Birds and girls alike.

Tap the glass gently. Bye, bird!
