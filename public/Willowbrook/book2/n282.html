Night 282

Day and Night

Month 10: Opposites

The biggest opposites of all.

Day:

sun up, light everywhere,

trucks and swings and lunches,

the whole town busy.

Night:

sun gone, stars out,

lamps and baths and stories,

the whole town settling.

And right now, look...

the trade is happening.

The window shows it.

Gold going. Blue coming.

First star... there.

Day hands the town to night.

"Take good care of them,"

says day.

"Always do," says night.

The trade happens every evening.

Gentle as a handoff,

parent to parent,

over a sleeping girl.
