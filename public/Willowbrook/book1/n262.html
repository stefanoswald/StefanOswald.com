Night 262

The Rain Walk

Month 9 — The Edge of Everything

The storm's morning-after dawned rinsed and dripping, the lane one long silver gallery of standing water — and the household, per the all-weather doctrine's oldest law and a package the far aunts' spring shipment had provided in prophetic advance, opened the wet world's franchise: the rain boots.

Dimensions: implausible. Color: yellow, per the ancient statutes. Installed: over protest (footwear's standing reception), forgotten per the fitter's rule within the block.

And the walk itself, logged as the season's field trip of record. The puddle — the supreme one, the sidewalk's low-spot capital, observed from stroller altitude all mud season per the franchise's long prologue — approached this morning on foot, hand-escorted, in equipment. The first stamp: tentative, one boot's sole pressed to the water's skin, the splash's answer received up the whole leg as news of the first order. The second stamp: committed. The third through fortieth: the franchise, exercised — both boots, full authority, the water leaping per the physics the bath had taught and the world now confirmed at civic scale, the umbrella's drum roofing the whole enterprise per the white sea's oldest arrangement, and the walker's laugh running the lane's length at the wet world's expense.

The page co-signs the franchise with the doctrine's whole portfolio. The puddle years — opening formally this morning, running per the veterans deep into the grade-school country — are the weather curriculum's practicum: the child who stamps the storm's leavings is filing the sky's repertoire under playable, the boots and the umbrella teaching at the body's own scale what the window's narration taught at the eyes' — that the world's weathers are equipment problems, not moods, per the northern nations' entire philosophy. The equipment's own syllabus rode along: the boots' new physics (traction's absence on the wet walk billed and paid, one landing, per the falls' flat-faced customs), the umbrella's handling apprenticed (carried eleven yards by the junior partner, mostly inverted, wholly magnificent). And the wet world's texture tour ran its stations per the eternal method: the dripping fence licked by providence's near miss (customs redirected), the worm delegations out in force on the drowned walk (the summit's protocols honored at each — stepped over, pointed, wished well per the going-home liturgy), the gutter's brown freight watched to the drain's own mystery.

Home per the towel-bin protocols, boots parked at the door beside the fleet — upright, yellow, dripping, on duty.

"Again," signed the franchise-holder at the window, aimed at the lane entire.

Tomorrow, honored the state, per the forecast's own promise.

The puddles, refilling on schedule all spring, held the appointment. The boots, the page certifies, never once stood dry again.

The franchise, exercised daily, ran for years. It was, of the year's many grand openings, the wettest and among the most faithfully renewed.
