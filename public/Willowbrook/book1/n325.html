Night 325

The Keeping Shelf

Month 11 — Lantern Season

The keeping shelf — the basement's keepsake harbor, stocked at the year's whole succession of honorable discharges — received at the month's last evening its first full curated tour, and the page attends per the album session's own precedent: the year's material history, walked at the shelf's term, piece by piece.

The collection, per the catalog the almanac supplied. The hospital band, the harbor's founding artifact — the circumference, the page certifies at every parent's identical astonishment, impossible: the wrist it certified now conducting orchestras, boarding tractors, seizing stethoscopes at clinical grade. The swaddle's fold-fold-tuck edition, washed to its keepsake softness, the fourth trimester's whole held-held-held retired at the rolling's first twist. The little tub, the sponge era's sloped harbor; the play gym's swatted star at its venerable shabbiness; the newborn fleet's smallest editions — the romper of the changeover's own committee-consulted exhibit, the socks at thumb's length, the first hat. The pacifiers' discreet drawer. The bottle's three a.m. survivor at Papa's dated requisition. The curl's envelope at its sealed term. The yellow boots, succeeded at one season's puddle mileage. And the shelf's latest consignments, harbored at the month's own dockets: the bee's wings at their furled term (the costume's office, the page notes, retired to the harbor over its wearer's standing objection and one negotiated final buzz), the album's out-takes at their archival box, the invitation's one spare copy — the party doctrine's own paper, filed in advance per the curators' unspoken concurrence at the shelf's whole coming registry.

The tour's own office, entered at the page's deepest file. That the keeping shelf is the household's second almanac — the year's ledger kept at fabric and rubber and sealed envelope, the entries walked at the hand's own reading — and its curation, per the veterans' testimony, the raising road's most quietly maintained institution: the harbors compounding at every changeover's consignment, the far books' shelves (the first backpack's coming berth, the training wheels' distant dock, the whole succession the series' volumes would harbor) already, per the curators' own tour-end silence, visible from this one. And that the tour's true finding ran per the album's twin arithmetic: the year, one shelf wide — the impossibly small editions certifying, per the only proof that ever fully lands, the growing's whole documented enormity: not that she was little once, which the memory holds anyway, but exactly, materially, this little — the band's circumference at the thumb's own measure, twelve months and one shelf from the walker asleep two floors up at her starfish term.

The tour closed at the lamp's own hour, the fleet harbored, the catalog shelved.

One artifact, the record notes, left the harbor at the closing: the bottle's survivor, returned to the kitchen's own shelf at Papa's silent re-requisition — some keepings, per the household's whole jurisprudence, keeping better in daylight.

Forty nights. The harbor: toured, certified, and — per the curators' one entered finding — impossible, and exactly true, and theirs.
