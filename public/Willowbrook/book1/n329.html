Night 329

The Eleventh Month, Counted

Month 11: Lantern Season

The audit convened at the winter kitchen's lamplit term, the porch's seasons closed per the calendar, the cocoa at the institution's standing winter service, and the page attends the session at its own documented weight: the eleventh chapter, the founding year's second-to-last.

The countables, entered at the veteran hand. One bee, buzzed at office; one lantern night observed and ceremonially gummed; one crossing walked at six days' veteran grade. One molar crowned at the parade's heavy class; one runner certified at the hallway's own gear; one mountain granted its upright franchise at the rail's term. One whole-milk audition passing at the committee's ballots; one bottle's notice served at the gentle clauses; one weaning page held past its schedule per its own counsel. One phrase, more nana, boxed, and the crackle's Tuesday sparks past the chalk's whole arithmetic. One gratitude table at four generations' gold; one album assembled at eleven thousand's cull; one invitation run converted at the first station to the year's own farewell tour; one keeping shelf toured at the impossible circumferences; one hill climbed at last to the town's own tree, the word left in the rings.

The uncountables, run at the chapter's deepest term. That the month had been the year's own packing, the album's assembly, the harbor's tour, the invitations' walked map, the willow's carried survey: the twelve-month country audited station by station at the closing weeks' own instinct, the household running, per the almanac's entry, the whole compiled geography one last time at ordinary rates before the candle's country renamed it. That the language's opening crackle had begun returning the year at its own registers, the well's poured everything arriving back per the echo's deepest law in her voice, her aim, her "home" spent at the table and the hill and the walk's own turning: the word, both auditors entered at the same line, being the year's entire ledger at one coin's term. And the eleventh chapter's own finding, said at the lamp per the institution's closing custom: the audits themselves stood at one remaining, the twelfth, the candle's own, the founding year's last session, and the auditors, at the cocoa's term, ran the only arithmetic the whole institution had ever secretly kept: the two people at the fog's first table and the two at this one, twelve chapters and one entire person apart, the craft compiled and the country built and the being-with banked at rates no page had budgeted...

"Thirty-six nights," said Papa, at the box's own shelf.

"Thirty-six," said Mama, the counting's own edition, per the porch's autumn precedent: some things you count.

The candle's box held its shelf. The album held its blank leaf. The bear, upstairs at his closing division, held the watch's last posted weeks.

Audited. Approved. Carried forward, packed, surveyed, worded, and thirty-six nights wide: to the candle.
