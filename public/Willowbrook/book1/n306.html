The Lantern Night

Month 11 — Lantern Season

The lantern night itself arrived at the season's appointed dusk — Maple Lane at its flickering annual best, every porch gold-lit per the ordinance, the lane's children at their costumed commerce door to door — and the household attended its citizen's first edition per the festival doctrine's compiled charter: the bee, aloft; the lane, toured; the liturgy, observed.

The tour, logged at the correspondent's own stations. The porches: received at the walking's carried altitude (the hour's crowds and the dusk's grades ruling the arms' transit per the safety statutes, the bee's own petitions honored at the quiet stretches — eleven yards of sidewalk walked at the wings' full documented dignity, the lane's applause per its year-long custom). The commerce itself: studied at the point's deepest enrollments — the costumed children at their door-knock liturgies, the candy's whole visible economy (the page notes the household's own participation ran per the age's statutes at observation grade: the sweets' franchise, per the feeding doctrine's standing counsel, holding years out; the cider donut's one ceremonial corner, gummed at the bakery stall's own gift, filing the season's flavor at the customs office's rapturous committee), the older editions' costumes pointed at per the breaking-news offices — the lane's own gold dog, the record certifies, encountered mid-tour in a hot-dog costume of considerable civic impact, received by the bee at the year's longest documented mutual regard: the two offices, dog-as-food and baby-as-bee, consulting each other's files at length across the sidewalk's dusk, the "fuff" issued at last at a taxonomic confidence the whole season ratified.

And the door's own turn, per the festival's closing station: the household's porch manned at the return's hour, the bee installed at the greeting's committee — the bowl's distribution observed at the state's knee, the costumed callers received at the stranger-file's compiled graduated protocols (the small ones cleared at sight; the masked editions consulted at the harbor's own rates; one skeleton, the record notes, filed provisionally pending better light) — and the "bye-bye" issued at every departure per the ceremonies' full offices, the wave's franchise running the evening entire.

The night closed per the fuse statutes at the liturgy's untouched seven: the bee struck to pajamas (the antennae surrendered at the treaty's honorable terms), the jack's gold flicker pointed at through the window per the season's whole compiled liturgy of lights, the coin spent at the lane's own dying commerce, settling register:

"Nigh-nigh."

The lane held its flicker past the sleeper's own term, porch by porch, the town's gentlest festival folding at its annual pace.

The bee hung at the closet's own harbor thereafter, wings furled, buzzed at in passing per custody's unlapsed rates.

First lantern night: observed, ceremonially gummed, waved entire. The candy years, the almanac forecast at the file's close, would arrive per their own statutes.

The wonder years, per the whole record: already in session. Fifty-nine nights. The season's lights, all of them hers now, counting down.
