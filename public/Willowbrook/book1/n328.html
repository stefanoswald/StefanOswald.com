Night 328

The Willow Walk

Month 11: Lantern Season

The Great Willow, the town's own tree, the hill's presiding gold, the night watch's whole standing chancery per every closer the year had filed, had run the book's entire term at the skyline's distance: pointed at from the lane, silhouetted at the harvest's rising, letting down its glow at the weighings nobody below could see.

The month's last Sunday, at the golden hour, the household walked up.

The pilgrimage, logged at the hill's own pace. The path: the brook's crossing at the stepping stones' watched term (the wading rights' coming season consulted at the point's standing petition; deferred, per the statutes, at the escort's compiled grades), the hill's long meadow taken at the archaeology's full franchise, the seed heads consulted, the late crickets' ledger heard at first person, the town assembling below at the climb's own reveal: the lanes, the lantern's roof, the square, the farm's far gold, the whole compiled map, the page certifies at the summit's turn, one hill wide: the year's entire geography, walked and named and RSVP'd, visible at a single standing survey.

And the tree itself: the long branches' country entered at the golden hour's own liturgy, the canopy's faint gold letting down per the town's whole discreet tradition (the light, the page notes at the records' own policy, unexplained and unremarked, per Willowbrook), the trunk's old term received at the flat-palmed ceremony the tractor's flank had certified, the citizen conducted at the branches' low margins per the gentle-finger's entire compiled treaty, and the visit's own office, held at the tree's term: the family seated at the roots' old furniture, the town below going gold then blue, the year, the page enters it at the almanac's own register, carried up the hill entire and set down, for one held hour, at the tree where the town keeps its weighings.

"Well," said Papa, at the survey's whole term.

"Maybe," said Mama, at the dialect's deepest, and the citizen, at the roots' own country, spent the hour's one coin at the canopy's gold, pointed, certain, per the whole year's compiled liturgy of lights:

"Home."

The tree, per the town's records, took the word into its rings, where the oak keeps its minutes and the brook its knowings, the willow keeps, per the registries, exactly this, and let its gold down a shade further over the three of them, per the weighings' own seal.

(From the highest branch, the record notes at the town's discretion, a round elderly regard attended the visit entire at the spectacles' own angle, and asked, per his office, nothing, the hour, per the order's one liturgy, being already the blessing.)

The descent ran at dusk's own gates, the sleeper at the shoulder line by the brook's recrossing.

Thirty-seven nights. The hill: climbed. The town: surveyed entire. The word: in the rings.

The candle's country, per the summit's whole standing view: below, lit, and, at every documented window, waiting.
