Night 191

The Snack Diplomacy

Month 7 — Explorers and Guardrails

The exchange occurred on the winter walk, at the bench, mid-snack, and the almanac would rank it above the month's louder milestones.

The puffs traveled their usual route — bag to maternal fingers to the reaching pincer (certified operational this month, the crane fully commissioned) to mouth. Standard commerce. Then, at puff eleven or so, the route reversed: the small hand rose, one puff pinched in the certified grip, soggy at the edges from prior consideration — and extended it. To Mama. Held it at her lips with the unmistakable full intent of the gesture, eyes on her face, waiting.

The gift. The first outbound gift of a lifetime, condition: pre-owned, value: total.

Mama ate the puff. (The page rules on this for all recipients, permanently: you eat the puff. Whatever its history, whatever its moisture — the transaction is the treasure, and refusal at this counter teaches economics nobody wants taught.) The giver's face at the acceptance ran the full parade: attention, delight, and something new the seventh month had been assembling in the wings — the satisfaction of having caused a feeling in another face on purpose.

The offering games ran all month from that bench forward, filed by the developmental texts under the same social flowering as the point: give-and-take as proto-conversation, the object passed where words will someday pass. She offered puffs, the ring, the crackle paper's sacred remnant, one mitten (repeatedly, a running bit), and received each item back or ate the returns as protocol demanded. Turn, turn, turn — the tennis of the babble months, now played with matter.

The page's counsel to the receiving public: play at full sincerity. Thank the giver. Admire the mitten. Return serves generously. The child rehearsing generosity on puffs is laying the ledger every future kindness draws on, and the exchange rate is set right here, at the bench, by how the first gifts land.

Eleven puffs in, the world had a new trade route.

It ran on soggy currency and closed at nap time, wealthy in both directions.
