Night 282

The Showing Point

Month 10 — High Summer

The pointing, the household's oldest instrument — the whole-arm aim of the stairs era, the triangle's spring completion, the market's daily order slip — revealed at the park, mid-month, its second and richer register, and the page stops the walk to mark it.

The event, per the almanac's bench-side entry. The airplane: high, silver, contrail-drawing, crossing the park's whole summer sky. The point: fired, full lock, tracking. And the lookback — this is the register's own signature — carrying no order at all: not the want (the imperative point's daily commerce — that cup, that duck, that cracker) and not even the confirm of the old joint-attention protocols, but the era's richest message, aimed and unmistakable: see this with me. The plane acquired nothing. The plane requested nothing. The plane was, purely, shared.

The declarative point, the literature names it, and the page gives the distinction its full developmental due. The imperative point treats the other as a tool — a fine and necessary commerce, the wanting economy's whole order system. The declarative point treats the other as a mind: the child holding an experience and choosing, at nine-tenths of a year old, to pass it across the gap — the airplane's silver fact posted from her head to her mother's for no wage but the having-it-together. It is, the researchers' own consensus runs, among the species' first purely social acts, the seed of every look at that, every sunset elbowed at a companion, every book recommended and photo sent and story told at every dinner of her whole conversational life: the experience economy, opening its first counter at a park bench under a contrail.

The parental craft, per the beam's eternal law but at the register's own rate: the shared thing received as shared — the plane admired at length, the wonder matched rather than serviced ("Airplane! Look at it go — all the way across") — because the declarative economy, the page counsels, is grown entirely by its reception: the child whose posts are received keeps posting, and the household's whole future of show-and-tell, of school days recounted and treasures brought home, of the teenage years' rationed but real look at this — all of it banks, the veterans confirm, at this counter, at this age, at whether the airplane was received.

It was received. At length. At the wonder's own matched rate.

"Duh!" the poster added — the coin spent declaratively now too, the page notes, the vocabulary's whole economy following the point's new register within the same week: the words themselves arriving less as orders and more as offerings — nana posted at the fruit bowl for the noticing, not the having; moo posted at the fence for the company of it.

The contrail dissolved at the sky's own pace, watched to its last silver by both members of the economy.

See this with me. The era's whole richest message, and the year's.

Received. Matched. Posted onward, per the way of it, for life.
