Night 335

The Last Month

Month 12: The Candle's Country

The calendar turned its final leaf at the month's first morning, the kitchen ceremony the household had kept at every turning since the fog, run now at the year's own last edition, and the page opens the twelfth month at the almanac's gates: the candle's country, entered.

The turning, logged at the ceremony's compiled term. The leaf: lifted at the senior hand, the last month's photograph (the album committee's selection: frame thirty-three's rug-gold anchor, promoted per the doctrine to the calendar's own closing berth) received at the wall's morning light. The chalk: the column's head taken at the countdown's formal arithmetic, thirty, the number boxed per the porch's autumn statute: some things you count. And the docket: resumed, the page marks the morning's whole finding at its plainest, at ordinary rates entire: the breakfast's Cheerio trials at their certified standard, the fourth meal's architecture at term, the zoomies' dusk franchise, the liturgy's four unamended steps: the last month opening, per the household's own deepest craft, not as a vigil but as thirty more days of the life the year had built, the extraordinary, per the whole book's method, kept at the ordinary's own faithful rates.

The page files the month's standing doctrine at its gates, per the veterans' counsel for every candle-country household. That the last weeks run their own weather, the retrospect's compiling tide (the album's leaves, the harbor's circumferences, the remember instinct rising at every ordinary station), the preparations' small logistics, the feelings' honest freight per the almost-question's whole branch-held both: the closing grieved and the opening lit, at once, at every documented dusk, and the craft, unchanged since the first month's landing pages: the days taken one at a time, the routines' load-bearing shapes held holy, the being-with banked at the ten-minutes doctrine's compounding rates, because the last month's true office, the page rules at the country's own gates, was never the counting down. It was the living through: thirty more ordinary days, each one the year's own whole method in miniature, fed, held, answered, delighted in, the real checklist's founding boxes, ticked at the last month's rates, daily, to the candle.

"Dis?" petitioned the docket's conductor at the calendar's new leaf, at the rug-gold anchor's own portrait.

"You," quoted the market, at the register the morning had earned. "That's you, at the rug, in the fall. And this month..." the chalk's boxed thirty consulted at the ceremony's close, "you turn one."

"One," echoed the country's own citizen, the coin's first documented minting, chalked at the sampler's December gold with the leaf's own date.

One. At the market's poetry, as ever: timed to its address.

Thirty nights. The country: entered. The docket: ordinary, extraordinary, hers.
