Night 324

Papa's Page

Month 11: Lantern Season

This page is Papa's, the way the weaning's was Mama's, the book pausing at the year's own closing weeks to give the second chair's whole compiled file its honest hour, and it convenes where his pages have always convened: the doorway's late watch, the sleeper at her starfish term, the moon at the glass per the oldest custom in the household's registry.

The audit, run at the watcher's own ledger. The offices held: the three a.m. kitchen's founding citizenship (the country's richest tired wealth, banked at the paced bottle's whole mannerly term), the burp symphony's percussion chair, the rumble's cello commission, the seed catalog's turnip nights, the low frequency's whole documented monopoly, the solo hours' patent-pending methods compiled at the headquarters transfers' unsupervised grades, the dawn shifts' rotating coffee, the stairs academy's chancellorship, the chase franchise's founding monster, the zoomies' delighted brakes. The crafts owned: the swaddle's fold retired at honors, the car seat's pinch test run at the tedium that grows old bones, the goose egg's field face held, the soup delivered at the ambush days' nine-second reads, the relief's whole technology, the page certifies at the marriage's own infrastructure, drilled to reflex at both chairs. And the file's deepest entries, run at the doorway's own register: the first laugh's belly-kiss authorship, the bear book's three hundred shuffled editions, the da-da certified at the window's own required minute, the steps' Wednesday catch missed by one room and the runner's every subsequent yard attended, the year, the watcher's ledger closed, having converted the second chair per its own quiet arithmetic into what the fourth month's page had promised and the twelfth confirmed: different, not lesser; first chair, in its section; the section, hers alone.

And the page's one counsel, filed at the watch's close for the second chairs of every reading household: the audit's habit itself, the doorway's late minute, the rise and fall attended past the need per the custom's founding text, being, per the whole year's evidence, the office's realest term: the watching that started at a bassinet's breathing checks compounding at the raising road's entire length into the thing the far books' fathers know at the graduations' own doorways: the watch never closes; it just gets quieter, and prouder, and learns to stand further back.

The sleeper turned at her term, one fist at the sleep sack's seam, per the whole archive.

"Nigh-nigh, walker," issued the watch, at the register the rumble was built on.

The moon countersigned per its policy. The doorway held its extra minute, per his.

Forty-one nights. The page: his. The section: hers. The watch: permanent, at both chairs, per the record, and per the record, never once heavy. Full.
