Night 338

The Remember Game, Part Three

Month 12: The Candle's Country

Part three took the lamp at the serial's appointed term, the album's spring leaves at the lap, and the tellers arrived, per the contract's own billing, at the year's biggest page: the walking one.

The telling, at the session's full production. "Then came the spring you stood up. First on everything, the couch, the gate, Grandpa's shin; he didn't spill his coffee; forty years of livestock in those ankles. Then on nothing at all: thirteen seconds, in the middle of the floor, while we counted behind our coffee cups and didn't breathe. And then..." the leaf turned to the boxed Wednesday, the signed entry's own photograph, "a Wednesday. Before lunch. Three feet of open floor, and you looked at me, and you picked up your right foot, it's in the book; we wrote it down with our own hands, and you walked. Into my arms. And the brook knew by midnight, because the brook knows everything first, and the whole town leaned toward this window, because it had been leaning all year."

And the session's own documented theater, entered at the almanac's tenderest December line: the subject, at her own retelling, the leaf consulted, the tellers regarded, the whole compiled file visibly running, rising at the lamp's gold from the lap's term, taking three commemorative strides across the nursery's floor, and arriving per the original's entire choreography into the catch: the step, re-performed at its own telling, the story and its subject at one term, and the catch, the page certifies at the record's own weight, held per the Wednesday's founding edition: swung, laughed, kept.

"You walked," ran the telling's close, at the register the serial reserved. "And then you ran, and you never once stopped, and the book says you never will, and the book is right."

"Go!" confirmed the cast, at her own leaf, per the coin's whole certified era.

Go. The serial's third session: adjourned at the catch's own term.

"More," per contract, at the lamp's dimming.

The last part tomorrow, honored the tellers: the talking one. The one where you start telling it back.

Twenty-seven nights. The biggest leaf: told, strode, caught. The duet: standing.
