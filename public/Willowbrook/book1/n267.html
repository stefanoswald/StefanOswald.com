Night 267

The Heat Returns

Month 9 — The Edge of Everything

The spring tipped into the year's first true heat at the month's end — the thermometer's opening argument of the second summer — and the page pauses at the yard's edge to mark what the afternoon proved before it proves anything else: the household had done all this before, and knew it.

The heat protocols, run from memory where the first summer ran them from lists. The sunscreen drill: ninety seconds per the season's compounded routine, the arms held out for it per the forecast the tube's first page had posted. The shade architecture: the oak's chess opened at the veteran's first move, the blanket pitched in the long cool. The hydration arithmetic: the water cup's service doubled unprompted, the milk's air-conditioning clause remembered. The hat treaty: renewed at a single session, the third season's precedent overwhelming. And the nursery's evening conversion — the fan's angle, the summer kit, the collar-check's thermometry — restaged in one pass per the heat-wave doctrine's second edition.

But the afternoon's new instrument, and the page's actual business: the wading tub — the big bath's outdoor cousin, two inches of hose-warmed water in the oak's shade, the brook's whole curriculum franchised to the yard — and its student, who had met her first summer horizontal and met this one on foot: the tub entered and exited at sovereign gait (the reach-rule's supervision absolute per the unamendable scripture, two inches being two inches), the pouring cup's seminars convened at open-air term, the kick factory's founding industry rediscovered at splash radii the bathroom never zoned for, and the water's oldest colleague — Esquire, ported out per tradition — bobbing his one expression at the season's new campus.

The page banks the afternoon's finding for the summer's whole docket ahead: the second year's seasons arrive to a household in possession of itself — the doctrines compiled, the equipment inventoried, the craft owned per the winter audit's own conclusion — and the difference shows not in the doing but in the weather of the doing: the first summer's heat had been managed; this one was met. The afternoon ran unhurried at every station, the state's attention spent not on the protocols (running themselves) but on the resident: the wading gait's comedy, the cup's tenth pour, the shade's slow gold — the being-with, the page's oldest dividend, purchased by competence and paid at the year's best rates.

The tub drained at the fuss's first honest note, per the wise-window statutes.

The evening ran cool per the converted nursery, the sleeper down at the season's adjusted gold.

Summer the second, opened. The household, veteran, held it easy.

And the almanac, closing the day, noted what the heat's return meant at the calendar's own register, in the hand that tracked such things:

Born at the last one's edge. The candle's season, incoming. Ten weeks.

Ten weeks. The page let the number stand, and the summer in with it.
