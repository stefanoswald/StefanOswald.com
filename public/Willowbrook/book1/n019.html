Night 19

Tummy Time Begins

Month 1 — Arrival

The blanket went down on the floor like a flag on a new planet, and Olivia went down on her tummy upon it.

She objected. Formally.

Tummy time is baby weightlifting, and nobody loves the gym at first. But those minutes on the belly build the neck that will hold the head that will steer the whole grand adventure — rolling, sitting, crawling, and one distant day, running away from bathtime.

So Mama kept the sessions tiny and kind. A few minutes, a few times a day, always awake and always watched. She lay down on the floor herself, nose to nose, which improved morale enormously. Some days the gym was simply Olivia on Papa's chest while he lay on the couch — that counts completely.

Olivia lifted her head one wobbling inch, like a small bulldozer starting up, and held it there, astonished at herself.

One inch. The first inch of everything.

Then she put her head down and filed a complaint, and Mama scooped her up, laughing, because the workout was over and the world was still enormous and mostly soft.
