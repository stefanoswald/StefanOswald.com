The Laundry Apprentice

Month 10: High Summer

The helping bureau, open since the coffee table's founding cloth, staffed through the garden's mud and the fleet's reviews, posted at the tenth month its first machine-grade commission, and the page logs the promotion at the bureau's own proud rates: the laundry apprenticeship.

The commission's duties, as the morning shift ran them. The handing-up: the wet load transferred basket-to-state piece by piece, the apprentice stationed at the basket's country, each item hauled two-fisted and hoisted to the waiting hand per a supply chain that ran, the almanac certifies, at eleven-sixteenths efficiency and total enrollment: the sock's small freight, the towel's whole-body negotiations (the hoist achieved at the third method; the applause embargoed per the audience statutes and paid at completion), the pillowcase worn briefly per the funnel's old tradition and surrendered to the line. The sorting's first apprenticeship: the dry load's socks culled from the fold at the state's narration ("find the socks, that's one!") per the category games the whole naming market had drilled. And the delivery service, the commission's crowning route: one folded stack's summit item, her own, carried per the fetch economy's expanding franchise up the hallway's converted line to the dresser's opened drawer, deposited (crumpled per the age's filing standards; refiled by the janitorial state at amnesty rates), the drawer shut at the apprentice's own two-handed ceremony, the round trip run thereafter daily at the era's own petition: the work, the page notes at the bureau's deepest ledger, requested.

The page re-files the bureau's whole standing doctrine at the promotion's rank, because the tenth month is where the veterans say the hiring window's habits set. That the work requested is the work kept: the apprentice enrolling at the family's real economy per the cupboard's oldest sovereignty math, not the toys' exile but the household's own verbs, scaled to reach, and the enrollment, honored now at machine grade, compounding toward the toddler years' true chores per the bureau's founding forecast. That the wages stay competence itself: the sock's drawer landed, the drawer's ceremony hers, the "I did it" of the coming vocabulary already running at the face's own registers, the builder's gravity, laundry edition, paid at every delivery. And the counsel's eternal arithmetic, chalked at the bureau's door since its founding and truer at every promotion: slower is the tuition; together is the degree, the load run at apprentice rates costing the morning twenty minutes and paying, per the whole book's accounting, the decade.

"Sock," minted the apprentice at the fold, mid-shift, coin the fourteenth or fifteenth, the ledger's chalk conceding the count to the echo season's volumes.

"Sock!" honored the state, at the market's full rate. "You found it. Up it goes."

Up it went. The drawer's ceremony ran entire.

The bureau's timesheet took the promotion in the senior hand, per its founding entry's own tradition:

Apprentice, machine grade. Still terrible. Still the best colleague on the floor. Wages raised: one drawer, her own. Retained, permanently, per the original terms, which were, the record shows, forever.
