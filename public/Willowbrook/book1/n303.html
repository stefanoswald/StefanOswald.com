The Tenth Month, Counted

Month 10 — High Summer

The audit convened at the porch swing's amber session with an institutional first, logged before the counting: the subject, present — the evening's gravel having declined the crib's first offer, the walker riding the audit itself out asleep at Papa's shoulder, the almanac balanced one-handed per the whole year's compiled crafts.

The countables, entered at the shoulder's own hushed register. The ledger's column run past chalking — the word-a-week arithmetic overrun by the echo season's volumes, the working count at twenty-odd coins and compounding: the no of the constitutional convention, the nigh-nigh of the country's own dialect, the home of the amber walk's last Tuesday. One beach banked whole, one fair received at shoulder altitude, one sprinkler franchise exercised daily at the gentlest documented rates. One tomato, eaten standing, yield total. One goose egg summited and rainbowed, both patients recovered, the face held at field grade. One kiss administered outbound at a paper cut's margins — the almanac's boxed tenderest. One bear fed nothing and everything at the dresser's dusk. One tractor boarded at four generations' ceremony, one property dispute adjudicated at nine-tenths and a tenth, one cup graduated neat, three hundred nights stoned and speaking both directions.

The uncountables, run at the amber's own length. That the year's grammar had turned again — the sharing economy's whole opening (the airplane posted, the coins spent declaratively, the see-this-with-me arriving at every window) having converted the household's oldest one-way offices into the company the ninth audit forecast: the being-with, the page's earliest dividend, now trading both directions at every documented counter. That the sovereignty's convention — the no's, the refusals, the mine era's opening docket — had seated, per the doctrine's own hopes, a parliament the state was honored at rather than besieged by: the will's arrival received, per the book's whole method, as the year's last great milestone rather than its first great war. And the tenth month's own finding, entered at the shoulder's rise and fall: the audits themselves were closing — two more at the institution's term, the eleventh and the year's own last — and the auditors, per the entry's steadier hand, had begun the counting the almanac was always secretly for: not the month's holdings but the whole year's — the fog's first survivors and the amber porch's fluent citizens being, per every page between, the same two people, compounded.

"Sixty-two nights," said Papa, at the arithmetic's own gate, over the shoulder's cargo.

"Maybe," said Mama, per the economy's whole restored term — and then, at the amber's own register, the dialect's deepest edition: "No. Sixty-two. Some things you count."

Eighteen. Counted. The crickets ran the ledger down the lane's whole length, hedge to hedge, per their office.

The transfer to the crib ran at midnight's own practiced grades, the audit's subject landing per the year's compiled crafts without one surfaced case.

Audited. Approved. Carried forward — asleep at the institution's own session, sixty-two nights wide, almost home.
