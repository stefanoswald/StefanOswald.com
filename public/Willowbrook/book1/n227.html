Night 227

The Swings Open

Month 8: The Velcro Weeks and the Thaw

The park reopened for the household's purposes on the first true-warm Saturday, and the season's inaugural business was conducted, by unanimous petition of one pointing arm, at the swings.

The bucket swing received its passenger per the spring protocols, the seat wiped of the winter's weather, the legs threaded, the grip established on the chains at the height her reach allowed, and the first push was administered at the gentlest setting, from in front, per the veteran counsel the page co-signs: pushing from the front keeps the faces meeting, converting the ride from a thing done to her into a game played between them, approach, retreat, approach, the peekaboo physics of it, the world's best gone-then-back running at pendulum frequency.

The result held the playground briefly still. The face at mid-arc, the wind's first real authorship of her hair, the stomach's small weightless discovery at the swing's crest, the whole apparatus of delight engaged at once, produced a laugh the almanac logged in the top tier of the year's collection: the full-body edition, continuous, self-renewing with each arc, the laugh other benches turned toward and caught like weather.

More, signed the hands at the first pause, with the certified sign of the sixth month. More, at the second pause. More, at the third, the signer's authority absolute, the pusher's arms unionizing quietly.

The page adds the swing's small syllabus to the record while the arcs run. The vestibular feast, the inner ear's whole apparatus, that quiet department behind every balance milestone from the tripod to the wagon, dining out at last on real motion. The rhythm shared, pat-a-cake's lesson at playground scale. And the trust exercised: the letting go into arc after arc, held by chains and bucket and the returning face, the safe-danger curriculum of the chase games now running on hardware.

The colleague arrived mid-session per the Tuesday custom relocated to Saturday. Lily, eleven days junior, installed in the neighboring bucket, and the two ran parallel arcs at their parallel frequencies, regarding each other at the crossings with the solemn mutual study of their whole acquaintance, now conducted at velocity.

The session closed at the far edge of the wise window, one arc past delight's peak, per the doctrine of ending while it's still a game.

The dismount was protested. The protest was noted, seconded by the neighboring bucket, and overruled by both administrations, gently, with snacks.

The swings, said the point, all the way home, backward over the stroller's hood. The swings. The swings.

Open for the season, confirmed the page. All of it, open for the season.
