The Town Delivers

Month 12 — The Candle's Country

The party's final week opened, and the town — unpetitioned, per its entire documented custom — began to deliver.

The parade, logged at the door's own accumulating term. Monday: the farm's truck at the drive's morning gold — the eggs' party ration at Grandpa Joe's own counting ("Henrietta's best; the Duchess contributed under protest; situation ongoing"), one crate of the cellar's winter apples per the orchard's own September provenance, and the chair's companion piece, unannounced at the truck's second trip: a matching step stool, star-carved per the set's own law ("for the sink, when she's washing up; the chair's just the start; furniture comes in families"). Tuesday: Rosa's advance columns — the rolls' party batch at the bakery's own crates, the cake's final confirmation run at the door's professional gravity ("gold star's crowned; you'll see Saturday; first cakes, I keep telling everyone"). Wednesday: the Hendersons' walk-shoveling, conducted at dawn's own unbilled term ahead of the week's forecast front (the knee's "snow before" arriving per calibration; the lane's whole shoveling economy, the page notes, running that morning at the party address's own priority per an ordinance nobody had passed and everybody kept). Thursday: the library's lantern-desk parcel per the Thursday liturgy's own hand — one laminated banner letter at the vault's typography ("we had an O to spare; libraries keep spares of everything") — and the plant-house's pickup consignment: the harbor's own party contribution, four small handprint cards at the colleagues' whole parallel authorship.

And the page's file at the parade's term, entered per the year's entire thesis at its final documented proof. That the town's delivering ran at no logistics the household had drafted — the provisioning arriving per the village's own quiet economy, the walked map's every station shipping unpetitioned at the candle's approach — because the party, per the invitation tour's whole founding finding, was the town's own: the year having been kept at every counter and fence line and desk of it, the keeping now delivering itself to the door at the week's accumulating gold — the village not attending the birthday but provisioning it, constituting it, per the twelve-month record entire: the book's own second author, arriving early, casseroles first.

"Dis? Dis? Dis?" ran the door's own conductor at the parade's every arrival, per the petitioning economy's full week.

Everybody, quoted the market, at every delivery, at the register the week had earned. That's everybody. They all love you. All year.

Nine nights. The town: delivering. The door: gold.

The week, per the almanac's entry: provisioned, shoveled, lettered, and — at every documented arrival — loved.
