Night 185

The Wrong Library Book Maybe

Month 7: The Maybe Lessons

Library day. Five books home. And at story time: "Dis isn't da pony one! Dis is... somebody else's grab!" (The five-book shuffle. A stranger's choice, swapped at the stamping desk. The pony book: somewhere across town, presumably confusing somebody else.)

The wrong book: Volcanoes. Real ones. Photos. Orange and enormous. "... Maybe..." She sighed, and opened it... "Mama. The mountains explode. Dere's melted rock inside the earf. It's called lava. We're standing on a ball of soup with a crust..."

(The volcano obsession: ignited. Three weeks. Every fact, retold at every meal. The why-journal gained a geology wing. The bath became eruption simulations. Miss Dana got briefed daily.) The slip: "Got da wrong book.

Seemed bad. Da wrong book was volcanoes. Now I know da earf is soup with a crust. Best wrong grab ever. Maybe caught." (The pony book, returned unread by whoever got it, was eventually checked out properly. Answer: "fine. No lava." Some wrong turns outrank the right ones. The jar knows.)
