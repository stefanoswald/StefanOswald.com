Night 356

The Party Preparations

Month 12: Almost Four

Party eve. The house: mobilized. The operations, by division: Streamers: Papa, ladder, gold and blue ("da year's colors... gold for da meadow, blue for da winter sky. Da house wears da year for one day").

The story corner: the birthday girl, personally. The games: staked out. The cake: due at ten. (The sky. Under cloth. Rosa's van. The whole block knows to watch for it.) And Theo, the secret-keeper, nine days strong now... patrolling his own operation: the couch-back office sealed, the gift wrapped, staged, guarded.

("Is tomorrow, wivy. Den no more secret. Secret is heavy.") (Secret is heavy. The story concurs. Nine days is a long haul for a small courier. Tomorrow he sets it down.) Last walkthrough, lights half- out, the house holding its breath of streamers: "It's all ready..."

She reported, at the lamp. "Da whole day's just... Waiting out dere. Assembled. Big day tomorrow. Da last day of free... I'm gonna do it good. You should finish a year like you finish a book.

All da way. No skipping da end." Finish a year like you finish a book. Goodnight, almost-Four. The house is dressed. The sky is in the van. The day is assembled. Sleep, you've got an ending to read tomorrow, and it's a good one.
