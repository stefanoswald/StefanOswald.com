Night 253

Brave for Someone Else

Month 9: Brave Is Scared Plus One Step

Waiting room, this week. And across the room: The recruit. White-faced. Shot day. His booster. The terror: visible from four chairs away. And the veteran... 0.5 Duchesses, watched-it-herself, armor-installed... crossed the waiting room and lent the whole rule: "Hey.

Shot day? I did mine. Wanna know everything? No surprises is half da fix. Free seconds. Dat's da whole pinch. I counted mine. You can count yours... counting makes it visible and visible is smaller.

Squeeze your mama's hand... dat's what dey're for. And here's da secret rating: it's half a Duchess... What's a Duchess? Da meanest chicken in da world. She pecked me once. Da shot is half dat.

You've probably had worse from a lego." (He had. Everyone has.) The recruit went in armed: the count, the squeeze, the Duchess scale. Came out: alive. Proud. "Half a duchess. Maybe less." And the veteran, collecting her own sticker at checkout: "Brave is easier with a briefing.

Somebody briefed me once. It's just... da rounds." Just the rounds. Courage: another thing the ground crew delivers. Waiting rooms: now on the route.
