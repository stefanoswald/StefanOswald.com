Night 194

The Too-Short Haircut Maybe

Month 7: The Maybe Lessons

The haircut went... short. (The trim. The curls bounced up after cutting, curl math; even professionals misjudge it, and the result was two inches past the plan.) The mirror consult: grim. "I look like a dandelion...

Maybe?" The maybes, arriving across the week: Morning one: No tangles. Morning three: "wash and go, Mama. My hair is fast now." The swim lesson: no soggy curtain- hair in the eyes. And week three: the springback.

Curls, cut, come back bouncier, curl math works both ways, "My boings are stronger." The slip: "Da haircut went too short. Seemed bad. Got fast mornings and better swimming and stronger boings. Hair grows. Maybes faster.

Caught." (The dandelion comment aged well too: "dandelions..." She noted later. "are da best flower. I insulted myself with a compliment." this month does that. Reframes all the way down.)
