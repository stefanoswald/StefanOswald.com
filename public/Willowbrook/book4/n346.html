Night 346

The Wish Research

Month 12: Almost Four

The wish. (The candle one. Four's wish. Rules, per tradition: one wish, sealed by secrecy, released by blowing, told to no one... "or it leaks out da telling hole.") And this year, befitting a reader and list-maker: The wish got researched.

(The notebook. A shortlist. Days of big think, consultancy provided by the bear... retained, as ever, for his listening.) Da fifth branch." (Struck through: "no... dat's a yet not a wish. I'll just climb it.

Wishes are for things you can't ladder to.") "2. Sno in summer.3. Da willow tells me the hole sentence." (Struck, and this one gently: "no. Da tree's saving it. You don't wish somebody's story out of dem early.

Not even a tree's.") "4. [The fourth entry is not struck through. It is also not written. Just a small drawing: two figures and a bear and a gold light, and around them, a circle.]" (A circle around everyone.

It notes only: whatever it was, precisely, it was the kind you draw instead of spell... and the kind, the far pages suggest, that mostly came true, year over year, by the ordinary magic of a family that keeps its circle drawn.) Sealed in the treasure box till Saturday. The bear: sworn. "You're da only one who saw. And you don't leak. Sealed. Ready. Four days.
