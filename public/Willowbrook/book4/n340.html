Night 340

Planning the Park Party

Month 12: Almost Four

Olivia, Lily, and June planned the birthday party at their park table.

"Invite everybody," Olivia said.

They made a list long enough to cover two pages. Then came the games: June proposed a bug hunt, Lily chose musical cushions, and Olivia wanted a story corner where she could read to the younger children.

"What kind of cake?" Lily asked.

Olivia drew a dark blue circle covered with small gold stars. "A constellation cake. Everyone gets one star."

Theo leaned over the table. "I get two."

"You get one on the cake and one on your napkin," Olivia negotiated.

Rain plans required the pavilion. Shy guests would have the quiet story corner. The invited dog would need a bowl of water.

By sunset the girls had solved everything except how to fit all the names on one invitation.

"Small writing," June suggested.

"Bigger paper," Olivia replied.

They chose bigger paper. A party for everybody should begin with room enough for everybody's name.
