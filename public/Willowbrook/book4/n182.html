Night 182

The Maybe Month Opens

Month 7: The Maybe Lessons

This month, the family word gets its own chapter. Maybe. (You know it by now. The spilled milk and the snowplow. The grocery crash and the spaghetti. Life doesn't happen to us, it happens for us.

Four books of it, working.) And this month's game, announced at breakfast: Maybe-catching. "When something seems bad, we say maybe, and we watch. And when it turns good, we catch it. Out loud. We're gonna catch a whole month of dem."

The jar went up on the counter, the kindness jar's cousin... labeled in crayon: The maybe jar. Every caught maybe: a slip inside. "How many will we catch? Maybe lots." (The word, already working. It never stops working.

That's the whole month ahead: proof, slip by slip, that the family's oldest magic is just patience wearing a question mark.)
