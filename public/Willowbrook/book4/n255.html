Night 255

The Apology to Marcus

Month 9: Brave Is Scared Plus One Step

Truth page. She did a mean thing. (The block corner. A dispute. Marcus knocked her build... accident, probably... and she said, hot- chested, in front of everyone: "You wreck everything. Nobody wants you at da blocks.") (...

Nobody wants you. The words that stick. She knew it the second they landed... watched them land... watched Marcus's face do the thing. Too late. Said.) The evening: heavy. The lamp: consulted. "I said a sticky- mean thing.

Da kind dat stays in people. Then you know what it needs... Da second-sorry. Da expensive one. In front of people, because da mean was in front of people." (The symmetry rule. Self-derived.

Public wounds need public mending. The hardest edition there is.) The next morning, at the blocks, unprompted, witnessed: "Marcus. Yesterday I said nobody wants you at da blocks. Dat was mean and it wasn't even true... you build da best ramps of anybody.

I was mad about my tower and I frew words instead. I'm sorry... Wanna build? You do ramps. I do towers. Dey're better togedder anyway." (Marcus, four, generous the way kids are when repair comes real: "Okay.

I'm sorry I knocked it. It was kind of on purpose. A little.") (A little. The full truth, surfacing both directions... which real repair always invites.) The ramp-tower alliance: founded that morning. The sticky words: unstuck the only way they ever unstick. Bravest step of this month. No ladder involved.
