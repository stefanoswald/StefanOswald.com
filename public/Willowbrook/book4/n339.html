Night 339

What Four Can Do

Month 12: Almost Four

Drawn up this week, at the lamp, in her own hand: "Wat for can do. A list." The items, as entered: "1. Reed. (For reeds. Free lerned but For gets good.)2. Rite leders. 3. Da deep end.

(Already mine. For keeps it.)4. Big sled hill. 5. Teach Theo. (Da leders. Da brave rule. Wotever he needs.)6. Stay up ladder. " And the last item, added after the lamp went low, discovered by Mama at morning: "7.

Miss free a littel. (Its allowed. Free was good.)" Miss Three a little. It's allowed. Three was good. (The wisest line on the list. Growing up in this family has never meant throwing the old years away, the frame keeps the lines; the chest keeps the sweaters; the story keeps everything.

Four gets the deep end and the letters and the whole hill. And Three... Three gets missed a little, honorably, line item seven, forever on the books.)
