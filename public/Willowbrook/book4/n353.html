Night 353

The Fourth Maybe Night

Month 12: Almost Four

On Night 353, the family lit a candle and brought down the maybe jar.

They retold the story that had begun the tradition. Nine days before Olivia's birth, the car would not start during a blizzard. Mama had been frightened. Papa had said, "Maybe it is happening for us."

Then a snowplow came down Maple Lane and cleared the way to the doctor.

This year Theo had practiced the final line.

"Perfect timing!" he shouted, nearly blowing out the candle.

Then each person offered a new maybe.

Olivia named the larger school waiting next year. "Maybe it is big in a way that helps me grow."

Theo named the big slide. Mama and Papa spoke their own quieter hopes.

They were not predicting that every frightening thing would become pleasant. The word simply kept one door open while they waited to learn the rest.

Together they blew out the candle and returned the jar to its shelf.

Four years of maybe slips waited inside, some answered, some changed, and some still becoming.
