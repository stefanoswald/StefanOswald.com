Night 153

Visit Day

Month 6: Preschool, Turns, and Shares

Before school began, Olivia visited her classroom with Mama.

Her name was printed above a cubby. Lily's cubby stood beside it. There was a round rug for stories, an easel by the windows, and a row of small rest mats in the corner.

Olivia checked the room in careful circles.

"Where does my backpack go? Where is the bathroom? Can Bobby Bear visit?"

Ms. Alvarez showed her the hook, the hallway door, and a high shelf where special bears could wait during class.

Olivia sat in the chair with her name on it. Her feet did not quite reach the floor.

"On the first day, will you tell us what to do?" she asked.

"One thing at a time," Ms. Alvarez promised.

On the way out, Olivia touched the edge of her cubby.

"Keep my place safe," she whispered. "I will be back."

Outside, she looked up at Mama. The building was still large, but now it contained a chair, a shelf, a teacher, and a place already waiting for her name.
