Night 140

The Bath Captain's Mate

Month 5: Little Brother Tags Along

The two-kid bath: years running now. But this month it got an org chart: Captain: her. (Seniority. The plug ceremony... hers since the old compromise, remains the captain's honor. Bye, water. Gurgle. Tradition.) First mate: him.

Duties, assigned and defended: the rinse cup. The duck deployment. (Esquire plus his duck, the birthday one, launched together, per fleet regulations.) And the splash contest: co-judged. (Everyone wins. The floor towel loses. Eternal.)

The mate performs his offices with two-year-old gravity... the rinse poured over her head gently, the ducks launched at the correct ceremonial moment. And at bath's end, the ceremony, run in order: Captain pulls the plug. "Bye, water!"

Mate waves it down. "Bye-bye wawa!" Both watch the spiral. (The old tornado. Two heads, one drain. The oldest physics show in the house, now with double attendance.) Two burritos, hooded, pink. "Good bath, crew.

Good bath, cap'n" The org chart holds. The fleet floats. The floor towel serves. All hands: washed, warm, accounted for.
