Night 11

The Restaurant of Mud

Month 1: Pretend!

The mud corner has gone upscale. Last year: mud pies. This year: a restaurant. "Welcome to Olivia's. Table for two? Dis way." The guests were seated on the garden bench. The menu, recited: "Today we got mud soup.

Mud cake. Leaf salad. And da special: mud with pebbles." (The special came garnished. Three pebbles, arranged. Presentation matters at this establishment.) The courses arrived on leaf plates, narrated: "Da soup is hot. Blow first."

(The guests blew on the mud. The mud was blown upon. Service here is immersive.) Theo, method actor, nearly tasted the special... "No. Pretend eating. Like dis...we don't really eat da restaurant." (House rule, posted after one close call: the food is art.

The art is dirt. Mmm at it only.) The bill: four buttons. The tip: a dandelion. "Thank you! Come again!" They came again. Daily. All summer. The restaurant earned zero stars from the health department and five from every guest it ever seated... the correct ratings, both, for the best worst restaurant in town.
