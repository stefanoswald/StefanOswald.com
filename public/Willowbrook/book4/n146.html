Night 146

The Berry Law, Amended

Month 5: Little Brother Tags Along

Berry season. The patch. And the ancient law, taught to its newest adherent: "Fee-oh. Da berry law: Half for da pail, half for da picker. Dat's da deal. Gampa follows it. I follow it.

Even da duchess follows it and she follows nothing." The new adherent's compliance rate, checked at the weigh-in: Pail: four berries. Picker: ... Olivia was facial. Purple. Comprehensive. Chin to eyebrows. "Fee-oh. Da pail gets half.

I halfed! You halfed your face!" (The check dissolved. Everyone's check dissolved. Grandpa's dissolved first... the whole quiet laugh, hat off, leaning on the post.) The new rule, drafted on the spot by the senior adherent: "Okay.

New law for twos: da pail gets some. Some is da two- year-old half." Some is the two- year-old half. (Agreed. The law scales by age now. Legal scholars take note: this is how all good law works... the principle constant, the percentage developmental.)

The pails went home light. The pickers went home purple. The pie got made anyway..." A some pie. "by the baker's note... and tasted, everyone agreed, like the best law on the books: half justice, half mercy, all berries.
