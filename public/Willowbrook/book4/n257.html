Night 257

The Lost Tooth Preview

Month 9: Brave Is Scared Plus One Step

A fence-line revelation this week: Mateo's tooth is gone. (Front one. The gap: magnificent. The grin: renovated. He whistled through it, showing off... a gap whistle, a whole new instrument.) "What happened to your tooth?!

Fell out. Dey do that. Baby teeth get wiggly and fall out and big ones grow in. Everybody's do. Yours will... Mine will?!" (The mirror, consulted at home, urgently. Every tooth: tested. All: firm. Relief and, note it... disappointment, both.)

The briefing, at the lamp: "Your teeth are baby teeth... starter set. Around five or six, they get wiggly, one at a time, and fall out, no blood to speak of, doesn't hurt, mostly it's weird and then exciting... and the big ones push up underneath. It's not breaking.

It's upgrading. Like da sweater becoming da hat." Like exactly that. Nothing loved gets lost. Even teeth. Remade. "And da fairy? The fairy is... a family decision. We'll decide when the first wiggle comes." The first wiggle: a year-plus out.

(Book five. The gap year. It's adorable. It affects her whistle for a month and improves it forever.) This month: the preview. Scheduled. Saved under upgrades, not fears. The body's next brave: on the calendar, welcome, weird-then- exciting, by the briefing. Exactly per the briefing.
