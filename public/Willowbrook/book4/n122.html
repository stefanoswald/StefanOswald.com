Night 122

The Fort Rules, Amended

Month 5: Little Brother Tags Along

Thursday. The fort. And a constitutional crisis: Theo is in everything now. The old fort had a Theo spot. The new Theo doesn't stay in spots. So: the new rule session. Three delegates. The Theo parts, as posted: One: Theo may enter da fort.

It's his too. Two: Theo may not knock da fort. Knocking is for da sacrifice tower only. Free: Theo gets a job every game. Four: if Theo naps, we save his spot and his snack.

The session closed with the fort intact and all parties snacked. "Being free with a two is hard..." She told Mama after. "But he's my two." He's her two. The constitution holds. It was always going to hold. She wrote it that way.
