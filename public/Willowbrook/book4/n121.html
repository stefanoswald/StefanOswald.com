Night 121

The Shadow With Feet

Month 5: Little Brother Tags Along

Theo is two now. Fully two. Walking- running-talking two. And his entire life plan, executed daily: Be where livy is. She builds: he arrives. She draws: he requires a crayon. She goes upstairs: feet on the stairs behind her.

She closes a door: knock. Knock. "Wivy?" (He says it "Wivy" now. The L is coming. Nobody corrects it. Some mispronunciations are treasure. The family hoards this one.) "He follows me all da times..." She reports.

The old complaint. The old glow under it. "Remember the schedule?" says Mama. "'He'll run behind you everywhere, calling your name, forever.'... Dis is da forever part?" This is the forever part. It started with a crawl aimed across a rug.

It doesn't end. It just changes speeds. "Okay..." She sighs, handing him a crayon. "But he colors on da paper, not my duck." Terms: set. Shadow: seated. Month: begun.
