Night 69

Why Is Theo Little

Month 3: Why? Why? Why?

Mid-game this week, mid-frustration the why came out sharp: "Why is Fee-oh so little? He can't do da good games!" The answer waited for calm, then went to the door frame. The ladder of marks.

"Find your first mark. That was you. You couldn't do any games. You ate the pieces. I did not. You absolutely did. Ask the almanac. Now find Theo's mark. He's not little, love. He's early.

He's exactly where the climb puts him... same wall, same ladder, different rung. You were his rung once. He'll be your rung. The whole family is just one long climb at different heights." She studied the wall.

The rungs. The math of it. "... So when he's free like me, I'll be..." Almost five. "And da games I like den will be too big for free-me's games...so somebody always gotta wait for somebody."

Somebody always has to wait for somebody. That's families. That's the ladder. The waiting is the together. She went back and simplified the game. (He thrived.) The ladder holds everyone. The wall has room going up forever.
