Night 196

The Double-Booked Thursday Maybe

Month 7: The Maybe Lessons

A scheduling error: Both friends. Same Thursday. Double- booked. The worry, real: "But Wiwi's my firsday friend and June's my park friend and what if dey... don't match?" (The friend-mixing fear. Ancient. Everyone's had it.

What if my worlds don't like each other?) "... Maybe," offered Mama, guilty and hopeful. And the maybe: The trio. (It took twenty minutes of triangle- awkward. The old two-games problem, three-player edition. And then June... quiet June, slow- talking June... said: "...my cousin plays a game called flood castle?"

Flood castle. The merged game. The game. Traveled to June's cousin via the recruit via the whole kid-network of town... come back around to its own founders as a stranger's treasure.) Three queens. One flooded castle.

The trio: founded. The slip: "Booked both friends by accident. Seemed bad. Now we're free. Da trio. And Flood Castle went around da whole town and came home. Caught." (The trio held for decades. Ask the far books. Some of the best institutions start as calendar errors. Most of them, maybe.)
