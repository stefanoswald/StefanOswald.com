Night 193

The Flat Tire Maybe

Month 7: The Maybe Lessons

The car: Flat. "... Maybe," said the back seat, on reflex now. And the maybe was: The walk home. Through blocks they'd never walked, the car blocks, the drive-past blocks... which held, it turned out: a little free library.

(A box on a post. Books inside. Take one, leave one. A whole tiny lantern- library, three blocks from home, undiscovered for years. One book taken. One, next trip... left.) A shortcut. And a cat.

The slip: "Da tire went flat. Seemed bad. Founded a book box and a shortcut and a new cat. Da car hides da town. Feet find it. Maybe caught." (The tire cost forty dollars. The book box gave for a decade. The town's best math, again: breakdowns pay walkers.)
