Night 187

The Sick Thursday Maybe

Month 7: The Maybe Lessons

Thursday: Lily sick. The gloom: familiar. "... Maybe," she said... practiced now, half-believing it in advance, which is the skill this month is building... and looked around for what the maybe might be. It was: Time.

A whole free Thursday. And the fort... beloved, worn, architecturally stale, got: The great renovation. New cushion walls. A second room. ("Da quiet room. For togedder-quiet. Wren gave me da idea without saying anything.

Dat's how Wren gives ideas.") A flag. A mail slot. An art wall. Theo's spot: upgraded to "da whole east wing." And Friday, when a recovered Lily arrived: "Surprise. The fort's a palace now." The slip: "Firsday broke.

Seemed bad. Built da palace instead. Now every Firsday after happens in a palace. Maybe caught." (The renovation became annual. Fort setup week. The maybes don't just fix the broken day... they upgrade all the days after. That's compound interest. The jar is teaching economics now.)
