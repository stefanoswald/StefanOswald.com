Night 202

Two Snow Days

Month 7: The Maybe Lessons

A blizzard closed Willowbrook for two whole days.

On the first morning Papa made pancakes while Olivia and Theo built a fort beneath the kitchen table. Later the family shoveled the walk, built a lopsided snow wall, and came inside for cocoa with wet mittens steaming by the stove.

On the second day, the wind softened. Mama read on the sofa. Papa played three quiet songs on the guitar. Olivia and Theo lay inside their blanket fort without inventing any game at all.

"We are doing nothing," Theo whispered.

"We are snowed in," Olivia corrected. "It only looks like nothing."

By evening the plows had cleared Maple Lane. Tomorrow everyone would return to work and school.

Olivia stood at the window and watched the last flakes cross the streetlight.

"The snow gave us two days," she said.

Mama put an arm around her. "The days were always there. The snow helped us notice them."

Olivia decided that was even better.
