Energy for Energy

We create connections with the power of reciprocity.

♥ Skill sharing and education
♦ Temporary trading and renting
♣ Bartering and purchasing

Lasting friendships are formed from simple beginnings like asking a neighbor for a spoonful of salt.
What you give in this world, you get back. We help make sure of that =)

Not only are we a cryptocurrency, but we are also the future of all e-commerce. Anything someone could offer for rent or sale will be available through our app, and any skill or work that someone can provide is also here. Additionally, we are a place that connects those who teach and those who desire knowledge. We do this through masterminds and workshops, both digitally and in person.
