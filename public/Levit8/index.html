Experiments

EXP 001 🎩 NexusCoach Testing
Hypothesis

A one-on-one AI mentor with adaptive micro-steps and gamified missions gets a beginner to their first successful rep faster than video instructions alone, with less overwhelm.

Measures

Time to first completed mission · struggle-button usage · lesson completion rate · return visits. Mentor is scripted in this build; live AI lands in a later iteration.
EXP 002 🧭 Intent Intake Testing
Hypothesis

Opening with "What do you want to accomplish?" and generating a personal path beats a fixed course menu on engagement and confidence.

Measures

A/B: intent intake vs course menu (50/50, force with ?variant=intake or ?variant=menu). Confidence before and after · time to first win · intake completion. Telemetry logs locally per device.
EXP 004 🧙 Vega, the Avatar Mentor Testing
Hypothesis

A mentor with a face, expressions, and memory of your journey beats a faceless UI on return visits and follow-through. Stage 0: static expressions + cross-experiment journey awareness. Next: live AI, voice, motion.

Measures

Suggestion follow-through (did they go where Vega pointed?) · return visits · quiz engagement · journey detection rate. Image slots ready for generated character art.
EXP 003 🎚️ Adaptive Difficulty Engine Backlog
Hypothesis

Auto-adjusting step size from struggle signals (not quiz scores) keeps learners in flow and reduces drop-off at the hardest lesson.

Measures

Drop-off point migration · micro-step entry rate · completion rate for the historically hardest lesson.

Lab principles

Move fast, expect failure

Most experiments will not survive. That is the point of a lab.

Measure learning, not completion

Time to first success and confidence growth beat checkmarks.

Build a platform, not a product

Every component must be reusable for the next domain: magic today, golf and enterprise onboarding tomorrow.

Teach only the next step

Confidence first. No lectures, no overload, no manuals.
