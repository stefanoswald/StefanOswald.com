How it works

One run. One time. Everything counts.

01

The clock never stops

From the start gate to the buzzer, one timer runs. Your score IS your raw time. No penalties, no bonuses, no fine print.

02

Redo it till it's done

Fall in the water? Climb again. Botch the memory grid? New pattern. Lose at chess? New game. Every retry happens on your clock.

03

10 shots or you're out

Paintball, archery, knife, ax: 10 shots to hit 3 targets, each. Use all 10 without all 3 hits and your run ends. Disqualified.

04

No buyouts. No mercy.

You can't skip a station, buy your way past, or take a penalty instead. Climbers get walled by archery, sharpshooters gas out on the Salmon Ladder — the champion does it all.
